How Much Does A Double Garage Door Electric Cost?

If you're looking to upgrade your residential garage door with an electric opener, you may be wondering how much this upgrade will cost. After all, you want to make sure that you get the best value for your money without spending too much. In this article, we'll take a look at the average cost of a double garage door electric and what factors can affect the price.

Average Cost of a Double Garage Door Electric

The average cost of a double garage door electric opener is around $500. This price includes the cost of the motor, the rails, and the installation. However, depending on the size and type of door you need, the costs can range anywhere from $400 to $800 for an average residential garage door. For a larger industrial-grade door, you can expect to pay anywhere from $800 to $1,500.

Factors that Affect the Cost of Your Double Garage Door Electric

In addition to the size and type of garage door you need, there are other factors that can affect the cost of your double garage door electric opener. The brand and model of the opener can also affect the cost, as well as the quality of the materials used. If you choose a higher-end model or a more durable material, then you can expect to pay more for your opener. Additionally, the installation process can add to the cost of the opener, depending on the complexity of the setup.

For example, if you need to install a new motor and rails, then the labor costs can add significantly to the total cost of the project. In addition, if you need to modify the door to accommodate the opener, then the costs can be even higher. Finally, the location of the installation can also affect the cost, as some areas may be more expensive than others.

DIY Installation or Professional Installation?

If you're comfortable with DIY projects and have some experience with garage door opener installation, then you may be able to save some money by installing the opener yourself. However, if you're not sure of the process or don't feel confident in your ability to install the opener safely, then it's probably best to hire a professional. Professional installers can handle the job quickly and safely, saving you time and money in the long run.

How to Find the Best Deal for Your Double Garage Door Electric

When looking for the best deal on your double garage door electric, it's important to shop around and compare prices. Look for discounts from local retailers and online stores, as well as special offers from manufacturers. Additionally, you can often find great deals on used openers, as long as you take the time to inspect them for any damage or defects.

Finally, you can try negotiating with the store or the installer to get a better price. If you're willing to purchase the opener and the installation services together, you may be able to get a better deal. Additionally, if you're purchasing multiple openers or a large order, many retailers will offer discounts or bundle deals.

Conclusion

A double garage door electric opener can be a great upgrade for your home, but you want to make sure that you get the best value for your money. The average cost of a double garage door electric opener is around $500, but the final price will depend on the size and type of door you need, as well as the brand and model of the opener. Additionally, you can save money by shopping around, looking for discounts, and even negotiating with the store or installer.

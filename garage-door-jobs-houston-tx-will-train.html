Garage Door Technician Training in Houston, TX

Once you’ve determined the type of job you want, the next step is to find a garage door technician training program in the Houston area. Types of Garage Doors You Might Work With

When working as a garage door technician, you’ll likely be working with a variety of different garage doors. Most residential garages have either a sectional garage door or a tilt-up garage door. Sectional garage doors are made up of several panels that are hinged together and roll up and down when opened and closed. Tilt-up garage doors, on the other hand, are single-piece doors that tilt up from the bottom and slide open. Commercial garages may have a variety of different types of garage doors. These may include rolling steel doors, fire-rated doors, and high-speed doors. It’s important to be familiar with all types of garage doors in order to be successful in the industry. You should also be aware of the various components and parts associated with each type of garage door, as well as the maintenance and repair procedures.

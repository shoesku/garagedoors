What is a Garage Door Draft Excluder?

A garage door draft excluder is a device that is placed on the bottom of a garage door to create a seal between the door and the floor, preventing air from escaping. The device is usually made of foam or rubber and has a handle or knob to make it easy to attach and remove. This can be a great way to keep the temperature in your garage consistent and comfortable, reducing energy costs and helping to maintain your home's overall energy efficiency.

Benefits of Using a Garage Door Draft Excluder

Using a garage door draft excluder can have many benefits. The most obvious benefit is the reduction in energy costs. By preventing air from escaping, you can keep your garage temperature more consistent, reducing the need to use air conditioning or heating to maintain a comfortable temperature. This can save you money on your energy bills and help you reduce your carbon footprint.

Another benefit of using a garage door draft excluder is that it can help to prevent pests from entering your garage. Many insects and rodents are drawn to warm, moist environments, and a draft excluder can act as a barrier to keep them out. This can help to protect your home from pests and keep them from entering your home and causing damage.

In addition, a garage door draft excluder can also help to reduce noise from outside. If you live in a busy neighborhood or area, the noise from traffic, people, and other sources can be distracting and disruptive. By using a draft excluder, you can help to reduce the amount of noise that comes into your garage, allowing you to enjoy a more peaceful environment.

Choosing the Right Garage Door Draft Excluder

When it comes to choosing the right garage door draft excluder, there are a few things to consider. The first is the size and shape of your garage door. Different types of draft excluders are designed to fit different sizes and shapes of doors, so you'll need to make sure you get the right one. Additionally, you'll also want to consider the material that it is made out of. Most draft excluders are made of either foam or rubber, and each material has its own advantages and disadvantages.

You'll also want to consider the installation process. Some draft excluders are relatively easy to install and can be done in just a few minutes, while others may require more time and effort. You'll want to make sure that you get one that is easy to install and that will last for a long time.

Finally, when it comes to choosing the right draft excluder, you'll also want to think about the cost. There are a variety of different types and brands available, so you'll need to make sure you get one that is within your budget.

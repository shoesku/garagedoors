Garage Door Spring Replacement Average Cost

Garage door springs are an essential component of any garage door. The springs help the door to open and close, allowing you to access your garage with ease. Unfortunately, these springs are not always reliable and will eventually wear out over time. When they do, they will need to be replaced. The cost of replacing a garage door spring can vary depending on the type of spring you need and the complexity of the job. In this article, we will discuss the average cost of garage door spring replacement.

Factors Affecting the Cost of Garage Door Spring Replacement

The cost of replacing your garage door springs can vary depending on a number of factors. The type of springs you need is one of the main factors that will affect the cost. If you need torsion springs, for example, these will be more expensive than extension springs. The size of the springs and the weight of the door will also impact the cost. If your door is heavy, you may need larger springs, which will also be more expensive. The complexity of the job is also a factor. If you have a complex door system, you may need to hire a professional to replace the springs. This will add to the cost.

Average Cost of Garage Door Spring Replacement

The average cost of replacing a garage door spring in 2023 is between $150 and $300. This cost can vary depending on the factors mentioned above. If you need to replace a pair of springs, the cost will likely be higher. If you are replacing the springs yourself, you may need to purchase additional tools, which could add to the cost. Also, if you hire a professional to replace the springs, you can expect the cost to be higher.

How to Save Money when Replacing Garage Door Springs

Replacing your garage door springs can be expensive, so it is important to try and save money wherever possible. One of the best ways to do this is to purchase the springs yourself. You can buy them online or at a local hardware store. This will help you to avoid paying for the mark up that a professional garage door company might charge. You should also consider purchasing the springs in bulk, as this can help to reduce the cost.

Are Professional Installations Necessary?

When it comes to replacing your garage door springs, it is not always necessary to hire a professional. If you have the right tools and experience, you may be able to do the job yourself. However, if you are not confident in your ability, it is best to leave it to the professionals. This will ensure that the job is done correctly and that you do not run the risk of damaging your door or the springs.

Conclusion

Replacing your garage door springs can be a costly task. The cost of the job will depend on the type of springs you need, the complexity of the job, and whether or not you hire a professional. The average cost of replacing a garage door spring in 2023 is between $150 and $300. To save money, you should consider purchasing the springs yourself and installing them yourself, if you have the right tools and experience.

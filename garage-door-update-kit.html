Updating Your Garage Door: A Guide To Installing A Garage Door Update Kit

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Are you looking to give your garage door a fresh look and improved functionality? Installing a garage door update kit can do just that. Garage door update kits provide homeowners with a cost-effective way to upgrade their garage door and keep it looking and working like new. In this guide, we’ll discuss what a garage door update kit is, the components of a kit, and how to install it. Read on to learn more.

What Is a Garage Door Update Kit?

A garage door update kit is a pre-packaged collection of components that are designed to enhance the look and performance of a garage door. Update kits typically include new springs, rollers, cables, and other hardware, as well as a new weather seal. The components of the kit are made to fit standard garage door sizes, and are designed to last longer than the original parts.

Benefits of Installing a Garage Door Update Kit

Installing a garage door update kit is a great way to upgrade your existing garage door without the expense of a complete replacement. Update kits can provide a range of benefits, including:

  • Enhanced appearance: A garage door update kit offers a great way to give your garage door a fresh, new look without having to replace the entire door.
  • Improved performance: New springs, rollers, and cables can help to improve the performance of your door, making it easier to open and close.
  • Protection from the elements: A new weather seal can help to protect your garage from the elements, keeping it dry and free from drafts.
  • Cost savings: Installing a garage door update kit is much less expensive than replacing the entire door.

Components of a Garage Door Update Kit

Garage door update kits typically include the following components:

  • Springs: The springs are one of the most important components of a garage door, as they help to counterbalance the weight of the door. The springs in the kit are designed to last longer than the original springs.
  • Rollers: The rollers help to keep the door running smoothly and reduce friction. The rollers in the kit are designed to be more durable than the original rollers.
  • Cables: The cables help to keep the door on track and provide tension to the springs. The cables in the kit are made of higher-quality materials than the original cables.
  • Hardware: The kit includes new nuts and bolts to replace any worn or damaged components.
  • Weather seal: The weather seal helps to keep the garage dry and free from drafts. The seal in the kit is designed to last longer than the original seal.

How to Install a Garage Door Update Kit

Installing a garage door update kit is relatively straightforward. However, it is important to bear in mind that the process involves working with springs, which can be dangerous if not handled properly. It is therefore recommended that you hire a professional garage door technician for the job. Below is a general overview of the installation process.

Step 1: Gather the Components

The first step is to gather the components of the kit and ensure you have all the necessary tools. Make sure you have the following before you start:

  • Garage door update kit
  • Ladder
  • Socket wrench
  • Screwdrivers
  • Pliers
  • Wire cutters
  • Safety goggles

Step 2: Prepare the Garage Door

Before you start the installation, you need to prepare the garage door. Start by disconnecting the power source to the garage door opener and removing any obstruction from the door’s path. Then, use the ladder to access the top of the door and remove the nuts and bolts that secure the springs. Be sure to use caution when removing the springs, as they are under tension and can be dangerous.

Step 3: Install the Components

Now you are ready to install the components of the update kit. Start by attaching the new springs to the top of the door. Then, install the new rollers, cables, and other hardware, making sure to tighten all nuts and bolts securely. Finally, attach the weather seal and reconnect the power source to the garage door opener.

Step 4: Test the Installation

Once you have installed the components of the update kit, it is important to test the installation before you use the door. Open and close the door several times to ensure it is operating properly. If all is working as it should, your garage door update kit is now installed and ready for use.

Conclusion

Installing a garage door update kit is a great way to upgrade your existing garage door and give it a fresh, new look. Update kits are relatively easy to install, and can provide homeowners with a cost-effective way to improve their door’s performance and increase its lifespan. Now that you know what a garage door update kit is and how to install it, you can start shopping for the right kit for your door. Good luck!

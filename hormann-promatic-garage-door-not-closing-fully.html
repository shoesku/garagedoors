Introduction

Garage doors are one of the most important parts of a home or commercial building. Not only do they keep the building secure, but they can also be used to add aesthetic value to the building. Many homeowners and business owners opt for Hormann Promatic garage doors to get the highest level of security, style, and convenience. However, even the most reliable garage door can sometimes malfunction. One of the most common issues is when the door does not close fully. If you find yourself in this situation, read on to learn what to do.

Common Reasons Why Your Hormann Promatic Garage Door is Not Closing Fully

There are several common causes for a garage door not closing fully. It could be due to the door spring being broken, the tracks being misaligned, or the door opener being out of sync. If the door is not opening or closing properly, it could also be that the motor is out of balance. Whatever the issue may be, it is important to get it resolved quickly and professionally so that you can enjoy the convenience and security of your garage door.

DIY Fixes

If you are the type of person who likes to take on DIY projects, you may be tempted to try to fix the door yourself. However, it is important to remember that a Hormann Promatic garage door is a complex system and should only be handled by a professional. Attempting to fix the door yourself could lead to further damage and even injury. If you are not sure about a repair, it is best to leave it to the experts.

Hiring a Professional

When it comes to repairing a Hormann Promatic garage door, it is best to hire a professional. Not only do they have the expertise and the right tools to make sure the repair is done correctly, but they can also provide you with advice on how to maintain the door in the future. The cost of hiring a professional will be significantly less than replacing the entire door, so it is definitely worth considering.

Tips to Avoid Future Issues

Once the repair is done, there are a few things you can do to ensure that your Hormann Promatic garage door continues to function properly. First, it is important to make sure that the door is lubricated regularly. This will help to prevent rust and keep it running smoothly. Second, it is important to check the tracks for any debris or dirt, as this can prevent the door from closing fully. Finally, it is a good idea to have a professional inspect the door annually to make sure everything is in working order.

Conclusion

If your Hormann Promatic garage door is not closing fully, it is important to take action quickly. DIY fixes should be avoided, as they can lead to further damage and injury. Instead, it is best to hire a professional to take care of the issue. Once the repair is done, be sure to follow the tips provided to help avoid future issues. With proper care, your Hormann Promatic garage door can continue to provide convenience, security, and style for years to come.

Common Garage Door Opener Issues

The most common garage door opener issues you may run into include a door that won't open or close, a door that opens and closes too slowly, or a door that opens and closes erratically. These issues can be caused by a variety of factors, including worn out parts, a malfunctioning remote, or a power outage. No matter what the cause, it's important to have your opener fixed as soon as possible.

How to Prevent Garage Door Opener Issues

In addition to hiring a professional repair service for garage door opener issues, there are a few things you can do to prevent them in the first place.

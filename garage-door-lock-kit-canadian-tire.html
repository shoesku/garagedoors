Types Of Garage Door Lock Kits

When it comes to garage door locks, Canadian Tire offers a variety of options. Depending on your needs, you can find a variety of models, including keyless entry locks, deadbolts, and combination locks. You can also find garage door locks with additional features, such as keypads, motion sensors, and timers.

Keyless Entry Locks

Keyless entry locks are a great option for those looking for a convenient way to secure their garage. These locks are activated by a keypad or a card reader, which can be programmed with a personal code. This makes it easy to access your garage without having to fumble around for keys. Furthermore, keyless entry locks are more secure than traditional locks, as they are harder to pick and require a code to open.

Deadbolts

Deadbolts are one of the most secure types of locks available on the market. These locks are mounted onto the door frame, making it difficult for intruders to pick or break. Additionally, deadbolts are relatively easy to install and come with a variety of features, including keyless entry and combination locks. When choosing a deadbolt, make sure to pick one that is designed to withstand forced entry.

Combination Locks

Combination locks are great for those who want to secure their garage without the hassle of keys. These locks are usually activated by a combination code, which makes it easier to access your garage without having to worry about losing your keys. Additionally, combination locks offer more security than keyless entry locks, as they are harder to pick and require a code to open.

Additional Features

Some garage door locks come with additional features, such as keypads, motion sensors, and timers. Keypads allow you to set a personal code to access your garage, while motion sensors detect movement and can trigger an alarm if someone tries to break in. Timers are also useful for those who want to set a specific time for the lock to activate or deactivate.

Benefits Of Using Garage Door Lock Kits

Installing a garage door lock kit can provide a number of benefits. Firstly, they can help to secure your home and protect your family from intruders. Secondly, they can also provide peace of mind by allowing you to control who has access to your garage. How To Choose The Right Garage Door Lock Kit

How Much Does It Cost To Get A New Garage Door Motor?

If you own a home, chances are you have a garage. It can be very convenient to have a garage that opens and closes with a motor, but if it doesn't work properly, it can be a huge hassle. If you're looking to replace your garage door motor, you're probably wondering how much it will cost. The answer might surprise you.

Factors to Consider

The cost of a new garage door motor will depend on a few factors. First, the type and size of the motor will make a difference. A larger motor will cost more than a smaller one. Second, the brand of the motor will also impact the price. Some brands are more expensive than others, so shop around to find the best deal. Lastly, the installation cost can vary depending on the company you choose to do the work.

Average Costs

On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from $400 to $700 for a new garage door motor. This cost includes the motor itself, as well as installation. If you choose to install the motor yourself, you can save some money. However, it's important to note that installation can be tricky, so it's best to hire a professional if you're not experienced in this kind of work.

Other Costs to Consider

In addition to the cost of the motor, there are a few other costs to consider. If your garage door is old and outdated, you may need to replace the entire door as well. This can cost anywhere from $500 to $2,500 or more. You may also need to purchase additional components such as a control panel or remote, which can add to the total cost. Lastly, there may be additional labor costs if the installation is complicated or if you hire a professional.

How to Save Money

If you're looking to save money on a new garage door motor, there are a few things you can do. First, shop around for the best deal. Compare prices from different stores or websites to find the best price. Second, if you're feeling brave, you can try to install the motor yourself. This can be tricky, but it can save you a lot of money. Lastly, if you're replacing the entire garage door, consider purchasing a kit that includes all the components you need. This can save you a lot of money in the long run.

Conclusion

Replacing a garage door motor can be a costly endeavor, but it doesn't have to break the bank. By shopping around and looking for deals, you can save some money. Furthermore, if you're feeling brave, you can try to install the motor yourself. This can be a bit tricky, but it can save you a lot of money in the long run. All in all, a new garage door motor can cost anywhere from $400 to $700, depending on the size and brand you choose.

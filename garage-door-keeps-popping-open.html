How To Keep Your Garage Door From Popping Open

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

If you have a garage door, you know the frustration of it continually popping open when you least expect it. It’s a common problem, but fortunately, it’s also one that’s easy to fix. In this article, we’ll go over the simple steps you can take to stop your garage door from popping open, so you don’t have to worry about it anymore.

Check the Springs

The first thing you should do is check the springs on your garage door. The springs help keep the door closed, so if they’re worn or damaged, that could be the cause of the problem. If you’re not sure how to check them, you can always call a professional to take a look. They can also help you replace them if necessary.

Make Sure the Lock is Working

The next step is to make sure the lock on your garage door is working properly. If it’s not, it could be the cause of the popping open. Make sure the lock is engaged properly, and that it’s not too loose. You may also want to make sure the key is turning properly, as that can also be a cause of the problem.

Check the Rollers

The rollers on your garage door can also be the cause of the popping open. If they’re worn or damaged, they can cause the door to open unexpectedly. You can check the rollers by lifting the door a few inches off the ground and letting it go. If the door opens or closes on its own, the rollers may need to be replaced.

Adjust the Track

If the rollers are in good condition, the next step is to check the track. If the track is out of alignment, it can cause the door to open unexpectedly. You can adjust the track by loosening the screws and adjusting the track until it’s level. Once you’ve done this, you can tighten the screws and test the door again.

Check the Opener

If the track is aligned properly, the next step is to check the garage door opener. If the opener is not working properly, it can cause the door to open unexpectedly. Make sure the opener is working properly, and that it’s not too loose. You may also want to make sure the safety sensors are working properly, as that can also be a cause of the problem.

Replace the Weather Stripping

The weather stripping around the door can also be a cause of the problem. If the weatherstripping is old and worn, it can cause the door to open unexpectedly. You can replace the weatherstripping yourself, or call a professional to do it for you. Either way, it’s an easy fix and will help keep your garage door from popping open.

Install an Automatic Closer

If you’re still having trouble with your garage door popping open, you may want to consider installing an automatic closer. This is a device that will close the door after a certain amount of time. You can find them at most hardware stores and they’re easy to install. This will ensure that your door stays closed and you don’t have to worry about it popping open again.

Conclusion

If your garage door keeps popping open, it’s important to take the proper steps to fix it. By following the steps above, you can ensure that your garage door stays closed and you don’t have to worry about it opening unexpectedly. With a little effort, you can keep your garage door from popping open and avoid any frustration.

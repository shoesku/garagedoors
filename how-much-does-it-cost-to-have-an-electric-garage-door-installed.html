How Much Does An Electric Garage Door Installation Cost?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

If you’re looking to upgrade your garage door, electric garage doors are a great choice. They offer convenience, security, and are relatively easy to install. But how much does it cost to get an electric garage door installed? In this article, we will explore the costs associated with electric garage door installation and some of the factors that can influence the final cost.

The Average Installation Cost

The average cost of electric garage door installation is between $600 and $1,000. This includes the cost of the door itself, labor costs, and any additional materials needed. This cost can vary greatly depending on the size of the door, the materials used, and the complexity of the installation. If you opt for a more expensive door with extra features, you can expect the cost to go up.

Factors That Influence the Cost

When it comes to electric garage door installation, there are several factors that will influence the final cost. The following are some of the main factors:

Size of the Door

The size of the door is one of the main factors that will influence the cost. A larger door will require more material and labor, and therefore will be more expensive to install. If you’re looking to keep costs down, you may want to opt for a smaller door.

Materials Used

The type of materials used will also play a role in the cost of installation. Doors made of wood, aluminum, or steel will all cost different amounts to install. If you’re looking for a more affordable option, wood is usually the least expensive option.

Labor Costs

Labor costs can also vary depending on the complexity of the installation. If you choose a more complicated door with extra features, the labor costs will be higher. Additionally, the cost of labor can vary depending on where you live and the company you choose to install the door.

Additional Costs

In addition to the cost of the door and labor, there may be some additional costs associated with the installation. This could include the cost of any additional materials needed, such as brackets or screws. The cost of these additional materials will depend on the complexity of the installation.

Tips for Saving Money on Installation

If you’re looking to save money on electric garage door installation, there are a few things you can do. First, opt for a smaller door. This will reduce the cost of materials and labor. Additionally, compare prices between different companies and look for any discounts or specials they may be offering. Lastly, you can try to do some of the work yourself, such as painting or staining the door. This can help to reduce the overall cost of installation.

Conclusion

Installing an electric garage door can be a great way to upgrade your home. However, it’s important to consider the cost of installation before making a purchase. The average cost of electric garage door installation is between $600 and $1,000, but this can vary depending on the size of the door, the materials used, and the complexity of the installation. When it comes to saving money on installation, opt for a smaller door, compare prices between different companies, and try to do some of the work yourself.

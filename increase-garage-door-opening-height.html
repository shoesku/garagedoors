Simple Ways To Increase Your Garage Door Opening Height In 2023

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Garage doors are an important part of any home. They provide a secure entry to your home and can also add a great aesthetic to the exterior of your home. However, one of the most common complaints about garage doors is that they don't open high enough. If your garage door isn't opening as high as you'd like, there are a few simple ways that you can increase the opening height.

1. Change the Track

One of the easiest ways to increase your garage door opening height is to change the track. Most garage doors are installed on horizontal tracks, which can limit the amount of height that your door can open to. By switching to a vertical track, you can increase the amount of height that your garage door can open to. This is a relatively simple and inexpensive process, and it can make a big difference in the amount of height that your garage door can open to.

2. Use a Higher Lift Kit

Another way to increase the height of your garage door is to use a higher lift kit. Lift kits are available for most garage door openers, and they can raise the height of your door by several inches. This is an easy and relatively inexpensive way to increase the height of your door, and it can make a big difference in the amount of height that your garage door can open to.

3. Adjust the Spring Tension

The spring tension in your garage door can also affect the amount of height that your door can open to. If the tension is too low, your door won't be able to open as high as it should. On the other hand, if the tension is too high, your door can open too far and cause damage to the door or the opener. Adjusting the spring tension can be a tricky process, so it's best to leave this job to a professional.

4. Install a Higher Spring

If you want to increase the height of your garage door without having to adjust the spring tension, you can install a higher spring. This is a simple process that can be done by a professional, and it can make a big difference in the amount of height that your garage door can open to. Just make sure that you get the right size spring for your door, as too large of a spring can cause damage to the opener.

5. Replace the Opener

If none of the above solutions work, you may need to replace your garage door opener. Most garage door openers are designed to open to a certain height, so if you need to open higher than what your existing opener can handle, you'll need to upgrade to a better model. Higher-end models usually have the ability to open higher, so if you need more height, this is the best solution.

Conclusion

Increasing the height of your garage door can be a great way to improve the functionality and aesthetics of your home. There are several simple ways that you can increase the height of your garage door, such as changing the track, using a higher lift kit, adjusting the spring tension, installing a higher spring, or replacing the opener. With a little bit of effort, you can easily increase the opening height of your garage door in 2023.

Types of Garage Door Openers

When it comes to installing a garage door opener in Vaughan, there are a variety of options to choose from. The most common type of garage door opener is the chain drive system, which is an affordable and reliable option. This type of garage door opener is powered by a chain that runs along the length of the track, lifting and lowering the door as it moves.

Another popular option is the belt drive system, which is quieter and smoother than the chain drive system. The belt drive system works by using a belt to move the door instead of a chain, which reduces the noise and makes it easier to open and close the door.

Finally, there are also direct drive systems, which are the most expensive option. These systems are powered by a motor that is mounted directly to the door, and they are the most reliable and durable option. However, they do require more maintenance than the other types of garage door openers.

Average Cost Of New Garage Door Opener Installation In 2023

Introduction

Installing a new garage door opener can be an expensive and time consuming task. It's important to understand the costs associated with the installation before beginning the project. Knowing the average cost of a new garage door opener installation in 2023 will help you budget for the project. In addition, you'll have an idea of the range of costs you may incur.

Types of Garage Door Openers

There are many different types of garage door openers available in 2023. The type of opener you choose will affect the cost of the installation. Common types of garage door openers include belt drive, chain drive, screw drive, and direct drive. Belt drive garage door openers are the quietest option, but they are also the most expensive. Chain drive openers are the most common choice, and they are less expensive than belt drive openers. Screw drive openers are the least expensive, but they are also the loudest. Direct drive openers are the newest type of opener and they are the quietest and most expensive.

Labor Costs

The labor costs associated with installing a new garage door opener vary. Most professional installers will charge an hourly rate for their services. The average hourly rate for a garage door opener installation in 2023 is around $50-$75. This rate may vary depending on the complexity of the installation and the installer's experience. Some installers may offer a flat rate for the installation, which can be more cost effective in the long run.

Materials Costs

In addition to the labor costs, there are also the costs associated with purchasing the garage door opener itself. The cost of the opener will vary based on the type and features you choose. Basic openers start at around $200, while higher end openers can cost up to $500. In addition to the opener, you will also need to purchase mounting hardware and other parts. The cost of these parts can range from $20-$50, depending on what is needed for the installation.

Installation Difficulty

The difficulty of the installation is another factor that can affect the cost. If the installation is more complicated, it may require more time and materials, which will increase the cost. If you are doing the installation yourself, you may need to purchase additional tools and equipment, which will also add to the cost. If you are hiring a professional installer, they may also charge additional fees for difficult installations.

Additional Costs

In addition to the costs of the opener and labor, there may also be additional costs associated with the installation. For example, you may need to purchase a new garage door sensor, which can cost around $50. You may also need to purchase additional parts such as mounting hardware or a new keypad. These additional costs can add up quickly, so it's important to consider them when budgeting for the installation.

Conclusion

Installing a new garage door opener can be a costly project. Knowing the average cost of a new garage door opener installation in 2023 can help you budget for the project. The cost of the opener itself and the labor costs associated with the installation will vary depending on the type of opener you choose, the difficulty of the installation, and any additional parts or materials that are needed. By considering all of these factors, you can determine the total cost of the project and budget accordingly.

Raising Your Range Rover's Garage Door Height

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Are you the proud owner of a Range Rover? If so, you may be wondering how you can raise the height of your garage door to accommodate the size of your vehicle. It is essential that you have ample clearance to enter and exit your garage safely, and you may be surprised to learn that it is possible to raise the height of your garage door in a few simple steps.

Measure the Height of Your Range Rover

The first step to raising the height of your garage door is to measure the height of your Range Rover. This will help you determine the height you need to raise the garage door so that it is the exact size for your vehicle. You can use a tape measure, ruler, or a yardstick to measure the height of your Range Rover from the ground to the top of the vehicle. Once you have the measurements, you can then begin the process of raising the height of your garage door.

Replace the Old Garage Door

If your garage door is the same height as the Range Rover, you may want to consider replacing the old garage door with a new one that is slightly higher. This will allow you to have the extra clearance you need when entering and exiting your garage. When replacing the garage door, you should also consider the type of material used to make the door. If you have a wooden garage door, you may want to consider a metal door, as this will provide more stability and durability.

Install a Lift Kit

If you are not able to replace the old garage door, then you may want to consider installing a lift kit. This is a great way to raise the height of your existing garage door without having to replace it. Lift kits are available for a variety of garage door sizes and can be installed by a professional or by yourself. Before you purchase a lift kit, you should measure the height of your Range Rover to ensure that the kit will fit properly.

Choose the Right Type of Door

When you are raising the height of your garage door, you should also consider the type of door you want. If you are looking for an aesthetically pleasing door, you may want to consider a custom door. Custom doors are available in a variety of colors and styles, so you can easily find one to match the style of your home. If you are more concerned with security, then you may want to consider a steel door. Steel doors are strong and durable, and they can provide extra security for your home.

Install the New Door

Once you have chosen the right type of door, you can then begin the process of installing the new door. Depending on the type of door you have chosen, you may be able to do this yourself. However, if you are not confident in your DIY skills, you may want to hire a professional to do the job. A professional will be able to make sure that the door is installed correctly, as well as ensuring that it is secure and safe.

Maintain Your Garage Door

After you have raised the height of your garage door, you should also take the time to maintain it. Regular maintenance will help to ensure that your new door is safe and secure, as well as providing the extra clearance you need for your Range Rover. You should inspect the door on a regular basis and make sure that all the parts are in good condition. If you notice any signs of wear and tear, you should contact a professional to have the door serviced.

Conclusion

Raising the height of your garage door to accommodate your Range Rover is a simple process. All you need to do is measure the height of your Range Rover, replace the old garage door, install a lift kit, choose the right type of door, and install the new door. Once you have done this, you should also take the time to maintain your garage door. With a bit of knowledge and effort, you can easily raise the height of your garage door and enjoy the extra clearance for your Range Rover.

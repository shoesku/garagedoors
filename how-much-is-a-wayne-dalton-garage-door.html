How Much Is A Wayne Dalton Garage Door?

If you're in the market for a new garage door, you may be considering a Wayne Dalton garage door. After all, Wayne Dalton has been around since 1954 and is one of the most recognized brands in the industry. But how much does a Wayne Dalton garage door cost?

The cost of a Wayne Dalton garage door will vary depending on the type, size, and features you choose. Wayne Dalton offers a variety of styles and materials, so you can find the perfect door for your home. Here's a breakdown of the different types of Wayne Dalton garage doors and what you can expect to pay for each.

Steel Garage Doors

If you're looking for a durable and cost-effective option, a steel garage door from Wayne Dalton is a great choice. Steel doors are available with a variety of different finishes, so you can find the one that best matches your home. Prices start at around $450 for a single-car steel garage door and can go up to $1,000 or more, depending on the size and features you choose.

Wood Garage Doors

If you're looking for a more traditional look, a wooden garage door is a great option. Wayne Dalton offers a variety of wood garage doors in both raised-panel and carriage-house styles. Prices start at around $900 for a single-car wood garage door and can go up to $2,000 or more, depending on the size and features you choose.

Aluminum Garage Doors

If you're looking for a modern look, an aluminum garage door from Wayne Dalton is a great choice. Aluminum doors are lightweight, durable, and come in a variety of finishes. Prices start at around $700 for a single-car aluminum garage door and can go up to $1,500 or more, depending on the size and features you choose.

Vinyl Garage Doors

If you're looking for a low-maintenance option, a vinyl garage door from Wayne Dalton is a great choice. Vinyl doors are durable, easy to clean, and come in a variety of colors and designs. Prices start at around $700 for a single-car vinyl garage door and can go up to $1,500 or more, depending on the size and features you choose.

Glass Garage Doors

If you're looking for a modern, stylish look, a glass garage door from Wayne Dalton is a great choice. Glass doors are available in a variety of styles and can be customized with a variety of finishes. Prices start at around $2,000 for a single-car glass garage door and can go up to $4,000 or more, depending on the size and features you choose.

Insulated Garage Doors

If you're looking for a door that will help keep your garage warm in the winter and cool in the summer, an insulated garage door from Wayne Dalton is a great choice. Insulated doors are available in a variety of materials and come with a variety of insulation options. Prices start at around $600 for a single-car insulated garage door and can go up to $2,000 or more, depending on the size and features you choose.

Garage Door Openers

If you're looking for an easy and convenient way to open and close your garage door, a garage door opener from Wayne Dalton is a great choice. Wayne Dalton offers a variety of garage door openers in both wired and wireless models. Prices start at around $200 for a single-car garage door opener and can go up to $500 or more, depending on the size and features you choose.

Conclusion

As you can see, the cost of a Wayne Dalton garage door can vary depending on the type, size, and features you choose. If you're looking for a durable, stylish, and cost-effective option, a Wayne Dalton garage door is a great choice. Prices start at around $450 for a single-car steel garage door and can go up to $4,000 or more, depending on the size and features you choose. So no matter what your budget is, you can find the perfect Wayne Dalton garage door for your home.

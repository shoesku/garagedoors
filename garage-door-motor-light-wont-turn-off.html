What To Do When Your Garage Door Motor Light Won't Turn Off

Introduction

A garage door motor is an important part of the garage door system. It is responsible for opening and closing the garage door. The motor light is an indicator of the motor's current state. If your garage door motor light won’t turn off, that means something is wrong and needs to be addressed quickly. This article will explain why the motor light won’t turn off, and how to fix it.

Why the Motor Light Won't Turn Off

The motor light won’t turn off for one of two reasons. The first is that the motor is overworked. The motor may be working too hard to open or close the garage door, which is causing the motor to overheat. The motor light is an indicator that the motor is overworked. If the motor is overworked, it needs to be serviced or replaced.

The second reason why the motor light won’t turn off is that the door is stuck. If the garage door is stuck in a certain position, the motor will keep running in order to maintain that position. The motor light will stay on until the door is freed from its position. This could be due to something blocking the track or something stuck in the track.

How to Fix the Problem

If the motor is overworked, the best solution is to call a professional garage door service to service or replace the motor. This will ensure that the motor is in proper working condition and will not overheat. A professional can also check for any other issues, such as worn out parts or other problems that may be causing the motor light to stay on.

If the door is stuck, the problem can often be fixed by freeing the door from its position. Make sure to check the track for any obstructions or objects that may be causing the door to get stuck. If you find anything, remove it and try to open and close the door. If the door still won’t move, it may be time to call a professional.

Conclusion

If your garage door motor light won’t turn off, it could be due to the motor being overworked or the door being stuck. If the motor is overworked, the best solution is to call a professional to service or replace it. If the door is stuck, make sure to check the track for any obstructions or objects that may be causing the door to get stuck. If you can’t find the problem, it may be time to call a professional.

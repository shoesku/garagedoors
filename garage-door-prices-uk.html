Garage Door Prices In The Uk In 2023

Introduction

Are you looking for a new garage door in the UK this year? If so, then you may be wondering what prices to expect for the various garage door options available. This article will provide an overview of the different types of garage doors on the market in the UK and their current prices in 2023. We will look at the cost of both manual and automatic garage doors, as well as any additional features that could impact the price. By the end of this article, you will have a better idea of what you can expect to pay for a new garage door in the UK this year.

Manual Garage Doors

Manual garage doors are the most basic type of door available and are typically the cheapest. These are typically made from either steel or timber and can be quite simple or have a few additional features such as insulation or a reinforced frame. Manual garage doors are opened and closed by hand and typically require little to no maintenance. Prices for a manual garage door in the UK in 2023 start at around £200 for a basic steel door, with prices increasing to around £800 for a more advanced timber door with additional features.

Automatic Garage Doors

Automatic garage doors are becoming increasingly popular in the UK and are often seen as a more convenient and secure option. These doors use a motorised system and are operated by remote control. They are typically made from steel and have additional features such as sensors and safety devices. Prices for an automatic garage door in the UK in 2023 start at around £500 for a basic model, with prices increasing to around £2,000 for a more advanced model with additional features.

Additional Features

If you are looking for an even higher level of convenience and security, then you may want to consider additional features for your garage door. These features can include remote control access, keypads to open and close the door, motion detectors, and even automatic opening and closing via a smartphone app. Prices for these additional features can range from £50 to £500, depending on the type of feature and the quality of the product.

Garage Door Installation

The cost of installing a garage door will depend on the type of door you choose and the complexity of the installation. Installing a manual garage door is typically a relatively simple process and can be done by a competent DIYer. However, installing an automatic garage door requires specialist knowledge and experience and is best left to a professional. Prices for installation can range from £100 to over £500 depending on the complexity of the job.

Conclusion

Garage door prices in the UK in 2023 can vary greatly depending on the type of door you choose and any additional features you opt for. Manual garage doors are typically the cheapest option, whilst automatic doors offer the convenience of remote control access. Prices for additional features such as motion detectors, keypads, and smartphone apps can range from £50 to £500. Finally, the cost of installation will also depend on the complexity of the job and should be factored into the overall cost of the project.

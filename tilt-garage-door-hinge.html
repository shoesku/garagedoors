What Are Tilt Garage Door Hinges?

Tilt garage door hinges are a type of hinge that is designed to provide extra support for your garage door. They are typically made of steel and are designed to be very strong and durable. The most common type of tilt hinge is the single-piece hinge, which consists of two pieces of metal that are connected together with a pin. The two pieces are then joined together and secured to the garage door using screws. This type of hinge is very secure and is often used on commercial and industrial doors.

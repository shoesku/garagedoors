What is a Garage Door Spring?

Garage door springs are a vital component of a garage door. They are responsible for the smooth operation of the door, making it easy for you to open and close it. The springs are under a lot of pressure, so they can wear out over time. When this happens, it's time to replace them.

Garage door springs come in two different types: torsion and extension. Torsion springs are mounted to the wall above the garage door. They are responsible for the lifting and closing of the door. Extension springs are usually mounted to the side of the garage door and are responsible for the door's opening and closing.

How Much Does it Cost to Replace Garage Door Springs?

The cost to replace garage door springs can vary widely, depending on the type of spring, the size of the spring, and the complexity of the job. Generally, torsion springs are more expensive than extension springs, with prices ranging from $100 to $500 or more. For extension springs, the cost is usually between $50 and $200.

It's important to note that the cost of the springs is only part of the overall cost of the job. You may also need to pay for labor and other materials, such as a new track, depending on the complexity of the job.

Do You Need a Professional to Replace Garage Door Springs?

It is highly recommended that you hire a professional to replace your garage door springs. Replacing garage door springs is a hazardous task and requires a certain level of expertise. If the job is not done correctly, it can lead to serious injury or even death.

A professional will know exactly what type of spring you need and how to safely install it. They will also be able to diagnose any other problems with your garage door, such as worn out tracks or faulty cables. They will also be able to make any necessary adjustments to ensure your door operates smoothly and safely.

How Long Do Garage Door Springs Last?

Garage door springs typically last between 7 and 10 years, depending on the type and quality of the spring. However, if you use your garage door frequently, the springs may wear out sooner. If you notice any signs that your springs need replacing, such as squeaking or difficulty opening or closing the door, it's best to contact a professional as soon as possible.

It's also a good idea to have your springs inspected and lubricated on a regular basis to ensure they remain in good condition and last as long as possible.

How to Find a Reliable Garage Door Repair Company?

When you're looking for a reliable garage door repair company, it's important to do your research. Start by asking family and friends for recommendations, and then check online reviews to get an idea of the company's reputation. You should also check to see if the company is licensed and insured.

Finally, be sure to get written estimates from any companies you're considering. This will help you make an informed decision and ensure you get the best service and value for your money.

Conclusion

Replacing garage door springs can be a daunting task, but it is an important one. Taking the time to do research and hire a reliable company will help ensure the job is done right the first time. Knowing how much it costs to replace garage door springs and how long they typically last will also help you make an informed decision. With the right information, you can keep your garage door in good working order for years to come.

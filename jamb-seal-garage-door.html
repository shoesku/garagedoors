Everything You Need To Know About Jamb Seal Garage Door

Garage doors are one of the most important parts of your home. They provide additional security and insulation, as well as aesthetic appeal. But, like any other component of your home, they can start to wear down after a few years. This is when it’s time to consider a replacement. But, before you do, it’s important to consider some of the different types of garage doors available to you.

One of the most popular types of garage door is the jamb seal. This type of garage door has been around for decades, and is still one of the most popular choices today. But, what is a jamb seal? And what are the benefits of choosing this type of garage door? Read on to find out.

What Is a Jamb Seal Garage Door?

A jamb seal garage door is a type of garage door that uses a jamb seal to create a tight seal between the door and the frame. The seal is made from a rubber or foam material, and is designed to keep the door in place, as well as to provide additional insulation. The seal also prevents air and moisture from entering the garage, which can be beneficial for energy efficiency.

The jamb seal is usually installed when the garage door is first put in place. It’s important to know that the seal should be checked and replaced every few years, or whenever it starts to show signs of wear and tear. If the seal isn’t replaced regularly, it may not provide the same level of insulation, or it may cause the door to become loose, which can be a safety hazard.

Benefits of a Jamb Seal Garage Door

There are several benefits to using a jamb seal garage door, including:

  • Improved energy efficiency: The jamb seal helps to keep cool air in during the summer and warm air in during the winter, which can help to reduce your energy bills.
  • Additional insulation: The jamb seal helps to keep moisture and air out of the garage, which can help to improve the overall comfort level in the space.
  • Enhanced security: The seal helps to keep the door in place, which can help to prevent intrusions.
  • Aesthetically pleasing: The jamb seal helps to keep the door looking sleek and modern, which can help to improve the overall look of your home.

How to Maintain Your Jamb Seal Garage Door

It’s important to keep your jamb seal garage door in good shape so that it can continue to provide the benefits mentioned above. Here are a few tips for keeping your door in good condition:

  • Regularly inspect the seal: Check the seal for any signs of wear and tear, such as cracks or tears. If you notice any damage, it’s important to have it repaired or replaced as soon as possible.
  • Check the weather stripping: Make sure the weather stripping around the door is in good condition and free of any damage.
  • Clean regularly: Use a mild detergent and a soft cloth to clean the door and the seal. This will help to keep it looking its best.
  • Keep the area around the door clear: Make sure there are no objects or debris near the door, as this can interfere with the seal and cause it to wear down more quickly.

Conclusion

A jamb seal garage door is a popular and effective choice for many homeowners. It provides additional insulation, energy efficiency, and security, as well as aesthetic appeal. However, it’s important to keep the seal in good condition by regularly inspecting it, cleaning it, and making sure the area around the door is clear. By following these tips, you can ensure that your jamb seal garage door will be in good shape for years to come.

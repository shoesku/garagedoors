What Is an Armoured Door?

An armoured door is a type of security door that is designed to provide a higher level of protection than traditional doors. It is constructed from thick steel or composite materials and is reinforced with additional layers of protection to make it stronger and more resistant to forced entry. The door is also fitted with a secure lock system and may include additional features such as a deadbolt, peephole, or even an alarm system. Armoured doors are typically more expensive than standard doors, but they can provide an extra layer of security for your home.

What Is a Garage Door?

A garage door is a type of door that is designed to open and close automatically. It is usually made from steel, aluminum, or wood and is generally operated by an electric motor. Garage doors are typically used to provide access to garages, carports, and other storage areas. They can be opened manually or remotely via a remote control or smartphone. Garage doors provide convenience and security, allowing you to easily access your vehicle or stored items without having to leave the safety of your home.

How To Use A Garage Door Light To Determine What Is Wrong With Your Door

If you are having issues with your garage door, you may be wondering what the red light on the garage door opener means. This light could mean that something is wrong with your door, and it is important that you take a few minutes to diagnose what is wrong. In this article, we will discuss the meaning behind the red light on your garage door, and what you can do to fix the issue.

What Does the Red Light on The Garage Door Mean?

The red light on the garage door opener typically means that the door is not in the closed position. This could be due to either the door not being completely closed, or something is blocking the door from completely closing. It is important to remember that if the red light is on, the door may not be completely closed, and you should be careful when opening the door.

How Can I Check To See If The Door Is Completely Closed?

If the red light is on, it is important to check to ensure that the door is completely closed. One of the easiest ways to do this is to look through the window of the garage door, or use a flashlight if necessary. If the door is open, you should be able to see a gap between the door and the ground. If the gap is not visible, the door is probably shut correctly.

What Could Be Blocking The Door From Closing?

If the door is not completely closed, there could be something blocking the door from closing. This could be anything from a box, to a ladder, to furniture. If you suspect something is blocking the door from closing, you should remove the object and try to close the door again. If this does not work, you may need to call a professional to come and take a look at your door.

What Should I Do If The Door Will Not Close?

If the door will not close, even after removing the object blocking the door, you may need to check the springs. The springs are responsible for balancing the door and keeping it closed. If the springs are broken, or not working properly, the door may not close completely. In this case, you should call a professional to come and take a look at your door.

What Should I Do If The Door Closes But The Red Light Remains On?

If the door closes but the red light remains on, you may need to reset the garage door opener. This can typically be done by pressing the reset button on the opener. If you are unsure how to reset the opener, you should refer to your ownerâ€™s manual, or contact the manufacturer for assistance.

Conclusion

When it comes to garage door lights, the red light typically means that the door is not in the closed position. To check if the door is closed, you should look through the window or use a flashlight. If the door is not closed, you should remove any objects that may be blocking the door from closing. If the door still does not close, you should check the springs and call a professional if necessary. If the door closes but the red light remains on, you should reset the garage door opener.

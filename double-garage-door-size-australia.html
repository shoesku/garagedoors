Garage doors have become an integral part of any home in Australia. The size of the garage door you choose will have a big impact on the look and feel of your home, as well as its security. If you’re looking to replace or upgrade your existing garage door, it is important to understand the different options available in terms of size and design. In this article, we’ll take a look at double garage door size in Australia and discuss the different options available.

Types of Double Garage Doors in Australia

When it comes to double garage doors in Australia, there are two main types to choose from: roller doors and sectional doors. Roller doors are made from a single sheet of metal that rolls up when opened and down when closed. They are generally easier to install and require less maintenance than sectional doors. Sectional doors are made from panels that slide up and down in tracks. They are more secure than roller doors, but they do require more maintenance, as the tracks need to be regularly cleaned and lubricated.

Standard Double Garage Door Size in Australia

The standard double garage door size in Australia is 5.2m (17’1”) wide and 2.4m (7’10”) high. This is the most common size of garage door and will fit most standard-sized garages. However, you can also get custom-made garage doors in other sizes to fit your specific needs. If you’re looking for a larger size, it is important to remember that you’ll need to get a custom-made garage door, as the standard sizes won’t be able to accommodate a bigger opening.

Factors to Consider When Choosing Double Garage Door Size in Australia

When it comes to choosing double garage door size in Australia, there are several factors to consider. The most important of these is the size of your garage. If you have a small garage, it is important to choose a garage door that is not too big, as it will make it difficult to open and close. On the other hand, if you have a large garage, you should opt for a larger garage door to make the most of the space.

In addition to the size of your garage, you should also take into account the amount of traffic that passes through it. If your garage is used frequently, it is a good idea to opt for a higher-quality garage door that offers better insulation and soundproofing. This will help to reduce noise levels in your garage and make it more comfortable.

Finally, you should also consider the look and feel of your home. You should choose a garage door that complements the style of your house and fits in with the overall design. For example, if you have a traditional style home, you might want to opt for a wooden garage door, whereas if you have a more modern home, a metal garage door might be more suitable.

Advantages of Double Garage Door Size in Australia

Double garage door size in Australia offers several advantages, such as increased security, better insulation, and improved soundproofing. By choosing a larger garage door, you will be able to fit two cars in the garage, allowing you to park two cars safely and securely. Additionally, larger garage doors offer better insulation, which helps to keep the temperature inside the garage more comfortable and reduce noise levels.

Furthermore, larger garage doors are more secure as they are more difficult to break into. This is especially important for those who live in an area with a high crime rate, as a larger garage door will make it more difficult for criminals to gain access to your property. Finally, larger garage doors are more aesthetically pleasing and can improve the look of your home.

Conclusion

Choosing the right double garage door size in Australia is an important decision that should not be taken lightly. It is important to take into account the size of your garage, the amount of traffic that passes through it, and the look and feel of your home before making your decision. By considering all of these factors, you can ensure that you choose the right double garage door size for your home.

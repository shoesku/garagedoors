What is Impact Glass?

Impact glass is a type of glass that is designed to resist breaking from impact. It is specifically designed to absorb the force of objects being thrown or hitting it, while also maintaining its strength and integrity. Impact glass is typically used in areas that require high levels of security, such as storefronts and homes. It is also commonly used in garage door windows to keep intruders out.

Durability and Strength

Impact glass is known for its strength and durability. It is a great option for garage door windows because it is able to withstand heavy impacts from objects such as rocks and baseballs. This type of glass is also highly resistant to scratches and other wear and tear, making it a great option for long-term use.

Troubleshooting When Your Garage Door Electric Eye Not Working

Having a garage door electric eye come with a lot of convenience. You don’t have to manually open and close the garage door every time you need access to your car. To ensure your safety, the electric eye is an important feature to have. However, if it’s not working, it can be a major inconvenience.

The electric eye is a device that is mounted on either side of the garage door. It sends a beam of light across the door opening and when the beam is broken, it triggers the garage door to open. If it’s not working, it’s likely because the beam isn’t being sent across the opening or the beam is being broken and the door is not opening.

What to Check For?

The first thing you should check is the power source. Make sure the power source is connected and that the power is on. If the power is off, you may need to reset the breaker or check the fuse. If the power is on, then you can move on to the next step.

The next thing to do is to check the electric eye itself. Make sure the electric eye is mounted correctly and that the wires are still connected. You should also check the lens of the electric eye to make sure it’s clean and not blocked. If it is blocked, then you need to clean it.

You should also check the alignment of the electric eye. The electric eye should be on the same line as the door so that the beam can travel across the opening. If it’s not aligned correctly, you need to adjust it so that the beam is being sent across the opening.

What If It’s Still Not Working?

If the electric eye is still not working after you’ve checked all of the above, then it may be time to call in a professional. A professional can come in and inspect the electric eye and determine why it’s not working. They may need to replace the electric eye or make some other adjustments.

It’s also possible that the electric eye has been damaged in some way. If this is the case, you will need to replace the electric eye. However, you should always call in a professional to do this as it can be a tricky job.

Finally, you should check to make sure the door itself is working correctly. If the door is not opening or closing correctly, then this could be the reason why the electric eye is not working. If this is the case, you should call in a professional to make the necessary repairs.

Conclusion

If your garage door electric eye isn’t working, then it’s important to troubleshoot the problem. Start by checking the power source, the alignment of the electric eye, and the cleanliness of the lens. If these don’t work, then you may need to call in a professional. A professional can inspect the eye and make the necessary repairs or replacements.

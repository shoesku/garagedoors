Solving The Mystery Of Sears Garage Door Opener Closing And Opening Again

Introduction

If you have a Sears garage door opener and it’s been randomly closing and opening again, you’re not alone. This problem has been reported by many owners of these openers and it can be really frustrating. In this article, we’ll explain why this is happening and provide some tips on how to fix it. Keep reading to find out more.

What Causes the Problem?

When your Sears garage door opener closes and then opens again, it’s usually because of a malfunctioning safety feature. This feature is meant to detect obstacles in the way of the door and prevent it from closing on them. Unfortunately, sometimes the sensor misreads the situation and will cause the door to open and close repeatedly.

How to Fix the Problem?

The best way to fix this problem is to adjust the sensitivity of the opener’s safety feature. To do this, you’ll need to locate the “Safety Sensitivity” button on your opener’s control panel. Then, press and hold the button until the LED light flashes three times. This should cause the opener to reset and adjust the sensitivity of the safety feature.

Alternative Solution

If the problem persists, you may need to check the alignment of the safety sensors. These sensors should be in line with each other and should not be obstructed by anything. If they are not properly aligned, you may need to adjust them with a Phillips head screwdriver. Make sure to check the alignment of the sensors regularly to avoid problems in the future.

Tips for Maintaining Your Opener

To ensure that your Sears garage door opener continues to work properly, it’s important to give it regular maintenance. Make sure to check the alignment of the sensors regularly, as well as the tension of the springs and the tightness of the bolts. It’s also a good idea to lubricate the moving parts of the opener with a lubricant specifically designed for garage door openers.

Conclusion

If your Sears garage door opener is randomly closing and opening again, it’s likely due to a malfunctioning safety feature. The best way to fix this problem is to adjust the sensitivity of the feature by pressing and holding the “Safety Sensitivity” button. If the problem persists, you may need to check the alignment of the safety sensors. Additionally, make sure to give your opener regular maintenance to ensure that it continues to work properly.

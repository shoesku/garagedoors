Types of Common Garage Door Repairs

Garage doors are an integral part of any home or business, and when they malfunction, it can be a major inconvenience. Fortunately, there are several types of garage door repairs that can be done to help get the door back in working order. Some of the more common repairs include broken springs, damaged tracks, and faulty openers. Knowing what type of repair is necessary can help you get the right help and save time and money.

Broken Springs

A broken spring is one of the most common reasons why garage doors fail to open and close properly. This can typically be caused by wear and tear, as well as by accidents. Springs are usually located at the side of the door and are attached to the cables that lift and lower the door. When a spring is broken, the door may become stuck in the track and will not open or close. A professional technician can replace the spring, which should fix the problem.

Damaged Tracks

Another common problem is when the tracks become damaged. This can be caused by a variety of factors, including rust, wear and tear, and even accidents. The tracks can become warped or bent which can cause the door to become stuck. A professional technician can inspect the tracks and make necessary repairs or replacements to ensure that the door operates properly.

Faulty Openers

Faulty openers are another common issue that can cause garage doors to malfunction. Openers are responsible for lifting and lowering the door, and when they fail, it can cause the door to become stuck. In some cases, the opener may need to be replaced, while in other cases, the issue can be resolved by adjusting the settings or lubricating the moving parts. A professional technician can help determine the cause of the problem and make the necessary repairs.

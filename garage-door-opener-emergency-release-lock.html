How To Use An Emergency Release Lock On Your Garage Door Opener

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Having a garage door opener with an emergency release lock is a must-have for any homeowner. It provides a convenient and secure way to open and close your garage door without having to get out of your car. It also ensures that your garage door is always secure, even when you’re away from home. But do you know how to use the emergency release lock on your garage door opener? Here’s what you need to know.

What is an Emergency Release Lock?

An emergency release lock is a special type of lock that can be used to manually override the automated garage door opener. It’s typically located on the side of the garage door track and can be activated by pulling a cord or lever. To open the garage door, simply turn the lever and pull the cord. This will disengage the automatic opener and allow you to open the door manually. Once you’ve opened the door, you can then re-engage the automatic opener by pushing the button on the wall or remote control.

When Should You Use an Emergency Release Lock?

The emergency release lock should only be used in cases of emergency. This could include instances where the power is out and you’re unable to open the door using the automatic opener, or if the opener has malfunctioned and you’re unable to open the door. It’s important to note that the emergency release lock should not be used as a regular means of opening the garage door, as it can cause damage to the opener and door.

How to Use an Emergency Release Lock

Using an emergency release lock is fairly straightforward. First, locate the lock on the side of the garage door track. It should be a lever or cord. Next, pull the lever or cord to disengage the automatic opener. You should then be able to open the door manually. Once you’ve opened the door, you can re-engage the automatic opener by pushing the button on the wall or remote control.

Safety Tips When Using an Emergency Release Lock

When using the emergency release lock, it’s important to take safety precautions. Always make sure the door is completely closed before pulling the lever or cord. This will help prevent injury or damage to the door. Additionally, be sure to keep children and pets away from the garage door while it’s open. Finally, make sure to re-engage the automatic opener before leaving the garage.

What to Do if the Emergency Release Lock is Broken

If the emergency release lock is broken or not functioning properly, it’s important to seek professional help. If you’re unable to open the door manually, you may need to call a professional garage door repair company. They will be able to diagnose and fix the issue quickly and safely.

Conclusion

Having an emergency release lock on your garage door opener is an essential safety feature. It allows you to open and close your garage door manually in case of an emergency. However, it’s important to use the emergency release lock properly to avoid injury or damage. If the lock is broken or not working properly, it’s best to call a professional garage door repair company.

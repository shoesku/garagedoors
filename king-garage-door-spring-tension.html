Everything You Need To Know About King Garage Door Spring Tension

Garage door springs are a critical part of any garage door system, providing the tension needed to safely and effectively open and close the door. In the case of King garage door springs, the tension must be just right to keep them functioning properly and ensure the safety of your door and the people using it. Read on to learn more about King garage door spring tension and how to adjust it.

What are King Garage Door Springs?

King garage door springs are a type of torsion spring that is used for the opening and closing of garage doors. They are typically made from steel and are placed over the garage door's tracks. When the door is opened, the garage door springs twist, providing the tension necessary to lift the door. Once the door is closed, the springs unwind, providing the tension necessary to keep the door in place.

What is Spring Tension?

Spring tension is the amount of force that is applied to the garage door springs when the door is opened or closed. Too much tension can cause the door to be too heavy and difficult to open and close, while too little tension can cause the door to be unstable and possibly lead to an accident. It is important to have the correct amount of tension on the garage door springs to ensure the safety and proper functioning of your garage door.

How to Adjust Spring Tension on King Garage Door Springs?

Adjusting the spring tension on your King garage door springs is a relatively simple process. First, you'll need to locate the tension adjustment mechanism on the springs. This is usually in the form of a tension rod or cable. You can then adjust the tension by turning the rod or cable clockwise to increase tension and counterclockwise to decrease tension. It is important to be careful when adjusting the tension, as too much or too little can cause the door to malfunction.

When Should the Spring Tension be Adjusted?

In general, the spring tension should be adjusted every six months or so. This is to ensure that the tension is at the correct level and that the door is functioning properly. If you notice that the door is difficult to open or close, or if the door is shaking or vibrating when it is opened or closed, it is likely that the spring tension needs to be adjusted.

How to Tell if the Spring Tension is Too High or Too Low?

If the spring tension is too high, the door will be difficult to open and close. You may also notice the door shaking or vibrating when it is opened and closed. If the spring tension is too low, the door may become unstable and may even fall off its tracks. In either case, it is important to adjust the spring tension as soon as possible to ensure the safety of your door and the safety of those using it.

Conclusion

Adjusting the spring tension on your King garage door is an important part of keeping your door functioning properly and safely. By understanding what spring tension is and how to adjust it, you can ensure that your door is functioning properly and safely. If you have any questions or concerns about adjusting the spring tension on your King garage door, it is best to consult a professional to ensure the safety of your door and the safety of those using it.

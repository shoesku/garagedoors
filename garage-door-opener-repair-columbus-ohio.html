Signs That Your Garage Door Opener Needs Repair

If you think that your garage door opener might need repair, there are a few signs to look out for. One of the most common signs that something is wrong is if your garage door opener is making strange noises. This could be a sign that the opener is running out of oil, or that something is wrong with the motor. Another tell-tale sign is if the opener doesn't open or close the door all the way. This could be a sign of a problem with the chain, or with the motor. Finally, if the remote isn't working, it could be a sign that the motor needs to be replaced.

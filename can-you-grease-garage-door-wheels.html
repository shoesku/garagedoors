Can You Grease Garage Door Wheels?

It’s not uncommon for garage doors to start making noise when they’re in need of a little TLC. Greasing garage door wheels is one simple DIY task that can help you avoid more serious issues. In this article, we’ll discuss the importance of greasing garage door wheels, when to do it, and how to do it safely. Let’s get started!

Why Grease Garage Door Wheels?

Greasing garage door wheels is important because it helps them to run smoothly. This is especially true if the wheels are made of metal. Metal wheels are more prone to rust and corrosion, and greasing them helps to protect them from further damage. Greasing also makes it easier for the wheels to roll along the tracks, reducing the amount of friction and wear and tear.

In addition to preventing damage to the wheels, greasing can help reduce the amount of noise your garage door makes. If your door is making loud, grinding noises when you open and close it, it’s likely that the wheels need some lubrication. Greasing the wheels can help to reduce the noise and make the door move more smoothly.

When Should You Grease Garage Door Wheels?

If your garage door is making unusual noises, it’s a good idea to check the wheels and see if they need to be greased. In general, it’s a good idea to grease your garage door wheels at least once a year. Doing it more often is even better, as it will help to ensure that the wheels remain in good condition and that your door operates smoothly.

It’s also a good idea to grease the wheels if you’re having trouble opening and closing your garage door. If the door is sticking or taking longer than usual to open, it’s likely that the wheels need to be lubricated. Greasing them can help to restore the door’s normal operation and make it easier to open and close.

How to Grease Garage Door Wheels

Greasing garage door wheels is a relatively simple task. First, you’ll need to open the door and then locate the wheels. They should be located near the top of the door, on either side. If you’re having trouble finding them, consult your owner’s manual for assistance.

Once you’ve located the wheels, you’ll need to get some lubricant. You can purchase a special garage door lubricant, or you can use a lubricant such as WD-40. Make sure to spray the lubricant onto the wheels in a well-ventilated area, as the fumes can be hazardous.

Once the wheels are lubricated, open and close the door several times to ensure that the lubricant is distributed evenly. You should also check the door periodically to make sure the wheels remain lubricated. If your door is still making unusual noises, you may need to repeat the process.

Conclusion

Greasing garage door wheels is an important task that can help to keep your door running smoothly and reduce the amount of noise it makes. Doing it at least once a year is a good idea, and it’s a relatively simple task that can be done in just a few minutes. If you’re having trouble opening and closing your garage door, greasing the wheels may be the solution.

If you’re still having trouble with your garage door, it’s a good idea to consult a professional. A professional can help you identify the source of the problem and suggest an appropriate solution. In some cases, it may be necessary to replace the wheels or other components of the door.

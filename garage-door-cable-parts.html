Is it time to repair or replace the garage door cables in your home? The answer is – it depends. In this article, we’ll discuss everything you need to know about garage door cable parts, including when to replace them and what type of cable to buy.

Garage door cables are an essential component of your garage door system. They run along the sides of the door, and they help to lift and lower the door. If the cables break, the door won’t open or close properly. They can also become worn out and need to be replaced.

When it’s time to replace your garage door cables, one of the first things you need to consider is the type of cable you should buy. There are two main types of garage door cables: torsion springs and extension springs. The type of cable you choose will depend on the type of garage door you have.

Torsion Springs

Torsion springs are the most common type of garage door cable and are found on most residential garage doors. They’re typically made from galvanized steel and are designed to withstand heavy use. They’re durable and can last for many years without needing to be replaced.

Torsion springs are usually sold in sets, and they come in a variety of sizes. When buying new garage door cables, make sure to buy the right size for your door. Buying the wrong size cables could result in the door not opening or closing properly.

Extension Springs

Extension springs are also commonly used on residential garage doors. They’re typically made from galvanized steel and are designed to be more flexible than torsion springs. They’re also less likely to break than torsion springs.

Extension springs are typically sold in sets, and they come in a variety of sizes. When buying new garage door cables, make sure to buy the right size for your door. Buying the wrong size cables could result in the door not opening or closing properly.

Installing Garage Door Cables

Installing garage door cables is a job that’s best left to a professional. It can be dangerous to attempt to install garage door cables yourself, as it involves dealing with heavy machinery and a lot of tension. A professional will be able to ensure that the cables are installed correctly, and that your garage door is operating safely.

When installing new garage door cables, make sure to use the correct tools and follow the manufacturer’s instructions. If you’re unsure of how to install the cables, it’s best to contact a professional.

Caring for Garage Door Cables

Once you’ve installed the new garage door cables, it’s important to take care of them. This will help to ensure that they last for many years. To care for your garage door cables, make sure to check them regularly for signs of wear and tear. You should also lubricate the cables and springs to prevent them from rusting or corroding.

If you notice any problems with your garage door cables, it’s best to contact a professional. They’ll be able to diagnose the problem and repair or replace the cables as necessary.

Conclusion

Garage door cables are an essential component of your garage door system. When they become worn out or broken, they need to be replaced. It’s important to know the type of cable you should buy and to have them installed by a professional. It’s also important to take care of your garage door cables to ensure that they last for many years. With the right care, your garage door cables can last for many years without needing to be replaced.

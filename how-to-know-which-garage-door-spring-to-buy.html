How To Know Which Garage Door Spring To Buy

Introduction

Garage door springs are essential components of any functioning garage door. Without them, the door would be unable to open or close properly. As such, it is important to make sure that you know which type of spring you need to buy for your garage door. This article will provide you with a comprehensive guide to buying the right garage door spring for your needs.

Types of Garage Door Springs

There are two main types of garage door springs: torsion springs and extension springs. Torsion springs are the most common type of garage door springs and are used on most residential garage doors. They are mounted above the door and are generally easier to maintain than extension springs. Extension springs are typically used on heavier garage doors and are mounted on the side of the door. Both types of garage door springs can be purchased from most hardware stores or online.

How to Select the Right Spring for Your Garage Door

When selecting the right spring for your garage door, there are a few factors to consider. First, you will need to determine the weight of your garage door. This can usually be found in the product manual or on the door itself. Next, you will need to determine the size of the spring you need. The size of the spring should be based on the weight of the door and the number of times it needs to be opened and closed each day. Finally, you will need to decide whether you want to purchase a torsion spring or an extension spring.

Installation of the Spring

Installing the spring is a fairly straightforward process. However, it is important to take the necessary safety precautions during installation. Make sure to wear protective gloves, goggles, and clothing to protect yourself from flying debris. Additionally, make sure that the door is in the closed position before attempting to install the spring. Finally, if you are not comfortable with the installation process, it is best to contact a professional for assistance.

Maintenance of the Spring

As with any component of a garage door, it is important to perform regular maintenance on the spring. The spring should be inspected at least once a year to ensure that it is functioning properly. Additionally, the spring should be lubricated with a silicone-based lubricant every six months to prevent corrosion and reduce wear. Finally, it is important to replace the spring if it has been damaged or is more than ten years old.

Conclusion

Knowing which garage door spring to buy for your garage door is essential for ensuring that it functions properly. Before purchasing a spring, make sure to determine the weight of the door, the size of the spring, and whether you need a torsion or an extension spring. Additionally, make sure to take the necessary safety precautions when installing the spring and to perform regular maintenance on the spring to ensure it lasts for many years to come.

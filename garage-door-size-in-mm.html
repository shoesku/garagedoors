What is Garage Door Size in mm?

Garage door size in mm is a measurement of the height and width of the door opening of a garage. Understanding the width and height of a garage door opening is essential for purchasing the right size door. While many garage door sizes are standard, there are variations in sizes depending on the size of the opening and the type of door.

Standard Sizes of Garage Doors

Standard sizes of garage doors typically range from 2200mm to 2500mm in width and 2000mm to 2400mm in height. However, the exact measurements of a garage door will depend on the size of the opening. The most common sizes are 2200mm x 2000mm, 2400mm x 2000mm, 2500mm x 2000mm, 2400mm x 2100mm, and 2500mm x 2100mm.

Types of Garage Doors

The most common types of garage doors are sectional, roller, and tilt. Sectional garage doors are made up of several panels that are connected together, allowing the door to open and close vertically. Roller garage doors open and close horizontally and are made of a single panel. Tilt garage doors open and close horizontally, but they are made of several panels.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a Garage Door Size

When choosing the size of a garage door, there are several factors to consider. First, consider the size of the opening. Measure the width and height of the opening to determine the size of the door. The type of door should also be taken into account. Sectional doors require more space for operation, while roller and tilt doors can be installed in smaller openings.

Tips for Measuring the Opening for a Garage Door

When measuring the opening for a garage door, it is important to measure the height and width of the opening from the inside of the frame. The measurements should be taken from the highest and lowest points of the opening, as well as the left and right sides.

Types of Materials Used for Garage Doors

Garage doors can be made from a variety of materials, including wood, steel, aluminium, and fibreglass. Steel is the most common material used for garage doors, as it is strong and durable. Aluminium is another popular choice, as it is lightweight and low maintenance. Wood and fibreglass are more expensive but offer more aesthetic options.

Benefits of Installing the Right Size Garage Door

Installing the right size garage door is important for several reasons. First, it ensures the door will fit properly and operate properly. A properly sized door will also help to prevent drafts and reduce energy costs. Finally, a properly sized door will provide a more secure fit, helping to protect your home and belongings.

However, the exact measurements depend on the size of the opening and the type of door. It is important to measure the garage door opening from the inside of the frame, and to consider the type of material used for the door. Installing the right size garage door will help ensure a proper fit and provide a more secure fit to protect your home and belongings.

Solving A Mystery When Your Garage Door Shuts Then Opens Again

If you’ve ever experienced a garage door that suddenly shuts and then opens again, you’re not alone. It’s a common issue that can be solved by troubleshooting your garage door opener. In this article, we’ll explain why this problem might happen and provide some tips on how to fix it.

Common Causes of the Garage Door Shutting Then Opening Again

If your garage door shuts and then opens again, it’s usually an indication that something is wrong with the photo eyes sensors. These are located at the bottom of the door frame, and they send a signal to the opener when the door is closed. If the photo eyes are blocked, it could cause the door to close and then open again.

Another common cause is if the safety reverse system is not functioning correctly. This system is designed to reverse the door if an obstruction is detected. If this system is not working correctly, it could cause the door to close and then open again.

Finally, it’s possible that the garage door opener itself is malfunctioning. If the opener is not sending the proper signals, it could cause the door to close and then open again.

Troubleshooting Your Garage Door Opener

If you’re experiencing the garage door shutting and then opening again, there are a few steps you can take to troubleshoot the issue. First, check the photo eyes sensors to make sure they’re not blocked. Second, check the safety reverse system to make sure it’s functioning correctly. Third, inspect the garage door opener to make sure it’s sending the proper signals.

If the issue persists after completing these steps, it’s possible that the opener itself is malfunctioning. If this is the case, you may need to replace the opener or have it serviced by a professional.

Preventing Future Issues

There are a few simple steps you can take to prevent future issues with your garage door. First, make sure the photo eyes sensors are kept clear of debris. Second, check the safety reverse system on a regular basis to make sure it’s functioning properly. Finally, have the opener serviced by a professional on a regular basis.

Conclusion

If your garage door shuts and then opens again, it’s usually an indication that something is wrong with the photo eyes sensors, the safety reverse system, or the opener itself. To troubleshoot the issue, check the photo eyes, check the safety reverse system, and inspect the opener. If these steps don’t solve the issue, you may need to replace or service the opener.

By taking a few simple steps, you can prevent future issues with your garage door. Make sure the photo eyes are kept clear, check the safety reverse system regularly, and have the opener serviced by a professional.

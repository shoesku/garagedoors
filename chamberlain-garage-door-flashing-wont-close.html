Common Reasons a Chamberlain Garage Door Flashing Won't Close

The most common reason a Chamberlain garage door flashing won't close is due to a faulty or misaligned photo eye sensor. The photo eye sensor is located at the bottom of the garage door and is responsible for detecting any obstructions in the doorway. If the photo eye is blocked, the garage door will not close. Additionally, if the photo eye is misaligned, the garage door will not close properly.

Another common issue is if the Chamberlain garage door opener is not receiving power. If the power to the garage door opener has been disconnected or is not working, the garage door will not be able to close. It is important to check the power to the garage door opener before attempting to troubleshoot the Chamberlain garage door.

Finally, the Chamberlain garage door may not be able to close if there is an obstruction in the way. If there is a large object blocking the path of the garage door, it will not be able to close. It is important to make sure that the path of the garage door is clear so that it can close properly.

Troubleshooting a Chamberlain Garage Door Flashing Won't Close

The first step in troubleshooting a Chamberlain garage door flashing won't close is to check the power to the garage door opener. If there is no power, the garage door will not be able to close. Once the power has been restored, the next step is to check the photo eye sensor. This sensor is responsible for detecting any obstructions in the doorway. If the photo eye is blocked or misaligned, the garage door will not close.

If the photo eye is clear and properly aligned, the next step is to check for any obstructions in the way of the garage door. If there is a large object blocking the path of the garage door, it will not be able to close. Once all obstructions have been removed, the Chamberlain garage door should be able to close.

Preventing Chamberlain Garage Door Flashing Won't Close Issues

The best way to prevent a Chamberlain garage door from flashing won't close is to regularly check the power to the garage door opener and to keep the photo eye sensor clean and properly aligned. Additionally, it is important to keep the path of the garage door clear of any obstructions. By following these simple steps, you can help ensure that your Chamberlain garage door will close properly.

Conclusion

Troubleshooting a Chamberlain garage door flashing won't close can be a simple and easy process. By checking the power to the garage door opener, the photo eye sensor, and the path of the garage door, you can quickly and easily resolve the issue. Additionally, by regularly checking these components and keeping the path of the garage door clear, you can help prevent future issues with your Chamberlain garage door.

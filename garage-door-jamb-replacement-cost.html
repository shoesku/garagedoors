Everything You Need To Know About Garage Door Jamb Replacement Cost

Have you been wondering about the cost to replace your garage door jamb? If so, you’re not alone. Replacing a garage door jamb is a common repair, and one that can be slightly expensive depending on the materials you use and the complexity of the job. In this article, we’ll explain everything you need to know about garage door jamb replacement cost, including what you should expect to pay and how you can save money on the job.

What Is a Garage Door Jamb?

A garage door jamb is the side frame of your garage door. It’s made up of two pieces of wood that are joined together and attached to the wall. The jamb helps to support the door, and it’s designed to keep the door from sagging. It also helps to keep the door from swinging open or shut unexpectedly.

When Do You Need to Replace Your Jamb?

Usually, you’ll need to replace your jamb if it’s been damaged by termites, rot, or water damage. You may also need to replace it if it’s been damaged by a car or other object. If the jamb is cracked or broken, then it’s time to replace it.

What Are the Different Types of Garage Door Jamb?

Garage door jamb is available in several different types, including steel, aluminum, and wood. Steel and aluminum jamb is the most common, and they’re both very durable. Steel jamb is also the most expensive option. Wood jamb is the least expensive, but it’s not as durable as the other types.

How Much Does Garage Door Jamb Replacement Cost?

The cost of replacing a garage door jamb will depend on the type of jamb you choose and the complexity of the job. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $200 to $1,000 for the job. The cost for a steel or aluminum jamb is usually higher than for a wood jamb, and the cost for a complex job such as replacing a jamb on an uneven or sloping wall may also be higher.

How Can You Save Money on Garage Door Jamb Replacement?

If you’re looking to save money on the cost of replacing your garage door jamb, there are a few things you can do. First, consider replacing the jamb yourself if you’re handy. This can be a difficult job, but with the right tools and some patience, you may be able to do it yourself and save some money. You should also consider buying the materials yourself, as buying them in bulk can often save you money.

Where Can You Find a Professional Garage Door Jamb Replacement Service?

If you don’t feel comfortable replacing your garage door jamb yourself, you can always hire a professional to do the job for you. There are many companies that specialize in garage door jamb replacement, and you can often find them through online searches and by asking around in your local area. Before hiring a company, make sure to check their reviews and make sure they have experience with the type of jamb you want to replace.

Conclusion

Replacing a garage door jamb can be an expensive job, but with the right materials and a bit of know-how, you may be able to save some money. Make sure to research the different types of jamb available and to compare costs before making any decisions. And if you don’t feel comfortable doing the job yourself, don’t be afraid to hire a professional. Good luck!

What is Garage Door Stop Side Top Molding?

Garage door stop side top molding is a type of protective trim that is used to keep the edges of the garage door from being damaged when the door is opened or closed. It is made from a variety of materials, including wood, aluminum, vinyl, and plastic, and can be found in many different colors and styles to match the look of the garage. The molding is typically installed on the top and sides of the door, and can help protect the door from dings, scratches, and other damage.

Types of Garage Door Stop Side Top Molding

There are several different types of garage door stop side top molding available. The most common type is the plastic molding, which comes in a variety of colors and styles to match any garage door. This type of molding is easy to install and is very durable, making it a great option for protecting the edges of the garage door. Another popular type of molding is the aluminum molding, which is also easy to install and comes in a variety of colors and styles. This type of molding is a bit more expensive than the plastic molding, but it is very durable and can withstand a lot of wear and tear. Vinyl and wood molding are also available, though they tend to be more expensive than the other two types.

Benefits of Garage Door Stop Side Top Molding

The primary benefit of installing garage door stop side top molding is that it helps to protect the edges of the garage door from dings, scratches, and other damage. This type of molding also adds a bit of style and sophistication to the garage, helping to make it look more attractive. In addition, this type of molding can help to reduce noise levels in the garage, as it helps to absorb the sound of the door opening and closing.

Installing Garage Door Stop Side Top Molding

Installing garage door stop side top molding is a relatively easy process and can be done by most DIYers. The first step is to measure the length and width of the door and then cut the molding to size. Once the molding has been cut, it is important to test it in the garage to make sure it fits properly. After the molding has been tested and is deemed to fit properly, it can be attached to the door using screws or nails. It is important to make sure that the screws or nails are securely fastened, as this will help to ensure that the molding stays in place.

Maintaining Garage Door Stop Side Top Molding

Maintaining garage door stop side top molding is relatively easy, as it does not require much maintenance. To ensure that the molding stays in good condition, it is important to periodically check it for any cracks, dents, or other damage. If any damage is found, it should be repaired or replaced immediately. Additionally, it is important to keep the molding clean and free of dirt and debris. This can be done by wiping it down with a damp cloth or a mild detergent.

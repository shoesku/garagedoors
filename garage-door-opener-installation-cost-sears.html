What Should You Expect to Pay for a Garage Door Opener from Sears?

Whether you want to upgrade your existing garage door opener or get a brand new one, Sears is a great place to shop for a variety of garage door openers. They have a wide selection of top-rated models from trusted brands like Craftsman, Chamberlain, and Genie. Plus, they have experienced technicians who can help you find the right opener for your needs and install it properly.

But how much is it going to cost to buy an opener from Sears and get it installed? That all depends on the type of opener you select, the features you want, and the labor costs for the installation. In this article, we’ll look at the garage door opener installation cost at Sears and what you can expect to pay.

Types of Garage Door Openers

The first step in determining your garage door opener installation cost is to select the type of opener you want. Sears offers three different types of openers: chain drive, belt drive, and screw drive.

Chain drive openers are the least expensive option, but they are also the loudest and least reliable. Belt drive openers are more expensive, but they are also quieter and more efficient. Screw drive openers are the most expensive option, but they are also the most reliable and efficient.

It’s important to choose the right opener for your needs, as the cost of installation will vary depending on the type of opener you select. Chain drive openers are usually the least expensive to install, while screw drive openers are usually the most expensive.

Features to Consider

The cost of garage door opener installation at Sears will also depend on the features you select. Some openers come with built-in features like motion sensors and WiFi connectivity. Other openers offer additional features like HomeLink compatibility and remote access.

The more features you select, the higher the cost of installation. Make sure you evaluate your needs and select the features that make sense for you. That way, you can avoid paying for features you don’t need and get the most value for your money.

Labor Costs

In addition to the cost of the opener and any features you select, you will also need to factor in the cost of labor for installation. Sears offers professional installation services, and the costs vary depending on the type of opener and the complexity of the installation.

You can expect to pay anywhere from $150 to $400 for professional installation. This cost includes labor and any additional parts that may be required for the installation.

Conclusion

Sears is a great place to shop for a variety of garage door openers, and they offer professional installation services. When estimating your garage door opener installation cost at Sears, make sure to factor in the cost of the opener, the features you select, and the labor costs for installation.

By taking the time to do your research, you can find the right opener for your needs and get it installed at a price that fits your budget. Then you can enjoy the convenience and security of a reliable garage door opener.

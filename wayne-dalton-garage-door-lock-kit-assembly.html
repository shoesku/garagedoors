How To Assemble A Wayne Dalton Garage Door Lock Kit

Assembling a Wayne Dalton garage door lock kit can be a daunting task if you don’t know what you’re doing. But with the right guidance and a few simple tools, you can easily assemble your own Wayne Dalton lock kit in no time! This article will guide you through each step of the process and provide you with the information you need to successfully assemble your kit.

What You’ll Need

Before you start assembling your Wayne Dalton lock kit, you’ll need to make sure you have all the necessary items. You’ll need the Wayne Dalton lock kit, a flat-head screwdriver, a Phillips-head screwdriver, and a drill with a bit set.

Step 1: Attach the Handle

The first step of assembling your Wayne Dalton lock kit is to attach the handle. Start by taking the handle and threading the two screws into the holes on either side. Then, tighten the screws with a Phillips-head screwdriver. Make sure the handle is securely attached before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Attach the Lock Cylinder

Once the handle is attached, you’ll need to attach the lock cylinder. Place the cylinder into the hole on the handle, making sure the notches are lined up correctly. Then, use the flat-head screwdriver to tighten the screws on either side of the cylinder.

Step 3: Install the Lock Assembly

Next, you’ll need to install the lock assembly. Take the lock assembly and insert it into the hole in the handle. Then, use the flat-head screwdriver to tighten the screws on either side of the assembly. Make sure the lock assembly is securely attached before moving on to the next step.

Step 4: Install the Strike Plate

Once the lock assembly is installed, you’ll need to install the strike plate. Take the strike plate and line it up with the hole in the door jamb. Then, use a drill to drill two pilot holes for the screws. Once the holes are drilled, use the Philips-head screwdriver to tighten the screws.

Step 5: Test the Lock

The last step of assembling your Wayne Dalton lock kit is to test the lock. Insert the key into the lock cylinder and turn it clockwise. If the lock opens and closes properly, then you’ve successfully assembled your Wayne Dalton lock kit!

Conclusion

Assembling a Wayne Dalton lock kit may seem daunting at first, but it’s actually quite simple. With the right tools and a bit of patience, you can easily assemble your own lock kit in no time. Just follow the steps outlined in this article and you’ll be able to successfully assemble your kit in no time.

Types of Garage Doors

When it comes to garage doors, there are many different types, styles, and materials to choose from. There are steel and aluminum garage doors, which are both durable, as well as wood and fiberglass garage doors. The most popular type of garage door is the roll-up style. This type of garage door is made from a single panel or section and rolls up and out of the way when opened. It is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance door that is easy to open and close.

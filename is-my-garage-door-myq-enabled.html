What is MyQ?

MyQ is a technology developed by Chamberlain, a leading garage door opener manufacturer. It allows homeowners to monitor and control their garage door from an app on their smartphone or tablet. With MyQ, you can check if your garage door is open or closed, as well as open and close it remotely. You can also set up notifications and alerts to let you know when the door is opened or closed.

Is My Garage Door MyQ Enabled?

If you purchased your Chamberlain garage door opener after 2012, then it's likely that it is MyQ enabled. However, you can also check the packaging of your garage door opener to see if it includes MyQ technology. If you still can't find the answer, you can contact Chamberlain directly to find out if your garage door is MyQ enabled.

How Do I Enable MyQ on My Garage Door?

If you have a MyQ enabled garage door, then you'll need to set up the MyQ app on your smartphone or tablet. This is fairly straightforward, and you'll find step-by-step instructions in the Chamberlain app. After you've downloaded and installed the app, you'll be able to connect your garage door to it and start using MyQ.

What are the Benefits of MyQ?

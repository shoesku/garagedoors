Tips To Fix Garage Door Spring Keeps Unwinding

Introduction

Garage door springs are essential components of a garage door system, as they are responsible for lifting the heavy garage door. However, if the springs are not maintained properly, they can become loose, resulting in the garage door spring keeps unwinding. This can be a major problem, as it can cause the garage door to become unbalanced and difficult to open and close. Fortunately, most of the time, you can fix the issue yourself without having to pay for costly repairs.

Check the Balance

The first thing you need to do is to check the balance of the door. To do this, open the door manually and then lift it up about halfway. If it stays in place, then it means the door is balanced. If it moves up or down, then it means that the balance is off and you need to adjust the springs. Make sure to also check the tension of the springs, as this can also affect the balance of the door.

Adjust the Springs

If you find that the springs are loose, then you will need to adjust them. This can be done by loosening the nuts on the bottom of the door. This will allow you to move the springs up or down, depending on which way the door is unbalanced. You can also use a tensioning tool to help you adjust the springs. Once you have adjusted the springs, make sure to re-tighten the nuts and check the balance again.

Lubricate the Springs

Another thing you can do to fix the issue of garage door spring keeps unwinding is to lubricate the springs. This will help to reduce the friction between the springs and the door, and can make it easier for the door to move. You can use a silicon-based lubricant, as this will last longer and provide better protection. Make sure to apply the lubricant evenly and not to over-apply, as this could cause the springs to become too slick.

Replace the Springs

If the springs are too worn or damaged, then you may need to replace them. This can be a complicated process, and it is best to hire a professional to do the job. They will be able to replace the springs quickly and safely, making sure that the door is properly balanced. If you decide to replace the springs yourself, make sure to follow the instructions carefully, as it is important to get the job done correctly.

Conclusion

Garage door spring keeps unwinding can be a major problem, as it can cause the door to become unbalanced and difficult to open and close. Fortunately, most of the time, you can fix the issue yourself without having to pay for costly repairs. Make sure to check the balance of the door, adjust the springs, lubricate the springs, and replace the springs if necessary. Following these tips can help you to get your garage door working properly again.

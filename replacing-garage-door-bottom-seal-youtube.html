Replacing Your Garage Door Bottom Seal? Here's How To Do It Yourself

Replacing a garage door bottom seal is a fairly simple project that most homeowners can do with a few basic tools. The bottom seal is essential in keeping the elements out of your garage, so it is important that you replace it if it starts to wear out. In this article, we will go over the steps you need to take to replace the bottom seal of your garage door.

Step One: Preparing the Area

The first step you will need to take is to prepare the area around the door. Start by cleaning the area, removing any dirt or debris that may have accumulated. Next, inspect the existing seal to determine the condition of it and if it needs to be replaced. If it does need to be replaced, you will want to measure the length of the old seal to ensure you get the right size when you go to purchase a replacement.

Step Two: Purchasing the New Seal

Once you have determined the size of the seal you need, you can go to a local home improvement store or online store to purchase a replacement. Make sure you get a seal that is specifically designed for your garage door. Depending on the type of door you have, you may need to buy a specific type of seal. It is also important to check for any warranties that come with the seal to make sure you are covered in case anything goes wrong with the installation.

Step Three: Installing the New Seal

Once you have purchased the replacement seal, it is time to install it. Start by removing the old seal, making sure to be careful when doing so. You may need to use a pry bar or other tools to get the old seal off. Next, take the new seal and place it in the opening, making sure that it fits snugly and that there are no gaps. Finally, secure the seal with screws or nails, depending on the type of seal you have purchased.

Step Four: Testing the New Seal

The final step is to test the new seal. Start by closing the garage door and then checking to see if there are any gaps between the door and the seal. If there are, you may need to adjust the seal to get a tighter fit. You can also try spraying water along the seal to see if any of it is seeping through. If it is, you will need to adjust the seal or use a sealant to fill any gaps.

Additional Tips

When replacing the seal, it is important to make sure that you get the correct size and type. If the seal is too small, it may not fit properly and could cause damage to the door or let in the elements. If it is too large, it may not stay in place and could cause the door to not close properly.

If you are having trouble with the installation, you may want to consider hiring a professional to do the job for you. While replacing the bottom seal is something that most homeowners can do, it is important to make sure that it is done correctly to ensure that it lasts for a long time.

If you want to take a look at how to replace a garage door bottom seal yourself, you can watch videos online that show the process. There are also many articles and tutorials available online that can help you get the job done right. With a little bit of research, you should be able to find all the information you need to replace the seal on your own.

Conclusion

Replacing a garage door bottom seal is a relatively easy project that can help extend the life of your garage door. Make sure you take the time to measure the old seal and purchase the correct size and type before you start the installation. If you have any questions or doubts about the process, you may want to consult a professional for help. With the right tools and a little bit of patience, you should be able to replace the seal on your own.

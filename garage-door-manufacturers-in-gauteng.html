What Are Garage Doors?

Garage doors are an integral part of many homes, providing a secure and convenient way to access the garage. They come in a variety of styles, materials, and sizes, and are usually made of wood, metal, or composite materials. While some garage doors are manually operated, many are automated and can be opened and closed with the touch of a button. Garage doors are also available with a variety of safety features, such as sensors to detect when someone is in the way, or lights that turn on when the door is in use.

It’s 2023, and the trendiest garage door colours are taking the world by storm. From bold and bright to subtle and sophisticated, the range of colours available for garage doors is ever-expanding. But which colours should you be looking at for your garage door this year? Read on to find out the trendiest colours for garage doors in 2023.

Green

Green is a classic colour, and it’s still going strong in 2023. It’s a great choice if you’re looking for a colour that is both calming and energising. Green is a colour that really stands out, too, so it’s a great choice if you want to make a statement with your garage door. There are lots of shades of green to choose from, so you’re sure to find one that fits your style.

Blue is another classic colour that is still trending in 2023. Blue is a great choice if you’re looking for a calming and tranquil colour for your garage door. You can choose from a range of shades from light and airy to deep and moody. Blue is a versatile colour, so it’s a great choice if you want to create a stylish and timeless look for your garage.

Grey is a popular colour choice in 2023, and it’s a great choice if you’re looking for a neutral and timeless colour for your garage door. Grey is a great choice if you want to create a sophisticated and modern look for your garage. Black

Black is a bold and dramatic colour, and it’s still trending in 2023. Black is a great choice if you want to create a strong and striking look for your garage door. You can choose from a range of shades, from glossy black to matte black. Black is a timeless colour, so it’s a great choice if you want to create a classic and timeless look for your garage.

White

White is a classic colour, and it’s still going strong in 2023. White is a great choice if you’re looking for a colour that is both calming and energising. White is a timeless colour, so it’s a great choice if you want to create a classic and timeless look for your garage. You can choose from a range of shades, from pure white to off-white.

Brown is a popular colour choice in 2023, and it’s a great choice if you’re looking for a neutral and timeless colour for your garage door. Red

Red is a bold and dramatic colour, and it’s still trending in 2023. Red is a great choice if you want to create a strong and striking look for your garage door. You can choose from a range of shades, from fiery red to deep red. Red is a timeless colour, so it’s a great choice if you want to create a classic and timeless look for your garage.

Orange

How To Troubleshoot A Flashing Craftsman Garage Door Keypad

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

If you own a Craftsman garage door opener, you know how convenient it is to be able to open your garage door with the tap of a keypad. But sometimes, the keypad can start to flash, which can be annoying and confusing. If you’re wondering why your Craftsman garage door keypad is flashing, then you’re in the right place. In this article, we’ll explain why your keypad might be flashing and how you can troubleshoot the issue. So read on to learn more!

What Causes a Craftsman Garage Door Keypad to Flash?

There are a few potential causes for a flashing Craftsman garage door keypad. The most common cause is a faulty connection between the keypad and the opener. This can be caused by a loose or broken wire or an incorrect setting on the opener. Another potential cause is a dead battery in the keypad.

It’s also possible that the keypad is not programmed correctly. If the keypad is not programmed to the correct code, it will not be able to “talk” to the opener. As a result, the keypad will flash when you press a key.

How to Troubleshoot a Craftsman Garage Door Keypad

If your Craftsman garage door keypad is flashing, the first thing to do is check the wiring. Make sure all the wiring is secure and in good condition. If there is a loose or broken wire, replace it with a new one. Also, check the settings on the opener to make sure they are correct.

Next, check the battery in the keypad. If the battery is dead, replace it with a new one. Once you’ve replaced the battery, you should be able to use the keypad again without any issues.

If the wiring and battery are fine, then the keypad may not be programmed correctly. To check this, press the “Learn” button on the opener and then enter the code into the keypad. If the code is accepted, then the keypad should work correctly. If not, try entering a different code.

What If the Issue Persists?

If the issue persists, it is likely that there is a more serious issue with the opener. If this is the case, you should contact a professional garage door company for assistance. A professional technician will be able to diagnose the issue and make any necessary repairs.

Conclusion

If your Craftsman garage door keypad is flashing, then it may be due to a faulty connection, a dead battery, or an incorrect setting. To troubleshoot the issue, check the wiring, battery, and settings. If the issue persists, it’s best to contact a professional garage door company for assistance. We hope this article has been helpful and that you are now able to troubleshoot your Craftsman garage door keypad!

What Is A Roller Garage Door Draught Excluder?

A roller garage door draught excluder is a product that is designed to prevent outside air from entering your garage. This is done by creating a seal around the outside of your door, which helps to keep the temperature inside your garage consistent. It also helps to reduce noise and dust levels, making your garage a much more comfortable place to be. The draught excluder helps to make your garage more energy-efficient as well, by preventing air from escaping and reducing the need for heating or cooling.

There are a variety of different types of roller garage door draught excluders available on the market. These range from simple strips of foam or rubber to more complex systems that incorporate springs and other components. The type of draught excluder you choose will depend on the size and shape of your garage door, as well as the climate you live in. It's important to choose one that is made from high-quality materials in order to ensure that it will last for many years.

How Does a Roller Garage Door Draught Excluder Work?

The way that a roller garage door draught excluder works is by creating a barrier between the inside and outside of your garage. This barrier is created by using foam or rubber strips that are placed around the outside of your door. The strips are designed to create a seal that prevents outside air from entering your garage. Some products also use springs and other components to further enhance the seal.

The strips are designed to be flexible, allowing them to move with the door as it opens and closes. This ensures that the seal remains tight and prevents air from entering your garage. What Are the Different Types of Roller Garage Door Draught Excluders?

There are a variety of different types of roller garage door draught excluders available on the market. The most common types are made from foam or rubber, and can be either self-adhesive or magnetic. Self-adhesive strips are the most popular option, as they are easy to install and don't require any additional tools or components. Magnetic strips are a good choice if you have metal garage doors, as they are designed to stick to the surface.

Other types of draught excluders include spring-loaded systems, which are designed to create a tighter seal around the door. These systems are more complex, but can offer a greater level of protection from outside air. There are also systems that incorporate brushes, which help to further reduce dust and noise levels inside your garage.

How to Install a Roller Garage Door Draught Excluder

Installing a roller garage door draught excluder is relatively straightforward, although you may need to seek the help of a professional if you're installing a more complex system. If you're using a self-adhesive or magnetic strip, simply clean the surface of your door and then place the strip around the outside. Make sure that the strips are pressed firmly onto the door, as any gaps could allow air to enter your garage.

If you're using a spring-loaded system, you will need to attach the springs to the frame of the door. Once this is done, attach the draught excluder to the frame and then adjust the tension of the springs to create a tight seal. If you're using a brush system, attach the brushes to the frame and then adjust the tension of the brushes to create a tight seal.

How to Maintain a Roller Garage Door Draught Excluder

Maintaining your roller garage door draught excluder is relatively straightforward. If you're using a foam or rubber strip, you should check the seal regularly to ensure that it is still tight and not letting any air through. If the seal is starting to weaken, you can apply some adhesive to the strip to help it stick. If you're using a spring-loaded system, you should check the tension of the springs regularly to ensure that the seal is still tight.

If you're using a brush system, you should check the brushes regularly to ensure that they are still in good condition. If the brushes are starting to wear down, you can replace them to ensure that the seal remains tight. You should also check the door frame and the door itself regularly to ensure that they are still in good condition.

Garage door hardware rails are a crucial part of any garage door system. A well-maintained garage door is essential to the safety and security of your home and family. That's why it's important to understand the different types of hardware rails available and how to maintain them. In this article, we'll discuss the different types of garage door hardware rails, their purpose, and how to keep them in good condition.

Types of Garage Door Hardware Rails

There are two main types of garage door hardware rails: overhead and side-mounted. Overhead rails are those that are mounted on the ceiling of your garage. They are the most common type of rail and are typically made of metal or wood. Side-mounted rails, on the other hand, are those that are mounted on the wall of your garage. These are generally made from plastic or metal.

Overhead Rails

Overhead rails are the most common type of garage door hardware rail. They are typically made of metal or wood and are used to hang and support the garage door. They are usually connected to the door tracks and the garage door opener. The rails are typically mounted on the ceiling of the garage, and the door tracks are then attached to the rails. Overhead rails are usually easy to install and provide a secure and reliable connection between the door and the opener.

Side-Mounted Rails

Side-mounted rails are those that are mounted on the wall of your garage. These are made from plastic or metal and are typically used to hang the garage door in place. Side-mounted rails are usually easier to install than overhead rails and provide a more secure connection between the door and the opener. They can also be used to add additional support to the door tracks.

Maintaining Garage Door Hardware Rails

It's important to keep your garage door hardware rails in good condition in order to keep your garage door functioning properly. Regularly inspect the hardware for signs of wear or damage and make sure to lubricate the rails periodically. You should also check that all the nuts, bolts, and screws are securely tightened. If you find any signs of damage or wear, be sure to replace the parts as soon as possible.

In addition to regular inspection and maintenance, you should also make sure to keep your garage door hardware rails clean. Dirt and debris can accumulate on the rails and cause them to wear out faster. Use a soft cloth to wipe down the rails on a regular basis to remove any dirt and debris.

Conclusion

Garage door hardware rails are an essential part of any garage door system. They provide a secure connection between the door and the opener and help keep the door in good working order. Be sure to inspect and maintain your garage door hardware rails regularly, and always replace any parts that are worn or damaged. Keeping your garage door hardware rails clean and in good condition will help keep your garage door functioning safely and securely for many years to come.

The cost of a garage door opener in the UK can vary greatly depending on the type of system you choose, the installation and the number of remotes you need. In 2023, you can expect to pay around £200 - £350 for a single-door garage door opener, with prices rising depending on the features of the system. The cost of a double-door opener can range from £500 - £800, depending on the manufacturer and the model. If you want to install an additional remote control, you should expect to pay around £50 - £75.

Types of Garage Door Opener

Garage door openers are available in a variety of styles, from basic manual systems to more advanced automatic ones. Manual openers require you to manually open and close the door, while automatic openers use either a remote or a sensor to open and close the door. Manual systems are typically simpler and cheaper to operate, while automatic systems offer more convenience and security.

If you choose an automatic system, you need to consider whether you want a belt-drive, chain-drive, or screw-drive opener. Belt-drive systems are generally quieter and smoother than the others, while chain-drive systems are more affordable. Screw-drive openers are the most expensive, but they are also the most reliable.

Installation Costs

The cost of installation for a garage door opener will also vary depending on the type of opener you choose. Some systems may require more than one professional to install, while others may be simpler and require only one professional. If you choose to do the installation yourself, you should expect to pay for the necessary tools and materials, as well as any additional parts that may be required.

The cost of installation will also depend on the size of the door, the type of opener, and the amount of time it will take to complete the job. Expect to pay anywhere from £100 to £400 for installation, depending on the size of your door and the complexity of the job.

Additional Costs

In addition to the cost of the opener and installation, you may also need to purchase additional accessories. These can include extra remotes, sensors, keypads, and safety beams. Depending on the type of system you choose, these accessories can cost anywhere from £20 to £100.

You may also need to purchase a new garage door if your existing one is damaged, outdated, or incompatible with the opener. Garage doors can range from £500 to £2000, depending on the size and material. You may also need to pay for professional installation if you choose to install a new garage door.

Finding the Best Garage Door Opener Cost UK

When looking for the best garage door opener cost UK, it is important to compare prices from different suppliers to get the best deal. You should also consider the features of each system and the installation costs. If you are buying online, make sure to read the reviews to make sure the product meets your needs.

It is also important to consider whether you need additional accessories or a new door. These can add significantly to the overall cost of the system, so it is important to factor them into your decision. Finally, make sure to ask any questions you may have before committing to a purchase.

Conclusion

In 2023, the cost of a garage door opener in the UK can vary greatly depending on the type of system you choose, the installation costs, and the number of accessories you need.

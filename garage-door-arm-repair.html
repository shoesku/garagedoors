Top Tips For Garage Door Arm Repair In 2023

Having a garage door arm that is working correctly is essential for a safe and convenient garage door. However, due to wear and tear, the arm may need to be repaired or replaced. In this article, we will go over some tips for garage door arm repair in 2023.

Assessing the Problem

The first step in garage door arm repair is assessing the problem. You should look for any signs of wear and tear such as cracks, loose or missing screws, or rust. If the arm is severely damaged, you may need to replace it. If the arm is only slightly damaged, you may be able to repair it with some simple tools.

Replacing the Arm

If you need to replace the arm, make sure that you buy the correct size. Measure the old arm to get the exact measurements. You should also make sure that the new arm is compatible with your garage door opener. If you are unsure of what size or type you need, it is best to consult with a professional.

Repairing the Arm

If you are able to repair the arm instead of replacing it, you will need some basic tools. A screwdriver, pliers, and a wrench are the most common tools needed for this job. If the arm is rusty, you may need to use a wire brush to remove the rust. Once the rust has been removed, you can use the screwdriver to replace any loose or missing screws.

Lubrication

Once you have repaired or replaced the arm, you should lubricate it. This will help ensure that it moves smoothly and lasts longer. You can use a lubricant such as WD-40 or silicone spray. Make sure that you do not use oil or grease as they can attract dirt and debris.

Checking the Hardware

You should also check the other hardware on your garage door to make sure that it is functioning properly. Make sure that the springs and rollers are in good condition and that the bolts are tight. If any of these components are worn or loose, you should replace them.

Testing the Arm

Once you have replaced or repaired the arm, you should test it to make sure that it works correctly. Open and close the door a few times to ensure that the arm is not sticking or jamming. If you find any issues, you may need to make further adjustments or repairs.

Call a Professional

If you are having difficulty repairing or replacing your garage door arm, it is best to call a professional. A professional can diagnose the problem and make sure that the repair is done correctly. They can also provide advice on how to maintain the arm and prevent future issues.

Conclusion

Garage door arm repair can be a daunting task, but it is important to make sure that your garage door is safe and secure. By following the tips listed above, you can make sure that your garage door arm is repaired or replaced correctly. If you are having any difficulty with the repair, it is best to call a professional for assistance.

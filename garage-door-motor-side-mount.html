What is a Garage Door Motor Side Mount?

A garage door motor side mount is a component of the motor that helps to ensure that the door opens and closes properly. Types of Garage Door Motor Side Mounts

There are two main types of garage door motor side mounts: the torsion spring and the extension spring. The torsion spring is a type of spring that is wound around an axle and is designed to provide a twisting force when the door is opened and closed. This type of motor side mount offers a greater degree of control over the speed of the door. The extension spring, on the other hand, is a type of spring that is stretched out when the door is opened and is designed to provide a pushing force when the door is opened and closed. This type of motor side mount provides less control over the speed of the door, but it is the most common type of motor side mount and is typically the most cost-effective option.

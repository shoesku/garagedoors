How to Install Vinyl Window Decals

Installing vinyl window decals is a relatively simple process. All you need is a few basic tools, such as a measuring tape, a utility knife, and a squeegee. Start by measuring the area you want to cover with the decal. Then, use the utility knife to cut the decal to the proper size for your garage door. Next, use the squeegee to remove any air bubbles from the surface of the decal and to make sure it is firmly attached to the garage door.

Once the decal is attached, it’s important to make sure it is securely attached. This can be done by adding a few strips of painter’s tape to the edges of the decal. This will help keep the decal in place and prevent it from peeling off over time. Once the decal is securely in place, you can enjoy the unique look it adds to your garage door.

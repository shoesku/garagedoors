Why You Should Hold The Button To Close Your Garage Door

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

When it comes to garage door openers, many people are unaware of the importance of holding the button to close the door. We’ve all seen the commercials: press the button on the remote and the door magically opens or closes. Unfortunately, the reality is that you should hold the button to close your garage door. Here’s why.

The Garage Door Safety Feature

The number one reason why you should hold the button to close the garage door is for safety. Most modern garage door openers come with a safety feature that reverses the door if it senses something in its path. This is designed to prevent a car, a pet, or a person from being struck by the door and injured. The technology behind it is actually quite simple. When the opener senses something in its path, it will reverse the door automatically.

However, if you don’t hold the button to close the door, the opener won’t be able to detect if something is in its path. So if something is in the way of the door, it won’t be able to reverse and the item will be crushed by the door.

The Importance of Maintenance

Holding the button to close the garage door is also important for maintaining the door opener. If you don’t hold the button, the opener won’t be able to detect any obstacles in its path and the door will keep going until it reaches the end of its track. This can cause the opener to strain and eventually wear out. Holding the button allows the opener to detect any obstructions and will prevent it from straining itself.

In addition, if the opener is not working properly, holding the button can help you detect the issue. If the door reverses while you are holding the button, then you know that the opener is working properly. If the door doesn’t reverse, then you know that there is a problem with the opener and it needs to be serviced.

Safety For Your Home

Holding the button to close the garage door is also important for the safety of your home. If the opener isn’t working properly, then it can be easy for someone to open the door and gain access to your home. By holding the button to close the door, you can ensure that the door is always securely closed.

In addition, if you have an older model opener that doesn’t have the safety feature, then you should definitely be holding the button to close the door. This will help prevent the door from being damaged or someone from gaining access to your home.

The Bottom Line

Holding the button to close your garage door is an important safety measure. Not only will it help prevent injuries, but it will also help maintain the opener and ensure that your home is secure. So the next time you press the button to close the door, be sure to hold it until the door is closed.

How To Properly Grease Your Genie Garage Door Track

Do you want your Genie garage door to open and close smoothly? You need to make sure it's properly greased. Greasing your garage door track is an essential part of its maintenance. It’ll help prevent rust and other damage, and it’ll also ensure that your door operates reliably. Here’s everything you need to know about greasing your Genie garage door track.

What Is a Genie Garage Door Track?

A Genie garage door track is the component that guides the door along its path. It runs along the edges of the door and helps it open and close. The track is usually made of steel, and it’s important to make sure it’s properly greased. That way, the door will move along it without any trouble.

Why Should You Grease Your Genie Garage Door Track?

If you don’t properly grease your Genie garage door track, it can cause a lot of issues. The track may start to rust, which can cause it to break. It can also cause the door to start making a lot of noise when it opens and closes. Plus, if the track isn’t properly greased, the door may not open or close correctly.

What Do You Need to Grease Your Genie Garage Door Track?

Before you start greasing your Genie garage door track, you’ll need to make sure you have the right supplies. You’ll need a grease gun, some grease cartridges, and a rag. You’ll also need a ladder or another tool to help you reach the track. Once you have all the supplies ready, you’re ready to start greasing your Genie garage door track.

How to Grease Your Genie Garage Door Track

Now that you have all the supplies you need, it’s time to start greasing your Genie garage door track. To start, you’ll need to open the garage door and climb up the ladder so you can reach the track. Once you’re up there, you can start to grease the track. First, attach the grease gun to the track, then insert a grease cartridge into the gun. Now you can start to grease the track. Move the gun back and forth along the track, making sure to cover the entire length of it. Keep going until you’ve used up all the grease. Once you’re done, you can use the rag to wipe off any excess grease.

How Often Should You Grease Your Genie Garage Door Track?

Once you’ve greased your Genie garage door track, you should do it again every six months or so. That way, you can make sure it’s always in good condition and that it’s working properly. You should also inspect the track regularly to make sure there’s no damage or rust.

Conclusion

Greasing your Genie garage door track is an essential part of its maintenance. It’ll help prevent rust and other damage, and it’ll also ensure that your door operates reliably. Make sure you have the right supplies, then follow the instructions above to grease your Genie garage door track properly. Don’t forget to check the track regularly and to grease it every six months.

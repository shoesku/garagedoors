Adding molding to your garage door is a great way to add style and character to your home. Lowes is one of the best places to get garage door molding. Here is a short guide on how to install garage door molding from Lowes.

Step 1 - Measure the Door

Before you start shopping for molding, you need to measure the door. Take a measuring tape and measure the width and height of the door. Write down the measurements and take them with you when you shop for molding. This will help you pick the right size and style of molding.

Step 2 - Selecting the Molding

Once you have the measurements, you can start shopping for the molding. Lowes has a wide selection of garage door molding, in various sizes and styles. You can choose from traditional crown molding, or something more modern, like a decorative beadboard pattern. Take your time and look through all the options, until you find the perfect molding for your door.

Step 3 - Install the Molding

Once you have selected the molding, you can start installing it. If you are inexperienced with carpentry, it is recommended that you hire a professional to install the molding. Lowes offers installation services, so you can have a professional come to your home and install the molding for you. If you want to do it yourself, there are a few steps you should follow. Start by cutting the molding to size, using a saw. Then, use a hammer and nails to attach the molding to the door. Make sure that the nails are driven in securely, and that the molding is attached securely.

Step 4 - Finishing Touches

Once the molding is installed, you can add some finishing touches. You can add some paint or stain to the molding, to match the color of the door. You can also add some trim around the edges, to give the door a finished look. Take your time, and make sure that the molding is installed correctly and looks great.

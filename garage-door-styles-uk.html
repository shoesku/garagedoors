The Different Types of Garage Doors Available to UK Consumers in 2023

Today, UK consumers have a wide variety of garage door styles to choose from. From traditional designs to modern, more contemporary styles, there’s something for everyone. In this article, we’ll take a look at the different types of garage doors available to UK consumers in 2023. We’ll discuss the pros and cons of each type of door, as well as the cost, so you can make an informed decision about which garage door style is best for your needs.

Up and Over Garage Doors

Up and over garage doors are the most popular type of garage door in the UK. They’re relatively easy to install and maintain, and they offer good insulation and security. They’re available in a range of materials, including steel, aluminum, and wood, and they come in a variety of colors and styles to suit any home. The downside is that they can be expensive, and they don’t always offer the best insulation or security.

Roller Garage Doors

Roller garage doors are a great option for those who want a more modern look for their garage. They’re usually made from aluminum or steel and are available in a range of colors and styles. The advantage of roller garage doors is that they offer good insulation and security, and they’re relatively easy to install and maintain. The downside is that they can be expensive and require more space than other types of garage doors.

Side-Hinged Garage Doors

Side-hinged garage doors are a great option for those who want a more traditional look for their garage. They’re usually made from wood and are available in a range of colors and styles. The advantage of side-hinged garage doors is that they offer good insulation and security, and they’re relatively easy to install and maintain. The downside is that they can be expensive and require more space than other types of garage doors.

Sectional Garage Doors

Sectional garage doors are a great option for those who want a modern look for their garage. They’re usually made from aluminum or steel and are available in a range of colors and styles. The advantage of sectional garage doors is that they offer good insulation and security, and they’re relatively easy to install and maintain. The downside is that they can be expensive and require more space than other types of garage doors.

Electric Garage Doors

Electric garage doors are a great option for those who want a modern look for their garage. They’re usually made from aluminum or steel and are available in a range of colors and styles. The advantage of electric garage doors is that they offer good insulation and security, and they’re relatively easy to install and maintain. The downside is that they can be expensive and require more space than other types of garage doors.

Wooden Garage Doors

Wooden garage doors are a great option for those who want a more traditional look for their garage. They’re usually made from wood and are available in a range of colors and styles. The advantage of wooden garage doors is that they offer good insulation and security, and they’re relatively easy to install and maintain. The downside is that they can be expensive and require more space than other types of garage doors.

Insulated Garage Doors

Insulated garage doors are a great option for those who want a more energy-efficient garage. They’re usually made from aluminum or steel and are available in a range of colors and styles. The advantage of insulated garage doors is that they offer good insulation and security, and they’re relatively easy to install and maintain. The downside is that they can be expensive and require more space than other types of garage doors.

The Cost of Garage Doors in the UK in 2023

The cost of garage doors in the UK in 2023 varies depending on the type and size of the door. Up and over garage doors typically cost between £500 and £1,500, while roller garage doors can cost up to £2,500. Side-hinged garage doors tend to be the most expensive, costing up to £3,000. Electric garage doors usually cost between £1,000 and £2,000, while wooden and insulated garage doors cost between £1,500 and £3,000.

Conclusion

When it comes to choosing the right garage door style for your home, there are a few things you should consider. Firstly, think about the size of your garage and the type of material you want. Then, think about the cost, as this will vary depending on the type of door you choose. Finally, consider the insulation and security of the door, as this will affect how much energy you save in the long run. With these factors in mind, you’ll be able to find the perfect garage door style for your home in the UK in 2023.

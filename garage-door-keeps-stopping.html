How To Fix Your Garage Door That Keeps Stopping?

Are you having trouble with a garage door that keeps stopping? It can be a real nuisance and can be a problem that needs to be fixed quickly. Every home owner has experienced a garage door that won’t stay open or close properly. It can be very frustrating and can cause a lot of inconvenience. In this article, we will discuss the different causes of this problem and how to fix it.

What Causes a Garage Door to Stop?

There are several reasons why your garage door may be stopping. The most common cause is a misaligned or worn out garage door opener. This is the most common issue and one that can be easily fixed. Another potential issue is a broken or malfunctioning garage door spring. This can also cause your garage door to stop working. Furthermore, a loose or broken cable can also be the cause of your garage door stopping.

How to Align a Garage Door Opener

If your garage door opener is the cause of your garage door stopping, then you will need to align it. The first step is to locate the opener’s manual. This will provide you with instructions on how to align the opener. You will need to adjust the settings on the opener to ensure that the door is properly aligned. It is important to follow the instructions carefully as failure to do so could result in further damage to the opener.

How to Fix a Broken Garage Door Spring

If your garage door spring is the cause of your problem, then you will need to replace it. This is a fairly simple process but you will need to have the right tools and equipment. You will need to get a new spring from your local hardware store. Once you have the new spring, you will need to remove the old spring and replace it with the new one. You will then need to adjust the tension on the new spring to ensure it is properly aligned.

How to Fix a Loose or Broken Cable

If your garage door has a loose or broken cable, then you will need to replace it. You will need to get a new cable from your local hardware store. Once you have the new cable, you will need to remove the old one and replace it with the new one. It is important to ensure that the cable is properly aligned and tensioned. If it is not properly aligned, then it can cause problems with the door.

How to Test Your Garage Door

Once you have fixed the problem with your garage door, you will need to test it to ensure that it is working properly. You should open and close the door several times to make sure that it is working properly. If the door is still stopping, then you may need to make further adjustments. It is important to ensure that the door is working properly before using it.

Conclusion

If your garage door keeps stopping, then it can be a real nuisance. There are several potential causes of this problem, including a misaligned garage door opener, a broken garage door spring, or a loose or broken cable. To fix this problem, you will need to align the opener, replace the spring, or replace the cable. Once you have fixed the problem, you should test the door to ensure it is working properly.

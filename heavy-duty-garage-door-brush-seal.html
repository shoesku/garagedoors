The garage door is the largest single entryway into your home, so it's important to keep it properly sealed. A heavy duty garage door brush seal is an effective way to seal the door from the outside elements and prevent air from leaking in or out. This type of seal is also great for keeping out dust, bugs, and other debris. In this article, we'll explain the benefits of a heavy duty garage door brush seal and how to install it.

What is a Heavy Duty Garage Door Brush Seal?

A heavy duty garage door brush seal is an effective way to seal the garage door from the outside elements. It helps to reduce air leakage and prevent dust, bugs, and other debris from entering the garage. The seal is made from flexible bristles that are mounted onto a heavy-duty aluminum frame. This creates a seal that is both strong and durable. The brush seal is also easy to install and can be used on any type of garage door.

Benefits of Installing a Heavy Duty Garage Door Brush Seal

Installing a heavy duty garage door brush seal can provide many benefits to homeowners. It can help to reduce energy costs by up to 30% by preventing air leakage. It also helps to keep out dust, bugs, and other debris so you don't have to worry about cleaning up the garage. Additionally, a brush seal can also add to the aesthetic of your home by giving it a neat, finished look.

How to Install a Heavy Duty Garage Door Brush Seal

Installing a heavy duty garage door brush seal is relatively simple and can be done in a few easy steps. First, begin by measuring the width of the door opening and determine the size of the seal that you need. Then, mark the door opening and use a saw to cut the aluminum frame to size. Once the frame is cut, attach the bristles to the frame and then mount it to the door. Finally, attach the weatherstripping to the top and bottom of the frame to complete the installation.

Maintenance and Care of a Heavy Duty Garage Door Brush Seal

Heavy duty garage door brush seals require minimal maintenance and can last for many years. However, it's important to inspect the seal periodically to make sure it is in good condition. If you notice any damage, such as broken bristles or torn weatherstripping, it's best to replace the seal as soon as possible. Additionally, it's important to keep the seal clean by vacuuming away any dirt or debris.

The Bottom Line

A heavy duty garage door brush seal is a great way to seal the garage door from the outside elements and prevent air from leaking in or out. It can also help to reduce energy costs and provide an aesthetically pleasing look to your home. Installing a heavy duty garage door brush seal is relatively simple and can be done in a few easy steps. Additionally, minimal maintenance is required to keep the seal in good condition.

Understanding Your Home's Garage Door Requirements

When building a home in Australia, whether it is a new build or an extension, it is important to consider the size of the garage door you will need. The right garage door can add value to your home and make it more secure, so it's worth taking the time to get the measurements right. Here, we provide all the information you need on garage door dimensions in Australia, so you can make sure your new garage door fits perfectly.

Single-Car Garage Doors

If you are building a single-car garage, then the standard size for the door is 2400mm wide by 2130mm high. This size is suitable for most vehicles, including small cars, vans, and SUVs. It is likely that this is the size of garage door you will need, but there are also other sizes available if you need to accommodate larger vehicles such as 4x4s or pickup trucks.

Double-Car Garage Doors

If you are building a double-car garage, then the standard size for the door is 4800mm wide by 2130mm high. This size is suitable for most two-car garages, and it is likely that this is the size of garage door you will need. However, if you are building a larger garage then you may need to adjust the size of the door accordingly.

Types of Garage Doors

When it comes to choosing the type of garage door, you have a few options. The most popular type is the sectional door, which is made up of several sections that fold up when the door is opened. This type of door is usually made from steel, and is very strong and secure. Other popular types of garage door are roller doors, which roll up when opened, and tilt doors, which tilt upwards when opened.

Customizing Your Garage Door

If you need a garage door that is a size or shape that is not available in a standard garage door, then you may want to consider having a custom-made door. This type of door can be made to any size or shape, and you can choose the material, colour, and design to suit your home. This is a more expensive option, but it can be worth it if you need a door of a particular size or design.

Installing Your Garage Door

Once you have chosen the size and type of garage door that you need, the next step is to install it. Installing a garage door is a complex job that should only be carried out by a professional. They will be able to ensure that the door is installed correctly and that it is safe and secure.

Maintaining Your Garage Door

Once your garage door is installed, it is important to maintain it by regularly checking it for signs of wear and tear and carrying out any necessary repairs. This will ensure that your garage door remains secure and in good condition. It is also important to keep the door clean, as dirt and grime can affect the functioning of the door.

Conclusion

Garage door dimensions in Australia are an important factor to consider when building a new home or extension. Knowing the right size for your garage door is essential for ensuring that your garage door fits properly and is secure. There are a number of options when it comes to choosing the type of garage door, and you may even need to have a custom-made door if the standard sizes are not suitable for your needs. Finally, it is important to maintain your garage door by regularly checking it for wear and tear and making any necessary repairs.

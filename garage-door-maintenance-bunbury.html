Why Regular Maintenance is Important

One of the most important aspects of owning and maintaining a garage door in the Bunbury area is to ensure that it is regularly maintained. This is a critical component in keeping your door functioning properly and extending its life span. A well-maintained garage door can last anywhere from 10-25 years, depending on the type of door you have and how often it is serviced. Without regular maintenance, the lifespan of your door can be drastically reduced due to the wear and tear of everyday use.

Regular maintenance can also help you to identify any problems with your garage door before they become major issues. Catching potential problems early on can save you time and money in the long run. It is also important to ensure that all safety features of your garage door are always functioning properly. Not only will this help to keep you and your family safe, but it can also help to prevent any costly repairs or replacements that may be needed due to a malfunctioning safety feature.

Types of Maintenance

The type of maintenance that your garage door requires will depend on the type of door you have. The most common types of garage doors are roller doors, sectional doors, and tilt doors. Each type of door requires a different type of maintenance to keep them functioning properly.

Roller doors will require lubrication of the rollers and tracks as well as checking for any problems with the springs or cables. Sectional doors will require checking of the springs, cables, and tracks as well as lubrication of any moving parts. Tilt doors require lubrication of all moving parts as well as checking of the springs and cables.

It is also important to make sure that all the weather seals around your garage door are in good condition. This is important for both the safety and efficiency of the door. If the seals are damaged or worn, it can lead to drafts and inefficiency in the operation of the door.

Benefits of Professional Maintenance

Hiring a professional to perform maintenance on your garage door is often the best option. They will be able to inspect the door and identify any potential issues that could be causing it to malfunction. They will also be able to provide you with advice on how to best maintain your door to keep it functioning properly for years to come.

Professional maintenance will also give you peace of mind that the job is being done correctly. Attempting to perform maintenance on a garage door without the proper training and knowledge can lead to further damage or even injury.

Conclusion

Regular maintenance of your garage door is a critical component in keeping it functioning properly and extending its life span. It is important to have the door serviced regularly to ensure all safety features are functioning properly and that any potential issues are identified and fixed quickly. Hiring a professional to perform maintenance on your garage door is often the best option to ensure that the job is done correctly.

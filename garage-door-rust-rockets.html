How Do Garage Door Rust Rockets Work?

Garage door rust rockets work by creating a protective coating on the surface of your garage door. This coating is applied using a special applicator gun that is designed to evenly distribute the coating across the entire panel. The coating works by blocking the oxygen that rust needs to form, and the protective barrier it creates stops any further corrosion from occurring. The coating is also designed to be weather-resistant, so it won’t be affected by rain or snow.

Benefits of Installing Garage Door Rust Rockets

Installing garage door rust rockets is a great way to protect your garage door from corrosion and rust. By protecting it from these elements, you can keep your garage door looking like new for longer and save yourself money in the long run.

Costs of Installing Garage Door Rust Rockets

The cost of installing garage door rust rockets will vary depending on the size of your garage door and the number of panels you need coated.

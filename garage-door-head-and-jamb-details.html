Garage Door Head

Garage door heads are essential components of any garage door system. They are used to provide a secure and stable frame around the door and to support the weight of the door itself. The door head is typically made of steel or aluminum and is mounted onto the wall or ceiling of the garage. It is important to choose the right door head for your garage door system as it will provide the necessary security, strength and stability to the door.

When selecting the right door head, it is important to consider the design, material, size and type of the door head. The design of the door head should match the overall design of the garage door system. For example, a simple rectangular design may be more suitable for a single-panel door, while a more ornate or decorative design may be better suited for a multi-panel door. The material of the door head should be strong and durable, as it will be supporting the weight of the door.

The size of the door head should be based on the size of the door itself. It is important to ensure that the door head will be able to support the weight of the door, as well as any additional hardware or components that may be attached to the door. Additionally, the type of door head should be selected based on the type of garage door system. For example, a roller door head may be better suited for a roller door, while a sectional door head may be better suited for a sectional door.

What Are the Different Types of Garage Door Repairs?

When it comes to garage door repairs, there are many different types of issues that can arise. Some of the most common types of garage door repairs include:

  • Broken springs: Over time, the springs that help open and close the door can become worn out and break. If this happens, you will need to have them replaced to ensure the door opens and closes properly.
  • Damaged tracks: The tracks that help guide the door as it opens and closes can become damaged over time and need to be repaired or replaced.
  • Broken sensors: The sensors that detect when the door should open or close can malfunction or become damaged, resulting in the door not working as it should.
  • Damaged panels: The panels that make up the door can become worn out over time, resulting in dents and cracks. In this case, you’ll need to have them repaired or replaced.

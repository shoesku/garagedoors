Does Keeping The Garage Door Open Keep Your House Cooler?

Why Keeping the Garage Door Open Is a Good Idea

For many years, homeowners have been advised to keep the garage door open during the hot summer months to help keep their homes cooler. This may seem counterintuitive, since the garage is often the hottest part of a home. But there are several benefits to keeping the garage door open during the summer. For one, it allows hot air to escape, reducing the temperature of the living space. Additionally, it can reduce the strain on your air conditioning system, as it won’t have to work as hard to cool your home. Finally, it can help keep your home’s air circulation system running more efficiently, resulting in better air quality.

What Are the Drawbacks of Keeping the Garage Door Open?

Though keeping the garage door open during the summer can be beneficial, there are also some drawbacks to this practice. For one, leaving the door open can be an invitation for pests, such as rodents and insects, to enter your home. Additionally, if your garage is attached to the living area of your home, the open door can cause an increase in noise, leading to an unpleasant living environment. Finally, leaving the door open can be a security risk, as it can be easier for intruders to gain access to your home.

Tips to Keep Your Home Cool with an Open Garage Door

To make the most of the benefits of keeping your garage door open while avoiding the drawbacks, there are a few tips to follow. For one, it’s best to keep the door open during the day, when the weather is hottest. This will help keep the temperature in your home lower and your air conditioning system running more efficiently. Additionally, it’s important to keep your garage well-ventilated, as this will help reduce the risk of pests entering your home. Finally, it’s important to keep your garage secure, as it can be an easy target for intruders. Make sure to keep all windows and doors securely locked when the garage door is open.

Using a Fan to Help Keep Your Home Cooler

In addition to keeping the garage door open, you can also use a fan to help keep your home cooler. This is especially useful in homes where the garage is not attached to the living area. Using a fan to circulate air can help reduce the temperature in your home, as well as reduce the strain on your air conditioning system. Additionally, using a fan can help keep air circulating more efficiently, resulting in better air quality. However, it’s important to note that using a fan alone won’t be enough to keep your home cool on hot summer days.

Conclusion

Keeping your garage door open during the hot summer months can be a great way to keep your home cooler and reduce the strain on your air conditioning system. However, it’s important to keep in mind the potential drawbacks, such as pests, noise, and security risks. To make the most of the benefits of keeping the garage door open, it’s important to keep your garage well-ventilated, secure, and use a fan to circulate air. By following these tips, you’ll be able to keep your home cooler and more comfortable during the hot summer months.

Types of Garage Door Openers at Lowes

Lowes offers a wide range of garage door openers, including chain-driven, belt-driven, and screw-driven models. Chain-driven openers are the most common type of garage door opener and are known for their reliable performance. Belt-driven openers are quieter than chain-driven models and are ideal for homeowners who value quiet operation. Screw-driven openers are the most powerful type of garage door opener, but they are more expensive than the other models.

Cost of Garage Door Openers at Lowes

The cost of a garage door opener varies depending on the type and features. Chain-driven openers are generally the most affordable, with prices ranging from $100 to $300. Belt-driven openers typically range from $200 to $400, while screw-driven openers can cost anywhere from $400 to $600. You'll also need to factor in the cost of installation, which can range from $100 to $400, depending on the complexity of the job.

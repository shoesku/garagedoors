A garage door is a crucial element of any home. It serves as a secure barrier between your car and the world outside. But, do you know the dimensions of a 2 car garage door?

The most common garage door size for a two-car garage is 16 feet wide by 7 feet high. However, many homes have different measurements for their garage doors. Generally, the width of a two-car garage door will range from 14 to 18 feet. The height will typically range from 6 to 8 feet.

If you are looking for a garage door that is bigger than the standard two-car size, you will need to measure the opening of your garage. This will give you the specifics of what size door you need to purchase.

In addition to the standard sizes, you may also need to consider the design of the garage door. For example, if you want a door with windows, you will need to order a door that is slightly larger than the standard size. This is because the windows will take up some of the space.

Types of 2 Car Garage Doors

When it comes to garage doors, there are many different types to choose from. The most common type is the roll-up door. This type of door rolls up and down on tracks. The tracks are typically made of steel and the door is made from either steel or aluminum.

If you are looking for a more decorative look for your garage, you can opt for a carriage-style door. This type of door opens outwards and has a more classic look. It is typically made of wood.

You can also find insulated garage doors. These doors are designed to help keep your garage at a comfortable temperature. They are typically made of steel and are insulated with foam or insulation.

Finally, you can also find garage doors that are automatic. These doors are operated by remote control and open and close with the push of a button.

Choosing the Right 2 Car Garage Door

Choosing the right 2 car garage door for your home is important. You need to make sure that the door is the right size and that it matches the style of your home. You also need to make sure that the door is properly installed and that it is secure.

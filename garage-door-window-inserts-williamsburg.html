What Are Garage Door Window Inserts?

Garage door window inserts are a type of decorative trim that can be added to your home's garage door. These inserts are typically made from plastic, aluminum, or vinyl, and come in various shapes, sizes, and colors. They can be used to add a unique touch of style to your home's exterior, and can be used to match the overall design of your home's architecture. Garage door window inserts can be installed in any style of door, from double-hung to carriage-style doors.

Benefits of Garage Door Window Inserts

Garage door window inserts are a great way to give your home's exterior a boost. Not only do they add a unique touch of style, but they can also be used to help improve your home's energy efficiency. By installing window inserts, you can help reduce the amount of air that escapes through your garage door. This can help keep your home comfortable while also reducing your energy bills. Additionally, window inserts can also help to reduce outside noise, making your home quieter and more peaceful.

How to Install Garage Door Window Inserts

Installing garage door window inserts can be a relatively easy process if you have the right tools and materials. First, you'll need to measure the size of your garage door so you can purchase the correct size inserts. Once you have the inserts, you'll need to use a drill and screws to attach them to the door. Make sure to drill pilot holes first to ensure the screws don't damage the door. Once the inserts are installed, you can use caulk or sealant to seal any gaps to help improve energy efficiency.

How Much Does it Cost to Repair a Garage Door?

What Are Some Common Garage Door Repairs?

Garage doors are used daily and can experience wear and tear over time. Some of the most common garage door repairs include:

  • Replacing broken springs
  • Replacing worn-out rollers
  • Repairing damaged tracks
  • Fixing door openers
  • Replacing worn-out weatherstripping
  • Repairing damaged panels
  • Replacing broken cables

These repairs can be simple or complex, depending on the extent of the damage and the type of repair needed. For example, a broken spring is a relatively simple repair, while replacing damaged panels can be more complex and expensive.

What Factors Can Affect the Cost of Repair?

The cost of garage door repair may vary depending on a variety of factors, such as:

  • Type of garage door
  • Size of the door
  • Type of repair needed
  • Age of the door

The age of the door may also affect the cost of repair. Older doors may need more extensive repairs and may cost more to repair. Additionally, the type of repair needed can also affect the cost of repair.

What is a Garage Door Draught Excluder?

A garage door draught excluder is a device that is designed to reduce air leakage around your garage door. This helps to keep your home warmer in the winter and cooler in the summer, reducing your energy costs and increasing the comfort of your home. These devices are typically easy to install and can be purchased in a variety of styles and sizes.

The Benefits of Installing a Draught Excluder

One of the main benefits of installing a garage door draught excluder is the reduction in energy costs. By preventing air leakage around your garage door, you can reduce your energy costs by up to 10%. Not only will this help to reduce your energy bills, but it will also help to keep your home more comfortable.

Another benefit of installing a draught excluder is that it can help to reduce noise levels. This is especially beneficial if your garage is located close to your home, as noise from the street or other sources can easily be heard inside. By installing a draught excluder, you can help to reduce the amount of noise that enters your home, making it more comfortable and enjoyable to spend time in.

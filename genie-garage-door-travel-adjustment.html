What Is Genie Garage Door Travel Adjustment?

Genie garage door travel adjustment is an adjustment to the travel distance of the Genie garage door opener. This adjustment is a necessary part of Genie garage door opener installation and maintenance, and is something that should be done on a regular basis for optimum performance. Adjusting Genie garage door travel can be done in a few simple steps and requires minimal tools.

Tools Needed For Travel Adjustment

The tools needed to adjust Genie garage door travel include a flat head screwdriver, a ruler or measuring tape, and a drill. The drill should have a 7/32 inch bit to ensure the proper size holes are drilled. It is also important to make sure the drill bit used is a higher quality bit to reduce the chance of damaging the Genie garage door opener.

Adjusting Genie Garage Door Travel

The first step in adjusting Genie garage door travel is to determine the correct travel distance. This can be done by measuring the opening and closing travel distance of the Genie garage door opener. Once the correct travel distance has been determined, the Genie garage door opener must be turned off and the power supply to the garage door opener must be disconnected.

The Genie garage door opener must then be opened manually until the travel distance has been achieved. Next, the Genie garage door opener must be secured in place with a flat head screwdriver. Once the Genie garage door opener is secured in place, the holes for the travel adjustment must be drilled in the Genie garage door opener. The holes should be drilled at the same distance as the travel distance. Finally, the Genie garage door opener must be reattached to the power supply.

Benefits of Adjusting Genie Garage Door Travel

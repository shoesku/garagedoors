Tips And Tricks For Replacing A Hormann Garage Door Cable

Replacing a Hormann garage door cable is a relatively simple task, but it does require some basic understanding of the mechanics of a garage door and a willingness to get your hands dirty. The good news is that it can usually be accomplished in a few simple steps. In this article, we'll take a look at the steps you need to take to replace your Hormann garage door cable.

Step 1: Disconnect Power

Before you begin any work, it is essential that you turn off the power to the garage door. This will ensure your safety, as well as the safety of anyone else who may be in the area. If you are not sure how to do this, call a professional for assistance.

Step 2: Remove the Old Cable

Once the power is off, you can start to remove the old cable. Start by removing the mounting bolts from the cable drum. Then, pull the cable out of the drum, taking care to note the direction in which you should be winding the new cable. You may need to use a pair of pliers to help loosen any tight spots in the cable.

Step 3: Install the New Cable

Now that you have the old cable removed, you can begin to install the new one. Start by winding the new cable in the opposite direction of the way you removed the old cable. Make sure it is wound tightly, as the cable needs to be able to take the tension of the garage door when it is opened. Once the new cable is in place, secure it with the mounting bolts that you removed from the drum.

Step 4: Test the New Cable

Once the new cable is installed, it is important to test it before reconnecting the power. Open and close the garage door several times to make sure that it is functioning properly. If there are any issues, such as the cable slipping or the door not closing completely, you may need to adjust the tension of the cable. If it still does not seem to be working properly, you may need to call in a professional.

Step 5: Reconnect the Power

Once you are sure that the new cable is functioning properly, you can reconnect the power to the garage door. Make sure that the power is off before you start, and then follow the instructions provided by your garage door manufacturer for reconnecting the power. Once the power is reconnected, you should be able to open and close the door without any issues.

Conclusion

Replacing a Hormann garage door cable is a relatively simple task, and one that you can usually do yourself with a few basic tools. Just make sure that you disconnect the power before beginning, and follow the instructions provided by your garage door manufacturer. If you encounter any issues, don't hesitate to contact a professional for assistance.

What Does Edmonton Garage Door Weather Stripping Do?

Edmonton garage door weather stripping works by sealing off the gap between the door and the opening. This gap can be an open invitation to the elements, as it allows cold air, moisture and dust to come in, and warm air to escape. The weatherstripping works by providing a barrier between the door and the exterior environment, helping to keep your home at the desired temperature.

Types of Weather Stripping

There are a few different types of Edmonton garage door weather stripping available, including foam, plastic, rubber and vinyl. Each of these has its own advantages and features, so you’ll need to choose the one that suits your needs best. Foam weatherstripping is the most affordable option, but it does not last as long as some of the other options. Plastic and rubber weatherstripping are more durable and offer better insulation, but they can be quite expensive. Vinyl weatherstripping is the most durable and offers the best insulation, but it is also the most expensive of the options.

How to Install Garage Door Weather Stripping in Edmonton

Installing garage door weather stripping in Edmonton is not a difficult task, but it is important to take your time and make sure everything is done correctly. The first step is to clean the door frame and remove any dirt, dust or debris. Next, measure the width of the frame and cut the weatherstripping to fit. Be sure to leave a 1/8 inch gap between the weatherstripping and the door frame to ensure proper insulation. Finally, attach the weatherstripping to the frame with nails, screws or adhesive.

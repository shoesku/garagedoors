What Is A Double Garage Door?

A double garage door is a type of garage door that is designed to open and close using two separate doors that are connected to each other. The two doors are usually connected with a hinge and are usually opened and closed using one motor or one manual opener. The two doors can be opened and closed either simultaneously or individually, depending on the type of double garage door you have. Double garage doors are becoming increasingly popular in Cape Town due to their convenience and safety. They provide the added security of having two separate doors, as well as the ability to open and close the two doors at the same time.

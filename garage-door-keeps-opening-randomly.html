What To Do When Your Garage Door Keeps Opening Randomly

Introduction

Are you having trouble with your garage door opening randomly? This can be a huge inconvenience, as well as a safety hazard. Fortunately, there are some steps you can take to identify the cause of the problem and fix it. In this article, we’ll explain what to do if your garage door keeps opening randomly. Read on to find out more.

Check for Obstructions

The first thing you should check for is any obstructions in the way of the garage door. If there is something preventing the door from closing properly, it can cause it to open randomly. Look for any objects that may be blocking the door's path, such as toys, tools, or boxes. If you find anything blocking the door, remove it and try closing the door again.

Check the Sensors

If you don’t find any obstructions, then the next thing to check is the door’s sensors. Most modern garage doors come with safety sensors that detect if there is something in the way of the door. If these sensors are blocked or damaged, they can cause the door to open randomly. Check the sensors to make sure they are aligned properly and that there is nothing blocking them.

Check the Wiring

If the sensors seem to be working properly, then you should check the wiring. Over time, the wiring can become loose or damaged, which can cause the door to open randomly. If you are comfortable doing so, you can try to locate the wiring and inspect it for any damage. If you do find any loose or damaged wiring, you should contact a professional to replace it.

Check the Opener

If the wiring seems to be in good condition, then you should check the garage door opener. This is the device that controls the opening and closing of the door. If it is malfunctioning, it can cause the door to open randomly. Check the opener to make sure it is working properly, and if it is not, you should contact a professional for repair or replacement.

Check the Springs

The garage door springs are responsible for lifting and lowering the door, and if they are worn or damaged, they can cause the door to open randomly. If you are comfortable doing so, you can inspect the springs for any signs of wear or damage. If you find any, you should contact a professional to replace them.

Check the Tracks

Finally, you should check the tracks that the garage door travels on. Over time, the tracks can become bent or damaged, which can cause the door to open randomly. If you find any damage to the tracks, you should contact a professional to repair or replace them.

Conclusion

A garage door that keeps opening randomly can be a real nuisance, as well as a safety hazard. However, by following the steps outlined above, you should be able to identify and fix the cause of the problem. Be sure to check for any obstructions, verify that the sensors are functioning properly, check the wiring, inspect the opener, examine the springs, and inspect the tracks. If you do find any damage, be sure to contact a professional for assistance.

Garage doors these days come in a variety of styles and materials. They can be made of wood, metal, fiberglass, or vinyl, and are available in a range of sizes, shapes, and colors. While the look of a garage door is important, the hinges that hold it together are equally important. At Lowes, you have a wide selection of garage door hinges to choose from.

Types of Garage Door Hinges

Lowes offers several types of garage door hinges, including butt hinges, surface hinges, and strap hinges. Butt hinges are the most common type of hinge and are installed between the door and the frame. They are often used on lighter doors. Surface hinges are mounted on the outside of the door and frame, and they can support heavier doors. Strap hinges are usually used on larger, heavier doors and are mounted on the outside of the door and frame.

Materials Used in Garage Door Hinges

Garage door hinges at Lowes are typically made from steel, aluminum, or brass. Steel is the most common and economical choice, but it can rust over time. Aluminum is light and corrosion-resistant, but it is more expensive than steel. Brass is the most expensive option and is the most durable and corrosion-resistant, but it is also the heaviest. Depending on the type of garage door you have, you may need to use a specific material for your hinges.

Choosing the Right Size and Style of Garage Door Hinges

When selecting garage door hinges at Lowes, it is important to choose the right size and style for your door. The size of the hinge will depend on the size of the door and the weight that needs to be supported. The style of the hinge will depend on the look you are trying to achieve. Some hinges are designed to be hidden when the door is closed, while others are designed to be visible.

Installation of Garage Door Hinges at Lowes

Once you have chosen the appropriate type, size, and style of garage door hinges, you will need to install them properly. Depending on the type of hinges you have, installation may involve drilling or screwing the hinges into place. If you are unsure of how to install the hinges, you can always refer to the instructions provided by Lowes or consult a professional.

Conclusion

Garage door hinges are an important part of the overall look and security of your home. At Lowes, you have a wide variety of options to choose from in terms of types, materials, sizes, and styles of hinges. It is important to choose the right type, size, and style of hinges for your door, and to make sure they are installed properly. With the right hinges, you can ensure that your garage door is secure and looks great.

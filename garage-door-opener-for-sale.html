What to Consider Before Buying a Garage Door Opener

Before you jump into buying a garage door opener, there are a few things you should consider. First and foremost, you will want to think about the size of your garage door. Different openers can handle different sizes of doors, so you will want to make sure you buy one that is compatible with yours. Additionally, you should consider how much noise you want the opener to make when it is operating. Some are much quieter than others. You should also think about the type of opener you would like. Do you want a chain drive, belt drive, or screw drive opener? Finally, you should think about the features you want your opener to have. Some models come with built-in Wi-Fi, smartphone compatibility, and even voice activation.

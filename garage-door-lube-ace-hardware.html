The Benefits Of Lubricating Your Garage Door

It’s easy to forget about taking care of your garage door, but it’s an important part of your home. Proper maintenance of your garage door will help you avoid expensive repairs in the future. One of the most important maintenance tasks is to lubricate your door and its components. Lubricating your garage door will keep it working smoothly and quietly, improve its longevity, and help ensure that it’s safe to use. In this article, we’ll discuss the benefits of lubricating your door, the best lubricants to use, and how to properly lubricate your garage door.

The Benefits of Lubrication

Lubrication is essential for keeping your garage door functioning properly. It reduces friction and wear on the door’s moving parts, which helps keep the door operating quietly and smoothly. It also helps to reduce the amount of force needed to move the door, which means that it won’t require as much energy to open and close. Additionally, lubrication can help reduce the amount of wear on the door’s components, which can help extend the life of your door.

The Best Lubricants to Use

When it comes to lubricating your garage door, it’s important to use the right type of lubricant. You’ll want to avoid using oil-based lubricants, as they can attract dust and dirt, which can eventually cause the door to malfunction. The best lubricants to use on your door are those specifically designed for garage doors and hardware. Ace Hardware offers a number of different garage door lubricants, including Garage Door Lube. This lubricant is designed to reduce friction, reduce wear, and help keep your door running smoothly and quietly.

How to Lubricate Your Garage Door

Lubricating your garage door is a fairly simple process. First, you’ll need to open the door and inspect the different components. Look for any signs of wear or damage, such as rust or cracked components. If you find any, you should replace them before continuing with the lubrication process. Next, spray the lubricant on the components, making sure to cover all of the moving parts. Allow the lubricant to sit for a few minutes, then wipe off any excess. Finally, close the door and test it to make sure it’s operating smoothly and quietly.

Conclusion

Lubricating your garage door is an important part of its maintenance. It helps reduce friction and wear on the door’s components, which can help keep it running smoothly and quietly. It can also reduce the amount of force needed to open and close the door, and help extend the life of your door. When it comes to lubricants, you’ll want to use those specifically designed for garage doors, such as Garage Door Lube from Ace Hardware. And remember, lubricating your door is a simple process, so don’t forget to do it regularly.

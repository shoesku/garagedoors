As homeowners, we all want to ensure our safety and security. That’s why it’s so important to keep our garage door electric eye alignment in tip-top shape. But how do you know if your garage door sensors are properly aligned? Here’s what you need to know about garage door electric eye alignment and how to properly align your sensors.

What are Garage Door Sensors?

Garage door sensors are an important safety feature that helps prevent accidents and injuries. The sensors are small boxes mounted on either side of the garage door’s tracks. They detect when something is blocking the door’s path and will automatically reverse the motion of the door. This helps to prevent accidents from occurring when someone or something is in the garage door’s way.

What is Garage Door Electric Eye Alignment?

Garage door electric eye alignment is the process of ensuring the sensors are properly aligned. The sensors must be properly aligned in order for them to detect any obstructions in the path of the door. If the sensors are not properly aligned, the door will not detect any obstructions and could remain open when something is blocking its path.

How to Properly Align Your Garage Door Sensors

The best way to properly align your garage door sensors is to use a laser level. A laser level is a tool that projects a line of light onto a surface. With a laser level, you can easily adjust the sensors so that the line of light passes through both sensors. This will ensure that the sensors are properly aligned and that the door will detect any obstructions in its path.

Tips For Aligning Your Garage Door Sensors

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when aligning your garage door sensors:

  • Make sure the sensors are securely mounted and that they’re at the same height. If the sensors are not at the same height, it can affect the accuracy of the sensors.
  • Make sure the sensors are not blocked by anything. This will ensure that the sensors detect any obstructions in the path of the door.
  • Make sure to check the alignment of the sensors regularly. This will help ensure that the sensors are always properly aligned.

Conclusion

Garage door electric eye alignment is an important part of keeping your home safe and secure. Properly aligned sensors will help ensure that the door detects any obstructions in its path and will automatically reverse the motion of the door. With the help of a laser level, you can easily align your sensors and keep your home safe from potential accidents. Just remember to check the alignment of the sensors regularly to ensure optimal safety and performance.

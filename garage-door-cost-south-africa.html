How Much Does A New Garage Door Cost In South Africa?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Are you looking to replace your existing garage door with a new one? Or perhaps you’re building a new home and want to install a garage door? Either way, it’s essential to consider the cost of the project. In this article, we’ll discuss the cost of garage door installation in South Africa and provide helpful tips to keep the costs down. Let’s get started.

Average Cost of Garage Door Installation in South Africa

The average cost of a garage door installation in South Africa is between R5,000 and R20,000. This cost can vary significantly depending on the size, material, and style of the door. For example, a single-panel steel door costs between R3,000 and R5,000. On the other hand, a custom-made wooden door with intricately carved designs can cost up to R20,000.

In addition to the cost of the door itself, you also need to factor in the cost of labor. Professional installation of a garage door typically costs around R2,000. If the door requires additional reinforcement, the labor costs could range from R2,500 to R5,000.

Factors That Affect the Cost of Garage Door Installation

The cost of a garage door installation depends on several factors. Here are some of the most important ones:

Size

The size of the door is one of the most important factors that affects the cost. As a general rule, the bigger the door, the more expensive it will be. This is because larger doors require more material and a more complex installation process.

Type of Material

The type of material used for the door is also a major factor in the cost. Steel and aluminum are the most affordable options, while wood and fiberglass are more expensive. The type of material you choose will also affect the installation process and labor costs.

Style

The style of the door also plays a role in the cost of installation. A simple single panel door is typically the most affordable option, while a custom-made door with intricate designs and carvings can be quite expensive.

Installation Type

The installation process is another factor that affects the cost. Standard installation is typically the most affordable option, while a fully customized installation with reinforced support may be more expensive.

Tips to Keep the Cost Down

If you’re looking to save money on garage door installation, here are some tips to keep the costs down:

Opt for a Standard Size Door

A standard size door is typically the most affordable option. If you can, opt for a single-panel steel door instead of a custom-made door. This will save you money on material and labor costs.

Do-it-Yourself Installation

If you’re handy with tools, you might consider doing the installation yourself. Many hardware stores sell garage door kits that include all the necessary parts and instructions. However, it’s important to note that this can be a challenging project, so make sure you’re up to the task before attempting it.

Buy a Used Door

There are many used garage door options available on the market. Buying a used door can save you money on the up-front cost and may even come with a warranty. Just make sure to thoroughly inspect the door before purchase to make sure it’s in good condition.

Conclusion

Installing a new garage door can be a costly endeavor, but there are ways to reduce the cost. By considering the factors mentioned above and following the tips provided, you can save money on your garage door installation in South Africa. Good luck!

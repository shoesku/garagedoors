Tips On How To Install A Garage Door Top Seal From Youtube

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Garages can be a great asset to any home. Not only can they provide extra storage space or an area to work on car repairs, but they also help keep your car safe from the elements. However, if you don’t have a garage door top seal installed, your garage may be vulnerable to the elements. After all, the top of your garage door is the most exposed part to the outside weather, so you need to make sure that it’s sealed properly. Thankfully, installing a top seal is a simple process that you can do yourself with a few tools and some help from YouTube.

Tools You Need

Before you can get started, you’ll need to gather a few tools. The most important tool is a measuring tape. You’ll need this to measure the opening of your garage door so that you can order the right size top seal. You’ll also need a utility knife, a screwdriver, and a few screws. You’ll also need to get some weatherstripping, which should come with the top seal. Finally, you’ll need some clear silicone caulk, which is used to secure the top seal to the wall of your garage.

Finding the Right Video

The first step in installing a top seal is finding the right video. There are many videos available on YouTube, so it’s important to find one that is tailored to your specific garage door and installation. You should look for a video that is detailed, clear, and shows you how to install the seal in a step-by-step fashion. It’s also a good idea to look for a video that uses the same type of seal that you’re using, as different types of seals may require slightly different installation techniques.

Preparing the Area

Before you can start installing the top seal, you’ll need to prepare the area. Start by cleaning the area around the garage door. This will help ensure that the seal adheres properly and won’t have any issues. You should also make sure that the area is dry before you start installing the seal. Once the area is clean and dry, you’re ready to start installing the seal.

Installing the Seal

Installing the seal is actually quite simple. Start by measuring the opening of your garage door and cutting the seal to the right size. Then, use the screws and the screwdriver to secure the seal to the door. Finally, use the silicone caulk to seal the edges of the seal. Make sure to cover the entire seal to ensure a tight seal. Once the seal is in place, you can use the weatherstripping to finish off the installation.

Checking for Leaks

Once the seal is in place, you should check for any leaks. To do this, close the garage door and turn off the lights. Then, shine a flashlight from inside the garage to the outside of the door. If you see any light coming through, that means that there is a leak and you’ll need to check the seal. If the seal is properly installed, there should be no light coming through.

Maintenance Tips

Finally, it’s important to remember that the seal will need to be maintained. Over time, the seal can become brittle and cracked, which can lead to leaks. To prevent this, you should inspect the seal regularly and replace it when necessary. You should also apply a fresh coat of silicone caulk every few years to ensure that the seal remains secure.

Conclusion

Installing a garage door top seal is a simple process that you can do yourself with the help of YouTube. All you need is a few tools and some guidance from a video. Once the seal is installed, you should check for any leaks and maintain the seal regularly to ensure a tight seal. With a little bit of effort, you can make sure that your garage stays safe and secure from the elements.

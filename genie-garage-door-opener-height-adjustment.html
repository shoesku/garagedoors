How To Properly Adjust The Height Of A Genie Garage Door Opener

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Most homeowners don’t think about their garage door opener, until it begins to act up. One common issue is when the garage door opener won’t open all the way due to the height adjustment. This is not only a nuisance, but it can be a safety hazard. Fortunately, adjusting the height of your Genie garage door opener is a relatively easy task that can be completed in just a few minutes.

Tools Needed for Adjusting the Height

Before you begin, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Flathead screwdriver
  • Phillips head screwdriver
  • Ruler or measuring tape

Steps for Adjusting the Height

Once you have the necessary tools, you can begin adjusting the height of your Genie garage door opener. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Measure the Height of Your Garage Door

The first step is to measure the height of your garage door. You can do this either with a ruler or a measuring tape. Make sure you measure from the ground up to the top of the door. Write down this measurement for reference.

Step 2: Locate the Adjustment Screws

Next, you’ll need to locate the adjustment screws. These screws can usually be found at the back of the garage door opener. In some cases, they may be located on the side of the garage door opener.

Step 3: Adjust the Screws

Once you’ve located the adjustment screws, you’ll need to adjust them using a flathead or Phillips head screwdriver. You want to turn the screws in small increments until the door opener is at the right height. Use the measurement you took in step one as a reference.

Step 4: Test the Door

After you’ve adjusted the screws, test the door to make sure it’s opening properly. If the door isn’t opening all the way, you may need to adjust the screws a bit more.

Additional Tips for Adjusting the Height

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when adjusting the height of your Genie garage door opener:

  • Always use caution when working with the garage door opener.
  • Make sure the door is not obstructed in any way while you’re adjusting the screws.
  • If the door isn’t opening all the way, you may need to adjust the tension springs. It’s best to call a professional for this.

Conclusion

Adjusting the height of your Genie garage door opener is a relatively simple process that can be completed in just a few minutes. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps outlined above. With a little bit of patience, you should have no problem getting your garage door opener back to working order.

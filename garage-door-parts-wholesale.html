What Are Garage Door Parts?

Garage door parts are components that help make the garage door system work properly. These parts can be divided into two main categories: mechanical and electrical. Mechanical parts are used to move the door up and down, while electrical parts help control the opening and closing of the door. Examples of mechanical parts include springs, cables, track systems, rollers, and pulleys. Examples of electrical parts include remote control systems, motors, and circuit boards.

Why Buy Garage Door Parts Wholesale?

Wholesalers typically have a large selection of parts available, which can save time by eliminating the need to shop around for the right pieces.

What To Do When Your Garage Door Remote Stops Working

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

If you’ve ever been trying to open your garage door and found that your garage door remote isn’t working, you know how frustrating that can be. Not only does it mean you’ll have to get out of the car and manually open the garage door, but it can also mean that there’s something wrong with your garage door. Fortunately, there are some simple things you can do to try and fix the issue before you need to call a repairman.

Check the Remote

The first thing you should do when your garage door remote stops working is to check the remote itself. Make sure that the batteries are in good condition and that they are the correct type of battery. If the batteries are dead, replace them and see if that solves the problem. If it doesn’t, then you should look at the other possible causes.

Check the Garage Door Motor

The motor of your garage door is what powers the door up and down. If the motor is malfunctioning, then it can prevent the door from opening or closing. Check the motor to make sure it is getting power and that the wiring is in good condition. It’s also a good idea to inspect the motor to make sure it is not damaged or worn out.

Check the Sensors

The sensors on your garage door are what tell the motor when to open and close the door. If the sensors are blocked or malfunctioning, then the door will not open or close. Make sure the sensors are not blocked and that they are aligned correctly. If the sensors are not working, then you will need to replace them.

Check the Antenna

If your garage door has an antenna, make sure it is in good condition and properly connected to the motor. The antenna is what receives the signal from your remote, so if it is not working properly then the remote will not be able to open or close the door. Check the antenna to make sure it is properly connected and in good condition.

Check the Wall Switch

If your garage door has a wall switch, make sure it is in good condition and working properly. The wall switch is what tells the motor when to open or close the door. If the wall switch is not working, then you will need to replace it.

Check the Cables

The cables on your garage door are what connect the door to the motor. If the cables are worn or frayed, then they can prevent the door from opening or closing. Check the cables to make sure they are in good condition and properly connected to the motor.

Check for Loose Connections

If you have checked all the above items and still find that your garage door remote is not working, then you should check for any loose connections. Make sure all the wires and cables are securely connected and that none of them have been disconnected or broken. If you find a loose connection, then you will need to replace it.

Call a Professional

If you have checked all the above items and still find that your garage door remote is not working, then it’s time to call a professional. A professional will be able to inspect the door and motor to determine what is causing the issue and then repair it. They will also be able to recommend any replacement parts that may be needed to get your garage door working properly again.

Conclusion

If your garage door remote stops working, there are a few simple things you can do to try and get it working again. Check the remote, the motor, the sensors, the antenna, the wall switch, and the cables to make sure they are in good condition and properly connected. If none of these things fix the issue, then it’s time to call a professional to inspect and repair your garage door.

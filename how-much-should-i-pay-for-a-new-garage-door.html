How Much Should I Pay For A New Garage Door?

When it comes to making home improvements, a new garage door is a great investment. Not only does it add curb appeal to your home, but it also adds to its overall value. But with so many different types of garage doors on the market, it can be hard to know which one is the right fit for your home and budget. In this article, we’ll go over what you should consider when shopping for a new garage door, as well as how much you should expect to pay.

Types of Garage Doors

There are a wide variety of garage doors on the market, each with its own set of advantages and disadvantages. The most popular types of garage doors are:

  • Roller Doors: Roller doors are the most popular type of garage door, as they offer convenience and security. They’re also relatively inexpensive, with prices ranging from $200 to $800.
  • Sectional Doors: Sectional garage doors are the most common type of door. They’re easy to operate and maintain, and provide good insulation. Prices for these doors range from $500 to $1500.
  • Sliding Doors: Sliding garage doors are becoming increasingly popular due to their modern design. Prices for these doors range from $1000 to $3000.

Aside from these types of doors, there are also custom doors, which can be made to fit any size or style of garage. Custom doors are typically more expensive, with prices ranging from $2000 to $8000.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a Garage Door

Once you’ve decided on the type of door you’d like to install, there are a few other factors to consider. These include:

  • Size: The size of your door will have a big impact on the overall cost. The larger the door, the more expensive it will be.
  • Material: Garage doors are typically made from wood, aluminum, or steel. Steel is the most durable and secure option, but also the most expensive. Wood is the most affordable option, but requires more maintenance.
  • Insulation: Insulated garage doors are more expensive, but they offer a number of benefits, such as increased energy efficiency and noise reduction.
  • Style: Garage doors come in a wide variety of styles, such as traditional, modern, and carriage house. Choose a style that complements your home’s exterior.

Taking all of these factors into consideration will help you find the right garage door for your home and budget.

How Much Does a New Garage Door Cost?

The cost of a new garage door depends on the size, style, and material. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $200 to $8000 for a new garage door. Installing the door is an additional cost, which can range from $150 to $500.

How Can I Save Money on a New Garage Door?

There are a few ways to save money when buying a new garage door. First, you can shop around to find the best prices. Compare prices at different home improvement stores, as well as online retailers. You should also consider buying a used door, since they can be significantly cheaper than new ones. Finally, you can DIY the installation, which can save you a significant amount of money.

Conclusion

When shopping for a new garage door, it’s important to consider the size, material, insulation, and style. Prices for garage doors can range from $200 to $8000, depending on these factors. Shopping around, buying used, and DIYing the installation can all help you save money on a new garage door.

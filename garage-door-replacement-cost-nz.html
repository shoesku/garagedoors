If you’re looking for a new garage door for your home or business, you’re likely wondering how much it will cost you. Garage door replacement cost in NZ varies depending on the type of door you’re looking for and the complexity of the installation. Here we’ll discuss the different types of garage doors available, the average cost of installation, and some tips to help you get the most from your investment.

Types of Garage Doors in NZ

The most popular types of garage doors for residential and commercial applications in New Zealand are sectional and roller doors. Sectional doors are made up of separate panels that hinge together to form the door. These doors are available in a range of materials and finishes, including steel, aluminium, and wood. Roller doors are made of a single panel of corrugated steel or aluminium that rolls up and down on a set of tracks. The advantage of roller doors is that they require less space to operate, making them ideal for smaller garages.

Average Cost of Garage Door Replacement in NZ

The average cost of replacing a garage door in New Zealand is between $1,000 and $4,000, depending on the type of door, the size of the door, and the complexity of the installation. Generally, a basic steel sectional garage door will cost less than a more complex roller door installation. It’s also important to factor in the cost of labour for the installation. The average cost of labour for a garage door replacement in NZ is around $250 to $400.

Tips for Reducing Garage Door Replacement Cost

If you’re looking to get the most out of your garage door replacement, there are a few things you can do to reduce the cost. Firstly, consider buying a door that is already pre-made rather than having one made to order. This will save you money on labour and materials. Secondly, shop around for the best deals. There are a number of online stores and retailers in New Zealand that offer discounts on garage door replacements. Finally, consider hiring a professional contractor to do the work. This will ensure that the job is done properly and will save you money in the long run.

Conclusion

Garage door replacement cost in NZ can vary significantly depending on the type of door you’re looking for, the size of the door, and the complexity of the installation. Generally, a basic steel sectional garage door will cost less than a roller door installation. It’s important to factor in the cost of labour for the installation as well. There are a few things you can do to reduce the cost of your garage door replacement, such as shopping around for the best deals, buying a pre-made door, and hiring a professional contractor. By following these tips, you can ensure that you get the most out of your investment.

Garage door springs come in two main types, extension springs and torsion springs. Extension springs are the most common type of garage door springs and they are used to lift the door up and down. They are usually made of steel and are often referred to as “side-mounted” springs. The other type of garage door spring is a torsion spring, which is used to rotate the door open and closed. Torsion springs are usually mounted at the top of the door and are made of steel wire.

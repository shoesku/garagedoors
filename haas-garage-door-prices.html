What is Haas

Haas Door is a manufacturer of high-quality garage doors in the United States and Canada. They have been in business since 1954 and have earned a reputation for producing superior, durable doors in many different styles. They offer a wide selection of styles and materials, ranging from traditional wood doors to modern steel, aluminum, and vinyl doors. They also have a variety of accessories, such as springs, openers, and weatherstripping, to ensure your door is both safe and efficient.

Haas Garage Door Prices

The cost of a Haas garage door will depend on the size and material of the door, as well as the accessories you choose. Prices range from a few hundred dollars for a basic steel door to several thousand dollars for a luxury wood door. The average cost of a Haas garage door is around $1,500. This price range covers both standard single and double doors.

Steel Doors

Steel doors are the most affordable option on the market. They are strong and durable, and you can choose from a wide range of colors and finishes. Basic steel doors start at around $400 and can go up to $1,000 for a high-end model. Keep in mind that you will need to factor in the cost of installation, which can range from $200 to $500 depending on the complexity of the job.

Fiberglass Doors

Fiberglass doors are becoming increasingly popular due to their low cost and attractive appearance. Prices range from $800 to $1,500 for a standard single door. You can also choose from a wide range of colors and finishes, making them a great choice for those who want their garage door to stand out. Keep in mind that installation costs for these doors are usually higher than for steel doors.

Wood Doors

Wood doors offer a classic, timeless look that is sure to impress. Prices range from $1,500 to $3,500 for a standard single door. You can also choose from a range of hardwoods, such as oak, mahogany, and cherry. Keep in mind that wood doors require more maintenance than steel or fiberglass doors, and installation costs are generally higher as well.

Aluminum Doors

Aluminum doors are becoming increasingly popular due to their low cost and attractive appearance. Prices range from $800 to $1,500 for a standard single door. They are also lightweight and easy to install, and you can choose from a variety of colors and finishes. Keep in mind that aluminum doors tend to be less durable than steel or fiberglass doors, so you may need to replace them sooner.

Vinyl Doors

Vinyl doors are the most affordable option on the market. Prices range from $400 to $1,000 for a standard single door. They are also lightweight and easy to install, and you can choose from a variety of colors and finishes. Keep in mind that vinyl doors are not as durable as steel or fiberglass doors, and they tend to yellow over time.

Where to Buy

Haas garage doors can be purchased directly from the manufacturer or from a local dealer. You can also find them online at various retailers. To ensure you get the best price, be sure to compare prices and read customer reviews before making a purchase.

Conclusion

Haas garage doors are a great choice for those who want a durable and attractive door at an affordable price. Prices range from a few hundred dollars for a basic steel door to several thousand dollars for a luxury wood door. Be sure to compare prices and read customer reviews before making a purchase to ensure you get the best deal.

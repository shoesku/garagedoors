Types of Garage Door Parts

When it comes to garage door parts, there are a variety of different parts that you’ll need to consider. The most common garage door parts include springs, hinges, rollers, struts, cables, tracks, and openers. Each of these parts plays an important role in ensuring that your garage door operates properly.

Garage Door Springs

Garage door springs are one of the most important pieces of equipment when it comes to your garage door. They are responsible for supporting the weight of the door and helping the door open and close smoothly. If your springs are worn out or broken, it can lead to a malfunctioning door. It’s important to make sure that you replace your springs regularly to avoid any problems.

Garage Door Hinges

Garage door hinges are responsible for connecting the two sides of your garage door. They help ensure that the door opens and closes properly and that the door is secure. Without the hinges, your garage door could come off its tracks or not open or close properly. It’s important to make sure that your hinges are in good condition and that you replace them as needed.

Garage Door Rollers

Garage door rollers are responsible for helping the door open and close smoothly. They are located at the bottom of the door and help guide the door along its tracks. If you have a noisy garage door or if it’s not opening or closing properly, it could be because the rollers are worn out or broken. You’ll want to make sure that you replace your rollers regularly to avoid any problems.

Garage Door Struts

Garage door struts are responsible for helping to support the weight of the door. They are placed on the sides of the door and help to keep it in place. If your struts are worn out or broken, it can lead to a malfunctioning garage door. It’s important to make sure that you replace your struts regularly to avoid any problems.

Garage Door Cables

Garage door cables are responsible for helping the door open and close smoothly. They are located at the top of the door and help guide the door along its tracks. If you have a noisy garage door or if it’s not opening or closing properly, it could be because the cables are worn out or broken. You’ll want to make sure that you replace your cables regularly to avoid any problems.

Garage Door Tracks

Garage door tracks are responsible for helping the door open and close smoothly. They are located along the sides of the door and help guide the door along its tracks. If you have a noisy garage door or if it’s not opening or closing properly, it could be because the tracks are worn out or broken. You’ll want to make sure that you replace your tracks regularly to avoid any problems.

Garage Door Openers

Garage door openers are responsible for helping the door open and close smoothly. They are located on the sides of the door and help guide the door along its tracks. If you have an old or broken opener, it can lead to a malfunctioning garage door. It’s important to make sure that you replace your opener regularly to avoid any problems.

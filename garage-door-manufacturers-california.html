Types of Garage Doors

The first step in understanding which manufacturer is best for your project is to understand the different types of garage doors that are available. The most common types of doors are sectional, roll-up, and sliding. Sectional garage doors are the most popular type of door and are made up of several sections that are connected together. Roll-up doors are similar to sectional doors but are made up of one large piece of material that is rolled up when not in use. Sliding garage doors are the least common type of door and use two or more sliding panels that can be opened and closed with the use of a remote control.

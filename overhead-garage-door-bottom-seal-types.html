When it comes to overhead garage door bottom seals, there are a few different kinds to choose from. The right one for you will depend on the kind of door you have and your budget. In this article, we will discuss the different types of seals available, the pros and cons of each, and the best option for your door.

Vinyl Seal

Vinyl is a good option for a garage door seal. It is relatively inexpensive and easy to install. It can be used for both residential and commercial applications. The vinyl seal is designed to keep out dirt, dust, and moisture. It is also designed to help keep out any pests that may try to enter the garage. The downside to vinyl is that it can be easily damaged and can be difficult to repair. Also, it is not as durable as some of the other seal types.

Foam Seal

Foam is another good option for a garage door seal. It is easy to install, relatively inexpensive, and can be used for both residential and commercial applications. Which Type of Seal Is Right for You?

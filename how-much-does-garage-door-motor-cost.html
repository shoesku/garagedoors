How Much Does A Garage Door Motor Cost?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Having a functioning garage door motor is essential for anyone who needs to open and close their garage door. A garage door motor is responsible for ensuring that your garage door opens and closes properly and with minimal effort from the user. But just like any other household appliance, these motors can be expensive. So, how much does a garage door motor cost?

The cost of a garage door motor will depend on a few factors, such as the size and type of motor, the brand, and any additional features. Generally, the cost of a basic garage door motor can range from $200 to $400. This cost includes the installation, which can be done by a professional for an additional fee. However, if you choose to install the motor yourself, you may be able to save a few dollars.

Types of Garage Door Motors

There are several different types of garage door motors available, each with its own pros and cons. The most common types of garage door motors are:

  • Chain-driven motors: These are the most affordable type of garage door motor and are the most popular choice for homeowners. They are relatively quiet and easy to install.
  • Belt-driven motors: These motors are more expensive than chain-driven motors, but they are also quieter and smoother. They are a great choice for those who want a quiet, smooth operation.
  • Direct-drive motors: These motors are the most expensive type of garage door motor, but they are also the most reliable and the longest-lasting. They are also the quietest and offer the most features.

Features of Garage Door Motors

Garage door motors can come with a variety of features, such as remote access, keypad entry, and more. If you’re looking for a motor with more features, you may have to pay more for it. However, some features may be worth the extra cost, as they can make your life easier and more convenient.

For example, a motor with remote access allows you to open and close your garage door without having to get out of your car. This can be a great convenience, especially in bad weather or if you’re in a hurry. Some motors also include keypad entry, which makes it easy to give access to visitors or family members without having to give out a physical key.

The Cost of Professional Installation

If you don’t feel comfortable installing the motor yourself, it’s best to hire a professional to do the job for you. Professional installation can cost anywhere from $150 to $300, depending on the complexity of the job. This cost may also include the cost of the motor, so be sure to ask the installer for an itemized list of parts and labor.

Where to Buy a Garage Door Motor

When it comes to buying a garage door motor, you have several options. You can buy a motor from a home improvement store or online, or you can hire a professional to install a motor for you. If you choose to purchase a motor online, be sure to read customer reviews to ensure that you’re getting a quality product.

Conclusion

Garage door motors can be expensive, but they are essential for keeping your garage door functioning properly. The cost of a garage door motor can range from $200 to $400, depending on the size and type of motor and any additional features. If you hire a professional to install the motor, you may have to pay an additional fee. Be sure to shop around to find the best deal on a garage door motor.

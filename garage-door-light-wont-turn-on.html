Garage Door Light Won't Turn On - Troubleshooting Tips

Introduction

Dealing with a garage door light that won't turn on can be annoying and inconvenient. Not only does it make it difficult to see inside the garage, it can also be a safety hazard if you are not able to clearly see people and objects around the door. Fortunately, if your garage door light won't turn on, it can usually be fixed easily. Here are some troubleshooting tips to help you get your garage door light working again.

Check the Light Bulb

The first step when troubleshooting a garage door light that won't turn on is to check the light bulb. Make sure the bulb is properly screwed in and that it is the correct type and wattage for your fixture. If the bulb is burned out or not screwed in properly, it won't turn on. If the bulb looks fine, then it's time to move on to the next step.

Check the Fixture

If the bulb is not the problem, then it's time to check the fixture. Make sure the fixture is securely attached to the wall and not loose. If the fixture is loose, it may be preventing the light from turning on. Also, check the wiring of the fixture to make sure it is properly connected. If the wiring is not tight or secure, it may need to be tightened or replaced.

Check the Circuit Breaker

If the light bulb and fixture are both in good working order, the next step is to check the circuit breaker. If the breaker has been tripped, it will prevent the light from turning on. To reset the breaker, simply turn it off and then back on again. If the breaker trips again, then it's time to call an electrician for further troubleshooting.

Check the Wall Switch

The last step in troubleshooting a garage door light that won't turn on is to check the wall switch. Make sure the switch is in the "on" position and that it is securely attached to the wall. If the switch is loose or not in the proper position, then the light won't turn on. If the switch looks fine, then it's time to call an electrician for further troubleshooting.

Conclusion

Dealing with a garage door light that won't turn on can be annoying and inconvenient. Fortunately, if your garage door light won't turn on, it can usually be fixed easily. Start by checking the light bulb, fixture, circuit breaker, and wall switch. If all of these components look fine, then it's time to call an electrician for further troubleshooting. With these troubleshooting tips, you can get your garage door light working again in no time.

What is a Garage Door Spring?

Garage door springs are extremely important components of your garage door system. They are responsible for helping open and close the heavy garage door. These springs can be either torsion or extension springs. Torsion springs are mounted on a metal tube above the garage door opening, while extension springs are located on either side of the garage door opening. The springs are responsible for providing the tension required for the door to open and close. Unfortunately, garage door springs can break over time due to wear and tear.

The Average Cost of Fixing a Garage Door Spring

The average cost of fixing a garage door spring in 2023 will depend on the type of spring, the size of the spring, and the complexity of the repair. For torsion springs, the cost of repair can range from $100 to $500, depending on the size and complexity. Extension springs can range from $50 to $200. Additionally, if you have an automatic garage door opener, you may need to replace the opener if it has been damaged. The average cost of replacing an opener is between $200 and $300.

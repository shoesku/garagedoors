How To Fix A Garage Door That Keeps Opening When Trying To Close It

If your garage door keeps opening when you try to close it, it can be a huge inconvenience. It can be incredibly annoying and even dangerous if you're not careful. Fortunately, there are a few simple fixes that can help you get your garage door working correctly again. In this article, we'll take a look at what could be causing your garage door to keep opening and how to fix it.

What Causes a Garage Door to Keep Opening When Trying to Close It?

There are a few factors that could be causing your garage door to keep opening when you try to close it. The most common cause is a malfunctioning opener. The opener is the part of your garage door system that allows you to open and close the door with the push of a button. If the opener isn't working properly, it could cause the door to open and close erratically, which could cause it to open when you try to close it.

Another common cause of a garage door opening when it shouldn't is an issue with the safety sensors. The safety sensors are designed to detect objects in the path of the garage door and will automatically stop the door from closing if something is in the way. If the sensors are not working properly, they may not detect an object and the door could keep opening when it shouldn't.

Finally, an issue with the springs or cables on your garage door could be causing your door to keep opening when you try to close it. If the springs or cables are starting to wear out, they won't be able to properly lift the weight of the door, which could cause the door to open when you try to close it.

How to Fix a Garage Door That Keeps Opening When Trying to Close It

Once you've determined the cause of your garage door opening when it shouldn't, it's time to take steps to fix the problem. If it's an issue with the opener, you can try replacing the opener's motor or checking the wiring to make sure it's connected properly. If the issue is with the sensors, you can check to make sure they're properly aligned and free of obstructions. And if the issue is with the springs or cables, you'll need to either replace them or have them repaired by a professional.

If you're not comfortable trying to fix the problem yourself, you can always hire a professional garage door technician to diagnose and repair the issue. A professional technician will be able to accurately diagnose the issue and make any necessary repairs, so you can be sure that your garage door will be working properly again in no time.

Conclusion

If your garage door keeps opening when you try to close it, it can be a huge inconvenience. Fortunately, there are a few simple steps you can take to try to fix the problem. You can try replacing the opener's motor, checking the wiring, or checking the safety sensors to make sure they're properly aligned and free of obstructions. If the issue is with the springs or cables, you'll need to either replace them or have them repaired by a professional. With a little bit of effort, you should be able to get your garage door working correctly again in no time.

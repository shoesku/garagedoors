How To Install A Garage Door Seal On Concrete

What You'll Need

Installing a garage door seal on concrete requires only a few tools. You’ll need a measuring tape, a drill, a drill bit, a hammer, and a set of screws. Additionally, you’ll need a garage door seal specifically designed for concrete, which can be purchased at most home improvement stores.

Step 1: Measure the Door

Using your measuring tape, measure the width and height of the door and record your measurements. You'll need to know these measurements when purchasing the seal.

Step 2: Purchase the Seal

With your measurements in hand, visit a home improvement store and purchase the seal. Make sure to buy the correct size for your door. If you’re not sure which size to purchase, the store personnel can help you.

Step 3: Install the Seal

Unroll the seal and place it along the bottom of the door. Make sure the seal is snug against the door and that the ends meet perfectly. If they don’t, trim the seal with scissors.

Step 4: Mark the Holes

Using a pen or pencil, mark the holes on the seal where you'll need to drill. Make sure to mark the holes on the concrete, not the seal. If you make a mistake, you can replace the seal.

Step 5: Drill the Holes

Using your drill and drill bit, drill holes into the concrete where you marked the holes. Make sure the holes are deep enough for the screws to fit securely.

Step 6: Secure the Seal

Using your hammer and screws, secure the seal to the concrete. Make sure the screws are tight, but don’t over-tighten them or you may strip the screw.

Step 7: Test the Seal

Once the seal is secured, open and close the door several times to ensure the seal is secure and functioning properly. If the seal is loose, tighten the screws.

Step 8: Clean Up

When you’re finished, vacuum up any debris from the drilling and put away your tools.

Conclusion

Installing a garage door seal on concrete is a simple job that can be accomplished in just a few minutes. With the right tools and supplies, you can have your seal installed and functioning properly in no time.

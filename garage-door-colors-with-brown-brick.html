Best Garage Door Colors for Brown Brick Homes

When matching garage door colors to brown brick, you want to look for colors that create a contrast, but still blend in naturally. Darker colors tend to work best with brown brick, as they will provide enough contrast to be visible, but not so much that they look out of place. Some of the best colors for brown brick include navy blue, dark green, and dark grey.

Light colors such as yellow, tan, and even white can also work, but you need to be careful to avoid creating a clash. White, for example, can look great against light brown brick, but can look out of place against darker brown bricks. When in doubt, darker colors tend to be a safer bet.

Other Considerations When Matching Garage Door Colors to Brown Brick

When choosing a garage door color for your home, there are a few other factors you should consider. For example, the color of your roof can have an effect on the overall look. If you have a dark roof, you may want to choose a lighter color for your garage door to create a nice contrast. If you have a light roof, you may want to choose a darker color to help the garage door stand out.

You should also consider the color of your home's trim and shutters. If your home has white trim and shutters, a darker color may look odd. In this case, a lighter color may be a better choice. If your home has dark trim, you may want to choose a darker color for your garage door to create a more cohesive look.

Choosing the Right Color for Your Home

Choosing the right color for your garage door can take some time and thought. You should consider the color of your brick, the color of your roof, and the color of your home's trim and shutters. If you take all of these into consideration, you should be able to find the perfect color for your home.

Types of Garage Door Openers

There are many different types of garage door openers on the market. The most popular type is the chain drive opener, which uses a motor to move a chain that lifts and lowers the door. Chain drive openers are generally the least expensive, but they can be noisy and require more maintenance than other types. Another type of opener is the belt drive opener, which uses a rubber belt to operate the door. These openers are quieter than chain drive openers and require less maintenance, but they tend to be more expensive. Finally, there are screw drive openers, which use a threaded rod to raise and lower the door. They are the quietest and easiest to maintain, but they are also the most expensive.

Features to Look for in a Garage Door Opener

When shopping for a garage door opener, there are a few features you should look for. First, look for an opener that is compatible with your existing garage door. Most openers come with a compatibility chart, so you can easily find out if an opener will work with your door. Additionally, look for an opener that is compatible with your smartphone, so you can control your garage door from anywhere. Other features to consider include the number of remotes an opener comes with, the amount of horsepower it offers, and the warranty length.

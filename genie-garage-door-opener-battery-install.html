How To Install The Battery In A Genie Garage Door Opener

When your Genie Garage Door Opener isn't opening or closing your garage door, it might be time to check the battery. Installing a new battery can be a simple task for the do-it-yourself homeowner. Before you start, make sure you have the right battery for your Genie opener.

Steps to Replace the Battery in Genie Garage Door Opener

1. Locate the battery compartment on the back of the Genie opener. It should be labeled “Battery.”

2. Unscrew the screws that hold the battery compartment in place and remove it.

3. Take out the old battery and check it for any damage. If it is cracked or broken, it needs to be replaced.

4. Put the new battery in the battery compartment and make sure it is securely in place.

5. Replace the battery compartment and screws.

6. Press the “learn” button on the opener and follow the instructions in the owner’s manual to program the new battery.

Things to Consider When Replacing the Battery in Your Genie Garage Door Opener

1. Make sure you use the right type of battery for your Genie opener.

2. Replace the battery as soon as you notice any signs of wear and tear.

3. Make sure the battery is securely in place before replacing the battery compartment.

4. Follow the instructions in the owner’s manual when programming the new battery.

Tips to Extend the Life of Your Genie Garage Door Opener Battery

1. Make sure the garage door is properly balanced and the springs are adjusted correctly.

2. Keep the battery clean and free of dirt and debris.

3. Check the opener for any loose or worn out parts and replace them as necessary.

4. If the opener is not used often, disconnect the battery to avoid unnecessary drain.

Conclusion

Replacing the battery in a Genie Garage Door Opener is a simple task. Just make sure you have the right battery for your opener and follow the instructions in the owner’s manual to program the new battery. With proper maintenance and care, your new battery should last for several years.

Types of Garage Door Trim

Before you can decide which type of trim is best for your home, you need to be aware of the different types of trim available. Generally, there are three main types of trim available: vinyl, aluminum, and wood. Each of these trim types has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it’s important to consider the pros and cons of each to determine which type is the best for you.

Vinyl Trim

Vinyl trim is one of the most popular options for garage door trim near me. It is easy to install, durable, and cost-effective. It also comes in a wide range of colors and styles, allowing you to customize your trim to match the existing exterior of your home. However, it is not as durable as aluminum or wood, and it can be prone to scratching and fading over time.

Aluminum Trim

Aluminum trim is an excellent choice for garage door trim near me. It is lightweight, durable, and resistant to corrosion and fading. It is also easy to install and can be customized to match the look of your home. The main disadvantage of aluminum trim is that it is more expensive than vinyl or wood trim.

Wood Trim

Wood trim is a great choice for garage door trim near me, as it is strong, durable, and can be customized to match the existing exterior of your home. It also provides a classic, timeless look that can enhance the overall look of your home. However, wood trim is more expensive than vinyl or aluminum trim, and it can be prone to fading and warping over time.

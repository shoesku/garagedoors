What Size Garage Door Extension Spring Do I Need?

Garage door springs are an important part of the functioning of a garage door. They help in lifting and closing the garage door and make it easier to open and close. It is important to choose the right size of the extension spring for your garage door to ensure that it works properly. It is also important to ensure that the spring is correctly installed. This article provides an overview of what size garage door extension spring do I need.

How to Measure the Garage Door Extension Spring

The first step in determining the size of the garage door extension spring is to measure the door itself. The width and length of the door should be measured. This will give an idea of the size of the spring that is required. The spring should be long enough to cover the entire door width and should be able to support the weight of the door.

It is also important to measure the height of the door. This will help to determine the size of the spring that is required. The spring should be able to support the weight of the door at the highest point. To measure the height of the door, measure from the bottom of the door to the top. This will give an indication of the size of the spring that is required.

Types of Garage Door Extension Springs

There are two types of garage door extension springs. The first type is the torsion spring and the second type is the extension spring. The torsion spring is the most common type of spring and is usually used for heavier doors. The extension spring is used for lighter doors and is usually used for single panel doors. It is important to choose the right type of spring for the door.

When choosing the type of spring, it is important to consider the size and weight of the door. The heavier the door, the stronger the spring needs to be. It is important to choose a spring that is strong enough to support the weight of the door. If the spring is too weak, it could cause the door to fail to open or close properly.

Important Considerations When Choosing a Spring

It is important to consider the size and weight of the door when choosing a garage door extension spring. The spring should be able to support the weight of the door and should be strong enough to resist the forces that open and close the door. The spring should also be long enough to cover the entire door width.

It is also important to consider the type of door. The type of door will determine the type of spring that is needed. If the door is a single panel door, then the extension spring is usually the best choice. If the door is a double panel door, then the torsion spring is usually the best choice.

When choosing a spring, it is important to consider the installation instructions. The spring should be installed in accordance with the manufacturer's instructions. This will ensure that the spring is correctly installed and will prevent any damage to the door or the spring itself.

Conclusion

Choosing the right size of garage door extension spring is important to ensure that the door works properly. It is important to measure the door and determine the size of the spring that is required. It is also important to consider the weight of the door and the type of door when choosing the spring. It is important to follow the installation instructions to ensure that the spring is correctly installed.

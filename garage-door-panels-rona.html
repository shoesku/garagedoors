Why Choose Rona Garage Door Panels?

Rona panels provide superior protection against the elements, as well as keeping pests and intruders out of your home. The panels are made from high-quality materials that are designed to last, and they are easy to maintain and keep clean. They also come with a range of features to make them even more secure, such as reinforced locks and weather stripping.

Rona panels are also the perfect choice for anyone looking to make their home more energy efficient. The panels are designed to trap heat and keep your home warm in the winter and cool in the summer, reducing your energy costs.

How to Install Rona Garage Door Panels

Installing Rona panels is a relatively simple process that can usually be completed in a few hours. It’s important to make sure that the panels are installed correctly to ensure they are secure and provide the maximum level of protection.

First, you need to measure the opening of your garage door and purchase the right size panels. You should also make sure you have all the necessary tools, such as a drill, screws, and a level.

Next, you’ll need to mark the panels to make sure they fit correctly. You can then attach the panels to the garage door frame using screws. Make sure you use the proper screws for the material of the door and follow the instructions carefully.

Finally, you should install any weatherstripping or locks for added security. Again, make sure to follow the instructions carefully and test the locks before you finish the installation.

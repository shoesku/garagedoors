Cost Of Replacing A Garage Door Motor

There are some things that can go wrong with any garage door, and one of the most common issues is a broken garage door motor. If your motor stops working, you may be wondering what the cost of replacing it is. The good news is that replacing a garage door motor isn't too expensive, and it's something that you can do yourself if you are handy with tools. In this article, we'll take a look at the cost of replacing a garage door motor, as well as the steps involved in doing so.

Cost of Replacing a Garage Door Motor

The cost of replacing a garage door motor can vary depending on the type of motor you have and the complexity of the installation. Generally speaking, you can expect to pay anywhere from $100 to $300 for a new garage door motor. This cost includes the price of the motor itself, as well as any additional parts or accessories you may need for the installation. If you are hiring a professional to install the motor, you can expect to pay an additional $50 to $100 for labor.

Types of Motors

When it comes to replacing a garage door motor, there are two main types to choose from: belt-driven motors and chain-driven motors. Both types of motors are designed to open and close the garage door, but they are powered differently. Belt-driven motors are powered by an electric motor, while chain-driven motors are powered by a chain. Both types of motors are relatively easy to install and can be purchased at most home improvement stores.

Installation Process

If you are replacing your garage door motor yourself, the first thing you'll need to do is disconnect the power to the motor. This can usually be done by unplugging the motor or by flipping the circuit breaker. Once the power is disconnected, you can then remove the old motor and install the new one. This process is relatively simple and can usually be completed in a few hours. If you are hiring a professional to install the motor, the process will be similar but may take a bit longer.

Additional Costs

In addition to the cost of the garage door motor, you may need to purchase some additional parts or accessories for the installation. This could include things like a new belt or chain, as well as other hardware such as screws and bolts. The cost of these additional parts can vary depending on the type of motor you have, so it's important to factor this in when calculating the total cost of the project.

Conclusion

Replacing a garage door motor is a relatively simple and inexpensive project that can be completed in a few hours. The cost of replacing a garage door motor can vary depending on the type of motor you have, as well as any additional parts or accessories needed for the installation. If you are handy with tools, you can save money by replacing the motor yourself, or you can hire a professional to do the job for you. Either way, the cost of replacing a garage door motor is relatively reasonable and can be done in a relatively short amount of time.

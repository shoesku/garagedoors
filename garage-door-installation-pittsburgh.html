Common Types of Garage Doors in Pittsburgh

When it comes to garage door installation in Pittsburgh, there are a few common types of garage doors to choose from. Garage Door Installation Process in Pittsburgh

The process of installing a new garage door in Pittsburgh is relatively straightforward. First, the old door must be removed and disposed of. The new door must then be measured to ensure a proper fit. The tracks for the door must then be installed, followed by the door itself. Once the door is in place, the tracks must be adjusted for proper alignment. The door must then be lubricated and the springs must be connected. Finally, the opener must be installed and tested.

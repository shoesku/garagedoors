How Much Do Wood Garage Doors Cost?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

When most people think of garage doors, they usually think of steel or aluminum, but wood garage doors are actually becoming increasingly popular. Wooden garage doors offer a unique look that adds a touch of class to any home, while also providing superior insulation options and a variety of customization options. But how much do wood garage doors cost?

The cost of a wood garage door will depend on several factors, including the size, material, and type of door you choose. For example, a basic wooden garage door made of softwood will cost less than a custom-made hardwood door. The type of wood you choose will also impact the cost. Cedar, for example, is a popular choice for wood doors due to its durability and aesthetic appeal, but it will cost more than other woods such as pine or fir.

The size of the garage door will also affect the price. Generally speaking, larger doors will cost more, as will doors made of thicker wood. Additionally, the type of hardware and accessories you choose for your door will also affect the cost. For example, a basic garage door opener will cost less than one with advanced features such as remote access and safety sensors.

Also, the type of installation you choose will also have an impact on the price. If you choose to have a professional install your door, you can expect to pay more than if you choose to do it yourself. Professional installation will usually include additional labor costs, so it is important to factor that into the cost when estimating the total price of the job.

In general, the cost of a wood garage door can range from $800 to over $4000, depending on the size, material, and type of door you choose. A basic 16-by-7-foot wooden door with standard hardware will cost at least $800, while a custom-made hardwood door with advanced features can cost upwards of $4000. It is also important to note that the cost of installation will vary depending on the type of installation you choose.

Which Wood is Best for Garage Doors?

When choosing a wood for your garage door, there are several factors to consider. The most important factor is the type of wood, as some woods are more durable than others. Cedar is a popular choice for wood garage doors, as it is naturally resistant to rot and insects, and is also aesthetically appealing. Other woods such as pine or fir may also be suitable, though they may not be as durable as cedar.

The grade of the wood is also important. Higher grade woods are more expensive, but they are also more durable and better able to withstand the elements. Additionally, if you choose a door made of high grade wood, you may be eligible for a longer warranty. This may be beneficial if you are concerned about the longevity of your garage door.

Finally, the type of finish you choose for your wood door will also affect the cost. A basic varnish or sealant may be sufficient for a basic door, but if you want a more luxurious finish, you may have to pay extra for a higher quality finish such as paint or lacquer.

Conclusion

Wooden garage doors are becoming increasingly popular due to their unique look, superior insulation options, and a variety of customization options. The cost of a wood garage door will depend on several factors, including the size, material, and type of door you choose. In general, the cost of a wood garage door can range from $800 to over $4000, depending on the size, material, and type of door you choose. When choosing a wood for your garage door, it is important to consider the type of wood, the grade of the wood, and the type of finish you choose.

What is Garage Door Brush Seals?

Garage door brush seals are a form of weatherstripping that are installed around the perimeter of your garage door. They offer a highly effective barrier between the inside and outside of your garage, preventing the loss of heat or air conditioning and reducing the risk of pests or wildlife entering. Brush seals are constructed of flexible nylon bristles that are attached to a metal strip or a PVC or rubber base. The bristles are designed to be flexible enough to move with the movement of the door, while also providing a tight seal.

Brush seals can be used on all types of garage doors, including sectional doors, roller doors, tilt doors and canopy doors. They are also suitable for use on residential and commercial garage doors. The seals are available in a range of sizes and styles, so you can find one that is suitable for your specific garage door.

When Your Garage Door Key Is Not Working

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

If you’re having difficulty with your garage door key not working, then you’re probably wondering what to do. Fortunately, there are a few things you can try to get your key working again. In this article, we’ll discuss the steps you can take to troubleshoot and fix the issue. Read on to learn more!

Check the Lock Cylinder

If your key isn’t working, it could be because the lock cylinder is jammed or not working properly. To check the lock cylinder, you’ll need to open the garage door and look inside the lock. If you can see any signs of damage, then you’ll need to replace the lock cylinder. This should be done as soon as possible to ensure the safety of your belongings.

Check the Key

It’s also important to check the key itself. If the key is bent or damaged, it may not be able to turn the lock cylinder properly. If this is the case, you can buy a replacement key from most hardware stores. Make sure to get a key that is the same size and shape as the original key.

Check the Lock Alignments

If the key and the lock cylinder are in good condition, then the issue might be with the lock alignments. Check to make sure that the lock is aligned properly and that the key is turning in the right direction. If it’s not, then you’ll need to adjust the lock alignments.

Clean the Lock

Sometimes dirt, dust, and debris can get stuck in the lock and cause it to not work properly. To clean it, use a cotton swab or small brush to remove any dirt or debris. Make sure to be gentle, as you don’t want to damage the lock.

Lubricate the Lock

If the lock is still not working, then you can try lubricating it. Use a lubricant specifically designed for locks and apply it to the keyhole and the key itself. This can help to loosen any stuck parts and make the key turn more smoothly.

Replace the Lock

If none of the above steps have worked, then you may need to replace the lock. This is usually the last resort, as replacing the lock can be expensive and time consuming. Make sure to buy a lock that is designed for garage doors and is compatible with your key.

Call a Professional

If you’re still having difficulty with your garage door key not working, then it’s best to call a professional. A locksmith or garage door technician can assess the issue and suggest the best course of action. They can also install a new lock if necessary.

Conclusion

Having your garage door key not working can be a frustrating experience. Fortunately, there are a few things you can do to try and get it working again. Check the lock cylinder, key, and lock alignments and clean and lubricate the lock if necessary. If all else fails, you can always call a professional for assistance. We hope this article has been helpful and you can now get your key working again!

Garage Door Dimensions Nz - All You Need To Know In 2023

Are you planning to buy a new garage door for your home in New Zealand in 2023? If so, you’ll want to ensure you get the right size so that it fits properly. In this article, we’ll look at the basics of garage door dimensions NZ, so you can make an informed choice.

Garage Door Height

The standard height for a garage door in New Zealand is 2.4m. This is the height measured from the top of the roller door to the ground. However, some companies may offer doors that are slightly larger, up to 3m in height.

Garage Door Width

The size of the garage door will depend on the size and shape of your garage. The standard width of a garage door in New Zealand is 2.4m. However, if you have a large garage, you may be able to find a larger door. Many companies offer garage doors up to 3.6m in width.

Garage Door Thickness

The thickness of a garage door is an important factor to consider when it comes to installation. The standard thickness of a garage door in New Zealand is 40mm. However, some companies may offer thicker doors, up to 70mm in thickness.

Garage Door Weight

The weight of a garage door is important when it comes to installation. The weight of a garage door in New Zealand is usually between 50kg and 100kg. If the door is too heavy, then it may need to be reinforced with additional supports to make sure it is secure.

Garage Door Insulation

Insulation is an important factor to consider when choosing a garage door in New Zealand. Most garage doors come with some level of insulation, but some may offer more than others. The insulation helps to keep the garage cool in summer and warm in winter.

Garage Door Material

The material of a garage door is important when it comes to durability and maintenance. The most popular material for garage doors in New Zealand is aluminium, as it is lightweight and durable. Steel is also a popular choice, as it is strong and secure.

Garage Door Security

When choosing a garage door in New Zealand, you should consider the security features. Many garage doors come with automatic locks and security systems that can be activated remotely. This helps to deter potential intruders and protect your belongings.

Garage Door Cost

The cost of a garage door in New Zealand will depend on the size, material, and security features. Generally, the larger and more secure the door is, the more expensive it will be. However, there are deals available for those looking for a more affordable option.

Conclusion

When it comes to garage door dimensions NZ, the standard size is 2.4m high, 2.4m wide, and 40mm thick. However, it is possible to find larger doors, thicker doors, and doors with more insulation and security features. The cost of a garage door will depend on the size, material, and security features. So, make sure you consider all of these factors before making a purchase.

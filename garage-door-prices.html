Do Your Research Before Making a Decision

If you are in the market for a new garage door in 2023, you should know that prices have gone up significantly since the beginning of the decade. The days of getting a new door for less than $500 are long gone, and many of the top models can cost several thousand dollars. The best way to ensure you get the right door for your budget is to do your research and shop around before making a decision. Read on to learn more about the different types of garage doors, their prices, and their features.

Types of Garage Doors

Garage doors come in a variety of materials, including wood, steel, aluminum, and composite. Each material has its own advantages and disadvantages, and the price of each type of door will vary depending on the material. Wood is one of the most popular materials for garage doors, as it is durable, attractive, and easy to maintain. Steel and aluminum are both strong and durable options, but aluminum is usually more lightweight and less expensive. Composite doors are the most expensive option, but they are also the most durable and require the least amount of maintenance.

Features to Look For

When it comes to garage doors, there are a few features to look out for. Insulated doors are a great option if you want to keep your garage warm in the winter and cool in the summer. If you live in a windy area, look for a door with a wind rating of at least 20 mph, as this will help keep your garage door secure. Another important feature to look for is a remote opener, which allows you to open and close your garage door from the comfort of your car or home. This can be a great convenience.

Prices

The price of a garage door will depend on the material, size, and features you choose. A basic wood door will usually cost around $1,000, while a more advanced steel door can cost up to $3,000. Composite doors are the most expensive option, starting at around $4,000. Of course, prices will vary depending on the size and features you choose. It is important to do your research and compare prices before making a decision.

Installation Costs

In addition to the cost of the door itself, you will also need to factor in the cost of installation. Most companies offer professional installation services for an additional fee. Generally, installation of a single-car garage door will cost around $200 and double-car garage doors can be up to $400. Again, prices will vary depending on the size, complexity, and location of your garage.

Saving Money

If you want to save money on your garage door purchase, there are a few things you can do. First, shop around and compare prices from different companies before making a decision. You may also be able to get a discount by taking advantage of any promotions or discounts the company may be offering. Additionally, you can save money by installing the door yourself, although this is not recommended unless you have some experience with home improvement projects. Finally, you may be able to get a better deal by buying a package deal that includes the door and installation.

Conclusion

Garage door prices have gone up significantly in the past few years, but that doesn't mean you need to break the bank to get a new door. Doing your research and comparing prices from different companies can help you get the best deal possible. Additionally, you may be able to save money by taking advantage of promotions or discounts and installing the door yourself. No matter what you choose, make sure you take the time to make an informed decision so you get the best garage door for your budget.

What is a Garage Door Photo Eye?

A garage door photo eye is a small device that is mounted on either side of a garage door. It is used to sense the presence of an obstruction in the path of the door. If an obstruction is detected, the photo eye will send a signal to the garage door opener to stop or reverse the opening or closing of the door. It is a crucial safety device that prevents the door from closing on people or objects that may be in its path.

Why is the Correct Photo Eye Height Important?

The correct photo eye height is important for effective operation, safety, and longevity of your garage door system. If the photo eye is not the correct height, it may not be able to detect objects in its path. This could lead to the door closing on an obstruction and causing damage or injury. If the photo eyes are too low, it could also lead to the door not opening or closing properly, as the photo eyes may not be able to sense the presence of an obstruction.

What is the Correct Height for a Garage Door Photo Eye?

The correct height for a garage door photo eye is typically 6 to 8 inches above the ground. The photo eye should also be level with the bottom of the door when it is in the fully open position. This will ensure that the photo eye is able to detect any obstructions that may be in the path of the door.

What if I Have a Multi-Story Home?

For multi-story homes, the garage door photo eye height should be adjusted accordingly. How Do I Adjust the Height of My Garage Door Photo Eye?

In most cases, the height of the photo eye can be adjusted by loosening the mounting screws and adjusting the height of the photo eye. Once the desired height has been achieved, the mounting screws should be tightened to secure the photo eye in place. It is important to ensure that the photo eye is level when adjusting the height, as this will ensure that it is able to detect any obstructions in its path.

Should I Install a Backup System for My Garage Door?

In addition to installing the correct garage door photo eye height, it is also recommended to install a backup system for your garage door.

What To Know Before You Choose The Right Size Garage Door Frame Opening

Garage door frame openings come in a variety of sizes and styles. When you’re looking to install a new garage door, it’s important to measure your frame opening correctly to ensure a proper fit. Knowing the correct size of your garage door frame opening is critical to ensuring that your new door will fit seamlessly, and operate without any issues. In this article, we’ll discuss the factors you should consider when choosing the size of your garage door frame opening.

Standard Garage Door Widths

Standard garage door widths range from 8 feet to 18 feet. This is an important factor to consider when selecting the size of your garage door frame opening. If you plan to use a double door, the total width of the two doors combined should equal the width of the frame opening. If you plan to use a single door, it should match the width of the frame opening.

Standard Garage Door Heights

Standard garage door heights range from 6 feet to 10 feet. This is also an important factor to consider when selecting the size of your garage door frame opening. If you plan to install a double door, the total height of the two doors combined should equal the height of the frame opening. If you plan to install a single door, it should match the height of the frame opening.

Additional Considerations

In addition to the width and height of the garage door frame opening, there are several other factors that you should consider. These include the type of garage door, the type of garage door opener, and the type of insulation. For example, if you plan to install an insulated garage door, the frame opening should be slightly larger to allow for proper installation and insulation.

Choosing the Right Size Garage Door Frame Opening

When it comes time to choose the size of your garage door frame opening, it’s important to take all of the above factors into consideration. Measure your frame opening correctly, and take into account the type of garage door, the type of garage door opener, and the type of insulation that you plan to use. By doing so, you can rest assured that your new garage door will fit seamlessly and operate without any issues.

Hire a Professional

If you’re unsure about the size of your garage door frame opening, it’s best to hire a professional. A professional installer will be able to measure your frame opening accurately and provide you with the correct size for your new garage door. They will also be able to provide you with advice on the best type of garage door, garage door opener, and insulation to use.

Conclusion

Choosing the right size garage door frame opening is an important step in the installation of your new garage door. It’s important to measure your frame opening accurately, and take into account the type of garage door, the type of garage door opener, and the type of insulation that you plan to use. If you’re unsure about the size of your garage door frame opening, it’s best to hire a professional to ensure a proper fit. With the right size garage door frame opening, you can rest assured that your new garage door will fit perfectly and operate without any issues.

What Are Garage Door Springs?

Garage door springs are components that are used to keep a garage door in place. They are typically made of steel and are mounted on the sides of the door frame. The springs are tensioned in such a way that they keep the door in place when it is closed, and they help to reduce the amount of effort needed to open and close the door. Without the springs, it would be difficult to open and close the door on a regular basis.

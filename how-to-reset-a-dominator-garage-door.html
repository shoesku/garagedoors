How To Reset A Dominator Garage Door

Introduction

The Dominator is a popular brand of automatic garage door opener. It is an efficient and reliable opener with an array of features to ensure secure and safe access to your garage. However, at some point in time, you may need to reset your Dominator garage door. This may be due to a malfunction, an accident, or even a power surge. Fortunately, resetting your Dominator garage door opener is easy and can be done in a few simple steps.

Step One: Unplug the Opener

The first step to resetting your Dominator garage door opener is to unplug the opener from its power source. This will turn off the power to the opener and allow you to reset it. Once you have unplugged the opener, wait a few minutes before plugging it back in.

Step Two: Check the Battery

The next step is to check the battery of your Dominator garage door opener. If the battery is low, then it may be preventing the opener from working properly. Make sure to replace the battery if it is low or dead. This will ensure that the opener is able to work properly again.

Step Three: Check the Wiring

The next step is to check the wiring of your Dominator garage door opener. Make sure that all of the wiring is secure and that the connections are in good condition. If there are any loose wires, then they should be tightened or replaced. This will ensure that the opener is able to function properly.

Step Four: Check the Sensors

The next step is to check the sensors of your Dominator garage door opener. Make sure that the sensors are in good condition and that they are not blocked by any debris or objects. If the sensors are blocked, then the opener may not be able to open or close properly. This can be easily fixed by cleaning the sensors and making sure they are in good condition.

Step Five: Reset the Opener

The final step is to reset the Dominator garage door opener. To do this, press and hold the “Reset” button on the back of the opener for a few seconds. Once the opener has been reset, it should be working properly again. However, if the problem persists, then it may be best to contact a professional to check the opener.

Conclusion

Resetting your Dominator garage door opener is a simple process that can be done in a few simple steps. Make sure to unplug the opener from its power source, check the battery, check the wiring, check the sensors, and reset the opener. If the problem persists, then it may be best to contact a professional for assistance. With these simple steps, you should be able to reset your Dominator garage door opener in no time.

Are you thinking about installing a new garage door opener? If you’re looking for an affordable and reliable option, then you should consider getting one from Costco. Costco carries a wide selection of garage door openers and installation is surprisingly easy. In this article, we will discuss the various models available, installation costs, and the steps to take to have your new garage door opener installed.

Types of Garage Door Openers at Costco

Costco carries several different types of garage door openers. The main types are belt drive, chain drive, and direct drive. Each type of garage door opener has its own benefits and drawbacks, so you should carefully consider each option before making your purchase. Here’s a quick look at the differences between these types of garage door openers.

Belt Drive Openers

Belt drive openers are the quietest and most expensive option. The belt drive system uses a rubber belt to open and close the door, instead of a chain, which makes it significantly quieter than other types of openers. This is a great option for those who want a quiet garage. However, belt drive openers are more expensive than other types and may not be the best choice for those on a tight budget.

Chain Drive Openers

Chain drive openers are the most common type of opener and are usually the most affordable. The chain drive system uses a chain to open and close the door, which makes it slightly louder than other types. This is a great option for those who want an affordable opener that is still reliable. The main drawback of chain drive openers is that they can be louder than other types.

Direct Drive Openers

Direct drive openers are the newest type of opener and are the quietest of all three types. The direct drive system uses a motor to open and close the door, which makes it virtually silent. This is a great option for those who want a quiet garage and don’t mind paying a bit more for it. However, direct drive openers are the most expensive of all three types.

Costco Garage Door Opener Installation Costs

Once you’ve decided which type of garage door opener to purchase, you’ll need to consider the installation costs. Installation costs vary depending on the type of opener and the complexity of the installation. Generally, belt drive openers are the most expensive to install, followed by chain drive openers and then direct drive openers. The average cost for installation is around $250, but it can go as high as $500 depending on the type of opener and the complexity of the installation.

Steps to Install Your Garage Door Opener

Once you’ve purchased your garage door opener, you’ll need to install it. Installing a garage door opener is relatively easy and can be done in a few simple steps. Here’s a quick look at the steps you should take to install your new garage door opener.

Step 1: Read the Instructions

The first step is to read the instructions that came with your garage door opener. These instructions should provide you with detailed information on how to properly install your opener. Make sure to read the entire manual before beginning the installation process.

Step 2: Prepare the Area

The next step is to prepare the area for installation. This includes clearing any obstacles from the area and ensuring that the area is well lit. Make sure to check for any wires or pipes that could be in the way of the installation process.

Step 3: Install the Opener

Once the area has been prepared, you can begin the installation process. This includes mounting the opener on the ceiling and connecting the power and safety cables. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully and double-check all connections before powering on the opener.

Step 4: Program the Opener

Once the opener is installed, you’ll need to program it. This can be done using the remote or keypad that came with the opener. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully and double-check your programming before using the opener.

Conclusion

Installing a new garage door opener can be a great way to upgrade your home. Costco carries a wide selection of openers and installation is surprisingly easy. Make sure to consider the various types of openers available, the installation costs, and the steps to take to have your new garage door opener installed. With a little bit of preparation and knowledge, you’ll be able to get your new garage door opener up and running in no time.

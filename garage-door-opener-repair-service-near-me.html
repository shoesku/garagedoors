Common Garage Door Opener Problems

Garage door openers can malfunction due to a variety of reasons. Here are some of the most common problems that our technicians encounter:

  • Broken springs – If the springs on your garage door opener are broken, it can cause the door to malfunction or even become stuck.
  • Malfunctioning remote – If the remote isn’t working properly, it can prevent the door from opening or closing.
  • Faulty motor – If the motor is faulty, it can cause the door to be noisy or even malfunction.
  • Sensors – If the sensors on your garage door opener are faulty, it can cause the door to close too quickly or not close at all.
  • Worn out parts – Over time, the parts on your garage door opener can wear out and need to be replaced.

If you’re experiencing any of these issues with your garage door opener, it’s important to get it checked out by a professional.

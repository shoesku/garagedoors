Types of Garage Doors in Perth

When it comes to garage doors in Perth, there are a few types available. The most popular types are roller doors, sectional doors, and tilt doors. Roller doors are the most common type of garage door, as they're very secure and cost-effective. Sectional doors are also popular, as they come in a variety of sizes and can be installed to fit any space. Lastly, tilt doors are great for smaller garages, as they're easy to open and close, and don't take up too much space.

Cost of Garage Doors in Perth

The cost of garage doors in Perth varies depending on the type of door and the size of the door. Generally, roller doors will cost anywhere from $600 to $1,500, depending on the size. Sectional doors are usually a bit more expensive, ranging from $1,000 to $2,000. Lastly, tilt doors can cost anywhere from $300 to $600. It's important to note that the cost may vary depending on the brand and quality of the door.

How to Choose the Right Garage Door in Perth

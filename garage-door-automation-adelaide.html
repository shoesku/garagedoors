What Is Garage Door Automation?

Garage door automation is the process of installing an electric opener to your garage door, allowing you to open and close it from a remote location. This can be done using a smartphone app, a remote control device, or even a voice-activated system. Automating your garage door can be a great way to increase the security of your home, as well as making it easier to get in and out of your garage. It also helps to keep intruders out, as you’ll always know when your door has been opened or closed.

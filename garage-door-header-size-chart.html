Everything You Need To Know About Garage Door Header Size Chart

Buying a new garage door is an exciting experience, as you get to choose a design and style that fits your home, as well as a size that fits your needs. One of the most important decisions you’ll make when you buy a new garage door is the size of the header, or the area of the door that is the most visible. This is where your garage door header size chart comes in.

A garage door header size chart is a chart that helps you determine the measurements needed for your garage door header. This chart is usually found in the installation instructions for your door, but can also be found online. It will tell you the exact measurements for the height, width, and depth of your header.

It’s important to get the measurements right when you’re buying a new garage door. If the header size is too large or too small, it can look odd or even affect the way the door operates. The header size also affects the amount of weatherproofing your door will need.

Measuring Your Garage Door Header

To measure your garage door header size, you’ll need a tape measure and a piece of paper. You’ll also need to know the height, width, and depth of your door. Start by measuring the height of the door from the ground to the highest point. Then, measure the width of the door from side to side. Finally, measure the depth of the door from front to back.

Once you have all of these measurements, you’ll be able to find the correct size for your garage door header on the chart. The chart will list the sizes in terms of height, width, and depth. It will also list the recommended header size for that size door. It’s important to note that the chart is just a guideline, and the sizes may vary slightly from manufacturer to manufacturer.

Choosing a Garage Door Header Size

Once you have your measurements, it’s time to choose a size for your garage door header. Most manufacturers offer a variety of sizes, so you should be able to find one that fits your door perfectly. If you’re not sure which size to choose, you can always contact the manufacturer and they can help you determine the best size for your needs.

When you’ve chosen the size you want, it’s time to install the header. Make sure you follow the instructions that come with the header, as some may require special measurements or tools. Once you’ve installed the header, it’s a good idea to check for any gaps or air leaks between the header and the door. If there are any, you can use weatherstripping to seal them.

Conclusion

Using a garage door header size chart is the best way to make sure you get the right size for your door. It’s important to take your time and measure your door carefully before you buy a header, as the wrong size can affect the way the door operates. Once you’ve chosen the size, make sure you follow the installation instructions carefully to ensure a perfect fit.

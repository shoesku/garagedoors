What Kind of Cable Do You Need?

The type of cable you need for your garage door opener depends on the type of opener you have. There are a few different types of garage door openers, and each type requires a different type of cable. The most common types of openers are chain-driven, belt-driven, and screw-driven. Chain-driven openers use a metal chain and cables, belt-driven openers use a belt and cables, and screw-driven openers use a threaded rod and cables. Make sure you know which type of opener you have before you buy a cable!

Once you know the type of opener you have, you can buy the correct cable. Chain-driven openers use metal cables, belt-driven openers use polymer cables, and screw-driven openers use metal cables. The size of the cable you need depends on the size and weight of your garage door. Make sure you measure the length of your garage door and the weight of your garage door before you buy a cable.

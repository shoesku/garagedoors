Types of Garage Door Openers

Before you start shopping for a new garage door opener, it's important to understand the differences between various types. Chain drive openers are the most common type of opener, as they are reliable and affordable. They use a metal chain to lift the garage door, and are generally louder than other options. Belt drive openers are a more advanced type of opener, as they use a rubber belt to lift the door. This makes them a lot quieter than chain drives, but they usually cost more. Direct drive openers are the latest type of opener available, and they use a motor to lift the door. These openers are incredibly quiet, but they are also the most expensive option.

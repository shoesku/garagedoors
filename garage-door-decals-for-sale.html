What Are Garage Door Decals?

Garage door decals are a type of vinyl sticker or graphic that can be adhered to the outside of your garage door. They come in a variety of sizes and designs, so you can find one that fits your style and budget. There are also a variety of styles to choose from, such as sports logos, funny sayings, or even your own custom designs.

Why Use Garage Door Decals?

Garage door decals are a great way to add a unique touch to your home. Not only do they make your garage door look great, but they also protect it from the elements. The decals act as a shield from dirt, dust, and UV rays, ensuring that your door looks great for years to come. Additionally, garage door decals can also help to make your home stand out from the others in your neighborhood.

How Do I Install Garage Door Decals?

Installing garage door decals is a relatively simple process. First, you will need to clean the outside of your garage door with a mild detergent and water. Then, you will need to measure the area you want to cover and cut the decal to fit. Once you have the decal cut, you will need to peel the backing off and position the decal onto the door. Finally, you will need to use a squeegee to adhere the decal to the door.

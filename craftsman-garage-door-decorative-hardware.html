Decorative Hardware For Craftsman Garage Doors

Garage doors are an important part of the exterior of any home. Not only do they protect the vehicle and belongings inside, but they can also add aesthetic appeal to the home’s facade. Craftsman style garage doors have become a popular choice for homeowners in recent years, with their timeless look and classic appeal. To make a Craftsman garage door stand out even more, consider adding decorative hardware. From classic hinges and handles to decorative pulls and door knockers, there’s a variety of options available to make your garage door truly unique.

Why Choose Decorative Hardware for Your Garage Door?

Decorative hardware is a great way to add a personal touch to your Craftsman garage door. It can also be used to subtly accentuate the other features of the door, such as the paneling or trim. Decorative hardware can also be used to match the existing hardware on the rest of your home, creating a cohesive look and feel throughout. With the right decorative hardware, your Craftsman garage door can be transformed from a simple, functional piece of the home’s exterior to a beautiful, inviting feature that adds to the overall curb appeal.

Types of Decorative Hardware for Craftsman Garage Doors

When it comes to decorative hardware for Craftsman garage doors, there are a variety of options to choose from. The most popular types of decorative hardware include:

Hinges

Hinges are a great way to add a unique touch to your Craftsman garage door. There are a variety of styles and finishes to choose from, including hammered iron, bronze, and brass. Hinges come in a variety of sizes, so you can find the perfect size and style to match or complement the paneling and trim of your Craftsman garage door.

Handles and Pulls

Handles and pulls are a great way to personalize your Craftsman garage door. They come in a variety of styles and finishes, from simple, classic designs to ornately detailed ones. Handles and pulls can be used to create a more inviting look and can even be used to create a seamless transition from the rest of the home’s exterior.

Door Knockers

Door knockers are a classic choice for Craftsman garage doors. They come in a variety of sizes and styles, from vintage-inspired pieces to more modern designs. Door knockers can be used to create a more inviting look and can also be used to complete the look of the door.

Numerals and Letters

Numerals and letters can be used to personalize your Craftsman garage door. They come in a variety of sizes and styles, from simple, classic designs to ornate and decorative ones. Numerals and letters can be used to create a unique look and can also be used to distinguish your home from the others in the neighborhood.

Where to Buy Decorative Hardware for Craftsman Garage Doors

Decorative hardware for Craftsman garage doors can be purchased online or at a local hardware store. Online stores offer a wide selection of decorative hardware, from hinges and handles to door knockers and numerals. Local stores may have a smaller selection, but they will likely have more options for customizing the hardware to match the specific style of your Craftsman garage door. Whichever option you choose, make sure to measure your garage door before purchasing the decorative hardware so that it fits correctly.

Conclusion

When it comes to making a statement with your Craftsman garage door, decorative hardware is the way to go. From hinges and handles to door knockers and numerals, there’s a variety of options available to make your garage door truly unique. Decorative hardware can be used to accentuate the other features of the door, create a cohesive look throughout the home’s exterior, and even add a personal touch. Whether you choose to shop online or in a local store, make sure to measure your garage door before purchasing the decorative hardware so that it fits correctly.

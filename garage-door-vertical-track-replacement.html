How To Replace A Vertical Track On A Garage Door In 2023

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Garage doors are an integral part of keeping your home safe and secure. Unfortunately, like any other type of equipment, they can wear out over time. One of the most common problems that can occur with a garage door is a broken vertical track. If you find yourself in this situation and you’re looking for a way to replace it, you’ve come to the right place. Read on to learn how to replace a vertical track on a garage door in 2023.

Gather Your Materials

The first step in replacing a vertical track on a garage door is to gather the materials you’ll need. You’ll need a new vertical track, a hammer, a drill, a pry bar, a level, and a few screws. Make sure you have the right size track for your door, as the wrong size won’t fit properly. Once you have all the materials, you’re ready to start the process.

Remove the Old Track

The next step is to remove the old track. Start by disconnecting the opener from the old track. Then, use a pry bar to loosen the bolts that hold the track in place. Once the bolts are loose, remove the track from the door. Once the track is removed, you’re ready to move on to the next step.

Attach the New Track

Now it’s time to attach the new track. Start by lining up the new track with the old track. Make sure that the holes line up and the track is at the same height. Then, use the drill to attach the track to the door with the screws. Once the track is securely in place, you’re ready to move on to the next step.

Adjust the Track

The next step is to adjust the track. Start by adjusting the track so that it’s level. Use a level to make sure that the track is even. Once the track is level, you’re ready to move on to the next step.

Test the Track

Now it’s time to test the track. Start by reconnecting the opener to the track. Then, open and close the door to make sure that the track is working properly. If the track is working properly, you’re done! If not, adjust the track as needed and try again.

Secure the Track

The last step is to secure the track. Start by using the drill to tighten the screws that hold the track in place. Make sure that the screws are tight and the track is secure. Once the track is secure, your job is done!

Conclusion

Replacing a vertical track on a garage door can be a bit intimidating, but it doesn’t have to be. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can easily replace a vertical track on a garage door in 2023. So the next time your track needs replacing, you’ll know exactly what to do.

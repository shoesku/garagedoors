Do I Need to Replace My Garage Door Springs?

If you have a garage door at home, you know how important it is to have it in good working condition. Garage doors and their components can take a lot of wear and tear over time and sometimes, the springs that help lift and lower the door need to be replaced. Knowing the cost of replacing your garage door springs in New Zealand can help you make an informed decision when you need to repair or replace your garage door.

Garage door springs play a crucial role in the functioning of your garage door. They are responsible for lifting and lowering the door and if they become worn out, they can cause problems with the opening and closing of the garage door. It is important to replace them if they are no longer working properly. If you are looking to replace your garage door springs in New Zealand, you should know that the cost can vary depending on a few different factors.

Factors that Affect the Cost of Replacing Garage Door Springs

There are several factors that can affect the cost of replacing your garage door springs in New Zealand. The size of the door, the type of spring, and the type of garage door opener you have can all affect the cost. Here is a breakdown of each factor and how they can affect the cost of replacing your garage door springs.

Size of the Door

The size of the garage door plays a role in the cost of replacing the door springs. Bigger doors require bigger springs, which can be more expensive. It is important to measure the size of your door before you start looking for a replacement spring. This will help you get an accurate estimate of the cost of replacing the springs.

Type of Spring

There are two types of springs that are commonly used for garage doors - torsion springs and extension springs. Torsion springs are more expensive than extension springs, but they are also more durable and last longer. If you are looking to save money, you may want to consider getting an extension spring instead.

Type of Garage Door Opener

The type of garage door opener you have can also affect the cost of replacing your garage door springs. If you have an older model, you may need to get a different type of spring than if you have a newer model. This is because some older models may require a different type of spring than newer models.

Average Cost of Replacing Garage Door Springs in New Zealand

The average cost of replacing a single garage door spring in New Zealand is around $150. This includes the cost of the spring, the labour, and any other materials that may be needed. The cost can go up depending on the size of the door, the type of spring, and the type of garage door opener you have. If you are replacing more than one spring, you can expect to pay more.

How to Save Money on Replacing Garage Door Springs

If you are looking to save money on replacing your garage door springs in New Zealand, there are a few things you can do. The first thing is to compare prices from different companies. You can do this by researching online or by asking friends and family for recommendations. Another way to save money is to do the installation yourself if you are confident in your DIY skills. This is a great way to save money, but it is important to make sure you follow all safety guidelines.

Conclusion

Replacing your garage door springs in New Zealand can be a costly process. It is important to know the cost of replacing the springs so that you can make an informed decision. The cost of replacing a single garage door spring in New Zealand is around $150, but this price can vary depending on the size of the door, the type of spring, and the type of garage door opener you have. You can save money by comparing prices from different companies and by doing the installation yourself if you are confident in your DIY skills.

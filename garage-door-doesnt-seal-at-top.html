How To Fix Garage Door That Doesn't Seal At The Top

Introduction

It is important to have a well-sealed garage door to keep out the elements. It also helps to keep valuable items in your garage safe from intruders. Unfortunately, garage doors sometimes do not seal properly at the top, leaving an opening for the elements to come in. This can create an uncomfortable environment and can also be a security risk. Thankfully, there are a few easy fixes to help close the gap at the top of the door. In this article, we’ll discuss how to identify the problem and then suggest some potential solutions.

Inspect the Door

In order to determine the cause of the gap at the top of the door, you will need to first inspect the door. Look closely at the frame and the tracks that the door slides on. Look for any signs of damage such as dents or rust. Also, make sure the tracks are aligned properly and that the door is properly balanced. If you find any damage or misalignment, this could be the cause of the gap at the top of the door.

Check the Weather Stripping

The weather stripping around the perimeter of the door is an important part of creating a tight seal. Check to make sure the weather stripping is in good condition and that it is properly attached to the door. If the weather stripping is worn out, cracked, or damaged in any way, then it may need to be replaced. Additionally, if the weather stripping is not properly attached, it will not be able to create a secure seal.

Check the Door Sweep

The door sweep is a piece of rubber or vinyl that is attached to the bottom of the door and helps to create a tight seal. Over time, the door sweep can become worn out or misaligned. Inspect the door sweep to make sure it is in good condition and properly aligned. If the door sweep is not in good condition, it may need to be replaced.

Adjust the Door

If the door is not properly aligned or balanced, it may not be able to create a secure seal at the top. Try to adjust the door so that it is level and properly aligned. If necessary, you can use a level to ensure that the door is properly balanced. Additionally, make sure the tracks are properly aligned and that the door is able to move smoothly.

Replace the Rollers

The rollers are the components that allow the door to move smoothly along the tracks. Over time, the rollers can become worn out or damaged. Inspect the rollers and make sure they are in good condition and properly aligned. If the rollers are not in good condition, they may need to be replaced.

Check the Hinges

The hinges are the components that attach the door to the frame. Over time, the hinges can become loose or damaged. Inspect the hinges and make sure they are in good condition and properly aligned. If the hinges are not in good condition, they may need to be replaced.

Conclusion

If your garage door doesn’t seal at the top, it can be an annoyance and it can also be a security risk. Thankfully, there are a few easy fixes that can help to close the gap at the top of the door. First, inspect the door to make sure there is no damage or misalignment. Second, check the weather stripping and door sweep to make sure they are in good condition and properly aligned. Third, adjust the door to make sure it is level and properly aligned. Finally, check the rollers and hinges to make sure they are in good condition and properly aligned. With a few simple steps, you can help to ensure that your garage door seals properly at the top.

Types of Garage Doors in Jamaica

There are several types of garage doors available in Jamaica, and each type has its own benefits and drawbacks. Here’s an overview of the different types of garage doors available in Jamaica:

  • Roller Doors: Roller doors are the most common type of garage door used in Jamaica. They are easy to install, and they open and close quickly. They are also relatively inexpensive, making them a popular choice among homeowners in Jamaica.
  • Sectional Doors: Sectional doors are a type of garage door that opens and closes vertically. They are more secure than roller doors and provide more insulation. However, they are more expensive than roller doors and may require more maintenance.
  • Sliding Doors: Sliding doors are another popular type of garage door in Jamaica. They are easy to use, and they can be opened and closed from either side. They are also relatively inexpensive and require minimal maintenance.
  • Automatic Doors: Automatic doors are the most expensive type of garage door available in Jamaica. They open and close automatically, and they provide added convenience and security. However, they require more maintenance than other types of garage doors.

Garage Door Prices in Jamaica in 2023

Now that we’ve discussed the different types of garage doors available in Jamaica, let’s take a look at the prices. Prices vary depending on the type and size of the door, and the installation costs. Here’s an overview of the average prices of different types of garage doors in Jamaica in 2023:

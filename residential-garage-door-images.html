Options For Residential Garage Door Images

One of the most popular options is a carriage house design. This style typically has a paneled, or grooved, look that looks similar to a carriage house. Most garage doors are made of steel or aluminum and come in a variety of colors and finishes.

Raised panel designs are also a popular residential garage door images choice. These feature a flat panel that is raised from the base, giving the door a more modern look. Raised panels are available in many colors and finishes, so you can choose one that fits the style of your home.

Another popular option is a two-tone design. This type of door has a light and dark color for the panels, adding contrast and visual interest. You can also choose from various materials, such as wood, steel, and aluminum.

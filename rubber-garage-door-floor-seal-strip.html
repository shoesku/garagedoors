Installing A Rubber Garage Door Floor Seal Strip: Everything You Need To Know

It’s no secret that a garage door can be a major source of energy loss in a home, but there are simple and cost-effective ways to reduce this loss. One of the most effective ways is to install a rubber garage door floor seal strip. This rubber strip helps to seal the gap between the garage door and the floor, preventing air and moisture from entering the garage. Installing a rubber garage door floor seal strip is a relatively simple process, but there are several key factors to consider.

The first thing to consider is the type of garage door you have. If you have a sectional garage door, there are different types of floor seal strips available. For example, you can use a single piece seal, or a double piece seal. A single piece seal is a long strip of rubber that is installed along the entire length of the garage door. A double piece seal is two separate pieces of rubber that are installed on each side of the door.

The second factor to consider is the shape of the seal strip. There are several shapes available, including U-shaped, V-shaped, and Z-shaped. Depending on the shape of the door and the amount of space available, a different shape may be more suitable. For example, the U-shaped seal strip is the most common type of garage door seal strip and works well for sectional doors. The V-shaped seal strip is best for roll-up doors and the Z-shaped seal strip is suitable for both sectional and roll-up doors.

The third factor to consider is the size of the seal strip. The size of the seal strip should correspond with the size of the garage door. If the seal strip is too small, it will not fit properly and may not provide an effective seal. The seal strip should also be installed on a clean, dry surface.

The fourth factor to consider is the material of the seal strip. Rubber is the most common material used for the seal strip, but there are other materials available as well. The material should be chosen based on the climate and the environment in which the garage is located. For example, in cold climates, a vinyl seal strip may be more suitable, while in hot climates, a silicone seal strip may be more suitable.

The fifth factor to consider is the installation process. The installation of a rubber garage door floor seal strip is relatively simple, but there are several steps involved. First, the seal strip should be cut to the correct size. Next, the seal strip should be placed in the proper location, making sure that the edges are flush with the surface. Finally, the seal strip should be secured with screws or adhesive.

Installing a rubber garage door floor seal strip can be a simple and cost-effective way to reduce energy loss in a home. In addition to being an effective seal, a seal strip can also add a decorative element to a garage door. By taking the time to consider the type, shape, size, material, and installation process, homeowners can ensure that their rubber garage door floor seal strip will be effective and last for many years.

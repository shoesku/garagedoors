Using A Foam Insert To Seal Your Garage Door Bottom

Garage door bottom seals are a great solution for keeping out the cold, dust, and other debris. They also help to keep out pests, such as rodents and insects. However, it can be a challenge to find the right garage door bottom seal for your particular needs. That’s why some people turn to foam inserts to get the perfect fit. Here’s what you need to know about using foam inserts to seal your garage door bottom.

What are Foam Inserts?

Foam inserts are pieces of foam that are designed to fit perfectly into the gap between the bottom of the garage door and the floor. These inserts come in a variety of sizes and shapes, so you’re sure to find one that will fit your particular situation. They can also be cut to fit any size of garage door. Once inserted, the foam will fill the gap and help to create an airtight seal between the door and the floor.

Benefits of Using Foam Inserts

Foam inserts have a number of benefits. First, they are easy to install. All you need to do is cut the insert to the correct size and shape, slip it into the gap, and you’re done. Second, they are very effective at creating an airtight seal, so you won’t have to worry about cold air, dust, or pests entering your garage. Finally, they are relatively inexpensive, so you won’t have to spend a lot of money to get a good seal.

Types of Foam Inserts

When it comes to foam inserts, there are two main types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell inserts are made from a denser foam material, so they are better at creating an airtight seal. Open cell inserts, on the other hand, are made from a softer foam material, so they are not as effective at creating an airtight seal. Ultimately, it’s up to you to decide which type of foam is best for your particular situation.

Installing a Foam Insert

Installing a foam insert is a relatively easy process. First, you’ll need to measure the gap between the bottom of the garage door and the floor. Then, you’ll need to cut the insert to the correct size and shape. Once the insert is cut, you can slip it into the gap. Make sure to press it firmly into place, so that it creates an airtight seal. Finally, you may want to use some sealant around the edges of the insert, just to be sure that it’s secure.

Caring for Your Foam Insert

Once the foam insert is in place, you’ll need to make sure that it’s properly cared for. This means regularly inspecting the insert for signs of damage, such as tears or holes. If you find any damage, it’s important to repair it immediately, so that the insert continues to provide an effective seal. Additionally, it’s important to clean the insert regularly, so that it stays in good condition.

Conclusion

Using a foam insert to seal your garage door bottom is a great way to keep out the cold, dust, and other debris. They are easy to install, relatively inexpensive, and can create an effective airtight seal. Plus, they come in a variety of sizes and shapes, so you’re sure to find one that fits your particular needs. Just make sure to inspect and clean the insert regularly, so that it stays in good condition.

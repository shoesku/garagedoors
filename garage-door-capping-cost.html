Why Is Garage Door Capping Necessary?

Having a secure and well-maintained garage door is an important part of protecting your home, your family, and your belongings. In order to ensure that your garage door is secure, it is essential to have the right capping installed. Garage door capping is a type of weather proofing material that is designed to keep moisture, dirt, and other elements out of your garage. It also helps keep your garage door looking great and functioning properly. Garage door capping provides an additional layer of protection and can help reduce energy costs by keeping the temperatures in your garage more consistent.

What Are the Different Types of Garage Door Capping?

There are several different types of garage door capping available for your home. The most common types are vinyl capping, metal capping, and foam capping. Vinyl capping is the most common and is often the cheapest option. It is available in a variety of colors and can be easily painted to match the color of your garage door. Metal capping is a more durable option and is often more expensive than vinyl. It is available in a variety of colors and is often used in more extreme climates. Foam capping is the best option for those who live in areas with extreme weather conditions and is the most expensive option.

How Much Does Garage Door Capping Cost?

The cost of garage door capping will vary depending on the type of capping you choose, the size of your garage door, and the amount of labor involved in installation. Vinyl capping is typically the least expensive option, with prices ranging from $5 to $15 per linear foot. Metal capping is usually more expensive and can range from $7 to $25 per linear foot. Foam capping is usually the most expensive option, with prices ranging from $10 to $40 per linear foot. The cost of labor will also vary depending on the size of your garage door, the complexity of the job, and the experience of the installer.

What Are the Benefits of Garage Door Capping?

The primary benefit of having garage door capping installed is that it will help keep moisture, dirt, and other elements out of your garage. This can help reduce the amount of damage that can be caused by water and dirt getting inside your garage. Garage door capping can also help to reduce energy costs by keeping the temperatures in your garage more consistent. Additionally, it can help to improve the overall look and appeal of your home by giving your garage door a more uniform and polished appearance.

What Should You Look for When Choosing a Garage Door Capping Installer?

When selecting a garage door capping installer, it is important to choose someone who is experienced and knowledgeable in this type of installation. How Can You Save Money on Garage Door Capping Costs?

One of the best ways to save money on garage door capping costs is to do the installation yourself.

What Are Garage Door Hinges?

Garage door hinges are an integral part of the garage door system. They are the pieces that attach the door panels to the frame of the door. They come in various shapes and sizes so it’s important to make sure that the hinges you choose are properly suited to your specific door. There are two main types of garage door hinges – the butt hinge and the strap hinge. The butt hinge is the more common of the two and is used for standard sectional doors. The strap hinge, on the other hand, is used for heavier, more robust garage doors.

How Often Should You Check Your Garage Door Hinges?

It’s important to inspect your garage door’s hinges on a regular basis in order to ensure that they are working properly. It is recommended that you check the hinges at least once a year. This will help to ensure that the hinges are in good condition and will help to prevent any problems from occurring in the future. You can check the hinges by manually opening and closing the garage door several times and making sure that the door opens and closes smoothly. If you notice any sticking or squeaking, then it may be time to replace the hinges.

How To Replace Garage Door Hinges

If you find that your garage door hinges are worn or damaged, then it’s important to replace them as soon as possible. Replacing the hinges is relatively simple, but you should always make sure to use the proper tools and safety equipment. Begin by disconnecting the garage door from the power source and then remove the old hinges. When installing the new hinges, make sure to use the same type and size as the old ones. Secure the hinges with the appropriate hardware and then reattach the door. Once the door is reattached, make sure to test it to make sure it is functioning properly.

How To Lubricate Garage Door Hinges

Lubricating the hinges is another important step in keeping your garage door in good condition. Doing this regularly will help to reduce friction and ensure that the hinges move smoothly. You can use a lubricant specifically designed for door hinges, or you can use a light oil such as WD-40. Make sure to spray the lubricant onto the hinge pins, as well as the hinge itself. Once the hinges are lubricated, make sure to open and close the door several times to make sure that the lubricant is evenly distributed.

Conclusion

Garage door hinges are an essential part of the door’s system and should be regularly checked and maintained. Make sure to inspect the hinges at least once a year and replace any worn or damaged hinges as soon as possible. You should also make sure to regularly lubricate the hinges in order to reduce friction and ensure that the door operates smoothly. Following these tips will help to keep your garage door running like new for years to come.

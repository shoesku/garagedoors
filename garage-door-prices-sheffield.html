Garage Door Prices In Sheffield: What To Expect

Whether you're looking for a new garage door for your own home, or you're a contractor who needs to update the doors for a project, it's important to know what to expect when it comes to garage door prices in Sheffield. With so many different types of doors and materials available, it's important to have a good idea of the cost you can expect to pay. This guide will help you determine what you need to know to make an informed decision.

Types of Garage Doors in Sheffield

One of the first things to consider when looking at garage door prices in Sheffield is the type of door you're interested in. There are several types of doors available, and each one will have its own unique price. For example, you can choose from roller doors, sectional doors, tilt doors, and up-and-over doors. Each of these types of doors will have different prices based on the material used and the size of the door.

Roller Doors

Roller doors are a popular choice for Sheffield homeowners and contractors because they offer a lot of security and convenience. They are typically made from steel and feature a roller system that allows the door to open and close quickly and easily. Roller doors are usually the most affordable type of garage door and are available in a range of styles and sizes. Prices for roller doors start at around £250 and can go up to £600 or more depending on size and materials.

Sectional Doors

Sectional doors are another popular choice for Sheffield homeowners and contractors. These doors are made up of several panels that slide open and close along tracks. They are typically made from steel and can be insulated to help keep your garage warm in the winter. Sectional doors are more expensive than roller doors with prices starting around £400 and can go up to £800 or more depending on size and materials.

Tilt Doors

Tilt doors are a great option if you're looking for something different. These doors are made up of two panels that swing open and close like a regular door. They are usually made from steel and can be insulated to help keep your garage warm in the winter. Tilt doors are usually more expensive than roller or sectional doors with prices starting around £500 and going up to £900 or more depending on size and materials.

Up-and-Over Doors

Up-and-over doors are another popular choice for Sheffield homeowners and contractors. These doors are made up of two panels that swing open and close like a regular door. They are usually made from steel and can be insulated to help keep your garage warm in the winter. Up-and-over doors are usually the most expensive type of garage door with prices starting around £600 and going up to £1000 or more depending on size and materials.

Additional Costs to Consider

When you're looking at garage door prices in Sheffield, it's important to consider any additional costs you may incur. For example, if you're installing a sectional door, you may need to purchase additional hardware such as tracks, hinges, and springs. You may also need to hire an electrician to install the motor for an automated garage door. Additionally, you may need to hire a professional to install the door, which can add to the overall cost.

Conclusion

Garage door prices in Sheffield vary greatly depending on the type of door you choose and the materials used. Roller doors are typically the most affordable option while up-and-over doors are usually the most expensive. Be sure to consider any additional costs such as hardware and installation when you're looking at garage door prices in Sheffield. With the right information, you can make an informed decision and find the perfect garage door for your needs.

Different Types of Garage Door Locks

There are several types of locks available for garage doors in Northampton. These include manual locks, keyless entry systems, and smart locks. Each type has its own unique features and benefits.

Manual locks are the most common type of garage door locks. They require a physical key to open and close the door. This makes them more secure than other types of locks. They are also relatively inexpensive and easy to install.

Keyless entry systems are becoming increasingly popular in Northampton. These locks require a code or keypad to unlock the door. They are much more secure than manual locks, as the code can be changed regularly. They are also more convenient, as they can be operated remotely.

Smart locks are the latest innovation in garage door security. They are connected to a smartphone app and can be operated remotely. They are also very secure as they use encrypted codes to unlock the door.

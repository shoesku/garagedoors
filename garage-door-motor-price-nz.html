The Benefits of Installing a Garage Door Motor

If you are looking to improve the security of your home and add a bit of convenience to your life, then installing a garage door motor is a great idea. Garage door motors are motorised mechanisms that open and close the door automatically. This eliminates the need for you to get out of the car to open and close the door manually. It also adds an extra layer of security to your home as the door can be programmed to only open when a valid code is entered. Garage door motors are also great for convenience. They can be operated remotely and can be programmed to close automatically after a certain amount of time. This means you don’t have to worry about leaving the door open and it also reduces the chances of someone being able to gain access to your property without your knowledge.

Types of Motors Available in NZ

When shopping for garage door motors in NZ, you’ll find that there are a variety of different types available. The most common type is the chain-driven motor, which is the most affordable option. This type of motor uses a chain to operate the door. The main benefit of this type of motor is that it’s relatively easy to install and is usually quite affordable. Another popular option is the belt-driven motor, which is slightly more expensive than the chain-driven model. This type of motor uses a belt instead of a chain and generally offers a smoother operation and quieter operation. If you’re looking for a higher-end option, then you might want to consider a direct-drive motor. This type of motor is more expensive than the chain-driven and belt-driven models, but it offers a much smoother and quieter operation.

Choosing the Right Motor for Your Needs

When shopping for garage door motors in NZ, it’s important to consider your needs and budget. If you’re looking for a basic motor that offers a reasonable amount of convenience and security, then a chain-driven model should be sufficient. However, if you want a more advanced motor that offers a smoother and quieter operation, then you might want to consider investing in a belt-driven or direct-drive motor. It’s also important to consider the size of your garage door when choosing a motor. Most models are designed to fit standard-sized doors, so make sure to measure your door before purchasing a motor.

Average Cost of a Garage Door Motor in NZ

The cost of a garage door motor in NZ will depend on the type of motor you choose and the size of your door. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $200 - $500 for a basic chain-driven model. If you’re looking for a higher-end model, you’ll likely have to pay between $500 - $1,000.

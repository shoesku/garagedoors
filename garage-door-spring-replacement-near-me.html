Replacing garage door springs is a typical repair that homeowners need to do every once in a while. However, it is critical to get the job done correctly and safely. If you don’t know what you are doing, it is best to leave it to the professionals. If you are looking for “garage door spring replacement near me”, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll discuss why you should leave this job to the professionals and what to look for when selecting the right garage door repair service in your area.

Why You Should Leave Garage Door Spring Replacement to a Professional

Garage door springs are very dangerous to replace if you don’t know what you’re doing. The springs are under a lot of tension and are capable of causing serious injury. In addition, a spring that is not installed correctly can cause further damage to your garage door, leading to more costly repairs. It is best to leave this job to the professionals who know what they’re doing and have the right tools and safety protocols.

What to Look for When Selecting a Garage Door Spring Replacement Service

When selecting a garage door spring replacement service, it is important to do your research. You want to make sure you’re hiring a reputable company with experience in this type of repair. Look for reviews online, ask friends and family for recommendations, and check for customer complaints. You also want to make sure the company offers warranties on their work. This way, if something does go wrong, you are covered.

Types of Garage Door Springs

When selecting a garage door spring replacement service, you should also consider the type of spring you will need. There are two common types of garage door springs: torsion and extension. Torsion springs are the most common type and are typically mounted above the garage door. They are wound tightly and are capable of providing a lot of power when opening and closing the door. Extension springs, on the other hand, are mounted on the sides of the door and are designed to provide less power.

Cost of Garage Door Spring Replacement

The cost of garage door spring replacement will vary depending on the size and type of spring you need, as well as the labor cost. Typically, a single torsion spring can cost anywhere from $100-$200, while a pair of extension springs can cost between $150-$300. Labor costs can add up quickly, so make sure to ask the company for an estimate before hiring them.

Conclusion

Replacing garage door springs is a job that should be left to the professionals. If you are searching for “garage door spring replacement near me”, make sure to do your research and select a reputable company with experience in this type of repair. Consider the type of springs you need and be sure to get an estimate before hiring anyone. Hopefully, this article has helped you in your search for the right garage door spring replacement service.

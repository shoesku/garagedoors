Adding Decorative Hardware To Your Garage Door

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Do you want to give your garage door a facelift? Adding decorative hardware to your garage door is a great way to make it look more attractive. In this article, we'll discuss the different types of hardware you can use to customize your garage door, as well as the best ways to install them. By the end of this article, you'll be ready to give your garage door the look you've been wanting!

Types of Decorative Hardware

When it comes to adding decorative hardware to your garage door, you have a few different options. The most common type of decorative hardware is black decorative hardware, which is often made of metal and can come in a variety of styles and sizes. Other options include chrome, bronze, and brass hardware, which can add a unique touch to your garage door. Additionally, you can also find decorative hardware in a variety of shapes, such as stars, crosses, and other designs.

Tools You'll Need

Before you start installing decorative hardware on your garage door, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a drill and drill bits for making holes in the door, as well as a screwdriver for installing the hardware. Additionally, you'll need a level to make sure the hardware is straight. Finally, you'll need some sealant to make sure the hardware is securely in place and won't become loose over time.

Prepare the Garage Door

Before you start installing the hardware, you'll need to make sure the garage door is ready for the hardware. Start by cleaning the door with a mild soap and water solution, and then allow it to dry completely before beginning. Once the door is clean, you can use a level to make sure it is even. If not, you may need to adjust the door before you can install the hardware.

Installing the Hardware

Once the door is ready, it's time to start installing the hardware. Start by marking out where you want the hardware to go, and then use a drill to make the holes. Once the holes are drilled, you can insert the screws into the holes, and then use a screwdriver to tighten them. Make sure the hardware is secure and level before moving on to the next piece.

Securing the Hardware

Once all the hardware is in place, you'll need to make sure it is secure. Start by applying some sealant around each piece of hardware. This will help prevent it from becoming loose over time. Additionally, you'll want to check the hardware periodically to make sure it is still secure.

Finishing Touches

Once the hardware is installed and secured, you can give your garage door a finishing touch. If you have a wooden garage door, you can stain or paint it to match the hardware. Additionally, you can add some weather stripping around the door to help keep the elements out. Finally, you can add a new doorknob and lock to give your door a more finished look.

Conclusion

Adding decorative hardware to your garage door is a great way to give it a unique look. There are a variety of types of hardware you can choose from, and the best way to install it is by using a drill, screwdriver, level, and sealant. With the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can give your garage door the look you want. So, what are you waiting for? Get started and give your garage door the makeover it deserves!

5 Tips For Regular Garage Door Maintenance In Sunshine Coast

Having a garage door in your home is convenient for many reasons. However, like with any other appliance, regular maintenance is required to keep it in good working order. Here are 5 tips for regular garage door maintenance in Sunshine Coast.

1. Clean the Track and Rollers

The garage door track is the main component that helps the door to open and close properly. You should regularly check and clean the track and rollers to ensure they are free of dirt and debris. You can use a soft brush to clean the track and rollers. If you find any dirt or debris, you can use a vacuum cleaner to remove it.

2. Lubricate the Moving Parts

It is important to lubricate all the moving parts of the garage door to keep them working properly. You can use a light oil or silicone spray. Make sure to wipe off any excess lubricant after you have applied it. This will help to prevent any dust or dirt from sticking to the moving parts.

3. Check the Springs and Cables

The springs and cables of your garage door should be checked every month for any signs of damage or wear. Make sure to look for any frayed cables or worn-out springs. If you find any, you should replace them as soon as possible.

4. Inspect the Weather-Stripping

The weather-stripping on your garage door helps to keep out the elements. It should be inspected regularly for any signs of damage or wear. If you find any, you should replace the weather-stripping as soon as possible.

5. Check the Balance of the Door

The balance of your garage door should also be checked regularly. If the door is not balanced correctly, it can cause the door to open and close improperly. To check the balance, you can use a broom handle or other long object to press down on the middle of the door. If the door does not remain open, it is not balanced correctly and you should seek professional help.

Regular Maintenance for a Long Lasting Garage Door

Regular maintenance of your garage door is essential for its long-term use. By following these 5 tips for regular garage door maintenance in Sunshine Coast, you can be sure that your door will remain in good working order for many years to come. Make sure to check the door regularly for any signs of damage or wear, and take the necessary steps to repair or replace any damaged parts.

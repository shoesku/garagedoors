What is a Jackshaft Garage Door Opener?

A jackshaft garage door opener is a type of opener that is powered by a motor, but operates using a trolley system on the side of the garage door. It is a distinct type of opener because it uses a different system than the more common chain drive, belt drive, and screw drive openers. Jackshaft openers are more expensive than the other types of openers, but they come with several advantages. For one, they are quieter than the other types of openers, and they are also less likely to break down due to the lack of moving parts. They are also the most compact type of opener, making them ideal for garages with limited space.

Benefits of Choosing a Jackshaft Opener

The first benefit of choosing a jackshaft garage door opener is that it is much quieter than the other types of openers. This is because the motor is located away from the door, so it doesn't make any noise when it operates. This makes it ideal for homes that are close to neighbors, as the sound of the motor won't disturb them. Additionally, the lack of moving parts means that jackshaft openers are less likely to break down than the other types of openers. This makes them a great choice for people who want a reliable opener that won't need frequent repairs.

Types of Jackshaft Openers

There are two main types of jackshaft openers: chain drive and belt drive. Chain drive openers are the most common type, and they use a chain to move the trolley along the side of the garage door. They are the most affordable type of opener, but they are also the loudest. Belt drive openers, on the other hand, use a belt instead of a chain, which makes them much quieter. They are more expensive than chain drive openers, but they are also more reliable and require less maintenance.

Things to Consider When Purchasing a Jackshaft Opener

When shopping for a jackshaft garage door opener, there are a few things you should consider. First, you should make sure that the opener is compatible with your garage door, as some openers may not be compatible with certain types of doors. Additionally, you should consider the power of the motor, as this will determine how quickly and smoothly the door will open and close. You should also look at the features that come with the opener, such as battery backup, motion sensors, and other safety features.

Garage Door Panel Basics

Garage door panels are an essential part of any garage door system. They provide the structure and support for the door and its various components. In the United Kingdom, garage door panels come in a variety of materials, sizes, and styles, so it’s important to choose the right one for your home or business.

Garage door panels are typically constructed from either steel or aluminium. Steel is the most popular choice for garage door panels in the UK for its durability and strength. It’s also more affordable than aluminium and requires little maintenance. Aluminium is more lightweight, making it an ideal choice for those who need a more lightweight garage door system. It’s also more resistant to rust and corrosion, making it a great choice for those living in coastal regions.

Garage door panels also come in different sizes and styles. The most common type is the sectional panel, which is made up of several sections connected together. This type of panel is ideal for those who need to replace an existing garage door panel or want to add additional sections to their existing door.

Roller door panels are also popular in the UK. These panels are made from either aluminium or steel and feature a rolling mechanism. This allows them to be opened and closed with ease, making them a great choice for those who need to access their garage on a regular basis.

Choosing the Right Garage Door Panel

When it comes to choosing the right garage door panel, there are a few factors to consider. The size of the panel should match the size of your garage door. You should also consider the material you want to use. Steel is the most common option, but aluminium may be a better choice if you live in a coastal area or need a more lightweight option.

You should also consider the style of the panel. Sectional panels are the most popular option, but roller door panels offer a more modern look and are easier to open and close.

When it comes to installation, you have several options. You can hire a professional to install your new garage door panel, or you can do it yourself. If you choose the latter, make sure you read the instructions carefully and follow them to the letter.

Maintaining Your Garage Door Panel

Once you have installed your new garage door panel, it’s important to keep it in good condition. This means regularly checking the screws and bolts to make sure they are tight and secure. It’s also important to lubricate the moving parts of the door, such as the hinges and rollers, to prevent rust and corrosion.

You should also check the weatherstripping around the door. If the weatherstripping is worn or cracked, it can cause air leaks, which can lead to energy inefficiency. If the weatherstripping needs to be replaced, make sure you use a high-quality product that’s designed for garage doors.

Finally, it’s important to check the condition of the panels themselves. Look for signs of wear, such as scratches and dents, and make sure they are properly aligned and secured. If you find any problems, it’s best to replace the panel as soon as possible.

Conclusion

Garage door panels in the UK come in a variety of materials, sizes, and styles. It’s important to choose the right one for your needs, as well as maintaining it properly. By following these tips, you can ensure your garage door panel is safe and secure for years to come.

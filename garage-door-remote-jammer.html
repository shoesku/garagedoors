As technology advances, so do the threats to our security. In the past, criminals would use physical objects like lock-picking tools or even explosives to gain access to our homes. Now, criminals are using new and advanced methods to gain access. One of the most common methods is using a garage door remote jammer. This is a device that can interfere with the signal of a garage door remote and prevent it from opening the door. It is becoming increasingly popular among thieves and burglars, as it is a relatively easy way to gain access.

A garage door remote jammer is a small device that is easy to conceal. It works by emitting a signal that interferes with the signal of the garage door remote. This prevents the door from opening. It is important to note that this does not actually damage the remote, but simply interferes with it. This makes it much harder for the homeowner to open the door, as the signal coming from the remote is blocked.

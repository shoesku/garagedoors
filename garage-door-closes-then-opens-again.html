How To Fix A Garage Door When It Closes And Opens Again

You’re in the middle of leaving the house when you hear a loud thud. You look up to see your garage door opening and closing again after you thought it was already closed. This is a common problem that can be caused by a variety of factors. In this article, we will help you figure out why your garage door closes then opens again and how to fix it.

What Causes a Garage Door to Open and Close Again?

When a garage door closes and then opens again, it’s usually caused by one of three things: the safety sensors, the door springs, or the opener itself. The safety sensors are responsible for detecting any objects that may be blocking the door from closing. If there’s something blocking it, then it will keep the door from closing. The door springs provide the tension necessary to open and close the garage door. If the springs are worn or broken, the door may not open or close properly. Finally, the opener is responsible for sending the signal to the door to open and close. If the opener is malfunctioning, then the door may not open or close properly.

How to Fix a Garage Door That Closes and Opens Again

If your garage door closes and then opens again, the first thing you should do is check the safety sensors. Make sure there are no objects blocking the sensors that could be causing the issue. If there are, simply remove the objects and the problem should be solved. If the sensors are working properly, then you should check the door springs. If the springs are worn or broken, you should replace them. Finally, if the springs appear to be working properly, then the issue may be with the opener. You should check the connections and make sure that everything is in working order.

Other Troubleshooting Tips

If the problem still persists after you’ve checked all of the above, then you should try a few other troubleshooting tips. First, you should check the tracks and the rollers to make sure they’re not damaged or bent. If they are, then you should replace them. You should also check the cables and pulleys to make sure they’re not worn or damaged. If they are, then you should replace them as well. Finally, if you’re still having issues, then you should check the power supply to make sure it’s connected properly.

When to Call a Professional

If the problem persists and you’ve tried all of the troubleshooting tips above, then it may be time to call a professional. A professional technician will be able to diagnose the issue and provide a solution. They may be able to replace the opener, replace the door springs, or even replace the entire door. If you’re not comfortable attempting the repair yourself, then it’s best to call a professional to ensure that the job is done correctly.

Preventative Maintenance

The best way to prevent your garage door from closing and opening again is to perform regular maintenance. This includes regularly inspecting the safety sensors, door springs, and opener for any signs of wear or damage. You should also make sure that the tracks and rollers are clean and in good condition. Finally, you should lubricate all moving parts to ensure that everything is working properly. Regular maintenance will help to keep your garage door in tip-top shape and prevent it from randomly closing and opening again.

Conclusion

When your garage door closes and then opens again, it can be frustrating and even dangerous. However, the good news is that this problem can usually be fixed quite easily. Start by checking the safety sensors, door springs, and opener. If the problem persists, then you should try a few other troubleshooting tips such as checking the tracks and rollers or the power supply. If the problem still persists, then you should call a professional to diagnose and repair the issue. Finally, remember to perform regular maintenance to keep your garage door in good working condition.

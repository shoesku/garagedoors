Why Would I Need To Repair My Garage Door Cable?

Garage door cables are an essential part of the door's system and can be a source of many common problems. The cable may become frayed, worn or broken due to wear and tear, or it may simply become disconnected from the door's roller track. This can cause the door to become misaligned, making it difficult to open or close the door. In some cases, the cable may be snapped completely, rendering the door unusable until it is replaced.

In addition to malfunctioning, broken, or frayed cables, you may also need to repair your garage door cable if the tension on the cable is too high or too low. Too much tension can cause the door to come off its track, while too little tension can cause the door to not open or close properly.

How Much Does Garage Door Cable Repair Cost in the UK?

The cost of garage door cable repair in the UK can vary greatly depending on the type of cable and the extent of the damage. Generally speaking, the cost of a basic garage door cable repair can range from £50 to £150, though the cost may be higher for more complex repairs.

If the cable is broken, the repair may involve replacing the entire cable. This can be quite expensive, as the cost of the cable will vary depending on the size and type of the door. The cost of the cable itself may be around £50 to £100, with the installation costs adding to the overall cost.

What Does a Garage Door Cable Repair Involve?

Garage door cable repair usually involves replacing the existing cable or adjusting the tension on the existing cable. Replacing the cable involves removing the existing cable and installing a new one, while adjusting the tension on an existing cable involves tightening or loosening the tension on the cable.

The repair process may also involve lubricating the cables, as this can help to reduce the risk of the cables becoming frayed or broken. Lubricating the cables can also help to reduce the noise they make when they move.

Do I Need To Hire a Professional For Garage Door Cable Repair?

It is generally recommended that you hire a professional to repair your garage door cable. This is because the repair process can be complicated and dangerous if done incorrectly. A professional technician will have the experience and the knowledge to properly and safely repair the cable.

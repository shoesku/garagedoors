What is a Genie Garage Door Opener Track?

Genie garage door openers use a track system to move the door up and down. The track is made up of two metal rails that are attached to the ceiling and the garage door. The rails guide the door up and down and allow it to open and close smoothly. The track also helps support the door’s weight and keeps it from falling or shifting while in use.

The Genie garage door opener track is made up of several parts. These parts include the track itself, the track brackets, the track rollers, the track pulleys, and the track chain. Each piece is important for the functioning of the garage door opener, and if one piece is damaged or missing, it can cause the door to not function properly.

Types of Genie Garage Door Opener Track Parts

Genie garage door opener track parts come in a variety of styles and sizes. The most common types of Genie garage door opener track parts include:

  • Track Brackets: The track brackets are the metal brackets that attach the track to the ceiling and the garage door. They are available in a variety of sizes and styles to accommodate different garage door sizes.
  • Track Rollers: The track rollers are the small wheels that move along the track and help the door open and close. They are made of metal and come in a variety of sizes and styles.
  • Track Pulleys: The track pulleys are the small metal wheels that help the track move the door up and down. They are available in a variety of sizes and styles.
  • Track Chain: The track chain is the metal chain that moves the door up and down. It is available in a variety of sizes and styles.

It’s important to note that each type of Genie garage door opener track part is designed for a specific size and style of door. It’s important to measure your garage door before purchasing Genie garage door opener track parts to ensure that you get the right size and style for your system.

Everything You Need To Know About Garage Door Height Sizes Chart

When it comes to the size of your garage door, it can be difficult to decide what size to go for. Not only must you consider the size of the door itself, but also the dimensions of the frame, the space around it and even the height of the ceiling. To ensure your garage door is the correct size for your property, it is important to understand the size range available and how to measure it correctly.

What is the Standard Garage Door Height?

The standard garage door height is 7’. However, there are also 8’ and 10’ sizes available. If you have an attached garage with an exterior door, the standard size is 6’8”. You can also order custom sizes, but these tend to be more expensive. To make sure you get the right size, it is important to measure the height of the area where the door will be installed. This is best done from the floor to the ceiling.

What is the Standard Garage Door Width?

The standard garage door width is 16’. However, 8’, 10’ and 12’ sizes are also available. If you are ordering a custom size, the width should be measured from the trim to the edge of the frame. If you are installing a double garage door, make sure you measure the width of each door individually, as the two may be different sizes.

What is the Standard Garage Door Opening Height?

The standard garage door opening height is 6’6”. This is the height of the door opening, not the height of the door itself. When measuring the opening height, you need to measure from the top of the opening to the floor. The opening should also be measured from wall to wall.

What is the Standard Garage Door Opening Width?

The standard garage door opening width is 16’. This is the width of the door opening, not the width of the door itself. When measuring the opening width, you need to measure from one side of the opening to the other. The opening should also be measured from wall to wall.

What is a Garage Door Height Sizes Chart?

A garage door height sizes chart is a useful tool for helping you determine the correct size of garage door for your property. It shows you the range of sizes available and helps you find the size that will fit your opening correctly. You can find these charts online, in stores or in home improvement magazines.

How to Use a Garage Door Height Sizes Chart?

Using a garage door height sizes chart is easy. All you need to do is measure the height and width of the opening and then compare it to the chart. If the measurements match one of the sizes on the chart, then that size is the right one for your garage door. If not, then you will need to order a custom size.

Why is it Important to Get the Right Size Garage Door?

Getting the right size garage door is important for a number of reasons. Firstly, it ensures that the door will fit properly and open and close without issue. Secondly, it ensures that the door is secure, as a door that is too small can be easily forced open. Finally, it ensures that the door looks aesthetically pleasing, as a door that is too big or too small will look out of place.

Conclusion

Garage door sizes can vary greatly and it is important to get the correct size for your property. Measuring the opening and using a garage door height sizes chart is the best way to determine the right size. It is also important to remember that custom sizes are available, if needed.

What is a Garage Door Key Fob?

A garage door key fob is a wireless remote control device that provides convenient, secure access to your garage. It can be used to open and close your garage door from a distance, making it a great way to keep your home secure while providing easy access. The key fob is typically small enough to fit in your pocket, making it easy to carry around with you wherever you go. It also works with a variety of different garage door openers, so you can choose the one that best fits your needs.

How Does a Garage Door Key Fob Work?

A garage door key fob typically works by transmitting a signal to the opener. This signal is then received by the opener, which then opens and closes the door as it was programmed to do. The signal is usually transmitted via radio waves, so you can open and close your garage door from any location as long as you are close enough to the opener. The signal can also be coded to ensure that only authorized individuals have access to your garage.

What is a Garage Door Frame Extension?

A garage door frame extension is an upgrade to your existing garage door frame that can help increase the overall security of your home and improve the aesthetic appeal of your home. What Are the Different Types of Garage Door Frame Extensions?

How Do You Install a Garage Door Frame Extension?

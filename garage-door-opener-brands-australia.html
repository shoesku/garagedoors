Are you looking for a new garage door opener or just want to learn more about the brands available in Australia? Look no further. In this article, we’ll take a look at the five best garage door opener brands in Australia in 2023. Whether you want to go with a classic, reliable brand or a newer, technologically-advanced product, you’ll find the right one for you.

1. Chamberlain

Chamberlain is one of the most established and well-known brands in the garage door opener industry. They offer a wide range of products, from basic openers to more advanced ones with Wi-Fi connectivity and smartphone compatibility. They also make MyQ-enabled openers, which allow you to control your garage door from anywhere. Prices for Chamberlain openers start at around $200 and can go up to $500 for more advanced models.

2. Genie

Genie is another top brand in the garage door opener market. They provide a range of openers with different power levels, from 1/2 HP to 1 1/4 HP. Genie openers are compatible with a variety of home automation systems and are Wi-Fi enabled. Their openers are also designed to be very quiet and durable, making them a great choice for anyone who wants a reliable opener. Prices for Genie openers start at around $200 and can go up to $500 for more advanced models.

3. Meross

Meross is a relatively new brand in the garage door opener market, but they’ve made a big splash in Australia. Their openers are packed with features, including Wi-Fi connectivity and smartphone compatibility. They’re also very affordable, with prices starting at around $150. Meross openers are designed to be easy to install and use, making them a great choice for anyone who wants a simple and reliable opener.

4. Sommer

Sommer is a German company that has been making garage door openers for over 30 years. They offer a range of openers with different power levels, from 1/2 HP to 1 1/4 HP. They also have openers with Wi-Fi connectivity, smartphone compatibility, and MyQ-enabled technology. Prices for Sommer openers start at around $200 and can go up to $500 for more advanced models.

5. ATA

ATA is an Australian brand that has been making garage door openers for over 25 years. They offer a range of openers with different power levels, from 1/2 HP to 1 1/4 HP. They also have openers with Wi-Fi connectivity, smartphone compatibility, and MyQ-enabled technology. Prices for ATA openers start at around $200 and can go up to $500 for more advanced models.

Conclusion

When it comes to garage door openers, there are plenty of options available in Australia. Whether you’re looking for a basic opener or something more advanced, you’ll find the right one for you. The five brands we’ve discussed in this article are some of the best and most reliable brands in the market. They offer a variety of features and power levels, so you’re sure to find one that fits your needs.

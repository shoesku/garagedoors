Are you looking to buy a garage door lock handle? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll take a look at the different types of garage door lock handles and what you should consider before you buy one. By the end of this guide, you’ll have a good idea of which type of garage door lock handle is the best for your needs. So, let’s get started!

Why Should You Buy a Garage Door Lock Handle?

A garage door lock handle is an important tool for any homeowner. It provides you with an added layer of security for your garage, ensuring that only you or someone you authorize can access it. The garage door lock handle can also be used to keep the garage door closed when not in use, reducing the amount of energy you use to cool or heat the garage. In short, having a garage door lock handle can save you money, time, and effort.

Types of Garage Door Lock Handles

There are several types of garage door lock handles on the market today, each with its own unique features and benefits. The most common types of garage door lock handles include keyed entry, deadbolt, lever handle, and padlock. Let’s take a closer look at each type.

Keyed Entry

Keyed entry garage door lock handles are the most common type of lock handle. These handles require a key to open and close the garage door. They are typically easy to install, but they do not offer the highest level of security. If someone were to obtain your key, they would be able to enter your garage.

Deadbolt

Deadbolt garage door lock handles are more secure than keyed entry locks. They require a key to open and close the door, but they are also equipped with a strong, metal deadbolt that prevents the door from being opened with a credit card or other tool. Deadbolts are also more difficult to pick than keyed entry locks.

Lever Handle

Lever handles are a type of garage door lock handle that requires no key. Instead, they use a simple lever mechanism to open and close the door. This type of lock handle is extremely easy to use, but it is also less secure than other types of locks.

Padlock

Padlocks are a type of garage door lock handle that require a key to open and close the door. They are the most secure type of lock handle, but they are also the most difficult to install. If you’re looking for a secure lock handle, a padlock is your best option.

Things to Consider Before Buying

When shopping for a garage door lock handle, there are several factors you should consider. First, you need to decide which type of lock handle you want. As we’ve discussed, there are several types of lock handles to choose from. You should also consider the material and style of the lock handle. You want to make sure it matches the look of your garage door.

You should also think about the level of security you want. Some lock handles offer more security than others. If you live in a high-crime area, you’ll want to choose a lock handle that offers the highest level of security.

Finally, you should consider the cost. Different types of lock handles can range in price from a few dollars to hundreds of dollars. It’s important to set a budget and stick to it when shopping for a garage door lock handle.

Conclusion

Buying a garage door lock handle can be a daunting task. But with the right information and a bit of research, you can find the perfect lock handle for your needs. Consider the types of lock handles, the level of security you want, and the cost before you make your purchase. With a bit of effort, you can find the perfect lock handle for your garage door.

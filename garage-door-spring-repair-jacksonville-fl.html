When Do You Need Garage Door Spring Repair in Jacksonville, FL?

If your garage door is having difficulty opening or closing, then it could be due to a broken garage door spring. There are two types of springs used in garage doors: extension springs and torsion springs. Extension springs are located above the door opening and stretch when the door opens. Torsion springs are located above the door opening and twist when the door opens. If either type of spring is broken, you will need to have it replaced or repaired.

Signs of a Broken Garage Door Spring in Jacksonville, FL

If your garage door isn’t working properly, then it could be due to a broken garage door spring. There are several signs that could indicate a broken spring, including: the garage door won’t open or close; the door opens and closes unevenly; the door is off balance; or the door moves slowly. If you notice any of these signs, then it’s time to call a professional garage door spring repair service in Jacksonville, FL.

Advantages of Professional Garage Door Spring Repair in Jacksonville, FL

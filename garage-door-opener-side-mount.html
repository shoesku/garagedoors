The Benefits Of Installing A Garage Door Opener Side Mount

Are you in the market for a new garage door opener? Installing a side mount opener can provide you with a few benefits that you may not find in other types of door openers. In this article, we’ll take a look at why you should consider installing a side mount garage door opener in your home.

Easy to Install

One of the biggest advantages of installing a side mount garage door opener is that it is easy to install. Most models are designed to fit on the side of your garage door, meaning you don’t have to worry about cutting or drilling any holes. Additionally, the installation process is fairly straightforward and can be accomplished in just a few hours. Even if you’re not an experienced DIYer, you should be able to handle the installation process with relative ease.

Increased Safety

Another great benefit of installing a side mount garage door opener is the increased safety it offers. Since the opener is installed on the side of the door, it is much less likely to be damaged by debris or by people entering or exiting the garage. Additionally, the opener is located away from the moving parts of the garage door, which can reduce the risk of injury or damage.

Low Maintenance

Side mount garage door openers are also typically low maintenance. In most cases, all you need to do is keep the opener clean and lubricated. Additionally, most models are designed to last for several years, meaning you won’t have to worry about replacing it for a long time.

Cost Effective

Finally, side mount openers can be a cost effective option when compared to other types of garage door openers. Since they are typically easier to install, you may be able to save some money on the installation process. Additionally, most models are relatively inexpensive, so you won’t have to break the bank to get your new opener.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are a number of benefits to installing a side mount garage door opener. Not only is it easy to install, but it also provides increased safety and is relatively low maintenance. Plus, it can be a cost effective option when compared to other types of openers. If you’re in the market for a new garage door opener, a side mount model is definitely worth considering.

How To Fix A Garage Door Arm Yourself

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Are you having trouble getting your garage door to open and close properly? Are the arms attached to the springs not working as they should? You may need to replace the arms. Fortunately, it’s a relatively simple process. With a few tools and some patience, you can successfully fix a garage door arm yourself.

Gather the Tools and Parts Needed

Before you begin, gather the following tools and parts:

  • Screwdriver
  • Drill
  • Wrench
  • New arm for the garage door
  • Pulley wheels
  • Bolts and nuts

You may also need a ladder to reach the arms. It’s best to have all the tools you need before you start the project.

Measuring the Garage Door Arm

Once you have all the tools and parts, you’ll need to measure the garage door arm. This is important to make sure you get the right size arm for your garage door. Measure the arm from the end of the spring to the center of the hole. This is the length of the arm you’ll need to buy.

Remove the Old Arm

Once you have the right size arm, it’s time to remove the old one. Start by unscrewing the bolts that hold the arm in place. You may need a wrench or a screwdriver to do this. Once the bolts are removed, you can take the old arm off. Make sure to keep the bolts and nuts in a safe place.

Installing the New Arm

Now that the old arm is removed, you can install the new arm. Start by placing the new arm in the same position as the old one. Then, use the bolts and nuts to attach the arm to the springs. Make sure the bolts are tightened securely.

Check the Balance

Once the arm is securely in place, you’ll need to check the balance. Start by opening the garage door and checking the tension of the springs. If the tension is too tight, you may need to adjust the bolts. You can also use a drill to add or remove bolts, depending on the tension.

Replace the Pulley Wheels

Next, you’ll need to replace the pulley wheels. This is important to ensure the garage door opens and closes properly. To do this, you’ll need to unscrew the old pulley wheels and attach the new ones. Make sure the screws are tight and the wheels are securely in place.

Test the Garage Door Arm

Once you’ve replaced the arm and pulley wheels, it’s time to test the garage door. Open and close the door several times to make sure everything is working properly. If the door is working, then you’ve successfully fixed the garage door arm yourself.

Final Thoughts

Fixing a garage door arm is a relatively simple process. With a few tools and some patience, you can successfully fix the arm yourself. However, if you’re having trouble, it’s best to call a professional for help. They’ll be able to diagnose the problem and make sure the arm is fixed properly.

Why Does My Garage Door Keep Opening When I Close It?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Are you having issues with your garage door? Does it keep opening when you close it? If so, there are several possible causes of the issue. It could be an issue with the sensor, the springs, the opener, the track, or something else. This article will help you diagnose the issue and make the necessary repairs.

Check the Sensor

The first thing you should check is the sensor. Make sure there’s nothing blocking it, like a bike or a toolbox. The sensor should be in line with the garage door and free of any obstructions. If anything is blocking it, then it will prevent the door from closing properly. Also, make sure the sensor is clean and free of dust or dirt. If it is, then you may need to replace the sensors.

Check the Springs

The next thing to check is the springs. The springs are what provide the tension necessary to lift the door. If the springs are worn or broken, then the door won’t be able to stay closed. To check the springs, you’ll need to open the door and take a look at them. If you see any signs of wear, then it’s best to replace the springs.

Check the Opener

The next thing to check is the garage door opener. Make sure the opener is set to the correct speed and force. If the opener is set too low, then the door won’t be able to stay closed. You can adjust the speed and force settings on the opener to make sure the door stays closed properly.

Check the Track

The last thing to check is the track. Make sure the track is aligned properly and free of any obstructions. If the track is bent or misaligned, then the door won’t be able to stay closed. You can adjust the track to make sure it’s aligned properly.

Other Possible Causes

There are a few other possible causes of the issue. It could be an issue with the lock, the cables, or something else. If you’ve checked all the above items and the issue persists, then it’s best to call a professional for help.

Conclusion

If your garage door keeps opening when you close it, then there are several possible causes. The first thing to check is the sensor, then the springs, the opener, and the track. If those items are in good condition, then there may be an issue with the lock, the cables, or something else. If you’ve checked all the above items and the issue persists, then it’s best to call a professional for help.

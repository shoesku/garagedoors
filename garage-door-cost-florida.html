Garage Door Costs In Florida

Having a garage door installed in your Florida home is a great way to improve your home’s curb appeal and increase its value. But it’s also a big expense. To help you budget for this project, we’ve put together a guide to the average cost of garage doors in Florida.

Average Garage Door Cost in Florida

The average cost of a standard garage door in Florida is about $1,200. This price includes the cost of materials, labor, and installation. However, this price can vary greatly depending on the type of door you choose, the size of the door, and other factors. If you have a double garage door, for example, the cost could be as high as $3,000. And if you choose a more expensive door, such as one made of wood or with ornamental hardware, the cost could be even higher.

Factors That Affect Garage Door Costs

There are a few factors that will affect the cost of a new garage door. The first is the size of the door. If you need a larger door, it will cost more. The second is the type of door. If you choose a door made of wood or with ornamental hardware, it will cost more than a standard, steel door. The third is the material. If you choose a more expensive material, such as a wood door, the cost will be higher. And the fourth is the labor and installation. If you hire a professional to install the door, you’ll need to factor in the cost of labor.

Cost of Garage Door Installation

The cost of installation will vary depending on who you hire and the complexity of the job. Generally speaking, though, most professional garage door installers charge between $50 and $100 per hour for labor. If you’re installing a double door, the cost could be as much as $200 per hour. The cost can also vary depending on the complexity of the job. If you’re installing a door with ornamental hardware, for example, the cost could be higher than if you’re installing a standard door.

Additional Costs to Consider

In addition to the cost of the door and installation, there are a few other costs you should consider. The first is the cost of accessories. You’ll need to factor in the cost of things like a door opener, remotes, and other accessories. The second is the cost of a permit. Depending on where you live in Florida, you may need to obtain a permit to install your new garage door. Finally, you should also factor in the cost of any additional materials, such as insulation or weather-stripping, that you may need.

Get an Estimate

The best way to get an accurate idea of the cost of a new garage door is to get an estimate from a professional. A professional can help you determine the size, type, and material of the door that will best suit your needs and budget. They can also provide you with an estimate for the cost of materials and labor, as well as any additional costs you should consider.

The Bottom Line

The cost of a new garage door in Florida will vary depending on the size, type, and material of the door, as well as the cost of installation. To get an accurate estimate of the cost of this project, it’s best to consult with a professional. They can help you determine the best door for your needs and budget and provide you with an accurate estimate of the total cost.

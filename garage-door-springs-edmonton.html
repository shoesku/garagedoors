Maintaining Garage Door Springs In Edmonton

Introduction

Garage door springs are a crucial component of any garage door, allowing it to open and close with ease. In Edmonton, the cold weather and extreme temperatures can take a toll on these springs, making regular maintenance and upkeep important. In this article, we’ll explain why garage door springs are so important and what you need to do to keep them in good condition.

Why Garage Door Springs Are Important

The springs in a garage door are essential for its proper functioning. Without them, the door wouldn’t be able to open or close properly. Furthermore, the springs also help to reduce the strain on the motor of the garage door opener, which is why garage door springs should be properly maintained.

The two most common types of garage door springs are torsion springs and extension springs. Torsion springs are found on the top and side of the garage door, and they provide the force needed to open and close the door. Extension springs are located on the sides of the garage door, and they provide additional support when the door is opened and closed.

Garage door springs can wear out over time due to the extreme temperatures in Edmonton. If the springs are allowed to wear out, the garage door won’t be able to open or close properly. This can be a major inconvenience and can even lead to costly repairs.

How to Maintain Garage Door Springs

Maintaining garage door springs in Edmonton doesn’t have to be a difficult task. Regular inspection and maintenance can help to ensure your springs are in good condition. Here are some tips for keeping your garage door springs in good condition:

Inspect the Springs

It’s important to inspect the springs on a regular basis to ensure they are in good condition. Look for any signs of wear and tear, such as frayed cables, cracks, or corrosion. If you notice any of these issues, it’s best to have the springs replaced as soon as possible.

Lubricate the Springs

It’s important to lubricate the springs on a regular basis. You can use a lubricant such as WD-40 or a silicone spray to help keep the springs in good condition. Make sure to apply the lubricant in the areas where the springs come into contact with the door.

Replace the Springs

If your garage door springs are showing signs of wear and tear, it’s best to have them replaced. It’s best to have a professional replace the springs as this can be a difficult and dangerous task. A professional will be able to assess the situation and provide you with the best solution for your needs.

Conclusion

Garage door springs are essential for the proper functioning of your garage door. In Edmonton, the cold weather and extreme temperatures can take a toll on these springs, making regular maintenance and upkeep important. It’s important to inspect the springs on a regular basis and to lubricate them to keep them in good condition. If the springs are showing signs of wear and tear, it’s best to have them replaced by a professional.

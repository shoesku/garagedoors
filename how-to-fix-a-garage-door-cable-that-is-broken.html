Why Garage Door Cables Break

Garage door cables are the cords or ropes that attach the door to the motor mechanism that operates it. Over time, these cables can become worn, frayed, and eventually break. There are several reasons why this might happen. The most common one is that the cables are not properly tensioned when they are first installed. The tension needs to be adjusted on a regular basis in order for the cables to properly do their job. If it’s not, the cables can become loose, resulting in more wear and tear on them and eventually leading to a break. Other factors, such as environmental conditions, can also cause the cables to weaken and break.

How to Fix a Broken Garage Door Cable

At this point, you may be wondering how to fix a garage door cable that is broken. The answer is not an easy one. If the cable has broken completely, then it will need to be replaced. This is not a job that can be done by most homeowners and should be left to a professional. The professional will be able to properly tension the new cable, ensuring that it will last for many years to come. If the cable has not broken completely, then it may be possible to repair it. This would involve untangling the cable and then re-tensioning it. Again, this should be done by a professional.

Safety Precautions When Fixing a Garage Door Cable

When attempting to fix a broken garage door cable, there are some safety precautions that should be taken. First and foremost, the power to the garage door opener should be turned off. This will ensure that the door will not open while you are working on it. It is also important to wear protective gloves, as the cables can be sharp and cause injury. Additionally, make sure that you are properly supported so that you do not fall off the ladder or step ladder that you may be using. Finally, do not attempt to repair the cable yourself if you are not confident in your abilities.

Additional Tips for Making Sure Your Garage Door Cable Does Not Break

There are some additional tips that can be followed to make sure that your garage door cable does not break in the future. First, make sure that the cable is checked and re-tensioned on a regular basis. This will ensure that it does not become loose and start to wear out. Additionally, if you live in an area with extreme weather conditions, such as high heat and humidity, you should invest in a weatherproof cable. This will help to protect the cable from the elements and keep it from breaking. Finally, if you have any doubts about your ability to properly tension the cable, it is best to leave it to a professional.

Conclusion

A broken garage door cable can be a real nuisance, but it can be fixed. If the cable has broken completely, then it will need to be replaced by a professional. If it is still intact, then it may be possible to repair it. Be sure to take the proper safety precautions and make sure that the cable is properly tensioned. Additionally, check and re-tension the cable on a regular basis and invest in a weatherproof cable if you live in an area with extreme weather conditions. Following these tips will help to ensure that your garage door cable does not break in the future.

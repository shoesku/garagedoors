How Much Do Wood Look Garage Doors Cost?

Are you considering updating the look of your home with a new garage door? A popular choice these days is to choose a wood look garage door, which can give your home an updated and modern look. But you might be wondering – how much does a wood look garage door cost?

The cost of a wood look garage door varies greatly depending on the size and style of the door, the material used for the construction, the type of insulation, and the type of opener used. Generally, a good quality, 16’ x 7’ wood look garage door will cost between $2,000 and $3,500.

Factors That Affect the Cost of a Wood Look Garage Door

When considering the cost of a wood look garage door, there are several factors that can affect the price. The size, style, material, and insulation of the door will all affect the cost. Additionally, the type of opener used will also affect the price.

The size of the door will have a direct impact on the cost. The larger the door, the more expensive it will be. Additionally, the style of the door will affect the price. A basic door will be less expensive than a door with more detailed design elements.

The material used for the construction of the door will also have an effect on the cost. Wood look doors are typically made from steel, fiberglass, or vinyl. Steel doors are the most durable, but also the most expensive. Fiberglass and vinyl doors are less expensive, but may not be as durable.

The type of insulation used for the door will also affect the cost. If you choose an insulated door, it will be more expensive than a non-insulated door. Additionally, the type of opener used will affect the cost. Basic openers are less expensive than advanced openers with special features.

Benefits of a Wood Look Garage Door

A wood look garage door can give your home a modern and updated look without the maintenance of a real wood door. Wood look doors are typically made from steel, fiberglass, or vinyl, and are much more durable than real wood doors. Additionally, wood look doors are available in a variety of styles and designs, so you can customize the look of your home.

Wood look doors are also more energy efficient than real wood doors. They are typically insulated, which helps to keep the temperature in your garage more consistent. Additionally, wood look doors are typically more secure than real wood doors, making them a great choice for homeowners who want to increase the security of their home.

Conclusion

If you are considering updating the look of your home with a new garage door, a wood look door may be a great option. The cost of a wood look garage door can vary depending on the size and style of the door, the material used for the construction, the type of insulation, and the type of opener used. Generally, a good quality, 16’ x 7’ wood look garage door will cost between $2,000 and $3,500.

Wood look doors are a great choice for homeowners who want to update the look of their home without the maintenance of a real wood door. Additionally, wood look doors are typically more energy efficient and secure than real wood doors. So, if you are considering a new garage door, a wood look door may be the perfect choice.

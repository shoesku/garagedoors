The Basics of Garage Door Repair

Garage door repair can be a complicated and potentially dangerous process, so it’s important to understand the basics before you get started. The first thing to understand is the different components of a garage door and how they work together. The components include the door panel, the door opener, the track, the springs, and the rollers. Each component has its own role in opening and closing your garage door.

The door panel is the actual door itself. It’s made up of several panels of metal and is usually connected to the door opener. The opener is the motor that lifts and lowers the door panel. The track is the metal track that the door panel slides along when it is being opened or closed. The springs are the metal coils that help to lift and lower the door panel. Finally, the rollers are the small wheels at the base of the door that help to move it along the track.

When the door is opened or closed, the opener sends a signal to the door panel and the springs and rollers help to move it along the track. If any of these components aren’t working properly, it can cause the door to malfunction or not open or close completely. That’s when you need to call in a professional to help you fix the issue.

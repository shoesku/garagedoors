What to Consider When Shopping for a Garage Door

If you’re looking to upgrade or replace your existing garage door, you’ll need to consider a few factors before making a purchase. There are a range of materials and styles available, so you’ll need to determine what’s best for your home and budget. It’s also important to consider the size of the door and the type of opener you’ll need. The cost of garage door installation will also need to be taken into account. In this guide, we’ll take a look at the various factors to consider when shopping for a garage door, as well as the average cost of installation.

Types of Garage Doors

When shopping for a new garage door, you’ll need to decide between a variety of materials and styles. The most common types of garage doors are steel, wood, vinyl, and aluminum. Steel doors are the most popular and economical choice, as they are durable and easy to maintain. Wood doors provide a classic look, but require regular maintenance. Vinyl and aluminum doors are becoming increasingly popular, as they are lightweight, low-maintenance, and more affordable than wood. There are also a variety of styles to choose from, including sectional, carriage house, and traditional.

Size of the Garage Door

In order to get an accurate estimate of the cost of garage door installation, you’ll need to measure the size of the door. Standard single car garage doors are usually 8 feet wide and 7 feet high, but this may vary depending on the size of your car. Double car garage doors are usually 16 feet wide and 7 feet high. If you have an oversized vehicle, you may need to purchase a larger door. It’s important to get the measurements right, as it will affect the cost of installation.

Type of Garage Door Opener

Once you’ve chosen a garage door, you’ll need to decide what type of opener you’d like to use. There are three main types of garage door openers: chain drive, screw drive, and belt drive. Chain drive openers are the most popular and economical, but are noisier than the other two options. Screw drive openers are quieter and more reliable, but tend to cost more. Belt drive openers are the most expensive, but are the quietest and most reliable option. The type of opener you choose will also affect the cost of installation.

Average Cost of Garage Door Installation

The average cost of garage door installation can vary depending on the type of door you choose, the size of the door, and the type of opener you select. Most standard single car garage doors cost around $600-$1000 to install, while double car garage doors cost around $1200-$2000. Additional costs may be incurred if you choose a higher end door and/or opener. If you’re looking for a more affordable option, you can purchase and install a pre-made door and opener combo for around $500-$700.

DIY Garage Door Installation

If you’re looking to save money, you may want to consider installing the garage door yourself. Installing a garage door is a fairly straightforward process, but it does require some basic knowledge of tools and carpentry. If you’re comfortable with basic DIY projects, you can purchase a pre-made door and opener combo from a home improvement store and install it yourself. It’s important to note that this option will require some time and effort, so it may not be the best choice for everyone.

Hire a Professional for Garage Door Installation

If you’re looking for a more reliable, hassle-free option, you may want to consider hiring a professional for garage door installation. Professional installers are experienced and knowledgeable, and will be able to install your door quickly and safely. The cost of professional installation will vary depending on the size and type of door you choose, as well as the type of opener you select. On average, professional installation costs around $1000-$2000.

Conclusion

When shopping for a new garage door, there are a few factors to consider. You’ll need to decide between a variety of materials and styles, as well as measure the size of the door. You’ll also need to decide what type of opener you’d like to use. The cost of garage door installation can vary depending on the type of door and opener you choose, but most standard single car garage doors cost around $600-$1000 to install. If you’re looking to save money, you may want to consider installing the door yourself. Otherwise, you can hire a professional for around $1000-$2000.

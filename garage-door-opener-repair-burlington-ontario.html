The Different Types of Garage Door Openers

Before you hire a garage door opener repair service in Burlington, it’s important to understand the different types of garage door openers. Most people are familiar with the traditional chain-driven openers, which use a metal chain to lift and lower the garage door. However, there are other types of openers, such as belt-drive openers, which use a belt instead of a chain to open the door, and screw-drive openers, which use a threaded rod to lift and lower the door. Each type of opener has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it’s important to do some research and decide which one is right for you.

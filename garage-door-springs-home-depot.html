Types of Garage Door Springs Home Depot Offers

Home Depot offers a wide range of garage door springs, each designed for a specific purpose. The most common types of garage door springs are torsion springs, extension springs, and galvanized springs. Torsion springs are the most popular type of garage door springs and are used for heavier garage doors. Extension springs are designed for lighter garage doors, and galvanized springs are designed for extra durability and long-term use.

How to Choose the Right Garage Door Springs Home Depot Offers

When you are selecting a garage door spring from Home Depot, you need to take into account several factors. First, you need to consider the weight of the door.

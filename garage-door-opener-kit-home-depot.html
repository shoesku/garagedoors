What to Consider Before Buying a Garage Door Opener Kit

Types of Garage Door Opener Kits

When it comes to garage door opener kits, there are two main types to choose from: chain drive and belt drive. Chain drive openers use a metal chain to lift and lower the door, while belt drive openers use a rubber belt for a quieter operation. Both types of openers are reliable and offer different advantages. Chain drive openers are typically more powerful and are better for heavier doors, while belt drive openers are quieter and are better for lighter doors.

Chain Drive Openers

Chain drive openers are the most common type of garage door opener and use a metal chain to lift and lower the door. Chain drive openers are usually the most powerful type of opener, making them the best choice for heavier doors. They are also generally more affordable than other types of opener kits. However, they tend to be louder than belt drive openers, so they may not be the best choice for homes with living spaces close to the garage.

Belt Drive Openers

Belt drive openers use a rubber belt to lift and lower the door. They are quieter than chain drive openers, making them a better choice for homes with living spaces close to the garage. Belt drive openers are also easier to install and tend to be more reliable. However, they tend to be more expensive than other types of opener kits and are not as powerful as chain drive openers, so they may not be the best choice for heavier doors.

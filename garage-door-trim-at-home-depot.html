Why You Should Consider Installing It Yourself

Installing garage door trim yourself can save you time and money. It can be done in a single day and tends to be a relatively simple process. What You Need to Know Before Buying Garage Door Trim

Before you buy garage door trim, there are a few things you should consider. The first is the type of material you would like to use. PVC trim is durable and easy to install, while wood trim can provide a more classic look. You should also consider the type of color and style you would like. There are a variety of colors and styles available, so you should take some time to research your options.

Where to Buy Garage Door Trim at Home Depot

Home Depot carries a wide selection of garage door trim in various colors, styles, and materials. You can find it in the Home Improvement section of the store or online. How to Install Garage Door Trim

Installing the trim yourself is not difficult. You will need to measure and cut the trim to fit your garage door and then use a power drill to attach the trim to the door. You may also need some additional tools, such as a saw, tape measure, and level. If you are unsure about the process, you can always refer to the instructions that come with the trim or speak to a Home Depot associate.

Garage Door Trim Maintenance

Once the trim is installed, you will need to maintain it. If you have wood trim, it should be sanded and sealed every few years to prevent it from rotting. PVC trim can be washed with mild soap and water to keep it looking its best. You should also check the trim regularly for any signs of damage or wear and tear.

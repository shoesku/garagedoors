How Much Does A 16 X 7 Garage Door Cost?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

If you are looking to buy a garage door for your home, you may be wondering how much a 16 x 7 garage door costs. This is an important question to answer before you make a purchase, and there are several factors that will affect the total cost. In this article, we will take a look at the different costs associated with a 16 x 7 garage door, so you can make an informed decision.

Material Cost

The first factor that will affect the cost of a 16 x 7 garage door is the type of material used. Generally speaking, garage doors are made from either wood, steel, aluminum, or vinyl. Each of these materials has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to consider your needs before making a decision. Wood is the most expensive option, but it is also the most durable, and offers the most customization options. Steel is usually the most common type of garage door, and is very durable and affordable. Aluminum is lightweight and durable, but may not offer as much customization. Vinyl is the least expensive option, but it is not as durable as the other materials.

Installation Cost

Installation is another factor that will affect the cost of a 16 x 7 garage door. Generally speaking, if you are buying a door from a retail store, the installation cost will be included in the price. However, if you are purchasing from a professional installer, you can expect to pay an additional fee for the installation. Professional installers will usually charge an hourly rate, so the total cost will depend on the complexity of the job and the experience of the installer.

Additional Costs

In addition to the material and installation costs, there are also other costs to consider when purchasing a 16 x 7 garage door. For instance, you may need to purchase additional hardware, such as hinges, springs, and tracks. You may also need to purchase a remote control or keypad for automatic door openers. Additionally, if you are replacing an existing door, you may need to pay for removal and disposal of the old door.

Average Cost

The average cost of a 16 x 7 garage door varies depending on the material used, installation costs, and any additional costs. Generally speaking, you can expect to pay anywhere from $500 to $1,500 for a basic door. However, if you are looking for a more custom door, you may need to pay more. Additionally, if you are purchasing a door from a professional installer, you should expect to pay an additional fee for the installation.

Conclusion

Overall, the cost of a 16 x 7 garage door will depend on several factors, including the material used, installation costs, and any additional costs. Wood is the most expensive option, but it is also the most durable and offers the most customization options. Steel is usually the most common type of garage door, and is very durable and affordable. Aluminum is lightweight and durable, but may not offer as much customization. Vinyl is the least expensive option, but it is not as durable as the other materials. Ultimately, the best way to determine the cost of a 16 x 7 garage door is to contact a professional installer for an estimate.

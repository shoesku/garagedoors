Everything You Need To Know About Garage Door Torsion Spring Replacement In 2023

Garage door torsion springs are an integral part of your garage door system. The torsion springs are responsible for balancing the weight of the door and counterbalancing it when it is opened or closed. If your garage door torsion springs are not functioning properly or have worn out, it is important to replace them as soon as possible. Replacing the torsion springs on your garage door can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and knowledge, it can be done relatively quickly and easily.

What to Look for in Quality Torsion Springs

When it comes to replacing your garage door torsion springs, you want to make sure you are purchasing quality springs. The springs should be made from durable steel and have a minimum of 10,000 cycles before they need to be replaced. Additionally, you should look for springs that are coated with a corrosion resistant finish, as this will help them last longer and resist the elements. The springs should also be properly tensioned, so they can provide the right balance and counterbalance when the door is opened and closed.

The Tools You Need for Replacing Torsion Springs

When replacing your garage door torsion springs, you will need a few basic tools. These include an adjustable wrench, a drill, and a screwdriver. You will also need a ladder and a pair of pliers. Additionally, you may need to purchase a torsion spring winding tool, which can help you tension the springs properly. Lastly, you may want to invest in a pair of safety glasses, as this will help protect your eyes when you are working on the springs.

Safety Tips for Replacing Torsion Springs

When replacing your garage door torsion springs, safety is of the utmost importance. It is important to wear protective clothing and eye protection, as the springs can be dangerous if not handled properly. Additionally, you should make sure that the door is disconnected from the opener and locked open with a vise grip. This will help prevent the door from falling and potentially injuring you if the springs snap. Lastly, it is important to make sure that the springs are properly tensioned, as this will help ensure that the door is balanced and counterbalanced when it is opened and closed.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Replacing Torsion Springs

Once you have all the necessary tools and safety precautions in place, the process of replacing your garage door torsion springs is fairly straightforward. First, you will need to detach the garage door from the opener and lock it open with a vise grip. Next, you will need to remove the torsion springs from the door by loosening the screws and removing the springs. Once the springs are removed, you can then measure the diameter, wire size, and length of the new springs. After the new springs are measured, you can then install them in the correct locations and tension them using the winding tool. Lastly, you can reconnect the door to the opener and test it to ensure it is working properly.

Conclusion

Replacing your garage door torsion springs is an important task that should be done regularly and correctly. By following the steps outlined above, you can ensure that your springs are replaced safely and correctly, and that your garage door is functioning properly. Additionally, by investing in quality torsion springs, you can help ensure that they will last longer and provide a better balance and counterbalance when the door is opened and closed.

Types of Garage Door Hardware

Garage door hardware is essential for keeping your garage door functioning properly. There are a variety of different types of garage door hardware available, including hinges, springs, tracks, and rollers. The type of hardware you need depends on the type of garage door you have. Menards carries a wide selection of garage door hardware, so you’re sure to find what you need.

Garage Door Hinges

Garage door hinges are used to attach the door to the frame. There are several types of garage door hinges available, including strap hinges, butt hinges, and T-hinges. The type of hinge you need depends on the size and weight of your door. Menards carries a wide selection of garage door hinges in a variety of sizes, styles, and finishes.

Garage Door Springs

Garage door springs are used to counterbalance the weight of the door. There are two types of garage door springs: torsion springs and extension springs. Torsion springs are used for heavier doors, while extension springs are used for lighter doors. Menards carries a wide selection of garage door springs in a variety of sizes and styles.

Garage Door Tracks

Garage door tracks are used to guide the door as it opens and closes. There are several types of garage door tracks available, including curved tracks, straight tracks, and single-track systems. The type of track you need depends on the type of garage door you have. Menards carries a wide selection of garage door tracks in a variety of sizes and styles.

Garage Door Rollers

Garage door rollers are used to keep the door in place as it moves along the track. There are several types of rollers available, including steel rollers, nylon rollers, and ball-bearing rollers. The type of roller you need depends on the type of track you have. Menards carries a wide selection of garage door rollers in a variety of sizes and styles.

Garage Door Openers

Garage door openers are used to open and close the door. There are several types of garage door openers available, including chain-drive openers, belt-drive openers, and screw-drive openers. The type of opener you need depends on the type of garage door you have. Menards carries a wide selection of garage door openers in a variety of sizes and styles.

Garage Door Accessories

Garage door accessories are used to customize and improve the look and function of your garage door. There are several types of garage door accessories available, including locks, handles, and decorative trim. The type of accessory you need depends on the type of garage door you have. Menards carries a wide selection of garage door accessories in a variety of sizes and styles.

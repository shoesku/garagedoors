Overview of the Different Types of Garage Doors

Garage doors come in a wide variety of styles and designs and can be crafted from many different materials, including wood, aluminum, steel, and even fiberglass. No matter what type of home you have, there is a garage door type that will match it perfectly. To help you make a decision about which type of garage door you should choose, here is an overview of the different types of garage doors and their images.

Sectional Garage Doors

Sectional garage doors are one of the most popular types of garage doors and are commonly chosen for their convenience, safety, and style. Sectional garage doors are made up of several horizontal panels that are connected to one another, allowing them to move up and down on rollers. Sectional garage doors can be opened either manually or electronically, with the latter being more preferred for the enhanced convenience and safety. Sectional garage doors are commonly made of steel, aluminum, or wood and come in a variety of colors and finishes. Images of sectional garage doors can be found online and in home improvement stores.

Roll-Up Garage Doors

Roll-up garage doors are another popular type of garage door. They are made of a single panel that rolls up and down on tracks. They are usually made of steel, aluminum, or wood and come in a variety of colors and finishes. Roll-up garage doors are typically opened manually, but they can be installed with an electronic opener. Images of roll-up garage doors can be found online and in home improvement stores.

Carriage House Garage Doors

Carriage house garage doors are a unique type of garage door that adds a touch of classic charm to any home. Carriage house garage doors are made of wood and feature a traditional design with panels, windows, and decorative hardware. Carriage house garage doors are typically opened manually, but they can be installed with an electronic opener. Images of carriage house garage doors can be found online and in home improvement stores.

Modern Garage Doors

Modern garage doors are a great way to add a contemporary touch to any home. Modern garage doors are usually made of aluminum or steel and feature sleek, minimalist designs. They can be opened manually or electronically and come in a variety of colors and finishes. Images of modern garage doors can be found online and in home improvement stores.

Glass Garage Doors

Glass garage doors are becoming increasingly popular due to their modern style and ability to let light into the garage. Glass garage doors are usually made of aluminum or steel and feature large glass panels. They can be opened manually or electronically and come in a variety of colors and finishes. Images of glass garage doors can be found online and in home improvement stores.

Fiberglass Garage Doors

Fiberglass garage doors are a great choice for those looking for a durable and low-maintenance option. Fiberglass garage doors are made of a strong, lightweight material and are naturally resistant to rust and corrosion. They can be opened manually or electronically and come in a variety of colors and finishes. Images of fiberglass garage doors can be found online and in home improvement stores.

Conclusion

No matter what type of home you have, there is a garage door type that will match it perfectly. From sectional garage doors to modern garage doors, glass garage doors to fiberglass garage doors, there are plenty of images to choose from. Take the time to research the different types of garage doors and the images available online and in home improvement stores to find the perfect fit for your home.

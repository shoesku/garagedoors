What Are Roller Garage Doors?

Roller garage doors are a type of garage door that opens using a roll-up system. They are made from a single panel of steel, aluminium or fibre glass that rolls up around a barrel or drum when opened. Roller garage doors are a popular choice for many homeowners looking to replace their existing garage door, due to their simple operation and low price.

Roller doors are usually operated with a wall-mounted switch or remote control, and use a counterweight system for easy and smooth operation. They are usually fitted with a lock or security device to ensure that the garage is secure when closed.

The Benefits of Roller Garage Doors

Roller garage doors are a great choice for those looking to replace an existing garage door, due to their low cost and easy operation. They are generally cheaper than other types of garage door, such as sectional doors or up and over doors, and can be installed relatively quickly.

Roller doors also have a number of other benefits. For example, they are usually quieter than other types of garage door, thanks to their counterweight system, which means that they won't disturb the neighbours when you open and close it.

Roller doors also require less space to open and close than other types of door. This means that you can use the space inside your garage more effectively, as you won't need to leave space for the door to open. Roller doors are also a great choice for those with limited space, as they open vertically, rather than taking up floor space.

Cost of Roller Garage Doors in the UK

The cost of roller garage doors varies depending on a number of factors, including the size and type of door, the material it is made from and the installation costs. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from £400 to £1600 for a single roller garage door, with installation costs ranging from around £200 to £400.

It is important to bear in mind that the cost of a roller garage door also depends on the type of material it is made from. Aluminium and steel doors tend to be the most affordable, while fibre glass and wood doors are usually more expensive. It is also important to consider the cost of any additional features, such as security locks or remote controls.

Where to Buy Roller Garage Doors in the UK

Roller garage doors can be bought from a range of retailers in the UK, including hardware stores, DIY stores, online retailers and specialist garage door suppliers. When shopping for a new garage door, it is important to consider the quality of the product and the reputation of the supplier.

It is also important to compare prices from different suppliers, as the cost of roller garage doors can vary greatly. It is also worth checking if the supplier offers installation services, as this can be an additional cost.

Tips for Choosing a Roller Garage Door

When choosing a roller garage door, it is important to consider the size and type of door you require. It is also important to consider the material the door is made from and the level of security it offers. It is worth considering the cost of installation, as this can add to the overall cost of the door.

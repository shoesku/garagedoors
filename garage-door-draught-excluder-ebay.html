Garage door draught excluders ebay come in a variety of shapes and sizes, so it is important to choose the right one for your home. There are two main types of draught excluders available: strip and brush. Strip excluders are designed to be placed at the top of the garage door and are usually made from plastic or metal. Brush excluders are designed to be placed under the garage door and are usually made from brush or rubber.

When choosing a draught excluder, it is important to consider the type of garage door you have. If you have a single-panelled garage door, then a strip excluder is the best option. If you have a double-panelled garage door, then a brush excluder may be more suitable. It is also important to check the size of the gap between the door and the frame to ensure the excluder will fit correctly.

Once you have chosen the right draught excluder, it is important to install it correctly.

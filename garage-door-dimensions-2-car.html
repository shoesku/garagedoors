Garage Door Dimensions For A 2-Car Garage

If you are considering a two-car garage for your home, you should know the proper garage door dimensions. A two-car garage should have a minimum of 16-feet wide and 7-feet tall door. This will ensure that two cars can fit inside without any issues. But, depending on the type of vehicle, the size of the garage door may need to be larger.

Types of Garage Doors

The size of your garage door will depend on the type of garage door you choose. There are several types of garage doors available, including: sliding doors, overhead doors, side-hinged doors, and roll-up doors. Each type has its own unique set of dimensions and should be considered when selecting a garage door.

Sliding Garage Doors

Sliding garage doors are a great option for a two-car garage. These types of doors are typically made of wood or metal and slide along a track. The typical width of a sliding garage door is 16-feet and the standard height is 7-feet. However, depending on the size of your vehicles, you may need to select a wider door.

Overhead Garage Doors

Overhead garage doors are one of the most popular options and are typically made of wood or steel. They are operated by an automated motor and open vertically. Most overhead doors are 8-feet wide and 7-feet tall. However, you can find options that are larger or smaller depending on your needs.

Side-Hinged Garage Doors

Side-hinged garage doors are perfect for those who prefer a more traditional look. These types of doors are typically made of wood and open outward. The standard size for a side-hinged garage door is 16-feet wide and 7-feet tall. However, depending on the size of your vehicles, it may be necessary to select a wider door.

Roll-Up Garage Doors

Roll-up garage doors are becoming increasingly popular due to their convenience and versatility. These doors are typically made of steel and roll up into a cylinder above the garage opening. The standard size for a roll-up garage door is 16-feet wide and 7-feet tall. However, depending on the size of your vehicles, you may need to select a wider door.

Conclusion

When selecting a garage door for a two-car garage, it is important to consider the size and type of garage door you need. Sliding, overhead, side-hinged, and roll-up garage doors all have their own unique set of dimensions. The standard size for a two-car garage is 16-feet wide and 7-feet tall. However, depending on the size of your vehicles, you may need to select a wider door.

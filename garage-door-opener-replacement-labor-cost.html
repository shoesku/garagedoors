How Much Does It Cost To Replace A Garage Door Opener In 2023?

Replacing a garage door opener is a relatively simple task, but there are a few factors that can affect the overall cost. The average cost for a garage door opener replacement in 2023 is between $200 and $400, depending on the type, brand, and features you select. This cost includes the cost of installation plus any additional parts or labor that may be needed.

What Type of Garage Door Opener is Right For You?

When it comes to selecting the right type of garage door opener for your home, there are several factors to consider. The two most popular types of garage door openers are chain-drive and belt-drive openers. Chain-drive openers work by using a chain to open and close the garage door, whereas belt-drive openers use a rubber belt to do the same. Chain-drive openers are generally more affordable, while belt-drive openers are usually quieter and smoother.

Cost of Labor for Garage Door Opener Replacement

The cost of labor for a garage door opener replacement will depend on the complexity of the job and the location of the job site. If you have an older model opener, the cost of labor can be as low as $50, depending on the amount of time it takes to install and connect. For newer models, labor costs can range from $100 to $200 or more. It’s important to note that most professional garage door opener installers charge by the hour, so the cost of labor may vary depending on how long it takes to complete the job.

Additional Parts and Labor Needed For Garage Door Opener Replacement

If you are replacing an old garage door opener, you may need to purchase additional parts to complete the job. Common parts that may need to be replaced include the torsion spring, opener arm, and roller brackets. These parts can cost anywhere from $20 to $100 or more, depending on the type and brand of the parts. In addition, you may need to hire an electrician or handyman to run wiring for the opener if you are installing a newer model. This can cost anywhere from $50 to $200 depending on the complexity of the job.

Tips for Finding the Best Price for Garage Door Opener Replacement Labor

When it comes to finding the best price for garage door opener replacement labor, it’s important to shop around and get quotes from several different companies. You can check with local garage door repair companies as well as home improvement stores to get the best price. Be sure to inquire about any additional parts that may be needed and the cost of labor for the installation of those parts. Additionally, you may want to ask if the company offers any discounts or promotions that can help you save money on the total cost of the job.

Conclusion

Replacing a garage door opener can be a relatively simple task, but it’s important to factor in the cost of labor and any additional parts that may be needed. The cost of labor and parts can vary greatly depending on the type and brand of opener you select. Be sure to shop around for the best price and check for any discounts or promotions that can help you save money on the total cost of the job. If you have any questions, it’s best to consult with a professional garage door repair company for more information.

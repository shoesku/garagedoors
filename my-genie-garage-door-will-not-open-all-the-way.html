What To Do When Your Genie Garage Door Will Not Open All The Way?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Having a functioning garage door is important for many reasons. It ensures that your vehicle and any other items that you store in your garage are safe and secure. It also helps to increase the value of your home. When your Genie garage door will not open all the way, it can be a frustrating experience. Fortunately, there are a few things that you can do in order to get your garage door working properly again.

Check the Remote Control

The first thing that you should do is check the remote control. It is possible that the batteries may have become drained or the remote control may have been damaged in some way. If the batteries have become drained, it is important to replace them with new ones. If the remote control is damaged, you may need to purchase a new one.

Check the Sensors

Another important thing to check is the sensors. The sensors are responsible for detecting obstacles and preventing the door from closing. If the sensors are not functioning properly, the door will not open all the way. It is important to check the sensors regularly in order to ensure that they are functioning properly and are not blocked in any way.

Check the Tracks

The tracks are responsible for guiding the door up and down. If the tracks are blocked or damaged, the door will not open all the way. It is important to check the tracks regularly in order to ensure that they are functioning properly and are not blocked in any way. If the tracks are blocked, it is important to remove the blockage in order for the door to be able to open all the way.

Check the Springs

The springs are responsible for providing the necessary tension to open and close the door. If the springs are worn or damaged, the door will not open all the way. It is important to check the springs regularly in order to ensure that they are functioning properly and are not damaged in any way. If the springs are damaged, it is important to replace them in order for the door to be able to open all the way.

Check the Motor

The motor is responsible for providing the necessary power to open and close the door. If the motor is not functioning properly, the door will not open all the way. It is important to check the motor regularly in order to ensure that it is functioning properly and is not damaged in any way. If the motor is damaged, it is important to replace it in order for the door to be able to open all the way.

Check the Cables

The cables are responsible for providing the necessary tension to open and close the door. If the cables are worn or damaged, the door will not open all the way. It is important to check the cables regularly in order to ensure that they are functioning properly and are not damaged in any way. If the cables are damaged, it is important to replace them in order for the door to be able to open all the way.

Check the Limit Switches

The limit switches are responsible for detecting when the door has reached its fully open or closed position. If the limit switches are not functioning properly, the door will not open all the way. It is important to check the limit switches regularly in order to ensure that they are functioning properly and are not damaged in any way. If the limit switches are damaged, it is important to replace them in order for the door to be able to open all the way.

Check the Rollers

The rollers are responsible for providing the necessary support for the door as it opens and closes. If the rollers are worn or damaged, the door will not open all the way. It is important to check the rollers regularly in order to ensure that they are functioning properly and are not damaged in any way. If the rollers are damaged, it is important to replace them in order for the door to be able to open all the way.

Call a Professional

If you are still having trouble with your garage door not opening all the way, it is important to call a professional. A professional technician can inspect the door and determine what the issue is. They can also provide you with solutions on how to fix the issue. It is important to have a professional look at your door in order to ensure that it is functioning properly and safely.

Conclusion

If your Genie garage door will not open all the way, there are a few things that you can do in order to get it working properly again. It is important to check the remote control, sensors, tracks, springs, motor, cables, limit switches, and rollers in order to ensure that they are functioning properly and are not blocked or damaged in any way. If you are still having trouble, it is important to call a professional in order to get the issue resolved.

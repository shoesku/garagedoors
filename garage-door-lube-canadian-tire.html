The garage door is an integral part of your home. It can also be one of the most neglected parts of your home in terms of maintenance. It is important to keep your garage door well lubricated to ensure it works properly and lasts longer. Canadian Tire has a variety of garage door lubes to help you get the job done quickly and easily.

Why is it Important to Lube Your Garage Door?

Lubricating your garage door is important to ensure it continues to operate smoothly and quietly. A well-lubricated garage door will open and close easily, and help to prevent wear and tear on the moving parts. It will help to extend the life of your garage door and keep it working smoothly for years to come. If your garage door is making strange noises when it opens and closes, it may need lubrication.

What Type of Lube is Best for Garage Doors?

The type of lube you use for your garage door will depend on the type of door you have. Canadian Tire has a variety of lubes for different types of garage doors. For metal garage doors, you can use a light machine oil. For wooden garage doors, you can use a light mineral oil. If your garage door is made of plastic, you can use a silicone-based lubricant.

How Often Should You Lube Your Garage Door?

How often you need to lubricate your garage door will depend on the type of door you have and how often you use it. If your garage door is used often, you should lubricate it at least twice a year. If you have a wooden door, you should lubricate it once a year. If your garage door is rarely used, you can get away with lubricating it once every two to three years.

Where Can I Buy Garage Door Lube?

Canadian Tire is an excellent place to buy garage door lube. They have a wide selection of lubes for both metal and wooden garage doors. They also carry a variety of lubricants for plastic garage doors. Canadian Tire offers competitive prices, so you can be sure to find a lube that fits your budget.

How to Lube Your Garage Door

Lubricating your garage door is relatively simple. First, you will need to remove any dirt and debris from the garage door components. Then, using a cloth or paper towel, apply the lube to the moving parts of the door. Make sure to lube any hinges, rollers, and tracks. Finally, test the door to make sure it is operating smoothly.

Tips for Lubricating Your Garage Door

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your lube and keep your garage door running smoothly:

  • Make sure to use the right type of lube for your garage door.
  • Don’t use too much lube – a little goes a long way.
  • Always keep a clean rag handy to wipe away any excess lube.
  • Be sure to lubricate all of the moving parts of your garage door.
  • Check the lube levels every few months to make sure they are sufficient.

Conclusion

Keeping your garage door well lubricated is an important part of maintaining it. Canadian Tire has a wide selection of lubes for all types of garage doors. Lubricating your garage door is relatively simple, and can help to keep your door working smoothly for years to come. Make sure to use the right type of lube, and check the lube levels every few months.

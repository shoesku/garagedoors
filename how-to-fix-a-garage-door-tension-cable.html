How To Fix A Garage Door Tension Cable

Having a garage door that isn't working properly can be a huge inconvenience. But one of the most common problems is a broken tension cable. Luckily, a broken tension cable is relatively easy to fix, and you can do it yourself in just a few simple steps. Here's what you need to know about how to repair a broken garage door tension cable.

What is a Garage Door Tension Cable?

A garage door tension cable is a key component of your garage door’s operation. It is a metal wire that runs along the length of the door and is connected to the top and bottom of the opening. The tension cable is responsible for counterbalancing the weight of the garage door and helping it open and close smoothly. When the tension cable is broken, the door will not be able to function correctly.

Why is the Cable Broken?

The tension cable is typically made of metal, which means it is prone to rust and corrosion over time. This can cause the cable to become frayed and eventually break. In some cases, the cable can also be damaged due to improper installation or a malfunctioning door opener. In any case, if the cable is broken, it needs to be replaced as soon as possible.

How to Fix a Garage Door Tension Cable

Replacing a broken tension cable is a relatively simple process, but it is important to follow the instructions carefully to ensure that the job is done correctly. Here are the basic steps for replacing a garage door tension cable:

Step One: Prepare the Door

Before you start, make sure to unplug the garage door opener and disconnect the power to the door. This will prevent the door from accidentally opening while you are working on it. Next, you'll need to raise the door and secure it in place with a pair of vice grips. This will allow you to work on the cable without it getting in the way.

Step Two: Remove the Old Cable

Once the door is secure, you can begin to remove the old cable. Start by loosening the screws that hold the cable in place. Once the screws are loose, you can carefully remove the cable from the door. Be sure to keep track of where the cable was connected so that you can reattach the new cable in the correct place.

Step Three: Attach the New Cable

Now that the old cable has been removed, you can attach the new cable. Start by threading the cable through the pulleys at the top and bottom of the door. Make sure the cable is tight and secure. Once the cable is in place, you can tighten the screws to hold the cable in place.

Step Four: Test the Door

Once the cable is in place, you can test the door to make sure it is working properly. Plug in the garage door opener and open and close the door a few times to make sure the cable is working properly. If the door is working correctly, you can unplug the opener and reconnect the power to the door.

Conclusion

Replacing a broken garage door tension cable doesn't have to be a difficult process. With the right tools and some basic knowledge, you can fix the problem yourself in just a few simple steps. So if your garage door is in need of some repair, don't hesitate to take matters into your own hands and replace the tension cable yourself.

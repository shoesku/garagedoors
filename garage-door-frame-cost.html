What is a Garage Door Frame?

A garage door frame is the part of the garage door that is installed into the opening of the garage wall. It provides a sturdy and durable frame around the door, which can protect it from weather and other elements. In addition, it helps to keep the door secure and in place. The frame of the garage door is usually made of metal or wood and can come in a variety of sizes, styles, and colors.

What Does It Cost To Have An Electric Garage Door Fitted In 2023

Electric garage doors are a great way to add convenience and security to your home. The cost of having an electric garage door fitted in 2023 will vary depending on the complexity of the installation, the type of door you choose, and the brand. Some of the factors that will influence the cost of having an electric garage door fitted in 2023 include:

Size

The size of your garage door will have a major impact on the cost of the installation. In general, the larger the garage door, the more expensive the installation will be. If you have a small or slightly larger garage door, the cost of installation may be lower.

Style

There are a variety of different styles of electric garage doors that you can choose from, including roller doors, sectional doors, and tilt doors. Each of these styles of electric garage doors can have a different cost, depending on the complexity of the installation. Generally, roller and tilt doors are the most expensive, while sectional doors are the least expensive.

Brand

The brand of electric garage door you choose will also impact the cost of the installation. Different brands of electric garage doors have different features and will require different levels of installation complexity. High-end brands of electric garage doors may require more expensive installation fees than more basic models.

Labor

The cost of having an electric garage door fitted in 2023 will also include the labor costs associated with the installation. The complexity of the installation and the type of electric garage door you choose will have an impact on the labor costs. Generally, more complex installations will require more labor, and thus will cost more.

Additional Costs

In addition to the cost of the electric garage door and the labor costs associated with the installation, there may also be additional costs to consider. These can include the cost of any additional hardware, such as tracks, hinges, and remote controls. Additionally, there may be additional costs associated with the installation, such as the cost of professional inspection and testing.

DIY or Professional Installation

The cost of having an electric garage door fitted in 2023 can also be impacted by whether you choose to do the installation yourself or have it professionally installed. Generally, professional installation will cost more than a DIY installation, as the professional will need to factor in labor costs. However, if you are not confident in your DIY skills or if the installation is particularly complex, it is often best to have it professionally installed to ensure it is done correctly.

Other Considerations

When considering the cost of having an electric garage door fitted in 2023, it is important to factor in any additional costs that may be associated with the installation. This can include the cost of any additional hardware needed, the cost of professional inspection and testing, as well as any additional labor costs. Additionally, it is important to make sure you are getting a good quality electric garage door that will last for many years.

Conclusion

The cost of having an electric garage door fitted in 2023 will vary depending on a variety of factors, including the size, style, and brand of the door, as well as the complexity of the installation. Additionally, there may be additional costs associated with the installation, such as additional hardware, professional inspection and testing, and labor costs. It is important to factor in all of these costs when considering the total cost of the installation.

Deciding Whether To Install One Or Two Garage Door Springs

What Is A Garage Door Spring?

Garage door springs are an integral part of a functioning garage door. They are large springs that are attached to the garage door and help to open and close it. The springs are usually made of steel and have a long life span of up to 10,000 cycles. They are usually either single or double springs, depending on the weight of the door. The heavier the door, the more springs it will need.

When Do You Need Two Springs?

If the weight of your garage door is more than 600 pounds, you will likely need two garage door springs. This is because the single spring will not be able to bear the weight and may break, causing the door to crash. This could potentially be dangerous and cause damage to your property. Therefore, if you have a heavy garage door, it is important to install two springs.

How To Determine The Weight Of Your Garage Door

There are a few different ways to determine the weight of your garage door. One method is to measure the door from the bottom to the top. This should give you a rough idea of the weight. Another option is to measure the width and height of the door and use a calculator to determine the weight. If you are unsure about the weight of your garage door, it is best to consult a professional.

Benefits Of Installing Two Springs

Installing two garage door springs has several benefits. Firstly, it increases the life span of the door, as the two springs will be able to bear the weight of the door more evenly. Secondly, it reduces the amount of stress on the door as the springs will be able to open and close the door more easily. Finally, it makes the door more secure and less vulnerable to damage as the two springs will be able to hold the door in place more securely.

How To Install Two Springs?

Installing two garage door springs is a relatively straightforward process. Firstly, you will need to measure the width and height of your garage door. Then, you will need to purchase two springs of the appropriate size. Next, you will need to attach the springs to the door. Finally, you will need to adjust the tension of the springs so that the door is able to open and close easily.

Maintenance Of Two Springs

Like all parts of a garage door, garage door springs require regular maintenance and care. It is important to check the springs regularly for any signs of wear and tear. If the springs seem to be over-used or damaged, it is important to replace them as soon as possible. It is also important to lubricate the springs regularly to ensure that they are able to open and close the door smoothly.

Conclusion

Deciding whether to install one or two garage door springs is largely dependent on the weight of the door. If the weight of the door is more than 600 pounds, it is important to install two springs in order to ensure that the door is able to open and close safely. Installing two springs will also increase the life span of the door and make it more secure. Finally, it is important to maintain the springs regularly to ensure that they are able to open and close the door smoothly.

The True Value Of Your Garage Door Cable

If you’re looking to boost the value of your home and make your garage door more secure and efficient, then you should consider investing in a new garage door cable. Garage door cables are essential components of any garage door system and are typically used to connect the torsion springs to the cables and pulleys. When you install a new cable, it not only improves the overall performance of the garage door but can also make your home more secure.

Benefits of Installing a New Cable

A new garage door cable can bring about a wide range of benefits. Here are some of the most important ones:

  • Improved Security: A new cable can help make your garage door more secure and reduce the risk of a break-in. If you’re worried about the security of your garage, then a new cable can be a great way to further protect it.
  • Increased Efficiency: New cables are able to withstand more tension and pressure, meaning your garage door will open and close faster and with less effort. This can also reduce the strain on the motor, resulting in a longer lifespan.
  • Peace of Mind: A new cable can help ensure that your garage door is working correctly and safely. This can give you peace of mind knowing that your garage is secure.

Cost of Installing a New Cable

The cost of installing a new garage door cable will depend on the size and type of cable you need. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $100 to $400 for a new cable. You may also need to hire a professional to install the cable, which can further increase the cost. However, if you’re looking to boost the value of your home and make your garage door more secure and efficient, then the cost of a new cable is worth it.

Tips for Choosing the Right Cable

When choosing a new garage door cable, there are a few things you should keep in mind. Here are some tips for choosing the right cable:

  • Choose a cable that is the right size for your garage door. If you choose a cable that is too big or too small, it will not work correctly.
  • Choose a cable that is designed to withstand the weight of your garage door. The heavier your garage door is, the more tension the cable will need to be able to withstand.
  • Choose a cable that is made of durable material. The cable should be able to withstand extreme temperatures and moisture.
  • Choose a cable that is easy to install. If you’re not comfortable installing the cable yourself, then you should hire a professional to do it.

Caring for Your New Cable

Once you’ve installed your new cable, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for caring for your new cable:

  • Inspect the cable regularly for signs of wear and tear. Look for frays, cracks, or any other damage.
  • Check the tension of the cable regularly. If the tension is too low, it can cause the cable to stretch or snap.
  • Lubricate the cable regularly to prevent rust and corrosion.
  • Check the pulleys and springs for signs of wear and tear. If they’re worn, they may need to be replaced.

The True Value of Your Garage Door Cable

Installing a new garage door cable can bring about a range of benefits. It can improve the security of your home, increase the efficiency of your garage door, and give you peace of mind. The cost of a new cable can range from $100 to $400, depending on the size and type of cable you need. When choosing a new cable, make sure to consider the size, weight, and material of the cable. And don’t forget to care for your new cable by inspecting it regularly and lubricating it to prevent rust and corrosion.

Are you thinking of upgrading your existing garage door, or installing a new one? If so, you may be wondering how much garage doors cost in New Zealand. In this article, we will discuss the various factors that affect the cost of garage doors in New Zealand, as well as the average cost of different types of garage doors.

When it comes to garage doors, the cost can vary significantly depending on the type of door you choose, the size of the door, and the material it is made from. Steel doors are usually the most affordable option, and are generally the most popular choice for residential garages. They are also the most durable and require the least maintenance. The cost of a steel garage door will depend on the size and type of door you choose, as well as any additional features you may want, such as insulation or a motorized opener.

Wooden garage doors are a popular choice for those who want a more aesthetically pleasing option. However, they are more expensive than steel doors, as they require more maintenance and are less durable. The cost of a wooden garage door will depend on the type of wood you choose, the size of the door, and any additional features you may want, such as a motorized opener.

Aluminum garage doors are also an option, but they are not as popular as steel or wooden doors. They are also more expensive than other types of garage doors, as they require more maintenance and are less durable. The cost of an aluminum garage door will depend on the size and type of door you choose, as well as any additional features you may want.

Vinyl garage doors are becoming increasingly popular in New Zealand due to their low cost and low maintenance. However, they are not as durable as other types of garage doors, so they may not be the best option for those who want a long-lasting door. The cost of a vinyl garage door will depend on the size and type of door you choose, as well as any additional features you may want.

Motorized openers can be added to any garage door, and this will add to the overall cost. A motorized opener is a great way to add convenience and security to your garage, but it will also add to the cost of the door. The cost of a motorized opener will depend on the type you choose, as well as any additional features you may want.

The cost of installation and labour will also affect the overall cost of a garage door. Installation costs vary depending on the type of door you choose and the complexity of the job. It is important to note that the cost of installation and labour will be higher for custom-made doors. Labour costs also vary depending on the complexity of the job and the experience of the installer.

Average Cost of Garage Doors in New Zealand

The average cost of a garage door in New Zealand will depend on the type of door you choose, the size of the door, and the features you want. Steel garage doors are usually the most affordable option, and are generally the most popular choice for residential garages.

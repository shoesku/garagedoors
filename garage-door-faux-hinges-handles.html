What are Faux Hinges Handles?

Faux hinges handles are decorative handles that are designed to look like real hinges. They are usually made of metal, but can also be made of plastic or other materials. Faux hinges handles are often used to add a decorative touch to a garage door. They can be used to add a unique look and help to keep the door secure.

Types of Faux Hinges Handles

Faux hinges handles come in a variety of styles and designs. The most common type is the round or oval handle. This type is usually made of metal and is often found on commercial doors. Advantages of Faux Hinges Handles

Faux hinges handles have a number of advantages. First, they can add a unique look to a door. They can also help to secure the door by providing additional support. Additionally, they are usually more affordable than real hinges and can be easily replaced if they become damaged or worn.

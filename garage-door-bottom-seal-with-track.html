What is a Garage Door Bottom Seal?

A garage door bottom seal is an important component of your garage door system. It is used to seal the gap between the bottom of the door and the ground, preventing drafts and other elements from entering your garage. The seal also helps to keep moisture out, providing additional insulation against the weather. The seal is typically made of rubber or vinyl and attaches to the bottom of the door with either an adhesive or a track.

Garage Door Insulation Foil: In Or Out?

When it comes to protecting your garage from the elements, insulation is key. Insulation can help keep the temperature and humidity levels inside the garage stable, reducing energy costs and keeping your car and other stored items in top condition. One of the most popular methods of insulating a garage door is with a foil backing, also known as “bubble wrap”. But is it better to have the foil on the inside or outside of the door?

Insulating Your Garage Door

Insulating a garage door isn’t just about keeping the temperature and humidity levels stable, although that’s an important part of it. It’s also about protecting the door itself from damage. A well-insulated door is less likely to suffer from rust, warping, or general wear and tear. In addition, it can help reduce noise, both from outside and inside the garage.

When it comes to materials, there are a few options for insulating a garage door. The most popular is bubble wrap, also known as insulation foil. This type of insulation is made up of tiny bubbles of air, which act as a barrier between the inside and outside of the garage. It’s relatively easy to install, and it’s also quite affordable.

Foil On the Inside or Outside?

One of the questions that many people have when it comes to installing insulation foil on their garage door is whether it should be placed on the inside or the outside. The answer to this question will depend on a few factors, including the climate and the type of door you have.

If you live in a cold climate, it’s generally better to have the insulation on the inside of the door. This will help to keep the cold air out and the warm air in, reducing the amount of energy you need to heat your garage. On the other hand, if you live in a hot climate, it’s best to have the insulation on the outside. This will help keep the hot air out and the cool air in, reducing your need for air conditioning.

The type of door you have will also play a role in determining where the insulation should be placed. If you have a steel door, the foil should go on the inside. If you have a wood door, the insulation should go on the outside.

Other Considerations

In addition to climate and door type, there are a few other things you should consider when deciding where to place your insulation. For example, if your door is exposed to direct sunlight, it’s best to have the insulation on the outside. This will help to keep the sunlight from heating up the inside of the garage, reducing the need for air conditioning.

Another factor to consider is the type of insulation you’re using. Some types of insulation are designed to be installed on the inside, while others are designed to be installed on the outside. Make sure you read the instructions carefully to ensure you install the insulation correctly.

Get the Best Results

When it comes to insulating your garage door, it’s important to get the best results. Make sure you know the climate you live in and the type of door you have, so you can decide whether to put the insulation on the inside or outside. It’s also important to read the instructions on the insulation carefully and make sure you install it correctly. With the right insulation, you can keep your garage door protected from the elements and reduce your energy costs.

Types of Garage Door Frame Weather Stripping at Home Depot

When it comes to garage door frame weather stripping, Home Depot offers two main types of weather stripping: foam and rubber. Foam weather stripping is typically used for insulation and is available in a variety of sizes to fit your garage door frame. It is also relatively easy to install, as it is simply cut to size and then attached to the frame using nails or staples.

Rubber weather stripping is the best choice for preventing drafts, as it is designed to create an airtight seal. It is also a bit more expensive than foam weather stripping but is more durable and long lasting. Additionally, rubber weather stripping is available in a variety of colors, so you can choose the one that best suits your home.

Advantages of Using Garage Door Frame Weather Stripping

Using garage door frame weather stripping can help protect your home from the elements and reduce energy costs. Additionally, using weather stripping can also help reduce noise levels, making your home a more comfortable place to live.

Installing weather stripping can also help extend the life of your garage door.

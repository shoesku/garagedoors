How To Replace The Lock Barrel On A Roller Door

It's not uncommon for the lock barrel on a roller door to become worn or damaged over time. If your roller door is becoming difficult to unlock, it's likely time to invest in a new lock barrel. Replacing the lock barrel can seem daunting, but it's actually quite a simple task that can be easily done at home with a few basic tools. In this article, we'll take a look at the steps involved in replacing a lock barrel on a roller door.

Tools and Materials Needed

Before you start, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools and materials. You'll need a flathead screwdriver, a Phillips head screwdriver, and a pair of pliers. You'll also need a new lock barrel. Make sure you buy one that is the right size and style for your roller door. You can usually find these in your local hardware store.

Step 1: Remove the Old Lock Barrel

The first step is to remove the old lock barrel. Start by unscrewing the screws that hold the lock barrel in place on the door. Use the flathead screwdriver to do this. Once the screws have been removed, carefully pull the lock barrel out of the door.

Step 2: Install the New Lock Barrel

Now it's time to install the new lock barrel. Start by inserting the lock barrel into the door. Use the Phillips head screwdriver to tighten the screws that hold the lock barrel in place. Make sure the screws are tight, but not too tight as this could damage the lock barrel.

Step 3: Test the Lock Barrel

Once the lock barrel is in place, it's time to test it out. Pull the handle of the lock barrel to make sure it moves freely. If it does not, use the pliers to adjust the screws until it moves smoothly. You can also check the locking mechanism by inserting the key and turning it in the lock.

Step 4: Reattach the Handle

The final step is to reattach the handle to the lock barrel. Start by aligning the handle with the hole in the lock barrel. Use the flathead screwdriver to tighten the screws that hold the handle in place. Make sure the screws are tight, but not too tight as this could damage the handle.

Conclusion

Replacing the lock barrel on a roller door is a fairly simple process that can be easily done at home. All you need are a few basic tools and a new lock barrel. Remember to always test the lock barrel before reattaching the handle to make sure it works properly. With these steps, you should be able to easily replace the lock barrel on your roller door.

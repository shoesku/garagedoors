How To Change A Garage Door Spring Yourself — Step-By-Step Guide

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Introduction

Garage door springs are essential components of any garage door system. They help to counterbalance the weight of the door and make it easier to open and close. Without them, you’d be stuck with a door that’s too heavy to lift manually. Unfortunately, garage door springs don’t last forever. Over time, they become worn out and need to be replaced. Replacing a garage door spring isn’t something most people can do on their own, but if you’re feeling brave and want to save some money by attempting the job yourself, this guide is for you.

Step 1: Prepare Your Work Area

The first step to replacing your garage door springs is to prepare your work area. Make sure you have plenty of space and all the necessary tools before starting the job. You’ll need a pair of safety glasses, a pair of work gloves, pliers, a wrench set, a drill, and a ladder. You’ll also need to make sure the power is disconnected from the door before beginning. To do this, look for the power cord that runs from the motor to the wall and unplug it. Once you’ve done that, you can move on to the next step.

Step 2: Remove the Old Spring

Now it’s time to remove the old spring. Start by opening the door and inspecting the spring to determine what type it is. If you’re not sure, don’t worry — you can always look it up online. Once you’ve identified the type of spring, you can start taking it apart. First, use the wrench to loosen the bolts that hold the spring in place. Once the bolts are loose, you can use the pliers to pull the springs out. Be careful not to pull too hard, as the spring can be dangerous when it’s under tension.

Step 3: Install the New Spring

Now it’s time to install the new spring. Start by attaching one end of the spring to the door frame. Use the drill to secure it in place with bolts. Then, attach the other end of the spring to the door track. Once both ends are secure, you can use the wrench to tighten the bolts. Be sure to check the tension of the spring before moving on to the next step.

Step 4: Test the Spring

Once the new spring is installed, you’ll need to test it to make sure it’s working properly. Open the door and make sure it moves freely and smoothly. If everything is working as it should, you’re ready to move on to the next step.

Step 5: Reconnect the Power

Now it’s time to reconnect the power. Plug the power cord back into the wall outlet and then press the button on the wall control to open the door. If the door opens and closes without any issues, you’re all set.

Conclusion

Replacing a garage door spring isn’t something most people can do on their own, but with the help of this step-by-step guide, you can do it yourself and save some money in the process. Just remember to take all the necessary safety precautions and follow the steps carefully. If you’re still feeling unsure, you can always watch a few YouTube tutorials on the subject. Good luck!

A garage door cable ferrule is an important part of any garage door system. It is a metal ring that attaches the door cable to the door itself. The ferrule is used to make sure that the cable does not move around or become loose. The metal ring also prevents the cable from becoming frayed or damaged. Without the ferrule, the cable could become frayed and eventually break. This can cause serious damage to the door and the surrounding area.

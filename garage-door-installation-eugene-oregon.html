Types of Garage Door Installation Services

When it comes to garage door installation, there are two main types: single piece and sectional. Single piece garage doors are made from one large piece of material and are usually made of wood or aluminum. Sectional garage doors are made up of several pieces that are connected together. Sectional doors are usually made of steel, vinyl, or fiberglass. Both types of garage doors have their advantages and disadvantages, so it’s important to consider what type of door will best suit your needs before making a decision.

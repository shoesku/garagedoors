What Is The Average Cost To Replace A Garage Door In 2023?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

If you’ve been thinking about replacing your garage door, you may be wondering how much it’s going to cost. The average cost to replace a garage door in 2020 is between $1,000 and $4,000. This cost can vary depending on the size of the door, the materials used, and the type of garage door opener you select.

Factors to Consider When Understanding Garage Door Cost to Replace

When replacing a garage door, the cost can vary depending on many factors. Here are some of the most important factors to consider that can affect the cost:

  • Size of the door and opening
  • Material of the door
  • Type of garage door opener
  • Installation fees
  • Additional features

Size of the Door and Opening

One of the biggest factors that can affect the cost of garage door replacement is the size of the door and opening. Standard single-car garage doors range from 8 to 9 feet wide and 7 to 8 feet high. Double-car garage doors range from 16 to 18 feet wide and 7 to 8 feet high. If you have an oversized opening, you will need to pay extra for a custom-sized door.

Material of the Door

The cost of garage door replacement can also vary depending on the material used for the door. The most common materials used for garage doors are wood, aluminum, steel, and vinyl. Wood is the most expensive option, followed by aluminum and steel. Vinyl is the cheapest option, but it is also the least durable.

Type of Garage Door Opener

Another factor to consider when understanding garage door cost to replace is the type of garage door opener you select. The most common types of openers are chain drive, belt drive, and screw drive. Chain drives are the cheapest option, but they are also the loudest. Belt drives are the quietest, but they are also the most expensive. Screw drives are a good middle-of-the-road option.

Installation Fees

In addition to the cost of the door and opener, you will also need to pay installation fees. Most garage door companies charge an hourly rate for installation, which can range from $50 to $100 per hour. The amount of time needed to install a garage door depends on the size and type of door, so it’s important to get an estimate from the company before signing a contract.

Additional Features

Finally, you should also consider the cost of additional features. If you want a motorized opener, you will need to pay extra for the motor and remote control. You can also opt for additional safety features such as an automatic reverse mechanism, which can add to the cost.

Conclusion

Replacing a garage door can be a costly undertaking, but it is an important investment that can increase the value and curb appeal of your home. By understanding the factors that can affect the cost of garage door replacement, you can make an informed decision and get the best value for your money.

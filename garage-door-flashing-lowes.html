What is Garage Door Flashing?

Garage door flashing is a type of material that is used to protect the exterior of your garage door from the elements. It is usually made from aluminum or vinyl and is installed along the perimeter of the door. It helps prevent water from leaking into the garage and helps keep the door looking new for years to come. It’s also a great way to improve the overall look of your home.

Why Do You Need Garage Door Flashing?

Garage door flashing provides an important layer of protection for your home. It helps keep water out of your garage and prevents damage that could occur if the water leaks in. It also helps reduce air infiltration, which can lead to drafts and make your home less energy-efficient. In addition, it can also improve the aesthetics of your home’s exterior.

What Options Are Available From Lowes?

Lowes also offers flashing accessories, such as sealants and caulk, to ensure a tight seal.

How To Install Garage Door Flashing?

Installing garage door flashing is relatively easy and can usually be done in an afternoon. The first step is to measure the perimeter of your garage door and cut the flashing to size. Next, you’ll need to attach the flashing to your garage door using screws or nails. Finally, you’ll need to seal the edges with caulk or sealant to create a tight seal and prevent water from leaking in.

Installing a new garage door is a big job, and one that shouldn't be taken lightly. There are a variety of factors to consider, such as cost, quality, and style. Lowes provides a wide selection of garage doors, and can help you to select the right one for your home. Here, we'll discuss what to expect when installing a garage door from Lowes.

Garage Door Installation Cost at Lowes

So, how much does garage door installation cost at Lowes? Generally, installation costs start at around $400 and go up depending on the size and complexity of the job. This cost includes the door, tracks, hardware, and labor. Installing a basic single car garage door will generally cost around $400. Double car garage doors are more expensive, and can cost upwards of $600.

It's important to note that these prices are not set in stone. Depending on the complexity of the job, or the size of the door, the cost may be higher. It's always a good idea to get an estimate from Lowes before committing to a job.

Types of Garage Door at Lowes

Lowes provides a wide variety of garage door styles and materials. From classic wooden doors to modern steel doors, there is something to suit any style. Additionally, Lowes offers insulated doors which can help to save energy and money. It's important to consider the style and material of the door when selecting one.

Another factor to consider is the type of opener. Lowes offers several types of openers, including chain drive, belt drive, and screw drive. Chain drive openers are the most common, and the most affordable. Belt drive openers are quieter, but more expensive. Screw drive openers are also quiet, but more expensive than chain drive openers.

Garage Door Installation Process

Once you've selected your door, the installation process begins. The first step is to remove the old door, if one exists. This is done by disconnecting the springs and removing the tracks. The new door is then placed in position, and the tracks are re-installed. The springs are then connected, and the door is tested for balance.

Next, the hardware is attached, and the door is tested for proper movement and operation. Finally, the opener is connected, and the door is tested for proper operation. After the installation is complete, Lowes technicians will provide instructions on proper maintenance and use.

Garage Door Installation Tips

Installing a garage door is a big job, and one that shouldn't be taken lightly. Here are a few tips to keep in mind when installing a garage door from Lowes:

  • Measure twice and cut once. This is important when installing a garage door, as a poor fit can cause problems down the line.
  • Double check the tracks to make sure they are level. If the tracks are not level, the door may not open and close properly.
  • Always use safety equipment when working with springs and other moving parts.
  • Before operating the door, be sure to read the manual and follow all safety instructions.

Conclusion

Installing a garage door from Lowes is a great way to upgrade your home. The cost of installation can vary, so be sure to get an estimate before committing to a job. Lowes offers a wide selection of doors, and their technicians can help you to select the right one for your home. Additionally, they can provide tips and advice on proper installation and maintenance.

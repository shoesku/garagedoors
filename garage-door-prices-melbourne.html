Garage Door Prices In Melbourne

Are you considering a new garage door for your home in Melbourne? You’re in the right place. This article will provide all you need to know about garage door prices in Melbourne, from the different types of doors available to their cost.

Types of Garage Doors

When it comes to garage doors, there is a wide variety of types available for your Melbourne home. Roller doors are a type of door that rolls up and down, and are ideal for those with space constraints. Sectional doors are constructed with a series of panels, and they open vertically. Up-and-over doors are the most common type of garage door, and they swing outwards before lifting upwards. Finally, side-hinged doors pivot on either side of the frame.

Material and Style

The type of material and style you choose for your garage door will affect the price. Steel garage doors are the most affordable option, followed by aluminium and timber. You can also find doors made of fibreglass, vinyl, and composite materials. As for style, there are many different designs to choose from, including rustic, modern, and traditional.

Cost Factors

In addition to the material and style, there are a few other factors that can affect the price of your garage door in Melbourne. The size of the door, the complexity of the installation, and the reputation of the manufacturer all play a role in the total cost. If you’re looking for a customized design, you can expect to pay more as well.

Installation Costs

When it comes to installation costs, it’s important to factor in labour costs. Professional installers will charge more than a DIY installation, but they can also ensure that your garage door is installed correctly and safely. If you’re looking to save money, you can always hire a professional who is willing to work on an hourly rate.

Average Costs

The average cost of a garage door in Melbourne is between $1,000 and $2,500, depending on the type, size, and complexity of installation. If you’re looking for a more customised design, you can expect to pay up to $4,000. Installation costs can range from $500 to $1,500, depending on the type of door and the installer.

Additional Costs

In addition to the cost of the door and installation, there are a few other costs to consider. If you’re replacing an old door, you’ll need to factor in the cost of removal and disposal. You’ll also need to purchase a new remote control and any additional accessories, such as a keypad or a doorbell camera.

Warranty

When shopping for a new garage door, it’s important to look for a manufacturer that offers a warranty. Most manufacturers offer a five-year warranty on their products, but some offer longer warranties. It’s always a good idea to ask about the warranty before making a purchase.

Conclusion

If you’re looking for a new garage door in Melbourne, there are many types and styles to choose from. The cost of the door and installation will depend on the type, size, and complexity of installation. Additional costs, such as removal and disposal, may also apply. Be sure to ask about the warranty before making your purchase.

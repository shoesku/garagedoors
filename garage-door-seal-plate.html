What is a Garage Door Seal Plate?

If you’re a homeowner, you’ve probably heard of garage door seal plates. But what exactly are they? A garage door seal plate, also known as a bottom seal plate, is a piece of metal that’s attached to the bottom of the garage door. It’s designed to seal and protect the bottom of the door. It helps the door stay closed, ensuring that no air or moisture gets in or out of the garage.

The garage door seal plate is made of durable, weather-resistant material that’s designed to withstand the elements and protect the garage from the elements. It’s typically made of galvanized steel, which is corrosion-resistant and can last for years.

Why Do You Need a Garage Door Seal Plate?

Garage door seal plates are essential for keeping the garage door sealed and protected from the elements. Without a seal plate, air and moisture can easily get inside the garage, which can cause damage to the door and the contents of the garage. It can also cause rusting, which can reduce the life of the garage door.

Additionally, a garage door seal plate can help reduce energy costs. A well-sealed garage door can help keep the garage cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter, reducing the amount of energy needed to heat or cool the area.

Types of Garage Door Seal Plates

There are several types of garage door seal plates available, including:

  • Foam-filled garage door seal plates
  • Rubber seal plates
  • Flexible seal plates
  • Insulated seal plates

Foam-filled garage door seal plates are designed to provide the most effective seal, as they’re made of a dense foam material that seals tightly against the door. They’re also the most durable option and can last for years.

Rubber seal plates are a less expensive option, but they don’t provide as much protection as foam-filled seal plates. They’re also not as durable, so they’ll need to be replaced more often.

Flexible seal plates are made of a flexible rubber material that’s designed to expand and contract with the door. They’re less expensive than foam-filled seal plates, but they don’t provide as much protection.

Insulated seal plates are designed to provide extra insulation and help keep the garage cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter. They’re more expensive than other types of seal plates, but they offer the most protection.

How to Install a Garage Door Seal Plate

Installing a garage door seal plate is a relatively easy job that most homeowners can do themselves. The first step is to measure the length and width of the garage door. Then, you’ll need to purchase a seal plate that’s the same size as the garage door.

Once you have the seal plate, you’ll need to attach it to the bottom of the garage door. This can be done with screws, nails, or even adhesive. Make sure the seal plate is securely attached before closing the garage door.

Once the seal plate is installed, it’s important to check it periodically to make sure it’s still in good condition. If the seal plate becomes loose or damaged, it’s important to replace it as soon as possible to ensure the garage door stays sealed and protected.

Conclusion

Garage door seal plates are an essential part of protecting your garage from the elements. They’re relatively easy to install and can help reduce energy costs, as well as keep the garage protected from the elements. Be sure to check the seal plate periodically to ensure it’s still in good condition and replace it if needed.

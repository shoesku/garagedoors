How To Install A Genie Garage Door Opener In 2021

Installing a Genie garage door opener is a job that most homeowners can handle on their own. This guide will walk you through the steps of installing a Genie garage door opener, from start to finish. With a few simple tools, you can have a Genie garage door opener installed in no time.

Tools and Supplies Needed for Installation

Before you start installation of your Genie garage door opener, make sure you have all of the necessary tools and supplies. You will need a power drill, a ladder, a level, a screwdriver, and two adjustable wrenches. You will also need a Genie garage door opener kit, which includes the motor, the rail, the chain, and the mounting hardware.

Mounting the Rail

The first step in installing a Genie garage door opener is to mount the rail to the ceiling. Begin by using the power drill to create pilot holes for the mounting screws. Make sure the holes are spaced evenly and that the rail is level. Once the pilot holes are created, use the screws provided in the Genie garage door opener kit to mount the rail to the ceiling.

Attaching the Chain

Once the rail is mounted, attach the chain to the rail and the motor. Begin by attaching one end of the chain to the rail. Make sure the chain is placed at the spot indicated on the rail. Once the chain is attached, use the adjustable wrenches to secure it. Next, attach the other end of the chain to the motor. Make sure the chain is securely attached to the motor before moving on to the next step.

Mounting the Motor

The next step is to mount the motor to the ceiling. Begin by positioning the motor on the ceiling, making sure it is level. Once the motor is in place, use the power drill to create pilot holes for the mounting screws. Make sure the holes are spaced evenly and that the motor is level. Once the pilot holes are created, use the screws provided in the Genie garage door opener kit to mount the motor to the ceiling.

Connecting the Wiring

Once the motor is mounted, you need to connect the wiring. Begin by connecting the power cord to the power outlet. Make sure the power cord is securely plugged in. Next, connect the other end of the power cord to the motor. Make sure the connection is secure. Finally, connect the wall control unit to the motor. Make sure the connection is secure before moving on to the next step.

Programming the Genie Garage Door Opener

Once the wiring is connected, you need to program the Genie garage door opener. Begin by following the instructions in the manual provided with the Genie garage door opener kit. Program the motor and the wall control unit according to the instructions in the manual. Once the programming is complete, you are ready to test the Genie garage door opener.

Testing the Genie Garage Door Opener

The final step is to test the Genie garage door opener. Begin by pressing the wall control unit to open the garage door. Make sure the door opens and closes properly. If it does, then the installation was successful. If it does not, then you may need to adjust the settings in the Genie garage door opener or adjust the chain.

Conclusion

Installing a Genie garage door opener is a job that most homeowners can handle on their own. With the right tools and supplies, you can have a Genie garage door opener installed in no time. This guide has walked you through the steps of installing a Genie garage door opener, from start to finish. Now you can enjoy the convenience and security of a Genie garage door opener.

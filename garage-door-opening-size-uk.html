Understanding Garage Door Opening Sizes In The Uk

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Garage doors are a necessary part of any home’s exterior design, providing security and protection from the elements. In the UK, standard garage door sizes are determined by the width and height of the opening, and the available range of sizes gives homeowners plenty of choice when it comes to picking the right design for their property. This guide will help you understand the different garage door sizes, and how to choose the right size for your needs.

How to Measure Garage Door Opening Sizes

Before you start shopping, you need to measure the opening size of your garage door. This is the width and height of the gap where the door will be inserted. Measure the gap at the widest and highest points, and make sure to include any additional structural elements such as architraves or lintels. When measuring, remember that the UK’s standard sizes are usually listed in imperial measurements (feet and inches).

The width of your garage door opening will determine the range of sizes you can choose from, and the height is important for the correct operation of the door. Most standard garage door sizes range from 7’ (2.13m) wide to 10’ (3.05m) wide, with heights ranging from 6’6” (1.98m) to 8’ (2.44m). The most common sizes are 8’ x 7’, 9’ x 7’, 9’ x 8’, and 10’ x 8’.

It’s important to check the measurements of your existing opening before ordering any new garage door, as the standard sizes won’t fit all openings. If you’re unsure of your exact measurements, you can have an installer come out and take the measurements for you. This will also give you the opportunity to discuss the best door type and style to fit your needs.

Types of Garage Doors

Once you’ve established the size of your opening, you can start looking into the different types of garage doors available. The three main types of garage doors are roller, up-and-over and sectional. The roller door is the most popular type in the UK, as it’s easy to install and operate, and is available in a range of sizes. Up-and-over doors are available in a variety of materials, including wood and steel, and can be either manual or automatic.

Sectional garage doors are becoming increasingly popular, as they’re easy to operate and can be opened and closed with minimal effort. They’re also available in a variety of colours and styles, making them an attractive option for those who want to add a touch of style to their home’s exterior. All of these types of garage doors are available in the standard sizes listed above, so you should be able to find the perfect size for your needs.

Choosing the Right Garage Door

When it comes to choosing the right garage door for your home, there are a few factors to consider. First, think about the look and feel you want to achieve. You can choose from a range of materials, such as wood, steel, and PVC, and a variety of colours and styles. You should also think about the functionality of the door. Manual doors will require more effort to open and close, while automatic doors can be opened and closed at the touch of a button.

The security of the door should also be considered. You can choose from a range of doors with different levels of security, and it’s important to choose a door that will keep your home safe. Finally, consider the cost of the door. Prices can vary depending on the size, material, and style of the door, so it’s important to shop around to find the best deal.

Conclusion

Garage doors come in a range of sizes, so it’s important to measure the opening of your garage and understand the different types of doors available. You should also consider the style, material, security, and cost of the door, to ensure you get the perfect door for your home. With the right garage door, you can add value, security, and style to your home, so take the time to choose the right one.

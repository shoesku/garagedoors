Why You Should Fix Your Garage Door Frame Gap

A garage door frame gap is an issue that can cause major problems if left untreated. Your garage door is what protects your home from the elements and intruders, so it’s important to make sure it’s in good working order. A gap in the frame can let in moisture, pests, and even burglars. It can also cause the door to become unbalanced and difficult to open and close. That’s why it’s essential to repair the gap as soon as possible.

Signs Your Garage Door Frame Has a Gap

Before you can start fixing your garage door frame gap, you need to be able to identify it. There are several signs that can indicate a gap in the frame, including:

  • The door is difficult to open and close
  • The door is unbalanced
  • Gaps can be seen around the door frame
  • There is a draft coming from the door
  • The door is squeaking or making strange noises

How to Fix Garage Door Frame Gap in 2023

The best way to repair a garage door frame gap is to use a sealant. This will help to fill in any cracks and gaps, preventing moisture, pests, and intruders from entering your home. Before you begin, make sure to clean the door and frame with a damp cloth to remove any dust, dirt, or debris. Once the area is clean and dry, you can apply the sealant.

Step 1: Choose the Right Sealant

When it comes to choosing the right sealant for your garage door frame gap, you have a variety of options. Your best bet is to use a high-quality, waterproof sealant that won’t crack or peel over time. You can find these at most hardware stores or online.

Step 2: Apply the Sealant

Once you’ve chosen the right sealant, you can begin applying it to the garage door frame gap. Use a putty knife to spread the sealant over the gap, making sure to fill in any cracks or crevices. Then, use a damp cloth to smooth out the sealant and remove any excess. Allow the sealant to dry completely before closing the door.

Step 3: Check the Sealant

After the sealant has dried, it’s important to check it for any signs of wear or damage. If there are any visible gaps or cracks, you may need to reapply the sealant. If the sealant looks good, you can close the door and enjoy the peace of mind that comes with knowing your home is safe from intruders and the elements.

Conclusion

Garage door frame gaps can be a major problem if left untreated. Not only can they let in moisture, pests, and burglars, but they can also cause the door to become unbalanced and difficult to open and close. The best way to repair a garage door frame gap is to use a high-quality waterproof sealant. Simply apply the sealant to the gap and allow it to dry, then check it for any signs of wear or damage. With a little bit of work and the right sealant, you can keep your home safe and secure.

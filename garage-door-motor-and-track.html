Everything You Need To Know About Garage Door Motors And Tracks In 2023

Introduction

Garage door motors and tracks are an integral part of the modern home and are necessary for a functioning garage door. But what do you really need to know about them in order to make sure your garage door is properly maintained? This article will cover the basics of garage door motors and tracks, as well as the newest advancements in technology and safety that are available in 2023.

Types of Garage Door Motors

The most common type of garage door motor today is the chain-driven motor. This type of motor is driven by a chain, which is attached to the trolley and is responsible for lifting the door when activated. Chain-driven motors are typically the most affordable and are relatively easy to install. However, they do require regular maintenance and can be noisy.

Another type of garage door motor is the belt-driven motor, which uses a belt instead of a chain. These motors are quieter and more energy-efficient, but they are also more expensive. In addition, they can be difficult to install and require more regular maintenance.

The newest type of motor on the market is the direct drive motor. This type of motor is completely enclosed, making it extremely quiet and energy-efficient. Direct drive motors are the most expensive, but they require the least amount of maintenance and can last for up to 25 years.

Types of Garage Door Tracks

The most common type of garage door track is the single-track system. This type of track is usually made of steel and is attached to the wall of the garage. Single-track systems are relatively easy to install, but they can be noisy and are prone to wear and tear.

A double-track system is slightly more expensive but is much quieter and more durable. This type of track consists of two tracks that are connected to the ceiling of the garage and run along the length of the door. Double-track systems are more difficult to install but are much sturdier and can last for many years.

The newest type of track on the market is the curved-track system. This type of track is made of aluminum and is curved to fit the shape of the garage door. Curved-track systems are much quieter and more energy-efficient than single or double-track systems, but they are also the most expensive.

Advancements in Technology

In 2023, there are several new advancements in technology for garage door motors and tracks. One of the most popular is the use of smart technology, which allows you to control your garage door from your smartphone or other mobile device. Smart technology also allows you to monitor your garage door’s status and even receive alerts if there is a problem.

In addition, many manufacturers now offer safety features such as motion sensors, which will automatically close the door if it senses movement in the garage. There are also new energy-efficient motors and tracks, which can help lower your energy bills. Finally, many new motors and tracks are now available with a lifetime warranty.

Maintenance and Repair

In order to ensure that your garage door motors and tracks remain in good condition, regular maintenance is essential. This includes lubricating the tracks and checking for any signs of wear and tear. It’s also important to make sure the motor is working properly and that the safety features are functioning correctly.

If you notice any problems with your garage door motors or tracks, it’s important to contact a professional for repair. Attempting to repair the motors or tracks yourself can be dangerous and could cause more damage. Professional garage door repair technicians are experienced and equipped with the right tools to make sure your garage door is functioning properly.

Conclusion

Garage door motors and tracks are an important part of the modern home and are necessary for a functioning garage door. There are several types of motors and tracks available, as well as several advancements in technology that are available in 2023. It’s important to make sure your garage door motors and tracks are regularly maintained and that any repairs are handled by a professional.

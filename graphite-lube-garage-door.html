Why Choose Graphite Lube?

When it comes to garage door maintenance, there’s no better way to do it than with graphite lube. Graphite lube is a lubricant that helps keep your garage door running smoothly and efficiently. It helps reduce friction and wear on the door’s parts, which in turn helps prevent costly repairs down the line. Graphite lube also helps to protect the metal parts from rust and corrosion, and it can even help to reduce noise from the door opening and closing.

Graphite lube is an inexpensive and easy-to-use product that can help keep your garage door running like new for years to come. It is also a great way to prevent wear and tear on the door’s metal parts, which can help prevent costly repairs in the long run.

How to Use Graphite Lube

Using graphite lube is a relatively simple process. The first step is to clean the garage door and its parts, including the rollers, hinges, and tracks. Once these parts are clean, you can apply the graphite lube to them. You should apply the lubricant to the parts with a cloth or brush and then allow it to sit for a few minutes to allow it to penetrate the parts. Finally, wipe off any excess lubricant before closing the garage door.

It’s important to note that graphite lube is not designed to replace the lubricants that come with the door. It should be used in addition to any existing lubricants, as it can provide additional protection and lubrication. Additionally, it’s important to note that graphite lube should not be used on plastic or wooden parts of the garage door.

Periodic Maintenance

It’s important to keep up with regular maintenance on your garage door, including the use of graphite lube. This should be done at least once a year, although some experts recommend more frequent lubrication. This will help to ensure that your garage door is running smoothly, efficiently, and safely for years to come.

When to Call a Professional

It’s important to note that while graphite lube can be a great way to keep your garage door running smoothly and efficiently, it should not be used as a replacement for professional maintenance. If your garage door is not running as expected, it’s important to call a professional to inspect and repair it. This can help to prevent costly repairs down the line.

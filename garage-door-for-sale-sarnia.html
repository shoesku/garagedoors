Sectional Garage Doors

Sectional garage doors are one of the most popular choices for homeowners and businesses in the Sarnia area. These doors are composed of several panels that are connected by hinges and tracks. The advantage of this type of door is that it offers great flexibility. You can open and close the door in sections, allowing you to control the amount of air flow into the garage. Additionally, they are easy to maintain and can be operated manually or with a motor.

Roll Up Garage Doors

Roll up garage doors are also a popular option for Sarnia homeowners and businesses. These doors are composed of a single panel that rolls up when opened. They are typically made from heavy-duty steel and aluminum, making them extremely durable and reliable. Additionally, they are easy to maintain and can be opened and closed manually or with a motor.

Swing Out Garage Doors

Swing out garage doors are another popular choice for homeowners and businesses in Sarnia. These doors are composed of two panels that swing out when opened. Overhead Garage Doors

Overhead garage doors are also a popular option for homeowners and businesses in Sarnia. These doors are composed of several panels that open and close overhead.

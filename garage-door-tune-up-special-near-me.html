A garage door tune up service is a great way to make sure that your garage door is running smoothly. This type of service will help to improve the performance of your garage door, allowing you to open and close it with ease. The service includes a thorough inspection of all the parts of the garage door, including the tracks, hinges, and springs, as well as checking the alignment, lubrication, and balance of your door. The technician will also check the door’s weather-stripping and make sure that it is in good condition.

Benefits of Getting a Garage Door Tune Up

Regularly getting a garage door tune up can provide numerous advantages. For one, it can help to extend the life of your garage door, as well as reduce the chances of it breaking down. This service can also help to reduce the noise that your garage door makes when it is being opened and closed. Additionally, it can help to reduce the amount of energy that your garage door consumes.

Common Garage Door Tune Up Services

Most garage door tune up services include inspecting the door, lubricating the moving parts, adjusting the balance and alignment, and replacing any worn or damaged parts.

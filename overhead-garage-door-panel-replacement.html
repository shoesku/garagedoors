What is an Overhead Garage Door Panel?

An overhead garage door panel is the large panel of a garage door that lifts and lowers as the door is opened and closed. This panel is made of either wood or metal and is typically much heavier than a regular door. Garage door panels come in a variety of sizes and styles and are often used to provide added security and insulation to a garage. In some cases, a garage door panel may need to be replaced if it is damaged or has become worn over time.

What to Consider When Replacing an Overhead Garage Door Panel

When replacing an overhead garage door panel in 2023, you'll need to consider a few different factors. First, you'll need to make sure that you purchase a panel that is the correct size and shape for your garage door. Additionally, you'll need to decide whether you want to purchase a panel made from wood or metal. If you're looking for additional insulation and security, then a metal panel is typically the best option. Finally, you'll need to make sure that you purchase a panel that is compatible with your garage door's existing hardware.

Steps to Replacing an Overhead Garage Door Panel in 2023

Replacing an overhead garage door panel in 2023 is a fairly straightforward process that can be completed without the help of a professional. The steps to replacing an overhead garage door panel are as follows:

  • 1. Start by disconnecting the power to your garage door opener.
  • 2. Remove the existing garage door panel from the frame. This can be done by unscrewing the panel from the frame.
  • 3. Measure the existing panel to make sure you purchase the correct size and shape.
  • 4. Purchase a new panel and make sure it is compatible with your garage door hardware.
  • 5. Install the new panel in the frame and attach it with the appropriate hardware.
  • 6. Reconnect the power to your garage door opener.

How To Extend Garage Door Height

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Are you looking to create more space in your garage? Do you need to increase the height of your garage door? For all sorts of reasons, it is sometimes necessary to extend the height of a garage door. This can be done quite easily and cost-effectively if you know how. In this article, we will explain how to extend garage door height.

Steps to Extend the Height of Your Garage Door

1. Measure the Current Height of Your Garage Door

The first step in extending the height of your garage door is to measure the current height. This is important as it will help you determine what components you will need to achieve the desired height. Additionally, you will need to know the exact measurements in order to plan and purchase the necessary supplies. Use a tape measure to determine the height of the garage door from the top of the door frame to the floor.

2. Select the Appropriate Extension Kit

Once you have the measurements of your current garage door height, you can purchase an extension kit. Extension kits will come with all the necessary components to extend the height of your existing garage door. The kit will include a header, a track, and other components depending on the manufacturer. You will want to ensure that you select a kit designed for the type of garage door you have, such as a roll-up door or a sectional door.

3. Install the Header

The header is the most important component of the extension kit. It is the piece that will hold the garage door in place and provide additional support. First, you will need to measure and mark the location of the header on the wall. Then you will need to secure the header to the wall using a drill and screws. Make sure that the header is level and securely attached to the wall.

4. Attach the Track

Once the header is installed, you can then attach the track. The track is the piece that the garage door will move along. The track should be attached to the header using the screws that came with the extension kit. Make sure that the track is secure and level. Additionally, you may need to make some adjustments to the track in order for it to fit properly.

5. Install the Rest of the Components

The next step is to install the remainder of the components in the kit. This will include a spring, cable, and other necessary components. Depending on the type of garage door you have, you may need to make additional adjustments. Be sure to read the instructions that come with the extension kit carefully in order to properly install all the components.

6. Attach the Garage Door to the Track

Once all the components have been installed, you can then attach the garage door to the track. Again, depending on the type of garage door you have, you may need to make adjustments. Once the door is attached, you should open and close it a few times to make sure everything is working properly.

7. Adjust the Torsion Spring

The last step is to adjust the torsion spring. This is an important step as it is responsible for the tension in the garage door. It is important to make sure that the tension is set correctly as this will ensure that the garage door operates smoothly and safely. Again, you should refer to the instructions that came with the extension kit for the proper procedure.

Conclusion

Increasing the height of your garage door can be a great way to create more space and make it easier to access your garage. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can easily and cost-effectively extend the height of your garage door. Make sure to carefully read and follow the instructions that come with the extension kit and adjust the torsion spring correctly to ensure that your garage door is safe and operates properly.

What Is Garage Door Molding Seal?

Garage door molding seal is a type of protective seal that is applied around the edges of a garage door. It is designed to keep out dust, dirt, and moisture, while also providing an aesthetically pleasing finish. It is usually made of a flexible plastic material and is applied by rolling it onto the door frame. This type of seal is often used in combination with other types of weatherstripping, such as foam seals or rubber seals, to provide an even better barrier against the elements. Garage door molding seal is an important part of keeping your garage door looking good and functioning properly.

Why Do You Need Garage Door Molding Seal?

Garage door molding seal is essential for protecting the structural integrity of your garage door. It helps to keep out dirt, dust, and moisture, which can cause damage to the frame and other parts of the door. Additionally, the seal helps to keep the door looking good and prevents it from becoming damaged over time. Without the seal, the door can become warped, dented, and suffer from rust or other types of damage. In some cases, the seal is also used to provide additional protection against the elements, such as wind, rain, and snow.

How to Install Garage Door Molding Seal?

Installing garage door molding seal is relatively easy and can be done in a few simple steps. First, you need to measure the perimeter of the garage door and buy enough seal material to cover the entire surface. Once you have the seal material, you need to cut it to size and then apply it to the door frame. You can use a roller to apply the seal evenly and ensure that it adheres properly. Finally, you should inspect the seal to make sure it is properly applied and that there are no gaps or tears.

How to Maintain Garage Door Molding Seal?

Maintaining garage door molding seal is important to ensure that it continues to provide protection and stays looking good.

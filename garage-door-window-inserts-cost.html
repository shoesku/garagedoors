What is a Window Insert?

A window insert is a glass or plastic panel that is added to a garage door. It provides an aesthetically pleasing way to enhance the look of the door while providing a degree of additional security. Window inserts are available in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, and can also be customized to match the existing look of the door. They are also available in a variety of materials, including aluminum, steel, and fiberglass.

What are the Benefits of a Window Insert?

Window inserts provide a great way to add a custom look to a garage door. They also provide additional security, as they are more difficult to break into than a traditional door. Additionally, they are a great way to add natural light and ventilation to the garage, which can help to reduce energy costs. Finally, window inserts can help to reduce noise from outside, as they create a barrier between the garage and the outside world.

What are the Different Types of Window Inserts?

The most common types of window inserts are those that are made of glass. These are often used to provide a decorative element to the door, and can be customized to match the existing look of the door. Additionally, they can be tinted or frosted to provide additional privacy. Aluminum window inserts are also popular, as they are more durable and less likely to break than glass. They are also available in a variety of colors and can be customized to match the look of the door.

Types of Genie Garage Door Openers

Genie offers a wide variety of garage door openers, including chain-drive, belt-drive, wall-mount, and screw-drive openers. Each type of opener has its own set of parts, so it's important to know what type of opener you have in order to get the right replacement parts.

Chain-drive openers are the most common type of opener and are usually the least expensive. They use a chain to lift and lower the door. They are usually fairly reliable, but the chain can wear out over time and need to be replaced.

Belt-drive openers are more expensive than chain-drive openers, but they are more durable and quieter. They use a belt to lift and lower the door, which makes them much quieter than chain-drive openers. They are also much more reliable than chain-drive openers.

Wall-mount openers are the most expensive type of opener, but they are also the most reliable and quietest. They are mounted directly to the wall and use a motor to lift and lower the door. They are very reliable and require very little maintenance.

Screw-drive openers are the least expensive type of opener, but they are also the least reliable. They use a screw to lift and lower the door. They are not as reliable as other types of openers and require more maintenance.

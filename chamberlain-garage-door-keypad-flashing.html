If you have a Chamberlain garage door keypad and it’s flashing, you may be wondering what’s wrong and how to fix it. In this article, we’ll provide you with all the information you need to understand what’s wrong and how to fix it. Whether you need to replace the keypad, reprogram the keypad, or just reset it, we will explain how to do each of these.

What Causes the Flashing?

There are several things that can cause Chamberlain garage door keypad flashing. The most common cause is that the keypad needs to be reset due to a power outage or a problem with the opener. Another possible cause is that the keypad needs to be re-programmed due to a change in the code. In some cases, the keypad may need to be replaced if it’s been damaged or is faulty.

How to Reset the Keypad

If the Chamberlain garage door keypad is flashing, you may need to reset it. To do this, you will need to open the cover of the keypad and press the reset button. After doing this, the keypad should reset and the flashing should stop. If this doesn’t work, you may need to reprogram the keypad.

How to Reprogram the Keypad

To reprogram the keypad, you will need to open the cover of the keypad and press the program button. Once the light starts blinking, you will need to enter the new code. After entering the code, press the enter button and the keypad should be reprogrammed. If the keypad is still flashing, you may need to replace it.

How to Replace the Keypad

Replacing a Chamberlain garage door keypad can be a bit tricky, but it’s not too difficult. First, you will need to disconnect the power to the opener. Then, you will need to remove the cover of the keypad and disconnect it from the opener. Once this is done, you can install the new keypad and connect it to the opener. Once this is done, you should be able to reprogram the keypad and the flashing should stop.

As technology has continued to progress, so too has the evolution of garage door springs. Lowes is one of the most popular retailers to find garage door springs for sale, and for good reason. Not only does Lowe's have an extensive selection of springs, but they also boast some of the most competitive prices on the market.

If you’re in the market for new garage door springs, Lowes is the place to start your search. Here’s everything you need to know about Lowe’s garage door springs for sale in 2023.

Types of Garage Door Springs

Garage door springs come in two main types: torsion springs and extension springs. Torsion springs are located above the door, and are typically operated by a metal rod on the inside of the door. Extension springs, on the other hand, are located along the sides of the door and are operated by pulleys.

Both torsion and extension springs are available for sale at Lowe’s. The type of spring you choose will depend on the type of door you have and the weight of the door. For instance, if you have a heavy door, you’ll likely need torsion springs. On the other hand, if you have a lightweight door, extension springs will usually suffice.

Garage Door Spring Sizes

Garage door springs come in a variety of sizes, so it’s important to measure the existing springs on your door before you buy new ones. If you’re replacing both springs, you’ll want to measure the length, inside diameter, and wire size of each spring.

Lowes has a variety of standard and custom-made springs for sale. You can either buy two identical springs, or order two springs with different sizes. It’s important to note that custom-made springs will take longer to produce and may be more expensive.

Garage Door Spring Prices

The price of garage door springs can vary greatly depending on the size, type, and manufacturer. On average, torsion springs tend to be more expensive than extension springs. That said, Lowes offers competitive prices on both types of springs, so it’s worth taking a look at their selection before you buy.

In addition to the cost of the springs, you may also need to factor in the cost of installation. Installing new garage door springs isn’t a job for the inexperienced, and it’s best to leave it to a professional. The cost of installation will vary depending on the complexity of the job, the type of door, and the location of the door.

Choosing the Right Spring

When you’re shopping for garage door springs, it’s important to choose the right ones for your door. Not all springs are created equal, and some may not be able to handle the weight of your door. If you’re unsure about which springs to buy, you can always talk to a Lowe’s sales associate for advice.

It’s also important to note that garage door springs can be dangerous if they’re installed incorrectly. If you’re not experienced in working with garage door springs, it’s always best to leave the installation to the professionals.

Conclusion

Lowes is one of the best places to buy garage door springs in 2023. Whether you’re looking for torsion springs or extension springs, Lowe’s has an extensive selection of both standard and custom-made springs to choose from. They offer competitive prices and knowledgeable sales associates who can help you choose the right springs for your door.

When it comes to buying garage door springs, you get what you pay for. Although it may be tempting to buy the cheapest option available, it’s important to remember that the wrong springs can result in serious injuries. Make sure to do your research and talk to a Lowe’s sales associate before making a purchase.

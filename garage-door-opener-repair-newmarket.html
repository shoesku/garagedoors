Basic Troubleshooting Tips

Before you call a professional, there are a few things you can do to troubleshoot your garage door opener. First, check the power supply. Make sure that the power switch is on, and that the wall outlet is working. You can also try resetting the opener, or replacing the batteries in the remote control. If these simple steps don't solve the problem, it's time to call a professional.

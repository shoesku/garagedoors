There is no doubt that the garage door industry in Canada has changed significantly over the past decade. In 2023, it is estimated that there are over 8,000 different garage door businesses in Canada, employing over 25,000 people and generating around $2.7 billion in annual revenue. This makes it one of the most lucrative industries in Canada, and one that is in high demand for both skilled and unskilled labor. If you are looking for a job in garage door work in Canada, then you are in luck. In this article, we will go over the ins and outs of the garage door industry in Canada in 2023, as well as what kind of job opportunities you can find in the industry.

Overview of the Garage Door Industry in Canada

The garage door industry in Canada is made up of a variety of different businesses and services. These include garage door installation and repair services, garage door sales and service, and even garage door security services. There are also businesses that specialize in custom garage door designs, as well as those that offer garage door parts and accessories. As you can see, the industry is quite diversified and has a lot of potential for those looking for work in the industry.

The industry is also highly competitive, with many companies offering similar services and products. This makes it important for those looking for garage door jobs in Canada to have a good understanding of the industry and its dynamics. It is important to understand the different types of work that are available in the industry and what the average salary is for different positions. This will help you to find the right job in the industry to suit your skills and experience.

Types of Garage Door Jobs

There are a variety of different types of garage door jobs available in Canada in 2023. The most common type of job is that of a garage door installer and repair technician. These technicians are responsible for installing and repairing all types of garage doors, from manual to automated. They also need to have a good understanding of the different types of doors, their components, and how to make them work properly. As well as this, they may need to have a good understanding of electrical wiring and other related topics.

Other types of garage door jobs include sales and service personnel, as well as those who specialize in custom garage door designs. These individuals need to be knowledgeable about the different types of doors, their components, and how to properly install them. They also need to be able to answer customer questions and provide excellent customer service. Lastly, they may need to be knowledgeable about different types of security systems and how to properly install them.

Finally, there are also jobs available in the industry for those who are experienced and knowledgeable about garage door parts and accessories. This includes those who are able to repair and replace parts and accessories, as well as those who are able to install them. This type of job requires a great deal of technical knowledge and skill, and it is important that the individual has a good understanding of the industry and its dynamics.

Average Salaries for Garage Door Jobs in Canada

The average salary for garage door jobs in Canada in 2023 is around $45,000 per year. However, this number can vary significantly depending on the type of job and the location in which the job is located. For instance, those working in the larger cities such as Toronto and Vancouver may be able to make more money than those working in smaller towns and rural areas.

The amount you can make in the industry also depends on the type of job you have. For instance, garage door installers and repair technicians typically make around $35,000 to $40,000 per year, whereas those working in sales and service can make up to $50,000 per year. Those who specialize in custom designs and security systems can make even more. It is important to remember that salaries can vary based on experience and skill level, so it is best to research the industry and the different types of jobs available in order to get an accurate idea of what you can expect to make.

Conclusion

The garage door industry in Canada has grown significantly over the past decade, and in 2023, there are plenty of great job opportunities available. Whether you are looking for a job in garage door installation and repair, sales and service, or custom designs and security systems, you can find a great job in the industry. Be sure to research the different types of jobs available in the industry, as well as the average salaries associated with them. With the right knowledge and skill set, you can be sure to find a great job in the garage door industry in Canada in 2023.

Garage Door Gasket Track: All You Need To Know

Garage door gasket track is an important component of your garage door system. It helps to keep the entire door system secure and weatherproof, as well as ensures the door opens and closes smoothly. The gasket track is a long, narrow piece of flexible rubber with a channel in the middle that runs along the bottom of the door. It helps to seal any gaps between the door and the frame, preventing moisture and dirt from entering the garage.

Types of Gasket Tracks

There are two main types of gasket tracks available – metal and plastic. Metal gasket tracks are the most common, and they are made from galvanized steel. They are highly durable and resistant to rust and corrosion, making them suitable for outdoor use. Plastic gasket tracks are also available, and they are the cheaper option. However, they are not as durable as metal gasket tracks and are not suitable for outdoor use.

How to Install a Gasket Track

Installing a gasket track is relatively easy, and most tracks come with detailed instructions. It’s important to ensure that the track is securely attached to the door frame, and it’s best to use screws or bolts to ensure a secure fit. Once the track is installed, you should then be able to slide the gasket into the track easily. Make sure that the gasket is tightly secured, as any gaps will allow air and moisture to enter the garage.

Benefits of a Gasket Track

Having a gasket track installed on your garage door is beneficial for a number of reasons. Firstly, it helps to keep the entire door system weatherproof and secure, ensuring the door opens and closes smoothly. In addition, it helps to keep the garage insulated, as it prevents air and moisture from entering the garage. Finally, it helps to reduce noise from outside, as any gaps between the door and frame are sealed.

Maintaining Your Gasket Track

It’s important to ensure that your gasket track is properly maintained in order to ensure it remains in good condition. Regularly check the track for any signs of damage, such as cracks or tears in the material. In addition, you should check the track for any signs of wear and tear and replace the gasket if necessary. Finally, you should clean the track regularly using a damp cloth to remove any dirt and debris.

Replacing a Gasket Track

If your gasket track is beyond repair, it is important to replace it as soon as possible. You should first measure the track to ensure that you purchase the correct size, and then you can buy a new track from your local hardware store. It’s a good idea to purchase a track made from galvanized steel, as this is the most durable and reliable option. Once the new track is installed, you should then be able to slide the gasket into the track easily.

Conclusion

Garage door gasket track is an essential component of any garage door system. It helps to keep the entire door system secure and weatherproof, as well as ensures the door opens and closes smoothly. There are two main types of gasket tracks available – metal and plastic – and it’s important to ensure that the track is installed correctly. Finally, it’s important to regularly maintain your gasket track and replace it if necessary.

What You Should Know About The New Garage Door Cost In New Zealand

Owners of residential homes in New Zealand should be aware of the cost of a new garage door. It can be a significant investment, but one that pays off when it comes to the security, convenience, and aesthetics of your property. This article will cover the different types of garage doors, the cost of installation and maintenance, and other factors that may influence the price of a new garage door.

Types of Garage Doors

New Zealand homeowners can choose from a variety of garage door materials and styles. Steel, wood, and aluminum are all popular choices. Steel doors are the most common and cost-effective, while wood is more expensive and requires more maintenance. Aluminum is lightweight and more durable than steel and wood. Wood is also a great option for those who want a more traditional look for their home.

Cost of a New Garage Door

The cost of a new garage door depends on the type of door you choose, the size, and the installation. Steel garage doors are generally the most affordable and can start at around $200, while wood doors can cost around $500 or more. Installation costs can range from $100 to $500 depending on the complexity of the job.

Garage Door Maintenance

Garage door maintenance is important to ensure that your door is functioning properly and safely. Regular maintenance includes checking the door hinges and tracks to make sure they are not loose or broken, lubricating the doorâ€™s parts, and inspecting the springs for signs of deterioration. It is also important to inspect the weatherstripping and seals to prevent drafts and water leaks.

Factors That Affect the Cost of a New Garage Door

The cost of a new garage door can also be affected by certain factors. The size of the door is important as larger doors need more materials and labor to install. The type of material you choose will also affect the price. Additionally, the type of opener you choose can increase the cost. If you want an automated opener, you may have to pay more.

Additional Garage Door Accessories

You can also purchase additional garage door accessories, such as remote controls, keypads, and sensors. These items can add to the overall cost of your new garage door. Additionally, you should consider insulation if you want to reduce your energy bills. Insulation can also provide additional protection from the elements and provide a more comfortable environment in your garage.

Where to Buy a New Garage Door

You can purchase a new garage door from a variety of sources. Many home improvement stores carry a selection of garage doors, as do some hardware stores. You can also purchase new garage doors online from retailers, or you can hire a contractor to install the door for you.

Conclusion

The cost of a new garage door in New Zealand can vary depending on the type of door, the size, and the installation. Steel and aluminum doors are the most affordable, while wood doors can be more expensive and require more maintenance. Additionally, there are a variety of accessories available that can increase the overall cost. It is important to do your research and compare prices to make sure you are getting the best deal.

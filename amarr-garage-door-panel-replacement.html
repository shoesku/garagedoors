Replacing An Amarr Garage Door Panel

Why Replace a Panel?

Garage door panels may need to be replaced for several reasons. Over time, they may become worn or damaged from regular use. They could also be affected by weather, pests, or debris. Additionally, a panel may need to be replaced if the look of the door is outdated or if a home’s aesthetic needs a modern update. Whichever is the case, replacing a panel is a simple and cost-effective way to drastically improve the look of a home.

What You Will Need

Before deciding to replace a panel, make sure you have all the necessary supplies. You will need a Phillips head screwdriver, a hammer, a drill, and a level. Additionally, it’s important to make sure you have the correct size of panel for your door. All Amarr garage doors come with a serial number that can be used to order the correct size and color of panel.

Step One: Removing the Old Panel

The first step in replacing a panel is to remove the old one. To do this, start by unscrewing the hinges and bolts that are holding the panel in place. Be sure to take note of their positions so the new panel can be put back the same way. Once all of the screws and hinges have been removed, carefully slide the panel out of the door.

Step Two: Installing the New Panel

Once the old panel has been removed, the new panel can be installed. Start by sliding the panel into the same place as the old one. Next, use a level to make sure the panel is aligned correctly. If necessary, use a hammer and drill to adjust the hinges to the correct height and angle. Finally, use the Phillips head screwdriver to secure the panel in place.

Step Three: Secure the Tracks

After the panel is in place, it’s important to make sure the tracks are securely attached. Check the tracks for any loose screws or bolts. If any are found, use the screwdriver to tighten them. Additionally, use the level to make sure the tracks are in the correct position.

Step Four: Test the Door

Once the tracks are secured, the door should be tested to make sure it’s fully functional. Start by pressing the garage door opener to see if the door opens and closes smoothly. If it does, it’s a sign that the panel has been installed correctly.

Conclusion

Replacing an Amarr garage door panel is a simple and cost-effective way to drastically improve the look of a home. The process is straightforward and can be done in a few easy steps. First, remove the old panel and install the new one. Next, make sure the tracks are secure. Finally, test the door to make sure it’s fully functional. With the right supplies and a bit of patience, homeowners can successfully replace a panel and enjoy the results for years to come.

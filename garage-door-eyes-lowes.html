What are Garage Door Eyes?

Garage door eyes are sensors that are fitted above the garage door that detect obstructions while the garage door is closing. The door will automatically stop and reverse when an obstruction is detected. The sensors are connected to the door operator, so when the sensors detect an obstruction, the door operator stops and reverses the door. Garage door eyes are an important safety measure to ensure that no one is injured by the closing door.

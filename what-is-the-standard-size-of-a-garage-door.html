What Is The Standard Size Of A Garage Door?

Garage doors come in a range of sizes and configurations, so it can be difficult to know what the standard size of a garage door is. The standard size of a residential garage door is 8 feet by 7 feet, but there are several other sizes which are available. The most popular sizes are 9 feet by 7 feet and 10 feet by 7 feet. Depending on the size of your garage and the size of your vehicle, you may need to select a different size to ensure that your vehicle can fit comfortably inside the garage.

Why is the Standard Size 8 Feet by 7 Feet?

The standard size of 8 feet by 7 feet is the most popular size because it is the minimum size required to fit most cars. This is the size recommended by most manufacturers because it allows you to have plenty of space to move around the garage as well as enough room to store a vehicle. This size also allows for plenty of space to hang tools and store items. Some people may choose to go with a larger size if they have a larger vehicle or plan to use the garage for additional storage.

What Are the Benefits of Standard Sizes?

The main benefit of a standard size garage door is that it is easier to install and maintain. Standard size doors are typically sold as a kit with all the necessary parts, making it easier to assemble. Additionally, the components of standard sized garage doors are more widely available and easier to find replacements for if needed. This makes them a more cost effective choice for many homeowners.

What Are the Downsides of Standard Sizes?

The downside of standard sizes is that they may not always fit a specific application. If you have a large vehicle or plan to use the garage for additional storage, you may need to purchase a larger size to ensure that your vehicle or items can fit comfortably inside the garage. Additionally, standard sizes may not be the best choice for those who are looking for a more aesthetically pleasing look, as they may not match the overall design of the home.

What Other Sizes Are Available?

In addition to the standard size of 8 feet by 7 feet, there are several other sizes available. The most popular sizes are 9 feet by 7 feet and 10 feet by 7 feet. If you have a larger vehicle or need more storage space, these sizes may be more suitable for your needs. Additionally, there are custom sizes available, though these may be more expensive and will require professional installation.

What Are the Benefits of Custom Sizes?

The main benefit of custom sizes is that they are more likely to fit a specific application. If you have an unusual size requirement or want a more aesthetically pleasing look, custom sizes may be the best choice. Additionally, custom sizes can be designed to meet the needs of specific individuals or applications, such as those with limited space or those who require a higher level of security.

What Are the Downsides of Custom Sizes?

The main downside of custom sizes is that they are typically more expensive than standard sizes. This is because they require more materials and labor to construct and install. Additionally, they may require professional installation, which can further increase the cost. Additionally, custom sizes are not always available and may be difficult to find replacements for if needed.

Conclusion

The standard size of a residential garage door is 8 feet by 7 feet, but there are several other sizes which are available. The most popular sizes are 9 feet by 7 feet and 10 feet by 7 feet. If you have a larger vehicle or plan to use the garage for additional storage, you may need to select a different size to ensure that your vehicle can fit comfortably inside the garage. Additionally, custom sizes may be the best choice for those who are looking for a more aesthetically pleasing look or have a specific size requirement.

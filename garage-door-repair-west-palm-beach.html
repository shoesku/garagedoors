What Are the Most Common Garage Door Repairs?

The most common type of garage door repair West Palm Beach involves the springs. These are the components that help to open and close the door. Other common issues include problems with the tracks or the opener. The tracks can become misaligned, which will make it difficult for the door to open and close properly. The opener may also need to be repaired or replaced if it is not working correctly.

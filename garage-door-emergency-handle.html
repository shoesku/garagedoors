How To Use A Garage Door Emergency Handle

Garage doors are an important part of our everyday lives. They provide security and convenience, helping to make our homes safer and more organized. But, as with any kind of machinery, garage doors can sometimes malfunction or be difficult to operate. That’s why it’s important to know how to use a garage door emergency handle. This handle is located on the inside of the garage door and can be used to manually open the door in case of an emergency.

Types of Emergency Handles

There are two types of emergency handles for garage doors. The first is a manual handle, which is usually located on the inside of the door. This handle is usually a long metal rod that can be pulled down to manually open the door. The second type is an electric handle which is connected to an electrical circuit and can be activated by pressing a button or flipping a switch.

Using a Manual Emergency Handle

Using a manual emergency handle is a simple process. First, locate the handle on the inside of the garage door. It should be visible, and should be easy to reach. Once the handle is located, grasp it firmly and pull down. This will cause the door to open, allowing you to exit the garage. When you are ready to close the door, simply push the handle back up and the door should close.

Using an Electric Emergency Handle

Using an electric emergency handle is slightly more complicated than using a manual handle. First, locate the switch or button that activates the handle. This should be easy to find, and should be clearly labeled. Once the switch or button is located, press it. This will cause the handle to be activated, allowing you to open the door. When you are ready to close the door, simply press the switch or button again and the door should close.

Safety Precautions

When using an emergency handle, it is important to take a few safety precautions. First, make sure to keep the handle out of the reach of children. If the handle is within reach of small children, they could accidentally open the door, leading to potential danger. Second, be sure to check the handle regularly to make sure that it is functioning properly. If the handle is not working, it could lead to a dangerous situation in an emergency.

Benefits of an Emergency Handle

Using an emergency handle for your garage door can provide a number of benefits. First, it can provide a quick and easy way to open the door in an emergency. This can be especially useful if the power is out, or if the door’s opener fails. Additionally, an emergency handle can provide an extra layer of security. With the door open, it will be more difficult for an intruder to gain entry to your home.

Conclusion

Knowing how to use a garage door emergency handle can be a valuable skill, providing you with a quick and easy way to open the door in an emergency. There are two types of emergency handles available: manual and electric. It is important to take safety precautions when using an emergency handle, and to regularly check that it is functioning properly. With the right precautions, an emergency handle can provide an extra layer of security and peace of mind.

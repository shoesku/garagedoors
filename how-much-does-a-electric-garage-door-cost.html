How Much Does An Electric Garage Door Cost?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

If you’re looking to upgrade your garage door to an electric version, you may be wondering how much this will cost. Electric garage doors are a worthwhile investment, as they can save you time and money in the long run. In this article, we’ll explain how much an electric garage door typically costs and what factors affect the price.

Average Cost of an Electric Garage Door

The average cost of an electric garage door is between $800 and $1,400. This price range includes the installation of the door, the opener, and all necessary components. Depending on the type of door you choose and any extras you add, the cost could range from $400 to more than $3,000.

Factors that Affect the Price

The type of door you choose will be the biggest factor in the cost of your electric garage door. There are several types of doors available, ranging from steel to wood. Steel doors are the most popular, as they are durable and relatively inexpensive. Wood doors, on the other hand, are more expensive, but they can add a unique look to your home. The size of the door will also affect the cost.

Another factor that can significantly increase the cost of an electric garage door is any additional features or accessories you choose to install. Some features, such as automatic lighting, can add convenience and safety. You may also want to add a keypad for added security. These features will add to the overall cost of the door.

Finally, the cost of installation will depend on the complexity of the job. For example, if the door needs to be modified to fit the opening, this will add to the cost. It’s best to have a professional install the door to ensure the job is done correctly.

Benefits of an Electric Garage Door

Although electric garage doors can be more expensive than manual doors, they offer several advantages. For starters, they are more convenient and can be operated with the push of a button. This makes it much easier to open and close the door, especially if you have heavy items to move in and out of the garage.

Electric garage doors are also more secure than manual doors, as they can be locked from the inside or outside. This makes them a great choice for homes in areas with higher crime rates. Finally, electric garage doors are easier to maintain than manual doors, as they don’t require regular lubrication or other maintenance.

Should You Invest in an Electric Garage Door?

Electric garage doors can be a great investment if you’re looking for added convenience and security. They are also more energy efficient than manual doors, as they don’t require manual opening and closing. While the initial cost may be higher, the benefits can be worth the investment in the long run.

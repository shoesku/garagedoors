If you are looking to have your garage door cables replaced, the cost can vary dramatically depending on the type of door you have and the complexity of the job. On average, however, it is usually between $150 and $250 to have a professional come out and do the job correctly. This cost may be higher if the cables are broken or have been damaged beyond repair. It is important to have the cables replaced as soon as possible as they are an important safety feature and can help prevent potential injuries.

The Cost of Different Types of Garage Door Cables

The cost of replacing a garage door cable will depend on the type of door you have. If you have a chain drive garage door, the cost of replacing the cable can be between $150 and $350. A belt drive garage door cable replacement will cost between $190 and $450. The cost of replacing a screw drive garage door cable can be between $170 and $400. If you are replacing a torsion spring cable, the cost can range from $200 to $600.

What to Consider When Replacing a Garage Door Cable

When replacing a garage door cable, there are a few things you should consider. One is the type of cable you are replacing. Different types of cables have different costs associated with them. It is important to do research and find out the cost of the type of cable you need before you make a purchase. Additionally, you should consider the complexity of the job. If the cable is more difficult to replace, it may require more time and effort, and therefore, cost more.

Another thing to consider is the size of the cable. Make sure you get the correct size cable to fit your garage door. If you get a cable that is too small, it can cause problems with the operation of the door. On the other hand, if you get a cable that is too large, it can be difficult to install, and the cost may be more. Finally, you should consider the brand and quality of the cable. It is important to get a quality cable that is designed to last and can handle the weight and force of the door.

DIY vs Professional Garage Door Cable Replacement Cost

When it comes to replacing your garage door cable, you can either do it yourself or hire a professional to do the job for you. Doing it yourself can save you some money, but it is important to note that it can be a dangerous job. It is important to read and follow the instructions carefully and use the correct tools. If you are not comfortable or confident in your DIY ability, it is best to hire a professional for the job.

Hiring a professional to replace your garage door cable will cost more, but it is usually the safest and most efficient option. Professionals have the right tools and experience to do the job correctly and safely. Additionally, they can provide advice on the best cable for your door and install it correctly. The cost of hiring a professional will depend on their experience, the type of cable you need, and the complexity of the job.

Finding the Right Professional for the Job

When looking for a professional to replace your garage door cable, it is important to research and find the right one for the job. Look for one that has experience and is knowledgeable about the type of door you have and the type of cable you need. It is also important to make sure they are insured and licensed. Finally, it is important to compare costs and get quotes from a few professionals to make sure you are getting the best price.

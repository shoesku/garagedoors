Types of 12 x 7 Garage Doors

The first thing to consider is what kind of 12 x 7 garage door you want. There are many types of garage doors to choose from, including overhead, roll-up, and sliding garage doors. Each type of door has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it’s important to choose the right one for your needs.

Overhead garage doors are the most common type of garage doors. They are easy to install, relatively inexpensive, and provide good security. The downside is that they require a lot of maintenance, as they are prone to rust, denting, and other wear and tear.

Roll-up garage doors are also popular, as they are easy to install and provide more security than overhead garage doors. They require less maintenance than overhead doors, but they are more expensive. They are also more difficult to open, as they require more force to open them.

Sliding garage doors are great for those who want a modern look for their home. They are easy to install, require very little maintenance, and provide good security. The downside is that they are more expensive than other types of garage doors, and they take up more space.

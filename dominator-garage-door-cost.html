What Goes Into the Cost of a Dominator Garage Door?

When determining the cost of a Dominator garage door, there are several factors to consider. The size of the door is one of the most important factors, as larger doors are more expensive than smaller ones. The material used to construct the door is also important, as some materials such as steel, aluminum, and wood are more expensive than others. Additionally, the type of door you choose, such as a roll-up, tilt-up, or sectional door, will also affect the cost. Finally, the features you choose, such as insulation, remote access, and windows, will also influence the cost.

What is the Average Cost of a Dominator Garage Door?

The average cost of a Dominator garage door in 2023 can range anywhere from $500 to over $5000, depending on the size, material, and features you choose. For example, a 16-foot tall, single panel steel door with no windows or other features will typically cost around $500. On the other hand, a 20-foot tall, double panel wood door with windows and remote access will typically cost around $5000. It is important to note that these prices are just estimates, as the actual cost of a Dominator garage door will depend on the specific size, material, and features you choose.

How To Fix Garage Door Cable Broken On One Side

Introduction

Having a garage door cable break can be a very stressful situation. You're standing in the middle of your driveway, unable to open your garage door and your car is stuck inside! Not to worry though, in this article, we'll discuss the steps necessary to repair your garage door cable when it is broken on one side.

What Causes Garage Door Cable Breakage?

Garage door cables are made of metal and are subject to rust and wear over time. If your door has been installed for a while and hasn't been properly maintained, then it is likely that the cable or cables have become brittle with age and have worn out. This can cause the cables to snap when they are put under strain. It is important to regularly inspect your garage door cables to ensure that they are in good condition and to replace them if they are showing signs of wear.

How to Fix a Garage Door Cable When It Is Broken on One Side

If you have identified that the garage door cable is broken on one side, then the first step is to remove the broken cable from the door. This is done by loosening the nuts and bolts that secure the cable to the door, and then carefully removing the cable from the track. Once the cable is removed, you will need to purchase a new cable of the same size and type as the old one. Be sure to measure the length of the cable before buying the replacement so that it fits correctly.

Installing the New Cable

Once you have the new cable in hand, the next step is to install it. This is done by attaching the new cable to the door using the same nuts and bolts that were used to secure the old cable. Then, the cable should be placed into the track and the nuts and bolts should be tightened securely. Once the cable is in place, it should be tested to make sure that it is secure and can handle the strain of opening and closing the door.

Conclusion

Fixing a garage door cable when it is broken on one side is not an overly difficult task, but it does require some basic knowledge of how the door and its components work. Be sure to exercise caution when working on the door, as it is a heavy and potentially dangerous piece of machinery. If you are unsure of how to replace the cable, it is best to contact a professional for assistance. With the right tools and a bit of patience, you can have your garage door up and running again in no time.

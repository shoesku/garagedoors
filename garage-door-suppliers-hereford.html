Types of Garage Doors

The most common types of garage doors in the market include sectional, roller, and tilt-up doors. Sectional doors are made up of a number of sections that can be easily opened and closed. These doors are generally made of steel and are available in different sizes and colors. They are also easy to install and maintain.

Roller doors, on the other hand, are the most popular type of garage door. They are made up of several rollers and are available in different sizes and colors. These doors are designed for easy installation and are quite durable. They are ideal for both residential and commercial applications.

Tilt-up doors are also a popular type of garage doors. They are made from a single panel, which is attached to a frame and can be opened and closed with the help of a motor. They are usually made of aluminum and are available in different sizes and colors. They are easy to install and maintain and are ideal for commercial applications.

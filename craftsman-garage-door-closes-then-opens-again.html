Solving Craftsman Garage Door Problems: What To Do When It Closes Then Opens Again

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

As a homeowner, it’s often the small, everyday problems that can really stress us out. One of the most common issues is when a garage door closes then opens again, seemingly out of its own accord. It’s an annoying problem and can be quite intimidating, as it can often seem like a sign of a much more serious problem.

Fortunately, if you’re experiencing this issue with your Craftsman garage door, the problem is likely to be minor and can be easily fixed. This article will take a look at the likely causes of this problem and how you can solve it.

What Causes a Garage Door to Close Then Open Again?

The most common cause of a Craftsman garage door closing then opening again is that the safety sensors at the bottom of the door are not aligned correctly. These sensors are designed to stop the door from closing if there’s an object in the way, or if the door is not correctly installed. When the sensors are misaligned or blocked, the door will close then open again.

Another possible cause of this issue is that the remote control is not synced correctly with the door. This can be caused by a faulty remote or by a weak battery. If you’ve recently changed the battery, it’s worth making sure that it is correctly installed as this can be a common cause of the problem.

How to Fix a Craftsman Garage Door Closing Then Opening Again

Check the Safety Sensors

The first step is to check the safety sensors at the bottom of the door. Make sure that they are aligned correctly and that there are no objects blocking them. If there are, this could be the cause of the problem. If the sensors are correctly aligned, try covering one of them with a piece of paper. If the door closes and then reopens again, this confirms that the sensors are the cause of the problem.

Check the Remote Control

If the sensors are correctly aligned, the next step is to check the remote control. Make sure that it is correctly synced with the door. If the battery is weak, replace it with a new one. If the battery is fine, try resyncing the remote by following the instructions in the user manual. This should solve the issue.

Check the Motors and Cables

If the problem persists, it’s worth checking the motors and cables. Make sure that the cables are not loose or damaged, as this could be the cause of the issue. If the cables seem fine, then the motors may need to be replaced. This is a job that’s best left to an experienced technician.

Conclusion

A Craftsman garage door closing then opening again can be an annoying problem, but it is usually caused by a minor issue. If the safety sensors are misaligned or blocked, or the remote control is not synced correctly, then this can cause the door to close and reopen. Fortunately, these issues can be easily fixed by following the steps outlined above. If the problem persists, it’s best to call a professional technician.

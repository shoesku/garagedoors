How To Fix A Garage Door Opener That Closes Part Way Then Opens

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Garage door openers are convenient, but sometimes they can malfunction and cause a lot of frustration. One of the most common issues is when your garage door opener closes part way then opens again. This can be incredibly annoying, and it can also be concerning for your safety. Fortunately, though, it is possible to fix this issue. Keep reading to learn more about what you can do to get your garage door opener to close all the way.

Check the Photo Eyes

One of the first things that you should check is the photo eyes. The photo eyes are the two sensors on either side of the door that detect when something is in the way of the garage door. If the photo eyes are misaligned, blocked, or dirty, this can cause the garage door to open again after it has closed part way. To check the photo eyes, make sure that they are aligned and facing each other. You should also make sure that there is nothing blocking them, such as leaves or debris. Finally, you should clean them with a damp cloth and make sure that they are not covered in dust or dirt.

Check the Springs and the Tracks

The next thing that you should check is the springs and the tracks. Make sure that the springs are not too tight or too loose. If they are too tight or too loose, this can interfere with the garage door's ability to close all the way. You should also check the tracks for any debris or obstructions, and make sure that they are greased. This will help to ensure that the garage door can move freely and close all the way.

Check the Limit Settings

Another thing that you should check is the limit settings. These are the settings on the motor that control how far the garage door will open and close. If the limit settings are too high, this can cause the garage door to stop closing part way and then open again. To adjust the limit settings, you will need to consult the manual for your particular garage door opener. If you do not have the manual, you can usually find it online.

Check the Wiring

The wiring of the garage door opener can also be a cause of the issue. Make sure that all of the wires are in good condition and securely connected to the motor. If there are any frayed or broken wires, you should replace them as soon as possible. This will help to ensure that your garage door opener is working properly.

Check the Remote

Another possible cause of the issue is the remote. Make sure that the batteries are fresh and that the remote is functioning properly. You should also check for any interference that could be blocking the signal from the remote to the motor. This could include other wireless devices, such as a cell phone or a wireless router. If you notice any interference, you should move the remote to a different location.

Check the Motor

Finally, you should check the motor of the garage door opener. If the motor is malfunctioning, this can cause the garage door to close part way and then open again. To check the motor, you should consult the manual for your particular garage door opener. If you do not have the manual, you can usually find it online. Alternatively, you can contact a professional garage door repair company to inspect and repair the motor.

Conclusion

If your garage door opener closes part way and then opens again, it can be incredibly annoying. Fortunately, though, there are a few things that you can do to fix this issue. First, you should check the photo eyes, the springs and the tracks, the limit settings, the wiring, the remote, and the motor. If you are unable to fix the issue yourself, you should contact a professional garage door repair company for assistance.

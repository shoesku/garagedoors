What is Electric Garage Door Height?

Electric garage door height is an important factor to consider when installing a new garage door. It is the distance from the ground to the top of the garage door. This measurement must be precise in order for the door to open and close properly.

It is important to choose the right electric garage door height for your home. This is because the wrong height can cause issues such as the garage door not opening or closing properly, or it could result in a gap between the door and the frame. Therefore, it is essential to ensure that you measure the electric garage door height correctly.

How to Measure Electric Garage Door Height

In order to measure the electric garage door height correctly, you need to measure from the ground to the top of the door. This should be done with a measuring tape or a laser measure. It is important to make sure that the measurements are precise and accurate.

It is also important to consider the type of garage door that you have. For example, if you have a manual garage door, then you need to measure the height from the ground to the top of the door frame. However, if you have an electric garage door, then you need to measure the height from the ground to the top of the door panel.

Factors to Consider When Choosing Electric Garage Door Height

There are several factors that you need to take into consideration when choosing the right electric garage door height. The first factor is the height of your garage ceiling. The height of the garage door should be equal to or slightly lower than the height of the ceiling.

Another factor to consider is the type of opener that you are using. If you are using a chain-driven opener, then you need to ensure that your door height is lower than the height of the chain. If you are using a belt-driven opener, then you need to ensure that your door height is higher than the height of the belt.

Finally, you need to consider the size of your vehicle. If you have a large vehicle, then you need to ensure that the door height is tall enough for your vehicle to fit through without any issue. If you have a small vehicle, then you need to ensure that the door height is low enough for your vehicle to fit through without any issue.

Conclusion

Choosing the right electric garage door height is essential for ensuring that your garage door opens and closes properly. It is important to measure the height precisely and accurately, and to take into consideration the height of your garage ceiling, the type of opener that you are using and the size of your vehicle. Doing this will ensure that you have the right electric garage door height for your home.

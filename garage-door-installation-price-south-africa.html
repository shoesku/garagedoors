Garage Door Installation Prices In South Africa For 2023

Garage doors are an essential part of any home and can greatly increase the security and privacy of your home. Having a well-functioning garage door is important, and if you are looking to install a new one, it is important to know the garage door installation price in South Africa for 2023.

The cost of a garage door installation in South Africa will depend on several factors, such as the size of the door, the type of materials used, the complexity of the installation, and the labour costs. The cost can range from as low as R2,000 to as high as R20,000, depending on the above factors.

Size of the Door

The size of the door is one of the most important factors that will affect the price of the installation. Larger doors will require more materials and labour, and as such, will cost more. Generally, the larger the door, the higher the price.

Type of Materials Used

The type of materials used in the installation will also affect the cost. Steel and aluminum are the most common materials used for garage doors, and they tend to be more expensive than other materials such as wood or vinyl.

Complexity of the Installation

The complexity of the installation will also have an effect on the cost. If the installation requires extra work, such as reinforcing the wall for the door, this will add to the cost.

Labour Costs

The labour costs for installing a garage door will vary from company to company, and will depend on the complexity of the job. Generally, a professional installation will cost more than a DIY installation.

Other Costs to Consider

In addition to the cost of the door and the installation, there are other costs to consider. These include the cost of any additional hardware, such as hinges, locks, and handles, which will add to the overall cost.

Average Cost of Garage Door Installation in South Africa

Overall, the average cost of garage door installation in South Africa for 2023 is between R2,000 and R20,000. This cost covers the door, the materials, the labour, and any additional hardware.

Conclusion

Installing a garage door is an important part of any home, and it is important to know the cost. The cost of installation in South Africa for 2023 will depend on the size of the door, the type of materials used, the complexity of the installation, and the labour costs. Generally, the cost of installation is between R2,000 and R20,000.

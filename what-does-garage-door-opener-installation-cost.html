What Does Garage Door Opener Installation Cost In 2023?

Introduction

Garage door opener installation cost can vary greatly depending on the type of opener you choose, the complexity of the installation and the number of extras you may need. In 2023, many homeowners are looking for garage door openers that are reliable, durable, and fit their budget. This article will provide an overview of what you can expect to pay for opener installation, as well as the different types of openers available and factors that can influence installation costs.

Types of Garage Door Openers

There are several different types of garage door openers available on the market in 2023. Chain drive openers are the most common, and they are typically the most affordable. They are also the loudest and least energy efficient option. Belt drive openers are quieter, and they are more expensive than chain drive openers. Jackshaft openers are the most expensive option, but they are also the most powerful and quietest.

Factors That Affect Installation Cost

The cost of a garage door opener installation in 2023 will depend on several factors. The type of opener selected will have the most significant effect on the cost. More complex installations, such as those involving multiple doors or special features, will also be more expensive. Additional components such as keypads, sensors, and remote controls can also drive up the cost of installation.

Average Installation Costs

The average cost for a garage door opener installation in 2023 can range from around $250 to over $500, depending on the type of opener, the complexity of the installation, and the number of extras you choose. Chain drive openers are typically the most affordable and can cost around $250 to install. Belt drive openers are slightly more expensive and can range from $300 to $400. Jackshaft openers are the most expensive option and can cost anywhere from $400 to $500.

DIY Installation

For those looking to save money, there are several garage door opener kits available in 2023 that allow homeowners to install the opener themselves. These kits come with detailed instructions and all of the necessary components, and they can be installed in a few hours. DIY installation is a great way to save money, but it should only be attempted by experienced DIYers or those with experience working with electrical components.

Additional Considerations

When selecting a garage door opener in 2023, it is important to consider the type of door you have. If you have a light-duty door, a basic chain drive opener should be sufficient. For heavier doors, such as those made of steel or wood, a more powerful opener such as a belt drive or jackshaft may be necessary. Additionally, think about the number of doors you need opened and whether you need any additional features such as a keypad or remote control.

Conclusion

Garage door opener installation cost in 2023 can vary greatly depending on the type of opener you choose and the complexity of the installation. Chain drive openers are typically the most affordable option and can cost around $250 to install. Belt drive openers are slightly more expensive and can range from $300 to $400. Jackshaft openers are the most expensive option and can cost anywhere from $400 to $500. Homeowners can also save money by installing the opener themselves with a DIY kit.

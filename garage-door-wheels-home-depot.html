What Are Garage Door Wheels?

Garage door wheels are one of the most important components of any garage door. They are used to roll the door open and close, and are essential for the proper functioning of a garage door. They are made of various materials, such as aluminum, steel, and plastic, and come in different sizes and shapes. Depending on the type of garage door you have, you will likely need different types of wheels.

Types of Garage Door Wheels

Garage door wheels come in a variety of styles and sizes. Different door types require different wheels, so it's important to choose the right ones for your door. Some of the most common types of wheels include: roller wheels, sliding wheels, and pivot wheels. Each type has its own benefits and drawbacks, so it's important to consider your door type and the desired function before selecting your wheels.

Roller Wheels

Roller wheels are one of the most common types of wheels used with garage doors. They are made of a lightweight material, such as aluminum or steel, and are designed to roll along the track of the door. They are great for light-duty doors, as they are easy to install and maintain, and are relatively affordable. However, they are not suitable for high-traffic doors, as they can wear down quickly under constant use.

Sliding Wheels

Sliding wheels are another popular option for garage doors. They are made of a heavy-duty material, such as stainless steel, and are designed to slide along the track of the door. They are great for heavy-duty doors, as they are more durable than roller wheels and can handle more frequent use. However, they are more expensive than roller wheels and require more maintenance.

Pivot Wheels

Pivot wheels are a newer type of wheel and are becoming increasingly popular. They are made of a strong material, such as stainless steel, and are designed to pivot along the track of the door. They are great for high-traffic doors, as they are extremely durable and require minimal maintenance. However, they are more expensive than other types of wheels and require a more complicated installation process.

How to Install Garage Door Wheels

Installing garage door wheels is a relatively easy task that can be completed in a few steps. First, you will need to measure the width and height of your door and purchase the correct size of wheels. Next, you will need to attach the wheels to the door using bolts or screws. Finally, you will need to check the wheels to make sure they are properly aligned and secured. Once you have completed these steps, your door should be ready to use.

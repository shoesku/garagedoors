Single Garage Door Dimensions

Standard single-car garage doors come in a variety of sizes and can be found in widths ranging from 8-feet to 18-feet wide. The most common single-car garage door size is 8-feet wide by 7-feet tall, which is ideal for a one-car garage. The next most common size is 9-feet wide by 7-feet tall, and then 10-feet wide by 7-feet tall, and so on.

The height of the door is usually the same regardless of the width of the door, but this does depend on the manufacturer. Generally, single-car garage doors range between 6-feet and 8-feet tall.

Double Garage Door Dimensions

Double-car garage doors are typically wider than single-car garage doors and can be found in widths ranging from 16-feet to 24-feet wide. The most common double-car garage door size is 16-feet wide by 7-feet tall. The next most common size is 18-feet wide by 7-feet tall, and then 20-feet wide by 7-feet tall, and so on.

The height of double-car garage doors usually range between 6-feet and 8-feet tall, although this does depend on the manufacturer.

Garage Door Dimensions in CM

Garage door dimensions are typically listed in feet and inches, but it is easy to convert those measurements to centimeters. To convert feet and inches to centimeters, simply multiply the number of feet by 30.48, and then add the number of inches multiplied by 2.54.

For example, a single-car garage door that is 8-feet wide and 7-feet tall would be 243 cm wide and 213 cm tall. A double-car garage door that is 16-feet wide and 7-feet tall would be 486 cm wide and 213 cm tall.

Choosing the Right Garage Door Size

When choosing the right size garage door, it is important to consider the size of your garage, as well as the size of your vehicle. You want to make sure that you have enough clearance for your vehicle to fit comfortably in the garage and not scrape against the walls or ceiling.

You should also consider the height of your garage door. If the door is too tall, it may increase the amount of energy that is lost through the door, and it may also be more difficult to open and close.

Installing a Garage Door

When installing a garage door, it is important to make sure that the door is properly supported. Most garage door manufacturers recommend that the door be supported by a minimum of two vertical tracks and two horizontal tracks. The tracks should be securely fastened to the walls of the garage and should be level with each other.

It is also important to make sure that the door is properly balanced. This will ensure that the door operates smoothly and efficiently. A properly balanced door will also help to reduce the amount of wear and tear on the door and its components.

Conclusion

Garage door dimensions are an important factor to consider when shopping for a new garage door. It is important to choose a size that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

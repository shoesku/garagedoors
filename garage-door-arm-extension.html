What is a Garage Door Arm Extension?

A garage door arm extension is an aftermarket accessory that can be installed on a garage door to increase its length. The extension is a metal arm that attaches to the existing garage door arm, extending it by a few inches to increase the reach of the garage door opener. The arm extension is available in a variety of sizes and materials, so you can choose the one that best suits your needs.

The Benefits of Installing a Garage Door Arm Extension

Installing a garage door arm extension has several benefits. First, it can increase the reach of the garage door opener, allowing you to open the garage door from farther away. This can be especially helpful if you have a large garage or long driveway. Second, it can improve the security of your garage, as it can make it harder for intruders to access the garage. Finally, it can provide additional convenience, as you can open the garage door without having to leave your car.

How to Install a Garage Door Arm Extension

Installing a garage door arm extension is relatively simple and can usually be done in an hour or two. First, you will need to find an arm extension that fits your existing garage door arm. Once you have found the right arm extension, you will need to attach it to the existing arm. This usually requires some basic tools, such as a drill, screwdriver, and wrenches. Once the arm extension is attached, you can then attach the opener to the arm extension and test it to make sure it is working properly.

Types of Garage Door Arm Extensions

Garage door arm extensions are available in a variety of materials, including aluminum, steel, and plastic. Aluminum arm extensions are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, making them ideal for use in damp or humid environments. Steel arm extensions are more durable and can withstand more weight, making them ideal for larger garage doors. Plastic arm extensions are the least expensive option, but they are also the least durable.

Where to Buy Garage Door Arm Extensions

Garage door arm extensions are widely available online and at local hardware stores. When shopping for an arm extension, it is important to make sure that it is compatible with your existing garage door arm. Additionally, it is important to make sure that the arm extension is of high quality, as low-quality arm extensions can break or malfunction.

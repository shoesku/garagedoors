5 Reasons Your Garage Door Keeps Opening Back Up

Introduction

It's a common problem for homeowners - you press your garage door opener, the door starts to go down and then stops, before opening back up again. It's a major nuisance, and can be dangerous if you're trying to close the door when a car is coming fast up the driveway. But thankfully, it's usually a relatively easy problem to fix. Here are the five most common reasons why your garage door keeps opening back up.

1. Broken Springs

Most garage door openers are designed to sense when the door is too heavy or too light. If the springs in your door are damaged or worn, it may cause the door to be too heavy for the opener to handle, and it will open back up. The solution is to replace the springs or have them repaired by an experienced garage door technician.

2. Worn Out Opener

If your garage door opener is older, it may not be able to handle the weight of the door, or it may be malfunctioning due to a worn out part. You may need to have the opener serviced or replaced. Additionally, you may need to have the door balanced to ensure that it is not too heavy for the opener.

3. Problems With the Sensors

Modern garage door openers are equipped with sensors that detect if something is blocking the path of the door. If the sensors are not properly aligned, it can cause the door to open back up. You can usually fix this problem by adjusting the sensors, or by having an experienced garage door technician do it for you.

4. Issues With the Limit Settings

When you first install your garage door opener, you have to set the limit settings. This tells the opener how far down and how far up it should go when you press the button. If the limit settings are not properly adjusted, the door may go too far down and then open back up. You can usually fix this problem by adjusting the limit settings.

5. Obstructions

If something is blocking the path of the door, it can cause it to open back up. Common obstructions include large items such as bicycles or furniture, or small items like rocks or toys. You will need to remove the obstruction to fix the problem. You should also periodically check for any obstructions that may have built up over time.

Conclusion

If your garage door keeps opening back up, it can be a major nuisance. Thankfully, it is usually a relatively easy problem to fix. The five most common reasons for this issue are broken springs, a worn out opener, problems with the sensors, issues with the limit settings, and obstructions. You can usually fix the problem yourself, or by having an experienced garage door technician do it for you.

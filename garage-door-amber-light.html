What is a Garage Door Amber Light?

A garage door amber light is a system of lights that are used to indicate when a door is open or closed. The system uses a combination of amber and white lights to indicate when a door is either open or closed. The amber light is used to indicate when the door is open, while the white light is used to indicate when the door is closed. The system is typically used in conjunction with a door opener, and is often found on both residential and commercial garage doors.

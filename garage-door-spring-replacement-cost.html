Having a functioning garage door is essential for keeping your home secure. If your garage door spring is broken, you will need to get it replaced as soon as possible. The cost of replacing a garage door spring can vary depending on the type of spring and the size of the door. In this article, we will cover the cost of replacing a garage door spring, what factors affect the cost, and tips for getting the best deal.

Types of Garage Door Springs

Garage door springs come in two types â€“ torsion and extension. Torsion springs are more secure and are typically found on newer garage doors, while extension springs are cheaper and more common on older garage doors. The type of spring you have will determine the cost of the replacement.

Torsion Springs

Torsion springs are the more expensive option, but they are also more secure and last longer than extension springs. They are mounted above the garage door and use tension to open and close the door. The cost of replacing a torsion spring will depend on the size of the door and the type of spring. Typically, torsion springs will cost between $150 and $400 to replace.

Extension Springs

Extension springs are cheaper and more common on older garage doors. They are mounted on either side of the garage door and use tension to open and close the door. The cost of replacing an extension spring will depend on the size of the door and the type of spring. Typically, extension springs will cost between $50 and $200 to replace.

Factors that Affect the Cost of Replacing a Garage Door Spring

There are several factors that can affect the cost of replacing a garage door spring. These include the size of the door, the type of spring, the complexity of the repair, and the local cost of labor. The size of the door will affect the cost because larger doors require larger springs that can be more expensive. The type of spring will also affect the cost as torsion springs are more expensive than extension springs. The complexity of the repair will also affect the cost as some repairs are more complex than others. Lastly, the local cost of labor will affect the cost as some areas have higher labor costs than others.

Tips for Getting the Best Deal on Garage Door Spring Replacement

When replacing a garage door spring, there are several tips you can use to get the best deal. First, shop around and compare prices from different companies. You should also ask for any discounts or promotions that may be available. Additionally, you should make sure to get a warranty for the replacement spring. Finally, you should read reviews of the company you are considering to make sure they have good customer service and offer quality products.

Conclusion

Replacing a garage door spring can be expensive, but there are several tips you can use to get the best deal. Always shop around and compare prices, ask for discounts or promotions, get a warranty, and read reviews before making a decision. With the right preparation and research, you can ensure you get the best price for your garage door spring replacement.

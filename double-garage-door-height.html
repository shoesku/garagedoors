Double Garage Door Height: What To Know In 2023?

In the year 2023, double garage door heights are a hot topic for many homeowners. Whether you're installing a new door or looking to replace an existing one, there are some important things to consider when it comes to double garage door height. This article will provide you with the information you need to make the best decision for your home.

Standard Double Garage Door Height

The standard double garage door height is 7 feet. This is the height of most two-car garage doors in the United States. However, depending on the manufacturer, the door could be slightly taller or shorter. If you are buying a new door, make sure to measure the opening of your garage to ensure you get the right size.

Custom Double Garage Door Height

If you are looking for a custom double garage door height, there are a few options available. First, you can have a door custom-built to fit your garage opening. This is the most expensive option, but it will ensure the door fits perfectly. Second, you can buy a kit that allows you to adjust the height of the door. This is a less expensive option, but you may need to hire a professional to help you with the installation.

Extra High Double Garage Door Height

If you are looking for an extra high double garage door height, you may need to look for a specialty manufacturer. These doors are typically 8 or 9 feet tall, and are designed for larger vehicles or to provide extra storage space. The cost of these doors is often much higher than standard doors, so make sure you get an accurate estimate before committing to a purchase.

Durability of Double Garage Door Heights

When it comes to double garage door heights, durability is an important factor to consider. Steel doors are often the best choice, as they are strong and durable. Make sure to choose a door with a thick gauge steel and a weatherproof coating to ensure it can stand up to the elements. Wood doors are also an option, but they will require more maintenance and may not last as long as steel.

Insulation of Double Garage Door Heights

Insulation is another important factor to consider when buying a double garage door height. Insulated doors can help keep your garage warm in the winter and cooler in the summer. The higher the R-value of the door, the better the insulation. Make sure to ask your supplier about the insulation of the door before you make your purchase.

Safety of Double Garage Door Heights

When it comes to double garage door heights, safety should be a top priority. Make sure the door has an automatic opener that is certified by Underwriters Laboratories (UL) or another safety organization. The door should also have a security system that will prevent unauthorized access. Finally, make sure the door is well-maintained so that it operates safely and smoothly.

Cost of Double Garage Door Heights

The cost of double garage door heights can vary significantly depending on the type of door you choose and the size of the opening. Steel doors are usually more expensive than wood doors, but they offer greater durability. Extra high doors may also cost more, so make sure you get an accurate estimate before committing to a purchase.

Conclusion

Double garage door heights are an important consideration for many homeowners in 2023. Make sure to measure your garage opening, choose the right door for your needs, and consider the cost, durability, insulation, and safety features before making a decision. This will help you get the best door for your home and the best value for your money.

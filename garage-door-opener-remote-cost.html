What You Should Know About Garage Door Opener Remote Cost In 2023

The cost of a new garage door opener remote can vary significantly depending on the features and brand. In 2023, the price can range from as low as $20 to as high as $200. The wide range of pricing can make it difficult to decide which garage door opener remote is right for you. In this article, we'll discuss the factors that influence the cost of a garage door opener remote and how you can make an informed decision when shopping for one.

Different Types of Garage Door Opener Remotes

When shopping for a garage door opener remote, you should first consider the type of opener you have. Most modern garage door openers use either a chain drive or belt drive system. Chain drive openers are the most common and are typically less expensive than belt drive openers. The type of opener you have will determine the type of remote you need. Chain drive openers use a universal remote, while belt drive openers require a specific remote model.

Features to Consider

When selecting a garage door opener remote, it is important to consider the features that you need. Some remotes offer additional features such as a keypad, battery backup, or a Wi-Fi connection. Depending on your needs, these additional features can significantly increase the cost of the remote. If you don't need these features, it may be more cost effective to opt for a basic model.

Brand Differences

The brand of the remote is also an important consideration when shopping for a garage door opener remote. There are many different brands available and each offers different features and prices. Generally speaking, more established brands will cost more than lesser known brands. It is important to research the different brands and read customer reviews before making a purchase.

Cost Factors to Consider

When considering the cost of a garage door opener remote, there are several factors to consider. First, you should consider the type of opener you have and the features that you need. Additionally, the brand of the remote can have a major impact on the cost. Established brands typically cost more than lesser known brands. Finally, the cost of installation and any additional features such as a keypad or battery backup should also be taken into consideration.

Buying Used or Refurbished

If you are looking to save money on a garage door opener remote, you may want to consider buying a used or refurbished model. Used and refurbished remotes can offer significant savings over buying a new model; however, it is important to make sure that the remote is in good working condition before making a purchase. Additionally, you should research the brand and read customer reviews before making a purchase.

Conclusion

The cost of a garage door opener remote can vary significantly depending on the type, features, and brand. In 2023, the price can range from as low as $20 to as high as $200. When shopping for a garage door opener remote, it is important to consider the type of opener you have and the features that you need. Additionally, the brand of the remote can have a major impact on the cost. Finally, you may want to consider buying a used or refurbished model to save money. By following these tips, you can make an informed decision when shopping for a new garage door opener remote.

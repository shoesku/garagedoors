Common Garage Door Problems

The first thing to understand is that garage doors are complex pieces of machinery, and many of the problems that can occur are related to the way they are installed, maintained, and operated. Some of the most common garage door problems in London include:

  • Damaged or broken springs
  • Incorrectly installed or faulty garage door openers
  • Misaligned tracks
  • Damaged or worn out rollers
  • Faulty safety sensors
  • Worn out seals

These are just some of the many problems that can occur with garage doors in London. It’s important to understand that some of these problems can be fixed easily, while others may require the assistance of a professional garage door repair company.

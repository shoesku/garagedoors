What Causes a Garage Door Not Opening All The Way?

There can be multiple reasons why a garage door won’t open all the way. One of the most common issues is a broken garage door spring. The spring is the mechanism that counterbalances the weight of the door and allows it to open and close. When the spring is broken, it usually means the door won’t open more than a few inches from the ground. This can cause a lot of inconvenience, as you’re unable to access your garage. Luckily, it’s easy to tell if the issue is with the garage door spring. If you can hear a loud snapping or popping noise when you try to open the door, it’s likely the spring is the culprit.

How to Fix a Garage Door Not Opening All The Way

If your garage door isn’t opening all the way, it’s best to try and fix it yourself before calling a professional. There are a few easy steps you can take that could solve the problem. The first step is to check the tracks. If the tracks are bent, loose, or otherwise damaged, it could be preventing the door from opening all the way. If you see any damage, try to straighten the tracks and make sure they’re properly secured. If the tracks look fine, then you should check the rollers. If the rollers are worn out, they won’t be able to support the door, which could cause it to stop opening. If the rollers appear to be in good condition, then you should check the springs.

Replacing the Garage Door Springs

If you’ve determined that the issue is with the springs, then you’ll need to replace them. You can purchase new garage door springs from a home improvement store. But before you purchase them, you should make sure you know the size of your springs. The size of the springs is determined by the weight of the door. If you don’t know the weight of your door, you can measure it with a tape measure. Once you know the size of the springs, you can purchase the correct replacements. Installing the new springs is a simple process and only requires basic tools.

Safety Tips for Working on a Garage Door

If you’re going to attempt to fix your garage door yourself, it’s important to remember to work safely. You should always wear protective gloves and eyewear to protect yourself from any debris or sharp edges. Make sure you’re working in a well-lit area, and never attempt to open the garage door while working on it. Additionally, if you’re not confident in your abilities, it’s best to call a professional to do the job. Fixing a garage door can be dangerous, and it’s important to remember to work safely.

Preventative Maintenance

The best way to prevent a garage door from not opening is to regularly maintain it. A simple way to do this is to lubricate the springs, tracks, and rollers every few months. This will help reduce wear and tear and keep the door running smoothly. Additionally, you should regularly check the tracks and rollers for any signs of damage. If you notice any issues, address them immediately to avoid major problems down the line. Regular maintenance will keep your garage door functioning properly and prevent it from not opening all the way.

Conclusion

If your garage door isn’t opening all the way, it can be a major inconvenience. Fortunately, there are a few steps you can take to diagnose and fix the problem. You should first check the tracks and rollers for any damage. If they appear to be in good condition, it’s likely an issue with the springs. Replacing the springs is a simple process, but it’s important to remember to work safely. Additionally, you should regularly maintain the door to prevent future problems. With the right knowledge and tools, you can easily fix a garage door not opening all the way.

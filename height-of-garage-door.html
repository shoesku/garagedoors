Tips For Choosing The Right Height Of Garage Door In 2023

If you're in the market for a new garage door in 2023, you're probably wondering what the right height is for your home. The height of a garage door is an important factor in ensuring that your garage is secure and that it looks good when it is closed. Choosing the right height of garage door can be tricky, so here are some tips to help you find the right fit for your home.

Consider Your Home's Size

The height of your garage door should be proportional to the size of your home. If your home is relatively small, then you may want to opt for a shorter garage door, such as one that is seven feet tall. However, if you have a larger home, then you may want to opt for a taller door, such as one that is eight feet tall. It's important to take the size of your home into consideration when choosing the right height of garage door.

Consider Your Home's Style

The style of your home can also help you determine the right height of garage door. For example, if you have a traditional style home, then you may want to opt for a classic garage door that is seven feet tall. However, if you have a modern home, then you may want to opt for a taller door, such as one that is eight feet tall. When choosing the right height of garage door, be sure to take into consideration the style of your home.

Consider Your Garage's Usage

The height of your garage door should also be based on the type of vehicle that will be entering and exiting your garage. If you have a tall SUV or truck, then you may want to opt for a taller door, such as one that is eight feet tall. This will give you more clearance when entering and exiting your garage. On the other hand, if you have a smaller vehicle, then you may want to opt for a shorter door, such as one that is seven feet tall.

Consider Your Budget

The height of your garage door will also have an impact on the cost of the door. Generally speaking, taller doors are more expensive than shorter ones. This is because taller doors require more materials and labor to install. If you're on a tight budget, then you may want to opt for a shorter door, such as one that is seven feet tall. However, if you have some extra money to spend, then you may want to opt for a taller door, such as one that is eight feet tall.

Consider Your Climate

The climate in your area can also play a role in determining the right height of garage door for your home. For example, if you live in an area with high winds, then you may want to opt for a taller door, such as one that is eight feet tall. This will help to keep the wind from blowing your garage door open. On the other hand, if you live in an area with mild winds, then you may want to opt for a shorter door, such as one that is seven feet tall.

Consider Your Home's Architecture

Your home's architecture can also impact the right height of garage door for your home. If your home has a classic look, then you may want to opt for a classic garage door that is seven feet tall. This will help to keep the look of your home consistent. However, if your home has a modern look, then you may want to opt for a taller door, such as one that is eight feet tall. This will help to keep the look of your home modern.

Consider Your Garage Door's Opening System

The height of your garage door can also be impacted by the type of opening system you choose. If you opt for a manual opening system, then you may want to opt for a shorter door, such as one that is seven feet tall. This will make it easier for you to open and close the door manually. On the other hand, if you opt for an automatic opening system, then you may want to opt for a taller door, such as one that is eight feet tall. This will make it easier for the automatic system to open and close the door.

Conclusion

Choosing the right height of garage door can be tricky, but these tips can help you find the right fit for your home. Be sure to consider your home's size, style, usage, budget, climate, and architecture when choosing the right height of garage door. Additionally, be sure to consider the type of opening system you choose when choosing the right height of garage door. With these tips, you can be sure to find the perfect height of garage door for your home in 2023.

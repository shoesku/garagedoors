Having a garage door opener is a great convenience, but it is important to understand the dimensions and requirements for installing a jackshaft garage door opener. This type of garage door opener is a great option for those who want to save space, since it mounts on the side of the garage door rather than overhead. With a few simple measurements, you can make sure your new jackshaft opener will fit perfectly.

What is a Jackshaft Garage Door Opener?

A jackshaft garage door opener is a type of opener that is mounted on the side of the garage door, rather than overhead. This type of opener is ideal for those who have low ceilings or limited space above the garage door. It is also a great option for those who want to avoid having a bulky opener hanging from the ceiling. Jackshaft openers are generally more expensive than traditional overhead openers, but they are well worth the cost for those who need the space savings.

Jackshaft Garage Door Opener Dimensions

Jackshaft openers come in a variety of sizes and styles. The most important dimension to consider is the headroom, which is the space between the ceiling and the top of the garage door. Generally speaking, most jackshaft openers require a minimum of 12 inches of headroom, but some models may require more. You should measure your headroom before purchasing an opener to make sure it will fit.

In addition to the headroom, you need to consider the side-room, which is the space between the side of the garage door and any walls or other obstructions. Most jackshaft openers require at least 4 inches of side-room, but some models may require more. Again, it is important to measure the side-room before purchasing an opener to ensure a perfect fit.

Benefits of Jackshaft Openers

Jackshaft openers are great for those who have limited space above the garage door. They are also more powerful than traditional overhead openers, and they require less maintenance since they do not have to be adjusted as often. Additionally, they come with a variety of features such as remote access, keyless entry, and battery backup. These features make it easy to open and close your garage door no matter where you are.

Jackshaft Garage Door Opener Installation

Installing a jackshaft opener is a fairly simple process, but it is important to make sure everything is installed correctly to ensure the safety and security of your home. Most openers come with detailed instructions, but it is always best to have a professional handle the installation. This will ensure that the opener is installed properly and that it meets all local building codes.

Conclusion

Jackshaft garage door openers are a great option for those who need to save space. They are more powerful than traditional openers, and they require less maintenance. However, it is important to understand the dimensions and requirements for installing a jackshaft opener in order to make sure it fits in your garage. With a few simple measurements, you can make sure your new opener will fit perfectly.

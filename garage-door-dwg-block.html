What is a DWG Block?

DWG stands for “drawing workgroup” and is a format for digital engineering drawings. These drawings are used in designing and constructing various components for homes, including garage doors. A DWG block is a single component of a garage door drawing, and it can be used to help you decide on the best design for your door. You can find the DWG blocks for the most popular models of garage doors online, or you can ask your manufacturer to provide them.

Why Use DWG Blocks?

Using DWG blocks to design and construct your garage door can be beneficial in a number of ways. For starters, it allows you to get creative with your design without having to worry about the technical aspects. How to Select the Right DWG Blocks?

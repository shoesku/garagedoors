What Are Garage Doors?

Garage doors are an essential part of any home – they provide a secure place to store cars, bikes, and other belongings. The Sims 4 includes a few basic garage doors, but for those who want to customize their homes, there are a variety of additional garage door downloads available. In this article, we’ll cover the basics of downloading and installing new garage doors for The Sims 4.

Where to Download Garage Doors

The official Sims 4 website has a section dedicated to downloadable content (DLC) where players can find new items to add to their game. Here, you’ll find a variety of garage door designs to choose from, including modern and classic styles. Additionally, there are several fan sites that offer a wider selection of garage doors for The Sims 4. Some of these sites are free, while others require a subscription.

How to Download and Install Garage Doors

Once you’ve found the garage doors you want to use in The Sims 4, the next step is to download and install them. The first step is to save the file to your computer. Once the file is saved, open The Sims 4 launcher and click on the “Downloads” tab. Then, click “Add Download” and select the file you saved earlier. The selected file will now be added to your Downloads list.

Adding Garage Doors to Your Home

Now that you have the garage doors downloaded, the next step is adding them to your home. To do this, open The Sims 4 and select the house you want to add the doors to. Once you’re in the house, you’ll need to enter “Build Mode” by pressing the F2 key. Once in Build Mode, select the wall where you want to add the garage door, then click on the garage door icon in the top-right corner of the screen. Select the garage door you downloaded earlier, then click “Place” to add it to your home.

Customizing the Garage Doors

The Sims 4 includes a variety of customization options for garage doors. You can change the color, texture, and style of the door. You can also adjust the size to fit the space. To do this, select the door you want to customize, then click on the “Edit” button in the top-right corner of the screen. Here, you can adjust the size and other settings.

Choosing a Garage Door Opener

Once you have the door installed, you’ll need to choose an opener. The Sims 4 includes several different types of openers, including automatic and manual. You can also choose to have a keypad on the outside of the door or an intercom system. To choose an opener, open the “Build Mode” menu and select the “Garage Doors” tab. Here, you can choose the type of opener you want.

Adding a Garage Door Remote

Once you have the opener installed, you’ll need to add a remote. The Sims 4 includes a few basic remotes, but you can also download additional remotes from the official Sims 4 website or from fan sites. To add the remote, open the “Build Mode” menu and select the “Garage Doors” tab. Here, you can choose the type of remote you want to add.

Conclusion

Garage doors are an essential part of any home, and The Sims 4 offers a variety of ways to customize them. You can download additional garage doors from the official Sims 4 website or from fan sites, and then install them in your house. Additionally, you can choose a garage door opener and remote to complete your setup. With the right setup, you can create a secure and stylish garage for your Sims.

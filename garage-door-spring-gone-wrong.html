Garage Door Spring Gone Wrong: What To Do?

Introduction

Doing regular maintenance and inspections of your garage door is important to keep it functioning well. However, even with regular maintenance, your garage door can still break. One common issue is a broken garage door spring. When this happens, it is important to know what to do in order to make sure your door is repaired properly and safely. This article will provide some tips on what to do when your garage door spring goes wrong.

What Causes a Broken Garage Door Spring?

A garage door spring is an important part of your door that helps lift the door as it opens and closes. It is under a lot of pressure and can break if it is not properly maintained. Some common causes of a broken garage door spring include corrosion, damage from weather, rust, and wear and tear over time. It is important to inspect the springs regularly to make sure they are not damaged or worn.

Safety Tips for Repairing a Broken Garage Door Spring

When you are repairing a broken garage door spring, it is important to take the necessary safety precautions. Make sure to wear protective gear, such as goggles and gloves, while working on the door. Do not attempt to repair the door while it is open, as this can be dangerous. If you are unsure of how to fix the door, it is best to call a professional garage door repair service.

Identifying the Problem

If you have identified that your garage door spring has gone wrong, it is important to determine the cause of the problem. If the spring is broken, you will need to replace it. If the spring is not broken, you may be able to adjust it or lubricate it to get it working again. If you are unsure of the cause of the problem, it is best to call a professional for assistance.

Replacing the Spring

If you need to replace the garage door spring, it is important to choose the right type of spring for your door. Make sure to measure the size of the existing spring and buy the same size spring to replace it. Before you begin, make sure to unplug the power to the door and wear safety gear as mentioned earlier. It is also important to use the right tools for the job. If you are unsure of how to replace the spring, it is best to call a professional.

Testing the Spring

Once you have replaced the spring, it is important to test the door to make sure it is functioning properly. You can do this by opening and closing the door a few times. If the door is not working properly, you may need to make adjustments or call a professional for assistance.

Preventive Measures

Once you have fixed the garage door spring, it is important to take preventative measures to make sure the problem does not occur again. Make sure to inspect the door regularly to check for any signs of wear and tear. You should also lubricate the door and springs regularly to reduce friction and wear. If you are unsure of how to inspect and maintain the door, it is best to call a professional.

Conclusion

A broken garage door spring can be a major inconvenience, and it is important to take the necessary steps to fix it properly and safely. Make sure to inspect and lubricate the door regularly, and if you are unsure of how to repair the door, it is best to call a professional for assistance. Taking preventive measures can help ensure that your door remains in good working order for years to come.

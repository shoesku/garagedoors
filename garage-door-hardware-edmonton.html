What is Garage Door Hardware?

Garage door hardware are the components that make up your garage door system, enabling you to open and close your door with ease. They come in a variety of styles and sizes and can be found in both residential and commercial buildings. Some of the most common types of hardware include rollers, springs, latches, locks, and tracks. Each of these components has its own unique purpose and must be installed correctly in order for the door to function properly. In Edmonton, there are a number of companies that specialize in the installation of garage door hardware, so it is important to do your research before selecting a particular company.

Types of Garage Door Hardware

Rollers are one of the most important components of any garage door system. They are used to guide the door along the track and ensure that it opens and closes properly. In Edmonton, there are several types of rollers available, including steel, nylon, and plastic. Steel rollers are the strongest and most durable, but they are also the most expensive. Nylon rollers are the most common and come in a variety of sizes and colors. Plastic rollers are the least expensive but are not as durable as steel or nylon.

Garage Door Springs

Garage door springs are used to help open and close the door. They are typically made from galvanized steel and are attached to the sides of the door. Springs are designed to take the weight of the door and help it open and close smoothly. The types of springs available include torsion, extension, and tension. Each of these springs has its own unique purpose and must be installed correctly in order for the door to function properly.

Garage Door Latches and Locks

Garage door latches and locks are used to secure the door in place when it is closed. They are typically made from steel, brass, or aluminum and can be purchased in a variety of styles. The most common types of locks are keyed locks, which require a key for entry, and deadbolts, which are locked from the outside. Latches can also be keyed or deadbolted.

Garage Door Tracks

Garage door tracks are used to guide the door along its path. They are typically made from steel and can be found in a variety of widths and lengths. The most common types of tracks are straight and curved. Straight tracks are typically used for single- or double-wide doors, while curved tracks are used for larger doors.

Garage Door Openers

Garage door openers are used to open and close the door automatically. They are typically powered by either a battery or an AC power source. Depending on the model, they may also be able to be programmed to open and close at certain times. Most models are equipped with safety features such as sensors that detect motion and stop the door from closing in the event of an obstruction.

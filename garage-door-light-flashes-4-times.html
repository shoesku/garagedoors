Do you have a garage door that flashes four times when you press the button? If so, you’re not alone. Many garage door owners experience this same issue and it can be a real pain. But understanding what’s causing it can help you get your garage door up and running again in no time. Here’s what you need to know about four times flashing lights on your garage door.

What Causes a Garage Door to Flash Four Times?

When a garage door flashes four times, it is usually caused by one of two issues. The first is a broken or faulty photo eye. This is a device that is installed near the bottom of the garage door and is used to detect any obstructions in the door’s path. If the photo eye is blocked or broken, the door will not close correctly and the light will flash four times. The second issue is a broken or faulty remote control. If the remote control is not working correctly, the door may not open or close correctly and the light will flash four times.

What Should You Do If the Garage Door Flashes Four Times?

If your garage door flashes four times, the first thing you should do is check the photo eye. Make sure that it is not blocked or broken. If it is, then you should replace it as soon as possible. If the photo eye is fine, then you should check the remote control. Make sure that the batteries are still good and that it is working correctly. If it is not, then you should replace it.

How Can You Prevent Your Garage Door From Flashing Four Times?

To prevent your garage door from flashing four times, you should regularly check the photo eye and the remote control. Make sure that they are both in good working order and that they are not blocked. You should also keep the area around the photo eye clear of debris and obstructions. Finally, you should test the remote control and photo eye regularly to make sure that they are both working correctly.

Conclusion

When your garage door flashes four times, it can be annoying and inconvenient. But understanding what’s causing it can help you get your garage door up and running again in no time. Make sure to regularly check the photo eye and the remote control to prevent four times flashing lights from occurring. If all else fails, contact a professional garage door technician for assistance.

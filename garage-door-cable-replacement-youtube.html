Why Should You Replace Your Garage Door Cable?

Garage door cables are an essential part of making sure your garage door operates smoothly and safely. If the cables become worn or frayed, they can snap or break, leaving your door stuck in an open or closed position. If the door is stuck open, it can be a hazard for your family and for anyone who may enter the garage. If the door is stuck closed, it can prevent you from accessing your car or any items you may have stored in the garage.

Replacing your garage door cable regularly is an important part of taking care of your garage door. Cables should be inspected every few months, and should be replaced if any signs of wear and tear are visible. Doing this regularly can help prevent any sudden breaks and ensure that your garage door is working properly.

The Tools You’ll Need to Replace Your Garage Door Cable

Before beginning the repair, you’ll need to make sure you have the right tools. The tools you’ll need to replace your garage door cable include:

  • Socket wrench
  • Mallet
  • Vise grips
  • New garage door cable

You may also need to purchase a new cable depending on the type of garage door you have. Be sure to double check the type of cable you need before beginning your repair.

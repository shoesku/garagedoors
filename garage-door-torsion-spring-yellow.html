Garage door torsion springs are an essential part of a garage door’s operation. They are the component responsible for opening and closing the garage door. Without them, the door wouldn’t be able to move. Because of this, it’s important to understand how garage door torsion springs work and how to take care of them.

What is a Garage Door Torsion Spring?

A garage door torsion spring is a metal spring found on the top of the garage door. It works in conjunction with the door’s opener to open and close the door. The spring is wound with tension and when the opener releases the tension, the door moves. Torsion springs come in different sizes, depending on the weight of the garage door.

What is a Yellow Garage Door Torsion Spring?

A yellow garage door torsion spring is a type of torsion spring that is treated with a yellow powder coating. This coating helps protect the spring from corrosion, wear and tear, and other environmental factors. It also helps the spring last longer. Yellow torsion springs are typically used on heavier garage doors, as they are stronger and more durable than other types of torsion springs.

How Long Do Yellow Garage Door Torsion Springs Last?

The average lifespan of a yellow garage door torsion spring is between 7 and 10 years. However, this lifespan can be affected by a number of factors, such as the amount of use the door receives, the frequency of maintenance, and the environment it is exposed to. With proper maintenance and care, the lifespan of a yellow torsion spring can be extended.

How to Maintain Yellow Garage Door Torsion Springs?

To ensure your yellow garage door torsion springs last as long as possible, it’s important to maintain them regularly. This includes cleaning the springs, lubricating them, and inspecting them for wear and tear. It’s also important to check the tension of the springs regularly, as this can affect how the door operates. If the tension is too high or too low, it can cause the door to malfunction.

When Should You Replace Yellow Garage Door Torsion Springs?

It’s important to replace yellow garage door torsion springs as soon as you notice any signs of wear and tear. This includes signs of rust, cracks, or fraying. If the springs are too loose or too tight, they should also be replaced. It’s best to have the springs replaced by a professional, as they have the expertise and tools to do the job correctly.

Where to Buy Yellow Garage Door Torsion Springs?

Yellow garage door torsion springs can be purchased from most hardware stores, home improvement stores, and online retailers. It’s important to buy the right size spring for your garage door, as this will ensure it operates correctly. Additionally, you should only buy springs that are made from high-quality materials and come with a warranty.

Conclusion

Garage door torsion springs are essential for the operation of a garage door. Yellow torsion springs are a type of torsion spring that is treated with a yellow powder coating for added protection. These springs typically last between 7 and 10 years, but with proper maintenance and care, the lifespan can be extended. It’s important to replace yellow torsion springs when signs of wear and tear appear, and they can be purchased from most hardware stores and online retailers.

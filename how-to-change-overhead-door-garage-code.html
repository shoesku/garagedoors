How To Change Your Overhead Door Garage Code

Introduction

If you have an overhead door garage and are looking to change the code, you are in luck! It is actually quite easy to do and can be done in a few simple steps. The process for changing your garage code is the same regardless of the model or brand of your garage door. Whether your garage is an older model or a newer one, the process to change the code is the same. This article will walk you through the steps you need to take to change your overhead door garage code.

Step 1: Locate Your Garage Opener

The first step in changing your overhead door garage code is to locate your garage opener. Most garage door openers are located near the ceiling of the garage. In some cases, they may be mounted on the wall. Some models may also be mounted on the side of the garage door. For safety purposes, it is important to make sure that the power is turned off before attempting to locate the garage opener.

Step 2: Remove the Cover

Once you have located your garage opener, the next step is to remove the cover. This is usually done by unscrewing the screws that hold the cover in place. Depending on the model of your garage, the screws may be located on the top or the side of the opener. There may also be a keyhole located on the cover. If this is the case, use a key to unlock the cover.

Step 3: Locate the Code

Once the cover is removed, you will be able to locate the code. There should be a small panel on the side of the opener with a series of numbers. This is your garage code. Make sure to write down the code so that you can refer to it if needed.

Step 4: Change the Code

Now that you have located the code, the next step is to change the code. Depending on the model of your overhead door garage, you may have to press a series of buttons or enter a new code. Refer to the manual for your specific model for instructions on how to do this. Once you have entered the new code, make sure to press the enter button to save the code.

Step 5: Reattach the Cover

Once you have changed the code, the last step is to reattach the cover. Make sure to line up the screws with the holes in the cover. Once you have secured the cover back in place, make sure to turn the power back on. Your new code should now be in effect.

Conclusion

Changing your overhead door garage code is easy to do and can be done in just a few simple steps. The process is the same regardless of the brand or model of your garage door. Make sure to refer to the manual for your specific model for instructions on how to change the code. Once you have changed the code, make sure to test it by entering the new code to make sure that it is working correctly. Following these steps will help ensure that your garage remains secure and protected.

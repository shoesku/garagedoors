Welcome to 2023 and the future of garage doors. Nowadays, garage door opening screens are becoming increasingly popular with homeowners who want to add convenience and security to their home. But what exactly are these screens and how do they work? In this article, we’ll explore what these screens are, the many benefits they have to offer, and how you can install them in your own home.

What Is a Garage Door Opening Screen?

A garage door opening screen is a type of protective device that is installed on the inside of a garage door. It is made up of two layers of fabric that are held together with a frame and spring system. The fabric is usually made of a strong material such as vinyl, which is designed to withstand any weather conditions. When the garage door is opened, the screen is automatically pulled down, and when the door is closed, the screen is automatically pulled up.

The purpose of the screen is to provide an extra layer of security for your home by preventing people from being able to see inside the garage when the door is open. This means that if someone is casing your home, they will not be able to see what is inside the garage and can't plan a robbery. Additionally, the screen also helps to keep bugs and other pests out of the garage when it is open.

The Benefits of a Garage Door Opening Screen

The most obvious benefit of having a garage door opening screen is the added security it provides. By preventing people from seeing inside the garage when the door is open, it reduces the risk of a burglary. Additionally, by keeping bugs and other pests out, it helps to keep your garage and home free from any unwanted pests.

Another benefit of having a garage door opening screen is that it can help to keep the inside of the garage cooler in the summer months. This is because the screen acts as an extra barrier between the inside of the garage and the outside temperatures. This can help to lower your energy bills, as the air conditioning won’t have to work as hard to cool the garage.

Finally, a garage door opening screen can also add a touch of style to your home’s exterior. Many of these screens come in a variety of colors and styles, so you can find one that best matches your home’s overall design. This can help to give your home a more modern, updated look.

How to Install a Garage Door Opening Screen

If you’re looking to install a garage door opening screen in your home, the process is relatively simple. The first step is to measure the area of your garage door where the screen will be installed. Once you have your measurements, you can purchase a screen that fits the size of your door.

Next, you will need to install the frame for the screen. This is usually done with a few screws and brackets. Once the frame is in place, you will then need to attach the fabric to the frame with the springs and hooks that come with the screen.

Finally, you will need to attach the screen to the garage door. This is usually done with a few screws and bolts. Once everything is in place, you can then open and close the garage door to make sure the screen is working properly.

Conclusion

Garage door opening screens are becoming increasingly popular with homeowners who want to add convenience and security to their home. These screens provide an extra layer of security by preventing people from seeing inside the garage when the door is open. Additionally, they can help to keep the inside of the garage cooler in the summer months, and can also add a touch of style to the home’s exterior. Installing a garage door opening screen is relatively simple, and can be done by anyone with basic DIY skills.

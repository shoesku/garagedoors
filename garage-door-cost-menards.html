If you're in the market for a new garage door, Menards is a great place to shop. With a wide selection of sizes, styles, and materials to choose from, Menards has something for everyone. The cost of a garage door from Menards will depend on the size, style, and material you choose. Here, we’ll provide an overview of what you can expect to pay for a new garage door from Menards.

Single-Wide Garage Doors

For single-wide garage doors, Menards offers a variety of sizes and styles. The cost of a single-wide garage door from Menards can range from $200 to $1,000, depending on the size, style, and material. The most popular size is 8' x 7', which is available in steel, fiberglass, and wood, and typically costs between $300 and $400. If you need a larger size, such as 9' x 7', expect to pay a bit more. For example, a 9' x 7' steel garage door from Menards typically costs between $500 and $600.

Double-Wide Garage Doors

For double-wide garage doors, Menards offers a variety of sizes and styles. The cost of a double-wide garage door from Menards can range from $400 to $1,500, depending on the size, style, and material. The most popular size is 16' x 7', which is available in steel, fiberglass, and wood, and typically costs between $600 and $800. If you need a larger size, such as 18' x 7', expect to pay a bit more. For example, an 18' x 7' steel garage door from Menards typically costs between $800 and $1,000.

Specialty Garage Doors

If you're looking for something a bit more unique than the standard sizes and styles, Menards also offers specialty garage doors. These doors can be customized to fit any size or style, and are available in steel, fiberglass, and wood. The cost of a specialty garage door from Menards can range from $400 to $2,000, depending on the size, style, and material. If you want a custom-made door that's made from a high-quality material, expect to pay at least $1,000.

Installation Costs

In addition to the cost of the garage door itself, you'll also need to factor in the cost of installation. Menards offers professional installation services for all of their garage doors. The cost of installation will depend on the size and type of door, but you can expect to pay anywhere from $100 to $500. It's important to note that professional installation is the safest and most effective way to ensure that your garage door is properly installed and functioning properly.

Additional Costs

In addition to the cost of the door itself and the cost of installation, there may be additional costs associated with your purchase. For example, if you need to replace the existing track or springs, you'll need to factor in the cost of the parts. You may also need to purchase additional hardware, such as hinges or rollers, if your new door is not compatible with your existing hardware. You should also factor in the cost of any additional features, such as keyless entry.

Conclusion

Menards offers a wide selection of garage doors in a variety of sizes, styles, and materials.

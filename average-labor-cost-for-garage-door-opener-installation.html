Average Labor Cost For Garage Door Opener Installation In 2023

Installing a garage door opener is a common job for the do-it-yourselfer, but it can also be a complex job for a professional. The cost of installation will depend on the type of opener you choose and the amount of labor involved. The following is an overview of the average labor cost for garage door opener installation in 2023.

Basic Installation Costs

For a basic installation, you can expect to pay anywhere from $150 to $300. This will include the cost of the opener, labor, and any additional materials that may be needed. This is a good price range for a standard door opener with a basic installation. It may also include some basic wiring and programming.

Advanced Installation Costs

For a more advanced installation, you can expect to pay anywhere from $400 to $750. This will include the cost of the opener, labor, and any additional materials that may be needed. This is a good price range for a more complex installation such as a high-lift opener, or a more complex wiring and programming job. It may also include additional features such as keyless entry or motion sensors.

Professional Installation Costs

For a professional installation, you can expect to pay anywhere from $800 to $1,500. This will include the cost of the opener, labor, and any additional materials that may be needed. This is a good price range for a professional installation that includes additional features such as backup power systems, additional safety features, and extensive programming.

Additional Costs

In addition to the above costs, there may be additional materials or services that will add to the total cost. This may include additional wiring, additional sensors, or additional programming. You may also need to purchase additional remotes or keypads for additional control. Be sure to factor in these additional costs when estimating the total cost of installing your garage door opener.

Factors That Affect Cost

The cost of installing a garage door opener can vary depending on a number of factors. These include the type of opener you choose, the complexity of the installation, and the amount of labor involved. It is important to get multiple quotes from different installers before making a decision. This will help ensure that you get the best price for the job.

DIY Installation

If you are a do-it-yourselfer, you may be able to install your own garage door opener. This is a great way to save money, but it is important to make sure you understand the process before attempting it. Be sure to read the installation instructions carefully and make sure you have the necessary tools and materials before beginning the job. If you are not comfortable with the installation process, it is best to leave it to a professional.

Conclusion

The cost of installing a garage door opener can vary depending on the type of opener you choose and the complexity of the installation. For a basic installation, you can expect to pay anywhere from $150 to $300. For a more advanced installation, you can expect to pay anywhere from $400 to $750. For a professional installation, you can expect to pay anywhere from $800 to $1,500. Be sure to factor in additional costs when estimating the total cost of the installation.

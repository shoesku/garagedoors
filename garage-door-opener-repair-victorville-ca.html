Common Problems With Garage Door Openers

The most common problem that Victorville residents experience with their garage door openers is that the motor will not start. In addition, some Victorville homeowners may experience problems with the sensors that control the opening and closing of the door. If the sensors are not working properly, the door may not open or close properly.

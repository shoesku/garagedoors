Types of Garage Door Motors

Before you can buy a garage door motor for sale in Port Elizabeth, you need to know what type of motor is most suitable for your needs. There are four main types of garage door motors, each designed for different applications. These include trolley motor, chain drive motor, belt drive motor, and screw drive motor. Each type of motor has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it’s important to understand the different types before making a purchase.

Trolley Motor

Trolley motors are the most popular type of garage door motor and are usually used for single-car garages. These motors move the door along a track, and are usually the most affordable type of motor. They are easy to install and maintain, and can be used with either manual or automatic garage door openers.

Chain Drive Motor

Chain drive motors are another popular type of garage door motor and are often used in two-car garages. These motors use a chain to move the door along a track, and are usually more expensive than trolley motors. These motors require regular maintenance and can be noisy, but they are very reliable and are often used with automatic garage door openers.

Belt Drive Motor

Belt drive motors are the most expensive type of garage door motor, but they are also the quietest and most reliable. These motors use a belt to move the door along a track and are usually used in large garages. They require less maintenance than chain drive motors, but they can be more expensive to purchase.

Screw Drive Motor

Screw drive motors are the least popular type of garage door motor, but they are still a viable option for some applications. These motors use a threaded rod to move the door along a track and are usually used in commercial applications. They are usually the most expensive type of motor, but they are also the most reliable and require minimal maintenance.

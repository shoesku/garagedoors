What Are the Different Types of Garage Door Prices in Durban?

The first thing you’ll need to decide is what type of garage door you want. There are several types available, each with their own unique benefits. Here’s a quick overview of the different types of garage doors, and their associated prices:

  • Roller Doors – These are the most common type of garage door and are made from steel or aluminium. They are generally the most affordable option, with prices starting from around R2500.
  • Sectional Doors – These are made from several different panels that are hinged together. They are more expensive than roller doors, with prices starting from around R5000.
  • Wooden Doors – These are the most expensive option, but they offer the most aesthetically pleasing look. Prices start from around R15000.
  • Automatic Doors – Automatic doors are becoming increasingly popular in Durban, as they offer convenience and security. Prices start from around R10000.

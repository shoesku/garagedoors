What’s The Average Cost Of A 16 X 7 Garage Door In 2023?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

It’s no surprise that the cost of a garage door in 2023 can be quite expensive. The average price of a 16 x 7 garage door can range anywhere from $1,000 to more than $3,000. The price of these doors depends on several factors, including materials, construction, insulation, and design.

Most 16 x 7 garage doors are made of steel, aluminum, or wood. Steel doors are the most common option, and they tend to be the most durable. Plus, steel doors require minimal maintenance and provide excellent security. However, steel doors are also the most expensive option.

Aluminum Garage Doors

Aluminum garage doors are lighter and less expensive than steel doors. They also provide decent security and require minimal maintenance. The downside is that aluminum is not as strong as steel and can be damaged more easily.

Wood Garage Doors

Wood garage doors offer the most elegant and classic look. They also provide excellent insulation and soundproofing. However, wood doors require more maintenance than steel or aluminum doors, and they tend to be the most expensive option.

Insulated Garage Doors

Insulated garage doors are designed to keep the temperature inside the garage more comfortable. These doors are usually made of steel and filled with foam insulation. Insulated garage doors are more expensive than non-insulated doors, but they can save you money in the long run by helping to reduce your energy costs.

Design Options

The design of your garage door can also affect the price. If you’re looking for a unique look, you can choose from a variety of materials, colors, and finishes. You can even customize your door with wood paneling, windows, and decorative hardware. If you opt for a custom design, be prepared to pay more.

Garage Door Openers

If you want a garage door opener, you’ll need to factor in the cost of the opener and installation. You can choose from various types of openers, including chain drive, belt drive, and direct drive openers. Chain drive openers are the most common and least expensive option, while belt drive and direct drive openers are more expensive but quieter.

Hiring a Professional Installer

It’s important to hire a professional to install your garage door. Professional installation will ensure that your door is properly installed and functioning correctly. Depending on the complexity of the installation, you can expect to pay anywhere from $200 to $400 for professional installation.

The Bottom Line

As you can see, the cost of a 16 x 7 garage door in 2023 can range significantly, depending on the materials, construction, insulation, design, and installation. If you’re looking for a quality door at a reasonable price, it’s important to do your research and compare prices. You should also factor in the cost of any additional features and accessories, such as a garage door opener.

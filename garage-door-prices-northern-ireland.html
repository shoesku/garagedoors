The Average Cost of a Garage Door in Northern Ireland

The average cost of a garage door in Northern Ireland is between £850 and £1400. This cost includes the door itself, installation, and any additional materials needed. Of course, the actual cost will depend on the size of the door and its materials. For example, a wooden garage door costs more than one made of steel or aluminium. Additionally, the cost can vary depending on the features and style of the door.

The Different Types of Garage Doors Available in Northern Ireland

In Northern Ireland, there is a wide range of garage doors on offer. Some of the most popular types of garage doors include sectional, roller, up and over, and side-hinged. Each type of door has its own unique benefits, so it’s important to consider all of the options before making a decision. For example, sectional garage doors are generally easier to install, while roller doors are more affordable.

Additional Costs to Consider When Buying a Garage Door in Northern Ireland

In addition to the cost of the door itself, there are a few other costs to consider when buying a garage door in Northern Ireland. If you’re installing the door yourself, then you’ll need to factor in the cost of these materials.

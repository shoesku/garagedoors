How To Adjust The Reversal Mechanism On A Chamberlain Garage Door

Introduction

When it comes to keeping your garage and home secure, having a garage door that is functional is a must. It is also important to have a garage door that is well-maintained, and that includes checking the reversal mechanism of your Chamberlain garage door. The reversal mechanism is an important safety feature that allows the door to automatically stop and reverse when it senses something in its path. This is essential for protecting those who may come in contact with the door while it is operating. If your Chamberlain garage door is not in the correct position or is not working properly, it is important that you adjust the reversal mechanism. This tutorial will show you how to do this in a few simple steps.

Step 1: Identify the Reversal Mechanism

The first step in adjusting the reversal mechanism on your Chamberlain garage door is to identify the mechanism itself. The reversal mechanism is typically located at the bottom of the door, just above the track. It is typically a small box with a lever and a set of sensors. It is important to locate this mechanism as it is the component that will be adjusted.

Step 2: Adjust the Reversal Mechanism

Once you have located the reversal mechanism, you can begin adjusting it. To do so, you will need to first turn off the power to the garage door. This is essential in order to ensure your safety and to prevent any damage to the door or mechanism. Once the power has been turned off, you can begin adjusting the mechanism. To do this, you will need to move the lever to the desired position. This will adjust the sensors and allow the door to reverse when it senses something in its path.

Step 3: Test the Door

Once the adjustment has been made, it is important to test the door to ensure that it is functioning properly. To do this, you will need to re-engage the power to the door. Once this is done, you can open the door and place an object in its path. The door should automatically reverse and stop once it senses the object. If the door does not reverse, you can adjust the reversal mechanism further until it does.

Conclusion

Adjusting the reversal mechanism on your Chamberlain garage door is an important step in maintaining its proper functioning. This tutorial has provided you with the steps necessary to do this. It is important to remember to always turn off the power to the garage door before attempting to make any adjustments. Additionally, always remember to test the door after making any adjustments to ensure that it is functioning properly. Following these steps will ensure that your Chamberlain garage door continues to operate safely and effectively.

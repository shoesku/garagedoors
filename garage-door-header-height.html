Garage Door Header Height: Everything You Need To Know In 2023

The garage door header height is one of the most important measurements you’ll need to consider when installing a new garage door. This measurement is the distance between the top of the door and the ceiling of the garage. It is important to get the measurement right for several reasons, including aesthetics, safety, and security. In this article, we’ll look at the importance of the garage door header height, what you should consider when selecting a door, and how to measure it accurately.

Why is Garage Door Header Height Important?

The garage door header height is important for several reasons. First, it affects the appearance of the door. The door should fit perfectly in the space provided, with no gaps or overhangs. If the door is too short, it won’t look right and could be dangerous. On the other hand, if the door is too tall, it won’t fit properly, leaving you with a gap that can cause problems with the weatherstripping and insulation.

In addition to aesthetics, the garage door header height is also important for safety and security. If the door is too short, it can be easily pushed open from the outside, allowing burglars to gain access to your home. On the other hand, if the door is too tall, it can be difficult to open and close, making it a hassle to use.

What to Consider When Selecting a Garage Door?

When selecting a garage door, it’s important to consider the size of the door and the size of the opening. Generally, you should select a door that is at least 1.5 inches wider than the width of the opening. This will help ensure that the door opens and closes properly. Additionally, you should consider the weight of the door when selecting a garage door. Heavier doors require more power to open and close, so you’ll need to make sure that your garage door opener can handle the weight of the door.

You’ll also need to consider the type of door you’re installing. Different types of doors require different header heights. For example, roll-up doors require a lower header height than overhead doors. It’s important to make sure that you’re installing the right type of door for your opening.

How to Measure Garage Door Header Height?

Measuring the garage door header height is important to ensure that the door fits properly. To measure the height, you’ll need to measure from the floor to the ceiling of the garage. You should also measure from the top of the door to the ceiling. If the measurements are different, you’ll need to adjust the door accordingly. Additionally, you should measure the width of the opening and the door to make sure that the door will fit properly.

When measuring for a roll-up door, you should measure from the floor to the ceiling, then subtract 1.5 inches. This will give you the correct header height for the door. For an overhead door, you should measure from the floor to the ceiling, then add 1.5 inches. This will give you the correct header height for the door.

What is the Standard Garage Door Header Height?

The standard garage door header height is 7 feet. This is the height that is generally recommended for most homes. However, it’s important to note that this may not be the right height for your home. If the measurements of your opening are different, you’ll need to adjust the door accordingly.

Conclusion

Garage door header height is an important measurement to consider when installing a new garage door. It’s important to get the measurement right for safety, security, and aesthetics reasons. When selecting a door, you should consider the size of the door, the size of the opening, and the weight of the door. Additionally, you should measure the height from the floor to the ceiling, then adjust the door accordingly. The standard garage door header height is 7 feet, but it’s important to measure your opening before purchasing a door.

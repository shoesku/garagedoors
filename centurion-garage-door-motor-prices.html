Introduction

Are you considering upgrading your garage door motor in 2023? You’re probably aware that the prices can vary quite a bit depending on the features and the brand you choose. The cost of the motor itself is only one of several factors to consider when shopping for a garage door motor. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the various aspects of garage door motor prices and explain how they impact your final decision.

Factors Affecting Garage Door Motor Prices

The price of a garage door motor can vary greatly depending on the type and features. Here are some of the main factors that will affect the cost of your motor:

  • Type of Motor – There are two main types of motors: chain-driven and belt-driven. Chain-driven motors are typically cheaper, while belt-driven motors are more expensive.
  • Size of Motor – The larger the motor, the more expensive it will be. Motors that are too small for the door size may have difficulty opening and closing the door, so it’s important to select the right size for your needs.
  • Power of Motor – Motors with higher horsepower will be more expensive than those with lower horsepower.
  • Brand – The brand you choose can have a big impact on the price. Premium brands usually cost more, while budget brands are usually more affordable.
  • Features – Motors with additional features such as remote control operation, automatic close, and security sensors will be more expensive than those without.

Average Prices for Garage Door Motors

The average price for a garage door motor in 2023 is around $500 to $1,000, depending on the type, size, and features. This is for a mid-range motor with basic features. For a premium motor with all the bells and whistles, you can expect to pay upwards of $2,000. On the other hand, budget motors can start as low as $200.

Centurion Garage Door Motors

Budget Models

If you’re looking for an affordable option, Centurion offers several budget models. These motors are typically chain-driven and have basic features such as manual open/close and a manual force adjustment. Prices for budget Centurion motors range from $200 to $500.

Mid-Range Models

The mid-range models from Centurion are a good choice for those who want more features than the basic budget models. These motors are typically belt-driven and may have features such as remote control operation, automatic close, and security sensors. Prices for mid-range Centurion motors range from $500 to $1,000.

Premium Models

For those who want the best of the best, Centurion offers several premium models. These motors are usually belt-driven and come with all the bells and whistles, such as soft start/stop, timer-to-close, and anti-jamming technology. Prices for premium Centurion motors range from $1,000 to $2,000.

Conclusion

Garage door motor prices can vary widely depending on the type, size, features, and brand. Budget models can start as low as $200, while premium models can cost upwards of $2,000.

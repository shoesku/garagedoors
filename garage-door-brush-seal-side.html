Why Install A Garage Door Brush Seal Side?

Garage doors are an essential part of a home or business, providing an extra layer of security against the elements, intruders, and other outside forces. That’s why it’s important to take the extra step and invest in a brush seal side for your garage door. This small addition can make a big difference in the overall security and efficiency of your garage door.

A brush seal side is a strip of bristles fitted onto the side of your garage door. The bristles are designed to create a tight seal when the door is closed, preventing any wind, dust, or other particles from entering the garage. This helps keep the garage and its contents safe from the elements and any potential intruders.

The brush seal side also helps keep your garage cool in the summer and warm in the winter, as it prevents drafts from entering the space. This helps keep your energy bills lower, as your HVAC system won’t have to work as hard to maintain the desired temperature.

The brush seal side also improves the overall efficiency of your garage door. Without it, the door would be prone to vibration, which can cause wear and tear over time. The brush seal side helps to reduce the vibration, which prolongs the life of the door and prevents wear and tear.

Installing a Garage Door Brush Seal Side

Installing a brush seal side is relatively easy and can usually be done in one afternoon. Most brush seal side kits come with all the necessary components, including the bristles and mounting hardware. Depending on the size of your garage door, you may need to purchase additional components or order a custom kit.

The first step is to measure the size of your garage door and purchase the appropriate kit. Once you have the kit, you can begin the installation process. Start by attaching the bristles to the mounting hardware, and then attach the hardware to the side of the door. Make sure the bristles are tight but not too tight, as this may cause friction and reduce the efficiency of the seal.

Once the bristles are securely attached, you can begin to attach the mounting hardware to the door. Most kits come with detailed instructions, but generally speaking, you will need to use screws and a drill to secure the hardware to the door. Make sure the screws are tight but not too tight, as this can cause the mounting hardware to loosen over time.

Maintaining a Garage Door Brush Seal Side

Once your brush seal side is installed, it is important to keep up with regular maintenance. This includes cleaning the bristles and making sure they are kept free of debris. You should also check the mounting hardware regularly to make sure it is secure and not loose. If the hardware is loose, it can cause the seal to be less effective.

It is also important to inspect the bristles regularly to make sure they are not worn or damaged. If the bristles are worn or damaged, they will not be able to create a tight seal and the efficiency of the seal will be reduced. If the bristles need to be replaced, you can usually purchase new ones from the same company that sold you the kit.

The Benefits of Installing a Garage Door Brush Seal Side

Installing a brush seal side for your garage door is an inexpensive way to improve the overall security and efficiency of your door. It helps to keep the space cool in the summer and warm in the winter, as well as prevents dust, wind, and other particles from entering the space. It also helps to reduce vibration and wear and tear, prolonging the life of the door. And finally, it adds an extra layer of security against potential intruders.

While the installation process is relatively simple and can usually be done in one afternoon, it is important to keep up with regular maintenance to ensure the bristles and mounting hardware remain in good condition. This will help to keep your garage door functioning properly and efficiently for years to come.

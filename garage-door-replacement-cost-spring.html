Replacing a garage door spring can be a daunting task for the average homeowner. It requires knowledge of the specific type of spring and the right tools to get the job done right. If you're looking to replace garage door springs, it's important to understand what you're getting into and what it will cost. Here is a breakdown of what you can expect to pay for garage door spring replacement in 2023.

What Are Garage Door Springs?

Garage door springs are an essential part of your garage door's operation. There are two types of garage door springs - torsion springs and extension springs. Torsion springs are typically located above the top of the garage door on a metal shaft and are used to lift the weight of the door. Extension springs are located on either side of the door and are used to balance the door when it's in the closed position. Both types of garage door springs are under tension and should only be adjusted or replaced by a professional.

What Is the Average Cost of Garage Door Spring Replacement?

The average cost to replace a garage door spring is between $150 and $250. This price can vary depending on the type of spring you need, the type of material used, and the size of the spring. The cost to replace two torsion springs, which are usually used on larger doors, can be up to $400. The cost to replace two extension springs, which are usually used on smaller doors, is usually around $200.

What Factors Affect the Cost of Garage Door Spring Replacement?

The cost of garage door spring replacement can vary depending on a variety of factors. These include the type of spring, the type of material used, the size of the spring, and the labor costs. Torsion springs are typically more expensive than extension springs. The type of material used can also affect the cost, as some materials are more expensive than others. Additionally, the size of the spring can affect the cost, as larger springs require more labor and materials.

What Are the Benefits of Replacing Garage Door Springs?

Replacing garage door springs can provide many benefits. First, it can help prevent damage to your garage door and keep it operating properly. Replacing the springs can also help reduce noise when the garage door is in use. Finally, replacing the springs can help increase the lifespan of your garage door.

What Should You Consider Before Replacing Garage Door Springs?

Before replacing your garage door springs, it's important to consider a few things. First, you should make sure you have the right tools for the job. Second, you should always use the correct type of spring for your door. Finally, it's important to make sure the springs are properly installed and adjusted. This can help prevent injury and ensure the springs last as long as possible.

Where Can You Find a Professional to Replace Garage Door Springs?

If you're looking for a professional to replace garage door springs, you can start by searching online for local garage door companies near you. You should also check reviews and compare prices to make sure you're getting the best deal. Additionally, you can also ask friends and family for recommendations. It's important to find a qualified and experienced technician who can properly install and adjust your springs.

How Often Should You Replace Garage Door Springs?

Garage door springs should be replaced every 5-7 years on average. This can vary depending on the type of spring and the amount of use it gets. If your springs are showing signs of wear or are not operating properly, it's important to get them replaced as soon as possible. Ignoring the problem can lead to more costly repairs down the road.

Conclusion

Replacing garage door springs can be a challenging task, but it's important to get it done right. Understanding the cost of replacement and the benefits it provides can help you make the best decision for your home. Additionally, finding a qualified professional to do the job can ensure that your springs are properly installed and adjusted.

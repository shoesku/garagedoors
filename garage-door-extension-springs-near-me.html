Extension Springs: Finding The Right Fit For Your Garage Door

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Extension springs are the key to keeping your garage door functioning properly. Without them, your door will be unable to open and close properly, leaving you unable to get in and out of your garage. That’s why it’s important to find the right fit for your specific garage door. Whether you’re replacing an existing spring or installing a new one, the right fit is essential. Here’s a quick guide to help you find the right extension springs for your garage door.

Measuring the Right Size

The first step in finding the right extension springs near you is to measure the size of the springs that you need. The length of the spring is important, as well as the diameter. To measure the length, you need to measure the distance between the two ends of the spring, not including the hooks. To measure the diameter, measure the widest part of the spring in between the two hooks. It’s important to be precise when measuring the size of your springs, as mismatched springs can cause all sorts of problems.

Understanding Different Types of Springs

There are two different types of extension springs to choose from – standard and heavy-duty. Standard springs are designed to be used with single-panel garage doors, while heavy-duty springs are designed for use with double-panel garage doors. It’s important to choose the right type of spring for your garage door, as mismatched springs can cause the door to become unbalanced and difficult to open and close.

Finding the Right Extension Springs Near You

Once you’ve measured the size of your extension springs and determined the right type for your door, you can begin the search for the right springs near you. Your local home improvement store is a great place to start, as they typically carry a variety of different types of springs. You can also search online for extension springs, as there are many online retailers that specialize in selling garage door parts. Another option is to contact a local garage door repair company, as they may have access to a wider selection of springs.

Installing Extension Springs

Once you’ve found the right extension springs for your garage door, it’s time to install them. This is a job best left to a professional, as it requires special tools and knowledge. If you’re not comfortable tackling the job yourself, it’s best to hire a professional garage door repair company to do the job for you. Not only will they be able to install the springs properly, but they can also make sure that your door is properly balanced and functioning properly.

Maintaining Your Extension Springs

It’s important to keep your extension springs in good condition in order to ensure that your garage door continues to function properly. To keep your springs in good condition, it’s important to check them regularly for signs of wear or damage. If you notice any signs of damage, it’s best to replace the springs as soon as possible to prevent further damage or injury. It’s also important to make sure that your springs are lubricated regularly to prevent them from rusting or becoming brittle.

Conclusion

Extension springs are an essential part of keeping your garage door functioning properly. If you’re in need of new springs, it’s important to measure the size and type of springs that you need and to find the right extension springs near you. Once you’ve found the right springs, it’s important to have them installed by a professional and to regularly maintain them to ensure that your garage door continues to function properly. With the right care and maintenance, your garage door should continue to open and close properly for years to come.

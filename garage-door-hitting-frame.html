What Causes a Garage Door to Hit the Frame?

If you have recently noticed your garage door hitting the frame, this is a sign that something is wrong. There could be a number of reasons why this is happening. Some of the most common causes include misalignment, worn out rollers, loose components, or a track that is not level. It is important to take a look at the mechanism of your garage door and identify the cause of the issue before attempting to fix it.

What Can You Do to Fix the Problem?

Once you have identified the cause of the issue, you can begin to look into the different ways to fix it. If the issue is misalignment, this can be easily fixed by adjusting the track or the hinges. If the problem is worn out rollers or loose components, you will need to replace these parts. If the track is not level, you will need to level the track and check for any obstructions that may be causing the issue. It is important to ensure that all components are correctly aligned and securely in place before you attempt to fix the issue.

Do You Need Professional Assistance?

If the issue is more complex than just misalignment or worn out parts, it may be best to call in a professional. Professional technicians will be able to inspect the mechanism and identify the root of the problem. They will also be able to advise on the best way to fix the issue and ensure that your garage door is in top condition. It is also a good idea to have a professional technician perform regular maintenance and check-ups on your garage door to keep it in good condition.

What Are the Benefits of Hiring a Professional?

Hiring a professional to fix your garage door has a number of benefits. First of all, they will be able to identify the issue quickly and accurately. This means that the issue will be fixed in a timely manner and without any further damage to your garage door. Professional technicians will also be able to provide advice on how to prevent further damage and what steps to take in the future to keep your garage door in top condition. Finally, having a professional technician complete the job will give you peace of mind that the job is done properly and that your garage is secure.

Conclusion

If you have noticed that your garage door is hitting the frame, it is important to identify the cause of the issue and take steps to fix it. Depending on the cause of the issue, you may be able to do this yourself. Alternatively, you may need to hire a professional technician to complete the job. In either case, it is important to ensure that the issue is fixed correctly and that your garage door is in top condition.

Glass Garage Doors Are Increasingly Becoming Energy Efficient

Garage doors have become a staple of home improvement projects in the last decade. As a result, homeowners have begun to look for ways to make their garage doors more energy efficient. Glass garage doors have become increasingly popular for their energy efficiency, as well as their modern, sleek look. In this article, we'll take a look at why glass garage doors are becoming more energy efficient and what you can do to make the most of this energy saving technology.

Why are Glass Garage Doors Becoming More Energy Efficient?

Glass garage doors are becoming increasingly energy efficient due to advances in technology and materials. The most common type of glass garage door is insulated glass. This type of glass is made with two or more layers of glass which are separated by an air gap. This air gap helps to reduce heat transfer and keep your garage cooler. Additionally, some glass garage doors are made with Low-E (low emissivity) glass. This type of glass helps to reflect heat from the sun, keeping your garage cooler in the summer months.

What Other Benefits Do Glass Garage Doors Offer?

In addition to their energy efficiency, glass garage doors offer several other benefits. For one, they provide a modern, sleek look that can add value to your home. Additionally, glass garage doors are easier to maintain than traditional wooden garage doors and are less likely to crack or break. Finally, glass garage doors are more secure than traditional wooden doors, as they are less likely to be forced open.

How Can You Make the Most of Your Glass Garage Door?

To make the most of your glass garage door, it's important to take advantage of the energy saving features. Make sure to keep the air gap between the glass panes clean and free of debris. Additionally, if your glass garage door is made with Low-E glass, make sure to use it as much as possible during the summer months to reflect the heat from the sun and keep your garage cooler.

Conclusion

Glass garage doors are becoming increasingly energy efficient due to advances in technology and materials. By taking advantage of the energy saving features of your glass garage door, you can keep your garage cooler and reduce your energy bills. Additionally, glass garage doors offer a modern, sleek look that can add value to your home. With all of the benefits of glass garage doors, it's no wonder why they are becoming increasingly popular.

What are Garage Door Seals?

Garage door seals are an important part of any residential garage. They are used to keep out drafts, dust, and pests from entering your home. The type of seal you choose will depend on the type of door you have and the climate you live in. The most common types of seals include bottom seals, side seals, top seals, and threshold seals. Bottom seals are designed to seal the gap between the door and the floor. They are usually made of rubber or vinyl and come in a variety of shapes and sizes. Side seals, also known as edge seals, are used to seal the gap between the door and the side walls of the garage. They are usually made of foam or rubber and come in a variety of shapes and sizes. Top seals, also known as header seals, are used to seal the gap between the door and the ceiling. They are usually made of foam or rubber and come in a variety of shapes and sizes. Lastly, threshold seals are used to seal the gap between the door and the threshold. They are usually made of rubber or vinyl and come in a variety of shapes and sizes.

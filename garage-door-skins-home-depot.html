What are Garage Door Skins?

Garage door skins are essentially large protective covers that are designed to fit over the existing garage door. These covers come in a variety of colors and styles, allowing you to customize the look of your garage door to match the aesthetic of your home. The covers are made from durable materials such as aluminum or steel, making them resistant to damage from the elements. This means that they can be used both indoors and outdoors, allowing you to take advantage of the extra protection they offer.

What Benefits Do Garage Door Skins Offer?

Garage door skins offer a number of benefits. First and foremost, they help to protect the existing garage door from damage, such as weathering and rusting.

Types of Garage Door Insulation

When it comes to insulating your garage door in Melbourne, you have a few different options. The two most common types of insulation are foam board and reflective insulation. Foam board is a common type of insulation that has a rigid core made of polystyrene or polyurethane. This type of insulation is typically attached to the back of the garage door and can be easily installed without any special tools. Reflective insulation, on the other hand, is a thin sheet of material that is designed to reflect heat away from the garage door. This type of insulation is usually attached to the inside of the door and can be easily installed with a few simple tools.

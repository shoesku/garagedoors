What Is The Standard Size Of A Single Garage Door In Australia?

When it comes to purchasing a garage door for your home in Australia, one of the first questions you'll need to ask yourself is what size of door do I need? It's important to get the size of your single garage door right, so you can ensure the maximum storage space and ease of access to your property. With that in mind, let's take a look at the standard size of a single garage door in Australia.

The Standard Single Garage Door Size

The standard single garage door size in Australia is 2150mm wide and 2150mm high. This size is considered to be the most common, and it's suitable for the majority of single garages. However, it's important to note that there may be some variations in the size of your garage door, depending on the manufacturer and your individual requirements.

The Benefits of the Standard Size

The most obvious benefit of the standard single garage door size is that it is easy to find a door that fits your space. As it is the most common size in Australia, it's easy to find a door that is the right size for your garage. This also makes it easier to find accessories such as remote openers and weather seals that are compatible with your door.

The standard size also makes it easier to install the door. As the door is already the right size, it doesn't require any cutting or fitting. This means that the installation process is much quicker, and it is also less likely to cause any potential damage to the door or your garage.

Things to Consider Before Purchasing a Single Garage Door

Before you purchase a single garage door, there are a few things that you should consider. Firstly, consider the type of door you want. There are a range of styles available, from sectional doors to roller doors, and you should choose the one that best suits your needs. You should also consider the materials the door is made from, as this will affect its durability and lifespan.

You should also consider your budget. Garage doors can be expensive, and you should make sure that you are getting the best value for money. If you are on a tight budget, then you may want to consider purchasing a second-hand door, as these can be much cheaper than purchasing a brand new one.

Finally, you should think about the size of the door. Although the standard single garage door size in Australia is 2150mm wide and 2150mm high, you may need a larger or smaller door depending on the size of your garage. You should measure the opening of your garage door to ensure that you get the right size for your needs.

Conclusion

The standard single garage door size in Australia is 2150mm wide and 2150mm high. This size is suitable for the majority of single garages and makes it easier to find a door that fits your space.

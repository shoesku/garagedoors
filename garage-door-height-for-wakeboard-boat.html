Choosing The Right Garage Door Height For Your Wakeboard Boat

If you’re looking to buy a new wakeboard boat, you’ll find that you’ll need to consider the proper garage door height before you make your purchase. In order to ensure that your boat can fit inside your garage, you’ll want to ensure that you’re buying the right size of garage door for the boat. Here’s a look at how to choose the right garage door height for your wakeboard boat so that you can be sure that it fits perfectly.

Measure the Height of Your Boat

The first step in choosing the right garage door height for your wakeboard boat is to measure the height of your boat. Make sure to measure from the highest point on the boat to the bottom. This will give you a good indication of how tall your boat is and what size garage door you’ll need to get. It’s important to keep in mind that you’ll also need to account for the height of the trailer that the boat is sitting on. Make sure to measure the height of the trailer as well.

Account for the Height of Your Vehicle

Next, you’ll need to account for the height of your vehicle. If you’re going to be storing your wakeboard boat in the same garage as your vehicle, then you’ll need to consider the height of your vehicle when you’re buying a garage door. You’ll want to ensure that the garage door is tall enough so that both your vehicle and your boat can fit inside the garage. If you’re buying a separate garage door for your boat, then you won’t need to worry about this step.

Determine the Installation Height

Once you’ve measured the height of your boat and accounted for the height of your vehicle, you’ll need to determine the installation height of the garage door. This is the height at which the garage door will be installed, and it is usually lower than the height of the garage door itself. This is because the garage door needs to clear the opening of the garage door when it is opened and closed. You’ll want to ensure that the installation height is tall enough for both your vehicle and your boat to fit inside the garage.

Choose the Right Size Garage Door

Once you’ve determined the installation height of the garage door, you’ll be able to choose the right size garage door for your wakeboard boat. You’ll want to ensure that the garage door is tall enough to fit both your vehicle and your boat inside the garage. The most common sizes for garage doors are 8 feet, 9 feet, 10 feet, and 12 feet. However, you can also find garage doors that are taller if you need them.

Choose the Right Type of Garage Door

Once you’ve chosen the right size of garage door for your wakeboard boat, you’ll need to decide on the type of garage door that you want. The most common types of garage doors are sectional and roll-up. Sectionals are the most common type of garage doors and they open and close by rolling up and down. Roll-up garage doors open and close by rolling up and down like a window shade. It’s important to consider the size of your garage before choosing between the two types of garage doors.

Choose the Right Material for Your Garage Door

Finally, you’ll need to choose the right material for your garage door. The most common materials for garage doors are wood, steel, and aluminum. Steel is the most durable and the most expensive, while aluminum is the least expensive but the least durable. Wood is the most aesthetically pleasing and is often used in residential applications. It’s important to consider the size of your garage before choosing the right material for your garage door.

Conclusion

Choosing the right garage door height for your wakeboard boat can be a difficult task. However, with a little bit of research and planning, you can make sure that you’re buying the right size garage door for your boat. Make sure to measure the height of your boat and trailer, account for the height of your vehicle, determine the installation height of the garage door, and choose the right size, type, and material for your garage door. By following these steps, you can be sure that your wakeboard boat will fit perfectly inside your garage.

The Benefits of Insulated Garage Doors

When it comes to home improvement projects, many homeowners don't think about their garage doors. After all, it's not a room that you use every day. But when it comes to energy efficiency, having an insulated garage door can make a huge difference in your heating and cooling bills. An insulated garage door helps keep the temperature in the garage consistent and can also help reduce noise coming in from outside.

Types of Garage Door Insulation

There are several types of garage door insulation available, and the type you choose will depend on your budget and the type of door you have. Foam board insulation is one of the most popular and affordable options. This type of insulation is often easy to install and can provide a great deal of energy efficiency. Other options include reflective insulation, which reflects heat away from your garage, and insulated vinyl, which is more durable and can provide better insulation.

Things to Consider When Buying Insulation for Your Garage Door

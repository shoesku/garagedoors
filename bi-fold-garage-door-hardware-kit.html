What are Bi Fold Garage Door Hardware Kits?

A bi fold garage door hardware kit is a set of hardware that allows you to open and close your garage door by folding it in half. The kit contains the necessary components to attach the door to the frame and the wall. It usually includes two hinges, two top and bottom tracks, a latch, and a bunch of screws. The hardware is simple to install, and you can usually do it yourself in a matter of a few hours.

How Does Bi Fold Garage Door Hardware Work?

Bi fold garage door hardware works by allowing the door to fold in half when it is opened. The hardware is attached to the top and bottom tracks that are mounted to the garage door frame. When the door is opened, the hinges allow the door to fold in half and be stored in the corner of the garage. This allows the door to be opened or closed without taking up a large amount of space.

Benefits of Bi Fold Garage Door Hardware

There are several benefits to installing bi fold garage door hardware. The first is that it allows you to open and close your garage door without taking up a lot of space. This is especially helpful if you don’t have a lot of room to spare in your garage. It also makes it easier to open and close your door, as you don’t have to worry about trying to fit the door in the frame. Additionally, it adds an extra layer of security to your garage, as it is more difficult to break into than a traditional door.

Types of Bi Fold Garage Door Hardware

There are several different types of bi fold garage door hardware. You can find kits that are designed for single or double doors, as well as those that are designed for overhead doors. You can also find kits that come with all the necessary hardware, or you can purchase the components separately. Depending on your budget, you may find that purchasing the components separately is more cost-effective.

How Much Does Bi Fold Garage Door Hardware Cost?

The cost of bi fold garage door hardware varies depending on the type and size of the kit you purchase. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $50 to $200 for a standard kit. However, if you opt for more expensive components, such as a motorized opener or a more durable track, you may end up paying more.

Where to Buy Bi Fold Garage Door Hardware

Bi fold garage door hardware is available online and in most home improvement stores. If you shop online, you’ll be able to compare prices and find the best deal. Additionally, you may be able to find discounts or coupons that you can use to save even more money. It’s a good idea to read customer reviews before making a purchase, so you can make sure you’re getting a quality product.

Conclusion

Bi fold garage door hardware is a great way to improve the security and convenience of your garage. It’s easy to install and fairly inexpensive compared to other types of door hardware.

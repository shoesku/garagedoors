What is LiftMaster?

LiftMaster is a leading manufacturer of garage door openers and security products. Founded in 1954, the company has been providing homeowners and businesses with reliable, secure and innovative products that improve their everyday lives. From residential garage door openers to commercial gate operators and access control systems, LiftMaster has continued to be a leader in the industry.

LiftMaster's products are designed to provide a safe, secure and convenient way to open, close and operate garage doors, gates and access control systems. They offer a wide range of products from basic, entry-level to professional-grade systems. With an extensive range of products, LiftMaster can meet the needs of all types of customers, from DIYers to commercial operators.

Why Did LiftMaster Close?

In early 2021, LiftMaster announced that it was closing its doors due to financial difficulties. The company had been struggling for some time and was unable to find a new investor or partner to help with its operations. As a result, the company was forced to close. At the time of the closure, LiftMaster CEO and President, George T.

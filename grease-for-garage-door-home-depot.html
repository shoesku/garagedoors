Types of Grease for Garage Doors

There are several different types of grease that can be used to lubricate a garage door. The most common type is silicone-based grease, which is designed to reduce friction and lubricate the moving parts of the door. Other types of grease include lithium-based grease and synthetic-based grease.

Silicone-based grease is the most commonly used type of grease for garage doors. It is easy to apply, and it is very effective in reducing friction. It also provides a good seal, which helps to keep the door from rusting.

Lithium-based grease is less common, but it is a good choice for those who want a more durable lubricant. This type of grease is known for its ability to resist corrosion and its ability to protect against wear and tear. Synthetic-based grease also provides good lubrication, but it is not as resistant to corrosion as lithium-based grease.

How to Apply Grease for Garage Doors

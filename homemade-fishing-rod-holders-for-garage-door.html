How To Make A Homemade Fishing Rod Holder For Garage Door

Introduction

Do you love fishing? Are you the kind of person who loves to go out on a fishing trip but hates having to lug around your heavy fishing gear? If so, you’re in luck! With a few simple tools and materials, you can easily make a homemade fishing rod holder for your garage door to keep your gear organized and out of the way. This guide will show you step-by-step how to make your own fishing rod holder and get the most out of your next fishing trip.

Step 1: Gather the Materials

The first step in making your homemade fishing rod holder is to gather the necessary materials. You’ll need a drill and drill bits, two pieces of 2x4 lumber, a saw, some screws, and a few other materials. Depending on the size of your garage door, you may also need to adjust the measurements for the lumber. For a standard two-car garage door, the lumber should measure about 36 inches in length.

Step 2: Cut the Lumber

Once you have all the materials, you’re ready to start building your fishing rod holder. Start by cutting the two pieces of lumber to the desired length. Use a saw to make quick and precise cuts. The pieces of lumber should be cut to 36 inches in length, with one piece being slightly wider than the other.

Step 3: Drill Holes

Next, you’ll need to drill holes into the two pieces of lumber. The holes should be drilled in the center of each piece of lumber and should be slightly larger than the screws you’ll be using to secure the pieces of lumber together. Make sure to use the right drill bit for the job and safety glasses to protect your eyes.

Step 4: Assemble the Fishing Rod Holder

Once the holes have been drilled, it’s time to assemble the fishing rod holder. Place the two pieces of lumber together so that the holes line up with each other. Then, using the screws, secure the pieces of lumber together. Make sure to use a screwdriver to tighten the screws and make sure that the pieces of lumber are secure.

Step 5: Mount the Fishing Rod Holder

Now that the fishing rod holder is assembled, it’s time to mount it to the garage door. Measure the distance from the top of the garage door to where the fishing rod holder will be mounted. Then, using the drill, secure the fishing rod holder to the door with screws. Make sure to use the right drill bit for the job and to use screws that are long enough to securely hold the fishing rod holder in place.

Step 6: Add the Fishing Rods

Once the fishing rod holder is securely mounted to the garage door, you can add the fishing rods. Depending on the size of the fishing rods you have, you may need to adjust the spacing of the screws to accommodate the different sizes. Make sure to leave enough space between the rods so they don’t get tangled up when you’re fishing.

Conclusion

Making your own fishing rod holder for your garage door is a great way to keep your fishing gear organized and out of the way. With a few simple tools and materials, you can easily make a homemade fishing rod holder and get the most out of your next fishing trip. Plus, it’s a great way to save money and have some fun building something yourself!

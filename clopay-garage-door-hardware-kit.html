What Does a Clopay Garage Door Hardware Kit Include?

A Clopay garage door hardware kit includes everything you need to give your garage door a new look and improved functionality. Benefits of Installing a Clopay Garage Door Hardware Kit

Installing a Clopay garage door hardware kit is a great way to upgrade your home. Here are some of the benefits you can look forward to:

  • Improved security: The included hardware helps to secure the door in place, making it more difficult for an intruder to break in.

  • Improved function: The included hardware and accessories can help to improve the performance of your door, making it easier to open and close.

How to Install a Clopay Garage Door Hardware Kit

Installing a Clopay garage door hardware kit is a fairly straightforward process. Here are the steps you'll need to take:

  1. Remove the existing hardware from your garage door.
  2. Unpack the hardware kit and check that all the pieces are included.
  3. Mount the new hardware to the door, starting with the hinges and brackets.
  4. Install the decorative hardware, such as the door handles, pull handles, and trim.
  5. Check that all the hardware is secure and properly aligned.

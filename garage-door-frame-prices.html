Garage Door Frame Prices In 2023

Are you thinking of adding a garage door frame to your home in 2023? If so, you’ve likely started to research the materials, styles, and prices of garage door frames. To help you make an informed decision, we’ve put together this comprehensive guide to garage door frame prices in 2023.

Factors Affecting Garage Door Frame Prices

Before we get into the average costs of garage door frames, it’s important to understand the factors that influence their prices. Some of the primary factors affecting garage door frame prices include:

  • Material: The material used to make the garage door frame is a major factor in its overall cost. Steel and aluminum are two of the most common materials used in the construction of garage door frames, but wood and vinyl frames are also available. The material you choose will have a major impact on the overall cost of your garage door frame.
  • Size: The size of the garage door frame will also affect its overall cost. Larger frames typically cost more than smaller ones, as they require more material and labor to install.
  • Style: The style of the garage door frame can also influence its cost. For example, frames with more intricate designs, such as those with decorative trim or raised panels, tend to cost more than simpler frames.
  • Installation: The cost of installation is often overlooked when it comes to garage door frames, but it can have a significant impact on the overall cost of the frame. If you’re hiring a professional to install the frame, be sure to factor in the cost of their labor when making your budget.

Average Costs of Garage Door Frames

Now that you understand the factors that affect garage door frame prices, let’s take a look at the average costs of the different materials. Keep in mind that these are just estimates and your actual cost may vary depending on the size, style, and installation of your garage door frame.

Steel Frames

Steel frames are the most common type of garage door frames, and they tend to be the most affordable option. On average, you can expect to pay between $500 and $1,500 for a steel garage door frame.

Aluminum Frames

Aluminum frames are slightly more expensive than steel frames, but they are also more durable and require less maintenance. On average, aluminum frames cost between $700 and $2,000.

Wood Frames

Wood frames are the most expensive option when it comes to garage door frames, but they also offer the most unique look. On average, wood frames cost between $1,000 and $3,000.

Vinyl Frames

Vinyl frames are the least expensive option when it comes to garage door frames, but they tend to be less durable than other materials. On average, vinyl frames cost between $400 and $800.

Finding the Right Garage Door Frame for Your Home

When it comes to finding the right garage door frame for your home, it’s important to consider both your budget and the look you’re trying to achieve. If you’re looking for an affordable option, steel or vinyl frames are your best bet. For a more unique look, wood or aluminum frames may be a better option.

No matter what material you choose, be sure to factor in the cost of installation when budgeting for your garage door frame. This will ensure that you’re not surprised by any hidden costs down the line.

Conclusion

Garage door frames can be a great way to add style and value to your home. By understanding the factors that affect the cost of garage door frames, you can make an informed decision on which material and style is right for you.

Steel and vinyl frames tend to be the most affordable options, while wood and aluminum frames offer a more unique look. No matter which material you choose, be sure to factor in the cost of installation when budgeting for your garage door frame.

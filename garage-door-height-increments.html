Garage door heights vary according to the type of door and the height of the opening. This article will help explain the different increments of garage door height and will provide some tips on measuring the height of your garage door.

Standard Garage Door Height Increments

The standard height for a garage door is typically 7 feet, but it can range from 6 to 8 feet. If you have a two car garage, then the door height should typically be 8 feet high. If you plan on building a new garage, or if you are replacing an existing garage door, it is important to know the height increments of the garage door you are installing.

The majority of garage door manufacturers offer garage doors in standard increments of 1, 4, 8 and 12 inches. These increments are based on the height of the opening, so you will need to measure the opening before you order the garage door.

Measuring Garage Door Height

Before you order a new garage door, it is important to measure the height of the opening. To do this, measure from the bottom of the door opening to the highest point of the opening. This will give you the exact height of the opening and will help you determine the right height of the garage door you need.

It is also important to measure the width of the opening as well. Most garage doors are available in widths ranging from 8 to 18 feet, so you will need to make sure the door you order is the correct width for your opening.

Custom Garage Door Heights

If you have an unusually tall or wide opening, you may need to order a custom garage door. Custom garage doors are available in widths and heights that are not available in the standard increments. Custom garage doors can also be made to fit odd-shaped openings, such as arched openings.

It is important to note that custom garage doors are usually more expensive than standard garage doors, so it is important to weigh the cost of a custom door against the cost of a standard door.

Installing Garage Door Height Increments

When installing a new garage door, it is important to make sure that the door is installed correctly. The door should be level with the opening, and it should be properly sealed to prevent air and water from entering the garage.

It is also important to make sure the door is properly balanced. If the door is unbalanced, it can cause the door to open and close unevenly, which can cause damage to the door and the opening.

Conclusion

Garage door height increments are important to consider when installing a new garage door. It is important to measure the height and width of the opening before ordering a garage door. If you have an unusually tall or wide opening, you may need to order a custom garage door. It is also important to make sure the door is installed correctly and balanced properly to ensure that it operates safely and efficiently.

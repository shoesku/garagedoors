Installing Garage Door Decals from Home Depot

Installing garage door decals from Home Depot is a fairly simple process. All you need is a few basic tools such as a screwdriver, a measuring tape, and a ruler. You'll also need some cleaners, a few pieces of masking tape, and a sponge.

When you're ready to install the decal, start by cleaning the garage door with a mild detergent. Then, use the measuring tape and ruler to measure the area where you want the decal to be placed. Make sure to measure carefully, as the decal needs to fit exactly.

Next, you'll need to apply the masking tape along the edges of the decal. This will help ensure that the decal will stay in place. Once the tape is in place, you can remove the backing from the decal and apply it to the door.

It's important to make sure that the decal is completely secured before you remove the masking tape. Once you've done this, you can use a damp sponge to remove any excess adhesive. Once the decal is dry, you're done!

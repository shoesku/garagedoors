Garage Door Cost In Canada - What To Expect In 2023

Are you looking to buy a new garage door in Canada in 2023? The cost of a garage door in Canada can vary significantly depending on what type of door you choose and who you choose to purchase it from. Knowing what to expect in 2023 can help you make an informed decision when it comes to buying a new garage door. In this article, we’ll go over the different types of garage doors, the cost of each type, and what to look for when shopping around.

Types of Garage Doors

When shopping for a new garage door in Canada, you’ll have plenty of options to choose from. The most common types of garage doors are sectional, rolling, and tilt-up. Sectional garage doors are the most popular option and are made up of interconnected sections that move up and down along a track. Rolling garage doors are constructed of one or more steel panels that roll up and down along tracks. Tilt-up garage doors are made of a single, solid panel that swings up and out from the bottom.

Cost of Garage Doors in Canada in 2023

The cost of garage doors in Canada can vary significantly depending on the type of door you choose, the size of the door, and the material it is made of. Steel garage doors are usually the most cost-effective option and can range in cost from $400 to $1,200. Wood garage doors are more expensive than steel, typically costing anywhere from $1,200 to $2,400. Aluminum and vinyl garage doors are usually the most expensive, ranging in cost from $2,000 to $3,000. Of course, these prices are just estimates and can vary depending on the company you purchase the door from.

Factors to Consider When Shopping for a Garage Door

When shopping for a garage door in Canada, there are a few factors that you should consider. The first is the size of the door. Measure the opening of your garage before you shop for a door to make sure you get the right size. The second factor to consider is the material the door is made from. Steel, wood, aluminum, and vinyl are all popular materials for garage doors and have different pros and cons. Lastly, consider the cost of the door. Shop around for the best price and don’t be afraid to negotiate.

Where to Buy a Garage Door in Canada in 2023?

There are a few different places to buy a garage door in Canada in 2023. You can buy a door from a local hardware store, home improvement store, or online. If you shop online, make sure you do your research and read reviews to ensure you’re buying a quality door. It’s also important to make sure the company you’re buying from is reputable and has a good return policy in case you’re not satisfied with the door. You can also hire a professional to install the door for you if you don’t feel comfortable doing it yourself.

Installing a Garage Door in Canada in 2023

Installing a garage door in Canada in 2023 can be a challenging task. If you’re not comfortable doing it yourself, it’s best to hire a professional. Professional installers will have the right tools and experience to get the job done right the first time. The cost of professional installation can vary depending on the complexity of the job and the company you hire, but it’s usually around $300 to $500. It’s important to remember that installing a garage door is not a DIY project and should only be done by a professional.

Conclusion

The cost of a garage door in Canada in 2023 can vary significantly depending on the type of door you choose, the size of the door, and the material it is made of. Steel garage doors are usually the most cost-effective option, while wood, aluminum, and vinyl can be more expensive. When shopping for a garage door, make sure to measure the opening of your garage and consider the cost, material, and size of the door. Finally, it’s best to hire a professional to install the door to make sure it’s done correctly.

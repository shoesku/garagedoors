What Is a Genie Garage Door Opener Keypad?

A Genie garage door opener keypad is a device that allows you to open and close your garage door without having to use a traditional key or remote. Instead, you can simply enter a code on the keypad, and the door will open or close as needed. This can be a great convenience when you’re coming home late at night, or if you’ve forgotten your keys. It can also provide an extra layer of security, as you can change the code as needed to ensure that only authorized individuals can access your garage.

Features to Look For in a Genie Garage Door Opener Keypad

When you’re shopping for a Genie garage door opener keypad, there are a few features you should keep in mind. First and foremost, the keypad should be easy to use, with clear instructions and a large, easy-to-read display. Additionally, look for a keypad that is compatible with your existing Genie garage door opener, as some models will only work with specific brands and models. Finally, make sure that the keypad is weatherproof, as it will be exposed to the elements when installed on the outside of your garage.

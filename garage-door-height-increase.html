Increase Your Garage Door Height For More Storage And Usability

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

If you are looking to maximize the amount of storage and usability of your garage, consider increasing the height of the garage door. A higher garage door allows for more headroom, making it easier to store items in the garage and move around without having to stoop or crouch. It also allows for a larger opening and makes it easier to move larger items into the garage without having to disassemble them first.

But before you start any project to increase the height of your garage door, there are a few things you should consider. First, you need to make sure that your garage door is capable of being increased in height. Not all garage doors are able to be increased in height, and if yours is not, you may need to purchase a new garage door. Second, you will need to know the current height of your garage door in order to determine how much height you can realistically add.

How High Can You Go?

The height of a garage door is typically measured in feet and inches, with the standard height for a residential garage door being 7 feet and 4 inches. Most garage doors can be increased by up to 8 feet, but some may be able to go even higher. Once you have determined the maximum height your garage door can be increased to, you will need to measure the current height of your garage door. This can be done with a tape measure, or you can also use a laser level or similar tool.

What You Need to Increase Your Garage Door Height

Once you have determined the maximum height your garage door can be increased to, the next step is to gather the materials you need to increase the height of your garage door. This includes the necessary hardware, such as hinges, brackets, and springs, as well as the materials needed to build the frame and panels for the new door. Depending on the type of door you have, you may also need to purchase additional insulation or weather stripping.

Safety Considerations

When increasing the height of your garage door, it is important to consider safety. If the door is too high, it can become difficult to open and close, and can also pose a dangerous hazard if it falls unexpectedly. For this reason, it is important to make sure that the door is properly secured and that any additional hardware or panels are securely attached. It is also a good idea to install a safety release mechanism to ensure that the door can be opened and closed safely.

Additional Considerations

In addition to safety considerations, you should also take into account the aesthetics of the new door. If the door is going to be significantly taller than the existing door, you may want to consider replacing the existing trim and windows with larger ones that better match the new size. Additionally, if the new door is significantly taller than the existing one, you may need to adjust the roofline of the garage to accommodate the extra height.

Hiring a Professional

Increasing the height of your garage door is not a project that should be undertaken lightly. If you are not comfortable with the project, or if you are unsure about the process, it is best to hire a professional to do the job for you. A professional will have the experience and know-how to do the job correctly and safely, and can also help you to select the right materials and components for the job.

Conclusion

Increasing the height of your garage door can be a great way to maximize the storage and usability of your garage. However, it is important to consider safety and aesthetic factors before beginning the project, and to make sure that you have all the necessary materials and components to complete the job. Additionally, if you are not comfortable with the project, it is best to hire a professional to do the job for you.

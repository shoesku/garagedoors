Identify Your Garage Door Parts For Easy Repairs

Are you looking to repair your garage door? With the right knowledge and tools, you can do it yourself. One of the first steps in any DIY repair is to identify the parts of the garage door. Without a basic understanding of the various parts, it can be difficult to diagnose and repair any issues.

The Different Parts of a Garage Door

A garage door has several different parts that work together to open and close. Here are the basic parts of a garage door:

  • Rollers
  • Cables
  • Tracks
  • Garage door opener
  • Garage door springs
  • Hinges
  • Garage door panels
  • Garage door weather stripping

Let's go over each of these parts in more detail.

Rollers

Rollers are what allow the garage door to move up and down along the tracks. They are usually made from steel and plastic. There are two basic types of rollers- steel rollers and nylon rollers. Steel rollers are the most common and usually last longer than nylon rollers.

Cables

Cables are what connects the garage door opener to the garage door. They are usually made from nylon-coated steel and are attached to the bottom of the garage door. The cables are tensioned to provide the necessary lift to open the garage door.

Tracks

Tracks are what the rollers roll on. They are usually made from steel and are mounted to the wall. Tracks are usually mounted in two sections: an upper track and a lower track. The rollers move along the tracks in order to open and close the garage door.

Garage Door Opener

The garage door opener is what provides the power to open and close the door. It is usually mounted to the ceiling and is connected to the garage door via the cables. The garage door opener consists of a motor, a drive system, and a control panel.

Garage Door Springs

Garage door springs are what provide the necessary tension for the door to open and close. They are usually made from steel and are mounted to the wall. There are two types of springs- torsion springs and extension springs. Torsion springs are the most common and provide the most tension.

Hinges

Hinges are what allow the garage door panels to open and close. They are usually made from steel and are mounted to the door panels. The hinges are what allow the door to be opened and closed manually.

Garage Door Panels

Garage door panels are the individual sections of the door. They are usually made from steel or aluminum and are connected to each other with hinges. The panels usually slide up and down along the tracks when the garage door is opened and closed.

Garage Door Weather Stripping

Garage door weather stripping is the material that is used to seal the door panels against the door frame. It usually consists of foam or rubber and is installed along the edges of the door panels. The weather stripping helps to keep out drafts and other elements that can enter the garage.

Now that you have a basic understanding of the different parts of a garage door, you'll be able to easily identify them and diagnose any issues. Remember, always consult a professional if you have any doubts or concerns.

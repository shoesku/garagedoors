Choosing the Right Parts for Your Garage Door

Types of Parts for Garage Door Near Me

When it comes to choosing the right parts for your garage door, there are a few different types to choose from. The most common type of parts are springs, rollers, and cables. Springs are the main mechanism that lifts and closes the door, and they come in a variety of sizes and strengths. Rollers attach the door to the track, and they can be made of plastic, steel, or nylon. Cables are the thin wire that hold the springs in place and keep the door from falling.

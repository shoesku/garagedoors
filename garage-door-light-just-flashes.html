What To Do When Your Garage Door Light Just Flashes?

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

Garage doors are vital components of the home. They not only provide the convenience of accessing your vehicle without going out of the house, but also protect the car and other valuables kept in the garage. But like any other machine, garage doors may stop functioning any time due to various reasons. One of the common problems you may face is when the garage door light just flashes. This problem can be irritating, but you don’t have to panic as you can fix it on your own.

What Causes The Flashing Light?

The most common reason for a flashing light is the power outage. If the power is interrupted, the garage door will not open or close properly, and the light will flash. In addition, the flashing light can also be caused by a malfunctioning sensor. The safety sensors are usually placed around the door and are responsible for detecting any obstruction that may be in the path of the door. If the sensors are not properly aligned, the door will not move, and the light will flash.

How To Fix Flashing Light?

The first thing you should do is check the power supply. Make sure that the power switch is on, and that the socket is connected properly. If there is a power outage, you can reset the circuit breaker or replace the fuse. If the power supply is functioning properly, you must check the safety sensors. If the sensors are not properly aligned, you can adjust them. To do this, you should loosen the nuts and bolts of the sensors, and then adjust the angle. Once the angle is correct, you can tighten the nuts and bolts.

What If The Light Keeps Flashing?

If the light keeps flashing even after adjusting the safety sensors, you should check the power supply again. In addition, you should also inspect the garage door opener. Make sure that the motor is properly connected, and that the wires are not damaged. If the motor is not functioning properly, you should replace it. If the wires are damaged, you should get them replaced as soon as possible.

Tips To Maintain The Garage Door

To ensure that the garage door functions properly, you should regularly inspect and maintain it. Make sure that all the nuts and bolts are tightened properly, and that there are no obstructions in the path of the door. In addition, you should also check the power supply and the safety sensors. You should also lubricate the moving parts of the door, such as the hinges, rollers, and tracks. This will help reduce friction and make the door open and close smoothly.

When To Call A Professional?

If the light keeps flashing even after you have checked the power supply and the safety sensors, you should call a professional. A professional garage door technician will be able to inspect the door and identify the problem. They will then be able to repair or replace the damaged parts, and ensure that the door is functioning properly.

Conclusion

A flashing light is a common problem that can be fixed easily. However, if the light keeps flashing even after adjusting the safety sensors and checking the power supply, you should call a professional garage door technician. They will be able to inspect the door and identify the problem. They will then be able to repair or replace the damaged parts, and ensure that the door is functioning properly.

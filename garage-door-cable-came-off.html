What To Do When Your Garage Door Cable Comes Off?

Introduction

When you find out that your garage door cable has come off, it can be quite a shock, but don’t worry, it’s not the end of the world! The good news is that if you take the right steps, you can soon get your garage door back to normal. In this article, we’ll be looking at what causes garage door cables to come off and what steps you need to take to get your garage door up and running again.

What Causes Garage Door Cables to Come Off?

Garage door cables are designed to be strong and sturdy, but sometimes they can become loose or even break altogether. This can happen for a variety of reasons. The most common causes of garage door cable failure are: age and wear-and-tear, poor installation, or an uneven balance of the door. It’s also possible that the cables were not properly lubricated and were allowed to dry out, leading to them snapping.

If your garage door cable has come off, it is important to check the other cables to make sure they are functioning properly. If one of the cables is damaged, it is likely that the other cables are also at risk of coming off. It is also important to check the tension of the cables and make sure they are properly adjusted.

How To Fix a Garage Door Cable That Has Come Off

If you find that your garage door cable has come off, you can usually fix it yourself. All you need to do is to reattach the cable to the springs and then adjust the tension. This can be done by hand or with a tensioning tool. If you are using a tensioning tool, make sure to follow the manufacturer’s instructions carefully.

Once the cable is reattached, you will need to adjust the tension. This can be done by hand or with a tensioning tool. If you are using a tensioning tool, make sure to follow the manufacturer’s instructions carefully. You should also check the other cables to make sure they are properly tensioned and that the garage door is balanced.

When to Seek Professional Help

If you’re not comfortable or confident enough to fix the garage door cable yourself, it’s best to seek professional help. A professional will be able to inspect your garage door and safely reattach the cable. They will also be able to assess the condition of the other cables and make sure they are correctly tensioned.

If the cable is damaged or has snapped, it will need to be replaced. A professional will be able to do this safely and securely. They will also be able to advise you on any other maintenance that might be necessary.

Conclusion

Garage door cables can come off for a variety of reasons, but don’t worry, they can usually be fixed with a few simple steps. If you’re not confident enough to fix your garage door cable yourself, it’s best to seek professional help. If you follow the steps outlined in this article, you should soon be able to get your garage door back up and running again.

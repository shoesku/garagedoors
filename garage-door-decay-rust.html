What is Garage Door Decay and Rust?

Garage door decay and rust are two of the most common problems that homeowners face when it comes to their garage doors. Decay is caused by water and moisture seeping into the metal parts of the door, which can cause the metal to corrode and weaken. Rust is a form of corrosion that occurs when iron and steel are exposed to oxygen and moisture. Both problems can lead to serious damage if not taken care of properly. Fortunately, there are steps that homeowners can take to prevent and repair garage door decay and rust.

Preventing Garage Door Decay and Rust

The best way to deal with garage door decay and rust is to prevent it from happening in the first place. There are several things you can do to help protect your garage door from decay and rust. The first step is to make sure that your garage door is properly sealed. This will help keep out water and moisture and prevent decay and rust from forming. You should also make sure to regularly inspect your garage door and check for any signs of decay or rust. If you find any, it’s important to address the issue right away.

Cleaning Your Garage Door

Regularly cleaning your garage door is also important for preventing decay and rust. You should make sure to clean your garage door at least once a year. You can use a pressure washer to clean the exterior of the door and a mixture of soap and water to clean the interior. This will help remove any dirt or debris that could lead to decay and rust. Once your garage door is clean, you should inspect it for any signs of decay or rust.

Repairing Garage Door Decay and Rust

If you find signs of decay or rust on your garage door, it’s important to repair it right away. There are a few different methods you can use to repair decay and rust. The first method is to use a rust converter, which helps to convert the rust into a protective coating. You can also use a rust inhibitor, which helps to stop the rust from spreading. If the decay and rust are more severe, you may need to replace the affected parts of the door.

Painting Your Garage Door

Painting your garage door is another effective way to help prevent decay and rust. If your garage door is already painted, you should make sure to repaint it every few years to ensure that it is still protecting the metal from decay and rust. You can use a rust-resistant paint for best results. If you do decide to repaint your garage door, make sure to use a high-quality paint and follow the manufacturer’s instructions.

Garage Door Maintenance

The best way to prevent decay and rust on your garage door is to perform regular maintenance. This includes regularly inspecting the door for any signs of damage, cleaning the door with a pressure washer, and lubricating the moving parts. This will help to keep your garage door in good condition and prevent decay and rust from forming.

Conclusion

Garage door decay and rust can cause serious damage to your garage door if not taken care of properly. Fortunately, there are steps that homeowners can take to prevent and repair garage door decay and rust. These steps include regularly inspecting and cleaning the door, using a rust converter or inhibitor, and repainting the door with a rust-resistant paint. Taking these steps can help to keep your garage door in good condition and prevent decay and rust from forming.

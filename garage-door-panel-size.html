What Are Garage Door Panels?

Garage door panels are the individual pieces of material that make up the structure of a garage door. They can be made from various materials, including wood, aluminum, and steel. The size of the panels will vary depending on the type of garage door you have and its overall size. In most cases, the panels are connected by hinges and rollers, allowing the door to move up and down. Garage door panels are typically installed horizontally across the garage door opening, so they can be measured from side to side.

How to Measure Garage Door Panels

Measuring the size of your garage door panels is the first step in understanding the overall size of your garage door. As mentioned earlier, the panels are usually installed horizontally across the garage door opening and can be measured from side to side. To start, measure the width of each panel from the left side to the right side. This will give you the panel width. Then, measure the height of the panels from the top to the bottom. This will give you the panel height.

Standard Garage Door Panel Sizes

When it comes to standard garage door panel sizes, the most common width is 8’, though other sizes may be available depending on your specific needs. The height of the panels varies depending on the type of door, but most are between 6’ and 8’. In addition, the number of panels used in a single garage door can vary, with some doors having as few as two panels, and others having as many as eight.

Customizing Garage Door Panel Sizes

It is possible to customize the size of your garage door panels to fit your specific needs. If you need a larger or smaller panel size than what is available in standard sizes, you can have a custom panel made to fit your garage door opening. This is especially important if you have an older home with a garage door opening that is not a standard size. In these cases, it is best to have the panels custom made to ensure a perfect fit.

Benefits of Custom Garage Door Panel Sizes

Customizing the size of your garage door panels can have a number of benefits. First, it ensures that your garage door will fit your garage door opening properly and securely. This will help to reduce the risk of a malfunction or breakdown, as the door will be able to move up and down properly without any issues. Additionally, customizing the size of your panels can also help to make your garage door more energy efficient, as the panels will be able to close tightly and keep out the elements.

Types of Garage Door Panels

Garage door panels are available in a variety of materials, including wood, steel, and aluminum. Wood panels are the most popular choice, as they are relatively inexpensive and can be easily customized to fit any size garage door opening. Steel and aluminum panels are more durable and are often used on larger garage doors. It is important to note that steel and aluminum panels are more expensive than wood, so they may not be the best choice for all budgets.

Are Garage Door Panels Easy to Replace?

In some cases, it may be necessary to replace one or more of your garage door panels. Replacing a panel is relatively easy and can typically be done without professional help. However, it is important to make sure that the new panel is the same size and material as the original, as any discrepancies could cause the door to malfunction. Additionally, it is important to make sure the new panel is properly installed and secured, as a loose panel can cause the door to jam or even fall off its track.

Conclusion

Garage door panels come in a variety of sizes, materials, and shapes, making it easy to find the perfect option for your home. The size of the panels will depend on the type of door you have and its overall size, but most standard sizes are 8’ wide and 6’ to 8’ tall. If you need a different size, custom options are available. Additionally, it is important to make sure that the panels are properly installed and secured, as this will help to ensure the safety and longevity of your garage door.

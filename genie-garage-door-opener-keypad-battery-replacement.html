How To Easily Replace The Battery In Your Genie Garage Door Opener Keypad

If you have a Genie garage door opener keypad, keeping it in good working order is essential for keeping your home secure. Over time, the battery in the keypad will need to be replaced. The good news is that replacing the battery in a Genie garage door opener keypad is an easy task that can be done in a few simple steps. In this article, we’ll show you how to quickly and easily replace the battery in your Genie garage door opener keypad.

How to Tell When the Battery Needs to be Replaced

The most obvious sign that the battery in your Genie garage door opener keypad needs to be replaced is when it stops working. If you press the buttons on the keypad and nothing happens, you likely need to replace the battery. Other signs that the battery needs to be replaced include the keypad not lighting up, the keypad not responding to your commands, and the keypad beeping intermittently.

What Type of Battery Does the Keypad Use?

The type of battery that is used in your Genie garage door opener keypad will depend on the model and make of your keypad. Most Genie keypads use either a 9-volt battery or a CR2032 battery. It’s important to make sure you use the right type of battery to ensure the keypad works properly.

Where to Buy Replacement Batteries

Replacement batteries for Genie garage door opener keypads can be purchased at most home improvement stores, electronics stores, and online retailers. Make sure to check the type of battery your keypad uses before purchasing a replacement battery so you get the right one.

How to Replace the Battery

Once you’ve purchased a replacement battery for your Genie garage door opener keypad, the next step is to replace the old battery. To do this, you’ll need to open the keypad. Most Genie keypads have a removable lid that can be opened by pressing down on the latch. Once the lid is open, you’ll be able to see the battery compartment. Remove the old battery and insert the new one. Make sure the battery is properly inserted and then close the lid of the keypad.

Testing the Keypad

Once you’ve replaced the battery, it’s important to test the keypad to make sure it’s working properly. To do this, press the buttons on the keypad. The keypad should light up and respond to your commands. If it doesn’t, you may need to replace the battery again.

Conclusion

Replacing the battery in your Genie garage door opener keypad is an easy task that can be done in a matter of minutes. All you need to do is make sure you have the right type of battery, open the keypad, replace the old battery, and test the keypad to make sure it’s working properly. By following these steps, you can make sure your Genie garage door opener keypad is always in good working order.

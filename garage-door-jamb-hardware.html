Everything You Need To Know About Garage Door Jamb Hardware

Garage door jamb hardware is one of the most important components of your garage door system. Without the right hardware, your garage door could be exposed to potential security threats or damage. In this article, we'll explain what garage door jamb hardware is, why it's important, and what types of hardware are available for your garage door.

What is Garage Door Jamb Hardware?

Garage door jamb hardware consists of the components that attach the garage door to the jamb, or frame, of the garage. This hardware includes hinges, locks, and other related items. The hardware is used to secure the door in place and prevent it from being forced open. It also helps to keep the door from sagging or coming off its track.

Why is Garage Door Jamb Hardware Important?

Garage door jamb hardware is important for several reasons. First and foremost, it ensures the safety of your home by keeping the door secure. The hardware also helps to keep the door from sagging or coming off the track, which can be dangerous if the door is not properly installed. Finally, it helps to keep the door looking neat and tidy, as well as ensuring that it's in good working condition.

Types of Garage Door Jamb Hardware

There are several different types of garage door jamb hardware available. The most common type is the traditional hinge, which is used to attach the door to the jamb. Other types of hardware include locks, catches, and rollers. All of these components can be purchased separately or as part of a complete door system.

Hinges

Hinges are the most common type of garage door jamb hardware. Hinges are typically made from either steel or aluminum and come in a variety of sizes and styles. Hinges are responsible for keeping the door securely in place and preventing it from sagging or coming off its track. When selecting hinges, it's important to make sure they are compatible with the type of door you have.

Locks

Locks are another important component of garage door jamb hardware. Locks are used to secure the door in place and prevent it from being forced open. They also help to keep the door looking neat and tidy. When selecting locks, make sure they are compatible with the type of door you have and also look at the different types of locks available.

Catches

Catches are another type of garage door jamb hardware. Catches are typically made from either steel or aluminum and are used to help keep the door in place. They are usually installed at the top of the door and act as a latch, preventing the door from being opened from the outside. It's important to make sure that the catch you choose is compatible with the type of door you have.

Rollers

Rollers are the last type of garage door jamb hardware. Rollers are typically made from either steel or aluminum and are used to help keep the door in place. They are usually installed at the bottom of the door and help to keep the door from sagging or coming off its track. When selecting rollers, make sure they are compatible with the type of door you have.

Garage door jamb hardware is an important component of your garage door system. By understanding what it is, why it's important, and the different types of hardware available, you can make sure your garage door is secure and operating properly. If you have any questions about selecting the right hardware for your garage door, contact a professional garage door installer.

How Much Does A Garage Door Motor Replacement Cost?

It’s no secret that garage door motors are expensive and if yours has broken down, you’re probably wondering how much a replacement will cost. The cost of a new motor can vary greatly depending on the type, size and brand of the motor, and the amount of installation work required. Let’s take a look at what goes into the cost of a garage door motor replacement.

Factors that Affect the Cost of a Garage Door Motor Replacement

The most important factor to consider when calculating the cost of a new garage door motor is the type of motor you need. The two main types of motors are chain-driven and belt-driven. Chain-driven motors are usually cheaper, while belt-driven motors are more expensive, but quieter and more efficient. The size of the motor is also important, as larger motors will cost more. The type of material used for the motor is also a factor – steel motors are cheaper, but aluminum motors are more expensive.

The brand of the motor is also important. Some brands are known for their high-quality parts, while others are more budget-friendly. The brand you choose will also determine how much installation work is required. Some brands may require special tools or additional parts, which will increase the cost of the replacement motor.

Finally, the amount of installation work required is an important factor. If you’re replacing an old motor, you may need to remove the old motor and install the new one. This requires special tools and knowledge, so hiring a professional to do the job will increase the cost of the replacement motor.

Average Cost of a Garage Door Motor Replacement

The average cost of a garage door motor replacement is between $200 and $500. The exact cost will depend on the type and size of the motor, the brand, and the amount of installation work required. If you’re hiring a professional to install the motor, you can expect to pay an additional $100 to $200.

Should You Hire a Professional to Install the Motor?

If you’re not confident in your ability to install the new motor, it’s best to hire a professional. Installing a garage door motor is a complex job that requires special tools and knowledge. A professional can ensure that the job is done correctly and that the motor is installed properly. This will reduce the chance of problems in the future.

Hiring a professional will also save you time. Installing a garage door motor is a time-consuming job, and it’s likely that you don’t have the time to do it yourself. Hiring a professional will save you the time and hassle.

Conclusion

The cost of a garage door motor replacement can vary greatly depending on the type, size and brand of the motor, and the amount of installation work required. On average, a new motor will cost between $200 and $500, with additional costs for installation. If you’re not confident in your ability to install the motor, it’s best to hire a professional.

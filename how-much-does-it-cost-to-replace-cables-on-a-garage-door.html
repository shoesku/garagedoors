What Does It Cost To Replace Garage Door Cables?

A garage door cable is a very important part of a garage door system. It is responsible for safely transferring the weight of the garage door so that it can open and close properly. Unfortunately, over time, these cables can break or become damaged due to wear and tear. If this happens, it is important to replace the cables as soon as possible to ensure the safety of your home.

When it comes to replacing garage door cables, the cost can vary widely depending on the type of garage door you have, the type of cable you need, and the company you hire to do the work. On average, most garage door cable replacements range from $150 to $250, with the higher end of the range including labor costs.

What Factors Affect the Cost of Garage Door Cable Replacement?

There are several factors that can affect the cost of replacing garage door cables. These include:

  • The type of garage door you have – If you have a manual garage door, the cost of replacing the cables is likely to be lower than if you have an electric garage door.
  • The type of cable you need – Different types of garage door cables have different costs. For example, some cables are heavier duty and may cost more than lighter duty cables.
  • The company you hire – Different companies charge different rates for their services. It is important to shop around to get the best price.

It is also important to note that some companies may require the installation of new cables as part of the replacement process. If this is the case, the cost of the replacement may be higher than if the cables were simply replaced.

How to Find a Company to Replace Garage Door Cables

When looking for a company to replace your garage door cables, it is important to do some research. You should look for a company that is experienced in replacing garage door cables and has a good reputation. You can find reviews online from other customers to help you make an informed decision.

It is also important to make sure the company you choose is licensed and insured. This will ensure that if something goes wrong, you are protected and can get the help you need.

What to Expect When Replacing Garage Door Cables

When you hire a company to replace your garage door cables, they will typically start by inspecting your garage door and making sure it is in good working order. They will then measure the cables and order new ones, if necessary. Once the new cables arrive, they will install them, test the door, and make any necessary adjustments.

The entire process usually takes between one and two hours, depending on the type of garage door you have and the company you hire.

Conclusion

Replacing garage door cables is an important part of maintaining a safe and secure home. The cost of replacing the cables can vary widely depending on the type of garage door you have, the type of cable you need, and the company you hire to do the work. It is important to shop around and find the best price for the job.

When looking for a company to replace your garage door cables, it is important to do your research. Look for a company that is experienced in replacing garage door cables and has a good reputation. Make sure the company you choose is licensed and insured to ensure that you are protected.

How Much Does A New Garage Door Cost In Nz?

When it comes to renovating your home, the garage door is an important area to consider. Not only does a new garage door add a great deal of aesthetic value to a home, but it also improves the security of your property. But how much does a new garage door cost in New Zealand? It all depends on the type of garage door you choose.

Types of Garage Doors

There are three main types of garage doors that you can choose from: sectional garage doors, tilt doors and roller doors. Each type of garage door has its own unique advantages and disadvantages. To help you make an informed decision, here are the details of each type.

Sectional Garage Doors

Sectional garage doors are one of the most popular choices in New Zealand. They are made up of several sections that are connected together, which helps to provide maximum strength and durability. These doors are also easy to open and close, which is a great benefit for those who use their garage often. The cost of a sectional garage door will depend on the size, material and type of opener, but generally it will cost between $1,000 and $4,000.

Tilt Doors

Tilt doors are a great choice if you are looking for a more economical option. They are made from a single panel that is mounted on hinges, which makes them easier to install and maintain. The cost of a tilt door is generally lower than the other types of doors, with prices ranging from $500 to $2,000.

Roller Doors

Roller doors are a great option if you want to save space, as they can be easily opened and closed without taking up too much space. They are made from a single panel that is rolled up when opened and down when closed. The cost of a roller door will depend on the size, material and type of opener, but generally it will cost between $1,000 and $3,000.

Factors That Affect Garage Door Costs

When it comes to choosing a new garage door, there are several factors that will affect the cost. These include the type of door, the size of the door, the material used to manufacture the door, the type of opener and the installation costs. It is important to consider all of these factors before making a decision.

Additional Costs

In addition to the cost of the door itself, there are also additional costs associated with the installation of a new garage door. This includes the cost of any additional materials such as screws, hinges and tracks, as well as the cost of professional installation. It is important to factor these costs into your budget when deciding on the cost of your new garage door.

Conclusion

A new garage door can be a great addition to any home, but it is important to consider all the factors when deciding on the cost. The type of door, size, material, opener and installation costs will all affect the overall cost of the door. In New Zealand, the cost of a new garage door can range from $500 to $4,000 depending on the type of door you choose.

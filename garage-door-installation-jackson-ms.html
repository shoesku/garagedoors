What’s Involved in Garage Door Installation?

Garage door installation involves more than just taking out an old door and putting in a new one. It also involves making sure the door is properly aligned and balanced, and that the components, such as the tracks and springs, are in good working order. It’s important to make sure the installation is done properly in order to ensure that your door is safe and secure. Once the installation is complete, the technician should provide you with a warranty on their work.

Common Problems to Look Out for During Garage Door Installation

When it comes to garage door installation, there are a few common problems that you should look out for. The most common issue is misalignment, which is when the door isn’t properly aligned or balanced. This can cause the door to be difficult to open or close, or for it to make loud noises when it moves. Additionally, if the tracks and springs aren’t properly installed, the door could be unsafe or could break down prematurely. Finally, if the door isn’t properly secured to the frame, it could easily be pushed in, allowing intruders to access your home.

What Should You Expect During the Installation Process?

When you hire a professional for garage door installation in Jackson, MS, you can expect them to begin by removing the old door. Then, they’ll measure the opening to ensure that the new door will fit correctly. Once the measurements are taken, they’ll install the new door, including the tracks and springs. Finally, they’ll adjust the door to make sure that it’s properly aligned and balanced, and that it’s secure and safe. The entire process should take around two to four hours, depending on the type of door you’ve chosen.

How Much Does Garage Door Installation Cost?

If you’re looking for a higher-end door, such as one made of wood or metal, the cost could be higher. Additionally, if you need to replace the tracks and springs, or if the installation is more complicated, the cost could also be higher.

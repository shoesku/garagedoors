Features to Consider

When shopping for a new garage door motor battery, there are a few features you should consider. Types of Batteries

There are several types of batteries available for purchase. The most common type of battery for garage door motors is the lead-acid battery. These batteries are known for their long-lasting performance and are generally the most affordable option. However, they are also the heaviest and require frequent maintenance. Lithium-ion batteries are becoming increasingly popular and are considered the best option for most applications. These batteries are lighter and require less maintenance, but they tend to be more expensive.

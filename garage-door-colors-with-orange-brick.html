What Color Should You Choose?

When it comes to choosing a garage door color to go with orange brick, the key is to find a color that complements the brick without overpowering it. Many homeowners choose to go with a neutral color like white, gray, or black. These colors are subtle and can help to bring out the beauty of the orange brick without clashing with it. If you want to add a bit of color to your home, there are a few other colors that can work well with orange brick. To bring out the warmth of the brick, try choosing a dark green, blue, or brown. Adding Accents

Another way to enhance the look of the orange brick is to add a few accent colors. Depending on the color of the brick, you can choose a few accent colors to add a bit of contrast.

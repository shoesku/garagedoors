Are you looking for a way to keep the bugs, dirt, and debris out of your garage while still letting the cool air in? Look no further than garage door pull up screens. These screens provide a great way to enjoy the summer breeze without any of the hassle of dealing with pesky insects or dirt. Plus, they are easy to install, making them a great DIY project for any homeowner.

Garage door pull up screens are designed to be easily attached to the frame of your garage door. With a few simple tools, such as a drill and a screwdriver, you can easily install a garage door pull up screen in no time. The first step is to determine the size of the screen you need. Once you have the size of the screen, you can begin to measure and mark the area where the screen will be installed. Be sure to account for any obstructions, such as doorknobs or hinges, before you begin drilling.

Installing the Screen

Once you’ve measured and marked the area for your garage door pull up screen, you can begin the installation process. First, you’ll need to attach the screen to the frame of your garage door. This is done by drilling holes in the frame and then using screws to secure the screen to the frame. Be sure to use the appropriate screws for your particular garage door.

Next, you’ll need to attach the screen to the track. This is done by attaching one end of the screen to the track and then using a series of clips and screws to secure the screen to the track. Be sure to check the instructions that come with the screen to ensure you are attaching it correctly. Once the screen is attached, you can then move on to attaching the pull up handle.

Attaching the Handle

The handle is the last step in installing a garage door pull up screen. To attach the handle, you’ll need to drill a hole in the frame of your garage door and then use a screwdriver to secure the handle to the frame. Be sure to use the appropriate screws for your particular garage door. Once the handle is attached, you can then test out the screen by opening and closing it to make sure it works properly.

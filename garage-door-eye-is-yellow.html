Causes of a Yellow Garage Door Eye

There are a few potential causes of a yellow garage door eye. The first is that the eye could be malfunctioning. This could be due to a build-up of dirt or dust, or a malfunctioning part. Another potential cause of the yellow eye is that it could be blocked or partially blocked.

How To Fix A Craftsman 12 Hp Garage Door Not Opening All The Way

If you have a Craftsman 12 HP garage door opener and it's not opening all the way, then you may be experiencing a common problem. Many Craftsman garage door openers have a safety feature that prevents the door from opening too far, and this can be a real nuisance. However, the good news is that this problem can be easily fixed. In this article, we'll take a look at how to diagnose and fix a Craftsman 12 HP garage door not opening all the way.

Diagnosing the Problem

The first step in diagnosing this problem is to determine if the problem lies with the garage door opener or with the door itself. If the problem lies with the opener, then it could be an issue with the safety feature. If the problem lies with the garage door, then it could be an issue with the springs or tracks. To diagnose the problem, you'll first want to check the settings on the opener itself. Make sure that the safety features are not set too high, and that the door is not being opened too far.

If the settings appear to be correct, then you can move on to checking the door itself. Start by examining the garage door springs and tracks. If either of these is damaged or worn, then it could be the cause of the problem. It's also possible that the problem lies with the door itself; if the door is warped or out of alignment, then it may not be able to open all the way. Once you've determined the source of the problem, you can move on to fixing it.

Fixing the Problem

If the problem lies with the opener, then you'll want to adjust the safety settings. The safety settings are located on the back of the opener, and you can adjust the settings to allow the door to open further. This should solve the problem. If the problem lies with the door itself, then you'll need to make some adjustments. If the springs or tracks are damaged, then you'll need to replace them. If the door is warped or out of alignment, then you'll need to adjust it so that it can open further.

Hiring a Professional

If you're not comfortable making the necessary repairs or adjustments to your garage door, then you may want to consider hiring a professional. A professional will be able to diagnose the problem and make the necessary repairs quickly and safely. In addition, a professional will be able to adjust the settings on the opener to make sure that the door can open all the way without any problems.

Conclusion

If your Craftsman 12 HP garage door opener is not opening all the way, then you may be experiencing a common problem. Fortunately, this problem can usually be fixed by adjusting the settings on the opener or making some adjustments to the door itself. If you're not comfortable making the necessary repairs, then you may want to consider hiring a professional. With a professional, you can be sure that the job will be done correctly and that your garage door will be able to open all the way.

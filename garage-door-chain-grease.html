The Benefits Of Greasing Your Garage Door Chain

Whether you’re just getting into the world of home repair and maintenance or you’ve been doing it for years, you’ve probably heard that you need to grease your garage door chain. In this article, we’re going to discuss why greasing your chain is so important and how you can do it yourself. Keep reading to learn why you should never neglect this important task.

Why Grease the Chain?

When you grease your garage door chain, it helps to keep everything running smoothly and prevents any unnecessary wear and tear. The chain is a very important part of the garage door and if it’s not properly lubricated, it can cause the door to become stuck and malfunction. Greasing the chain also makes sure that the chain doesn’t rust or corrode, which can be a common problem in more humid climates.

When Should You Grease Your Chain?

Ideally, you should grease the chain at least once a year. However, if you live in a particularly humid climate or your garage door is used often, you may need to grease the chain more frequently. If you notice that the chain is starting to move slowly or is making squeaking noises, it’s definitely time to grease it.

What Grease Should You Use?

When it comes to greasing your garage door chain, you should always use a high-quality grease specifically designed for garage door chains. Generally, these are white lithium greases that are designed to withstand extreme temperatures and humidity. It’s important to use the correct grease to ensure that the chain is properly lubricated and won’t malfunction.

How to Grease the Chain

Greasing the chain isn’t a difficult task and can usually be done in just a few minutes. First, you’ll want to open the garage door and clean the chain with a dry cloth to remove any dirt or debris. Once the chain is clean, apply the grease along the entire length of the chain, making sure to get into all the crevices and tight spots. Once you’ve applied the grease, open and close the garage door a few times to distribute the grease evenly and then wipe off any excess grease with a dry cloth before closing the door.

Other Maintenance Tips

In addition to greasing the chain, there are a few other maintenance tips that you can use to keep your garage door working properly. Make sure to check the springs and cables regularly and replace them if they appear worn or damaged. If you have a manual garage door, make sure to check the screws and bolts regularly to make sure none of them are loose or missing. Finally, if you have an automatic garage door, make sure to regularly inspect the sensors and replace the batteries if necessary.

Contact a Professional for Help

If you’re not comfortable greasing the chain yourself or need help with any other repairs or maintenance, you should contact a professional. A qualified technician will be able to diagnose any problems you may be having and recommend the best course of action. They will also be able to help you with any other maintenance tasks that you may not feel comfortable doing yourself.

Conclusion

Greasing your garage door chain is an important part of keeping it in good working order. Make sure to grease the chain at least once a year and clean and inspect it regularly. If you’re not comfortable greasing the chain yourself, contact a professional for help. With regular maintenance and care, your garage door will last for years to come.

What Are Garage Door Springs?

Garage door springs are an essential part of your garage door system. They are responsible for providing the necessary tension and balance needed to open and close your garage door smoothly and efficiently. Without them, you wouldn’t be able to open and close your garage door at all. There are two types of garage door springs: torsion springs and extension springs. Torsion springs are wound up when the garage door is opened and unwound when the door is closed. Extension springs on the other hand, are stretched when the garage door is opened and released when the door is closed.

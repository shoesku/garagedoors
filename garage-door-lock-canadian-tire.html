The Different Types of Garage Door Locks

When it comes to garage door locks, there are three main types: deadbolts, keyed locks, and combination locks. Deadbolts are the most secure option, as they require a key to unlock and are very difficult to pick. Keyed locks are slightly less secure, as they can be picked with the right tools, but they offer the convenience of not needing to carry a key. Combination locks are the least secure option, as anyone with the correct combination can unlock them. However, if you have a larger family, combination locks are a great way to make sure everyone can access the garage.

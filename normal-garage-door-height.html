What Is The Normal Height Of A Garage Door?

When it comes to the installation of a new garage door, it is important to understand the normal height of a garage door. This height will determine the size of the door you need to purchase and the amount of space you have available in your garage. The average garage door is usually 7 feet tall, while custom sizes can range from 6 feet to 8 feet. It is important to understand the size and measurement of your garage before making a purchase, as the right size door can make or break the success of your garage.

Factors to Consider When Determining the Height of Your Garage Door

When determining the normal height of a garage door, there are a few factors to take into consideration. The first is the size of your garage, as a larger garage may require a taller door. Additionally, you should consider the type of vehicle you will be parking in the garage. If you have a larger vehicle, such as an SUV or a truck, you may want to opt for a taller door. The standard height of a garage door is 7 feet, but you may want to opt for a taller door if you plan to park larger vehicles in the space.

Types of Garage Doors

Another factor to take into consideration when determining the normal height of a garage door is the type of door you would like to install. There are several different types of garage doors available on the market, and they come in a variety of heights. The most common types include roll-up, tilt-up, and sectional garage doors. Roll-up doors are the classic style of door and are usually the most cost-effective option. Tilt-up doors are a great choice for those who want to be able to open and close the garage door manually, as they can be tilted up to open and down to close. Sectional doors are more secure and energy-efficient, as they are made from several panels that are connected together. The average height of these types of doors is 7 feet, but you can also find taller doors if you need them.

Benefits of Installing a Taller Garage Door

Installing a taller garage door can be beneficial for a variety of reasons. For instance, taller doors allow you to park larger vehicles in the space without compromising the safety or security of the garage. Additionally, taller doors can provide additional insulation and energy efficiency, as they can keep the garage’s interior temperature more consistent. And finally, taller doors can add value to your home, as they can be seen as a luxurious and stylish feature.

Installation Considerations

If you decide to install a taller garage door, there are a few things you need to consider. Firstly, you should ensure that the door is installed correctly to ensure it opens and closes properly. Additionally, you should consider the weight of the door when determining the size of the door you will need. Heavier doors require heavier duty hardware, which can add to the overall cost of the installation. Finally, you should consider the cost of the door itself, as taller doors tend to be more expensive than standard-sized doors.

Conclusion

The normal height of a garage door is usually 7 feet, but custom sizes can range from 6 feet to 8 feet. It is important to consider the size of your garage, the type of vehicle you plan to park in the garage, and the type of door you would like to install when determining the normal height of a garage door. Installing a taller garage door can be beneficial for a variety of reasons, including providing additional insulation and energy efficiency, and adding value to your home. However, it is important to consider the weight of the door and the cost of the installation when selecting a taller door.

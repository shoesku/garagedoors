How To Fix A Garage Door That Won’t Close All The Way Down

Best Garage doors and references for house or residential with custom material and more design. We Share more picture design with HD quality images.

It can be a real headache when your garage door won’t close all the way down. Not only does it leave your home vulnerable to intruders, but it can also be an inconvenience when you’re trying to get in or out of your garage. Fortunately, there are a few things you can do to get your garage door to close all the way down. In this article, we’ll outline the most common reasons why your garage door won’t close all the way down and what you can do to fix it.

Check the Photo Eye

The first thing you should do when your garage door won’t close all the way down is to check the photo eye. The photo eye is a safety device that is designed to prevent the garage door from closing if something is blocking the path. If the photo eye is not aligned properly, or if it is blocked by dirt or debris, your garage door won’t close all the way down. To fix this, you’ll need to clean the photo eye and align it so that it is pointing directly at the other photo eye.

Check the Springs

If the photo eye is not the problem, then you should check the springs. The springs are what give the garage door its power and if they are worn or damaged, then the garage door won’t close all the way down. To check the springs, you’ll need to open the garage door and look for any signs of wear or damage. If you find any, then you should replace them with new springs. This is a job that is best left to a professional, so you should hire a garage door repair service to handle the job.

Check the Tracks

Another common cause of a garage door not closing all the way down is a problem with the tracks. The tracks are what the door slides on and if they are bent or damaged, then the door won’t be able to close properly. To check the tracks, you’ll need to open the garage door and look for any signs of damage. If you find any, then you should call a professional to straighten the tracks and repair any damage.

Check the Motor

Finally, if none of the other steps have solved the problem, then you should check the motor. The motor is what powers the door and if it is not working correctly, then the door won’t close all the way down. To check the motor, you’ll need to open the door and look for any signs of damage or wear. If you find any, then you should call a professional to repair or replace the motor.

Conclusion

If your garage door won’t close all the way down, then there are several things you can do to try and fix the problem. The first step is to check the photo eye, and if that doesn’t work, then you should check the springs, tracks, and motor. If none of those steps fix the problem, then you should call a professional garage door repair service to take a look and make the necessary repairs. With the right knowledge and tools, you can get your garage door working like new in no time.

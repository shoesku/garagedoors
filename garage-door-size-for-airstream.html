A Comprehensive Guide To Choosing The Right Size Garage Door For Airstream

Buying an Airstream is an exciting purchase, and it can be a great way to enjoy the open road and see the world. However, when it comes to protecting your Airstream, you will need to make sure you have the right size garage door. The size of your garage door will depend on a number of factors, including the size of your Airstream and the size of your garage. In this guide, we will cover the different sizes of Airstreams and how to choose the right size garage door for your needs.

What Size Is An Airstream?

Airstreams come in a variety of sizes, ranging from small campers to large luxury motorhomes. The most common sizes are 16, 20, and 24 feet. The size you choose will depend on how much room you need for your belongings and how many people you plan to travel with. Airstreams can also come with slide-outs, which provide extra space when you're parked.

What Size Garage Door Do I Need?

The size of your garage door will depend on the size of your Airstream and the size of your garage. If you have a 16-foot Airstream, you will need a garage door that is at least 19 feet wide and 8 feet tall. For a 20-foot Airstream, you will need a garage door that is at least 22 feet wide and 8 feet tall. And for a 24-foot Airstream, you will need a garage door that is at least 25 feet wide and 8 feet tall.

What Else Do I Need To Consider?

When selecting the size of your garage door, you will also need to consider the headroom of your garage. If your garage has a low ceiling, then you may need to opt for a smaller size door. Additionally, if you have a high ceiling, then you may need to opt for a larger size door to ensure that your Airstream fits inside.

What Are The Types Of Garage Doors Available?

There are several types of garage doors available, including sectional, roller, and tilt. Sectional garage doors are the most common type of door and they are typically made from panels that are hinged together. Roller doors are made from one solid piece of material and they roll up and down on a track. Finally, tilt doors are made from one solid piece of material and they tilt up and down on a track.

What Are The Benefits Of Installing A Garage Door?

Installing a garage door can provide many benefits for your Airstream. It can provide protection from the elements, such as rain and snow, and it can also provide a secure place to store your Airstream when it is not in use. Additionally, installing a garage door can also add value to your property.

What Are The Different Types Of Garage Door Openers?

When it comes to garage door openers, there are several types available, including chain, belt, and direct drive. Chain openers are the most common and they consist of a chain that is attached to the door, which is then pulled by a motor to open and close the door. Belt openers are quieter than chain openers and they use a belt instead of a chain to open and close the door. Finally, direct drive openers are the quietest type of opener and they use a direct drive motor to open and close the door.

Conclusion

Choosing the right size garage door for your Airstream is an important decision. The size of your Airstream and your garage will determine the size of the door you need. Additionally, you will need to consider the type of opener you want to use and the headroom of your garage. With the right size garage door, you can ensure that your Airstream is protected from the elements and secure when not in use.

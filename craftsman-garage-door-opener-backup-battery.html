How Does a Backup Battery Work?

A Craftsman garage door opener backup battery is designed to keep your garage door functioning during a power outage. The battery is installed inside the garage door opener, and it is connected to the power supply so that it will always be charged. When the power goes out, the battery takes over and powers the garage door opener, allowing you to open and close the door as normal. This ensures that your garage door will remain operational, even during a power outage.

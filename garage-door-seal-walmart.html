Types of Garage Door Seals

There are several different types of garage door seals available. The most common are the rubber and foam types. Rubber seals are the most commonly used type as they provide a tight seal and are relatively inexpensive. They are also easy to install and typically require only basic tools. Foam seals are less common, but they provide a tighter seal and are more durable than rubber seals. Both types of seals can be found at most home improvement stores.

What are Garage Door Seals Used For?

Garage door seals are used to keep out dirt, dust, and moisture from entering the garage. They also help to keep out insects, birds, and other pests. By keeping out these elements, garage door seals help to maintain the temperature inside of the garage and help to keep out odors. Additionally, they can help to reduce the amount of noise that enters the garage and help to keep out drafts.

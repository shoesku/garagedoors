What Is a Garage Door Draught Excluder Snake?

A garage door draught excluder snake is a simple device that can be fitted to the bottom of your garage door. It’s made from a flexible plastic material that is designed to fit snugly against the floor of your garage. The material is flexible enough to allow the door to be opened and closed, but when the door is closed, the draught excluder snake creates a tight seal that prevents cold air from entering the garage.

How Does a Garage Door Draught Excluder Snake Work?

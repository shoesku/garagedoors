Are you looking to upgrade your home's garage with a double garage door? If so, you'll need to know the standard double garage door dimensions you'll be working with. We'll go over the height, width, and depth of a standard double garage door, as well as the available sizes and some of the other features to consider while shopping.

Standard Double Garage Door Dimensions

The standard double garage door typically has an average width of 16 feet. The height of the door will depend on the type of garage door you choose, but the standard height is 7 to 8 feet. The depth of the door is an important consideration, as it will determine how much headroom you have in the garage for vehicles and other items that can be stored. The average depth of a double garage door is 12 to 14 inches.

Available Sizes

When it comes to double garage doors, there are several sizes available. You can find single and double doors available in sizes ranging from 8 feet wide to 18 feet wide. Some of the available door heights are 7 feet, 8 feet, and 9 feet. The depth of the door will also vary depending on the type, so make sure to check the product description before purchasing a double garage door.

Other Features to Consider

When purchasing a double garage door, you'll also want to consider some of the other features available. For example, you can choose from insulated doors, which offer more protection from the elements and help keep your garage cooler in the summer. You can also find doors with windows, specialty finishes, and unique hardware for a more customized look.

Choosing the Right Door for Your Needs

When it comes to choosing the right double garage door, it's important to consider your budget and the look you want for your home. You'll also want to consider the size of your garage, as well as the type of door you choose. The most important factor is to make sure the door is properly installed, as this will ensure the safety of your family and your belongings.

Conclusion

Double garage doors can be a great addition to any home, but it's important to know the standard double garage door dimensions before you buy. Make sure to consider the width, height, and depth of the door, as well as the available sizes and features. If you take the time to shop around and research the products you're considering, you'll be sure to find the perfect double garage door for your needs.

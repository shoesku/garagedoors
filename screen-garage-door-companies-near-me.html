What Are Screen Garage Doors?

Screen garage doors are a great way to add a little extra security to your home's garage. They are made of a durable mesh material that can withstand the elements, but still allow you to see through it. They are also designed to be easy to open and close, making them great for keeping large items like cars, motorcycles, and boats safe. There are many different kinds of screen garage doors, including ones that have a motorized opening and closing system.

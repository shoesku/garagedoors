Can You Increase Your Garage Door Height?

Introduction

Garage doors have become an integral part of any home, providing an easy way to store your vehicle and other items. But for some homeowners, the standard door height isn't enough. Fortunately, there are ways to increase your garage door height to make it more accommodating. In this article, we'll explore some of the ways you can go about doing this. Read on to learn more.

Replace Your Existing Door

One of the easiest ways to increase your garage door height is to simply replace it with a taller one. This is a great option if the existing door is old or damaged, as it allows you to get a new one that fits your needs. The downside is that this can be an expensive option, as you'll have to purchase a new door, pay for installation, and possibly make other modifications to ensure proper fit.

Add a Header

If you're looking for a more cost-effective option, you can consider adding a header above your existing door. This is a simple and straightforward way to increase the height of your garage door without having to replace the whole thing. The header can be made from wood or metal, depending on your budget and preference. It's important to note, however, that the header must be properly installed and secured to prevent it from falling down.

Add a Garage Door Lift

Another solution is to install a garage door lift. This is a great option if you want to add more height to your existing door, but don't want to replace it. The lift works by extending the door's track, allowing it to rise higher. It's important to note, however, that you'll need to make sure the lift is properly installed and maintained to ensure it works correctly.

Add a Strut

If you're looking for a more permanent solution, you can consider adding a strut. This is a metal bar that's attached to the top of the door and extends outward, allowing it to be higher. The downside is that this can be a costly option, but it can also be a great way to make sure your door is secure and stable. It's important to note, however, that you'll need to make sure the strut is properly installed and maintained to ensure it works correctly.

Conclusion

Increasing the height of your garage door can be a great way to make it more accommodating and provide more storage space. There are several options available, from replacing your existing door to adding a header, lift, or strut. It's important to consider all of your options before making a decision and to ensure that whatever option you choose is properly installed and maintained to ensure it works correctly.

FAQs

How much does it cost to increase the height of a garage door?

The cost of increasing the height of a garage door depends on the method you choose. Replacing the door is usually the most expensive option, while adding a header, lift, or strut can be more cost-effective. It's important to do your research and compare prices before making a decision.

How can I make sure my garage door is secure?

It's important to make sure your garage door is properly installed and maintained to ensure it is secure. This includes making sure the tracks are properly aligned, the door is balanced, and the springs are adjusted. Additionally, it's important to make sure the door is properly locked when not in use to prevent unauthorized access.

What are the benefits of increasing the height of a garage door?

Increasing the height of a garage door can make it more accommodating, allowing you to store larger items such as bikes, kayaks, and other recreational equipment. Additionally, it can provide additional security, as it can make it harder for someone to gain unauthorized access. Finally, it can also add aesthetic value to your home, as taller garage doors can make it appear more attractive.

Fortunately, a garage door lock feature has become a standard feature on most of the new garage door openers that are available in the market today.

A garage door lock feature is an important part of home security, as it ensures that your garage door stays closed and secure. The lock is designed to prevent an intruder from entering your garage, even if they have access to the garage door opener. The lock is usually activated with a code or a key and once it is engaged, the door will not open until the code or key is used. This ensures that the door stays closed and secure, even if someone has access to the garage door opener.

The garage door lock feature also helps to prevent accidental openings. If someone accidentally presses the garage door opener button, the door will not open until the code or key is used.

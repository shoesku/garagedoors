What is a garage door bottom seal bulb?

A garage door bottom seal bulb is a device designed to keep your garage clean and dry. It's a small, rubber-like seal that fits along the bottom of your garage door. As the door closes, the seal will compress, creating a tight seal that prevents dirt, dust, and moisture from entering your garage. It also helps to reduce drafts and lower energy costs. The seal also helps to keep pests and rodents from entering your garage as well.

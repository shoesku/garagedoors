What is a Garage Door Chain Belt?

A garage door chain belt is a type of drive belt that is installed on the track of a garage door. This belt is used to drive the door up and down to open and close it. The chain belt is an important component of a garage door system. It is responsible for providing a secure and reliable connection between the door and the track.

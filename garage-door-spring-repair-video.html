What is a Garage Door Spring?

A garage door spring is a metal coil that is attached to your garage door, and helps to lift and lower it. As the door is opened and closed, the spring is stretched and contracted, allowing it to open and close the door. Over time, the springs can become worn out, and will need to be replaced.

Why Do You Need to Repair Your Garage Door Spring?

Garage door springs are an essential part of your garage door's operation. If your garage door spring breaks or becomes too worn out, your door may not open or close properly. In some cases, the door may even become stuck in an open or closed position. If this happens, it's important to repair the spring as soon as possible, so that your door can continue to operate as normal.
